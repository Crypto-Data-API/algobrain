### 2. Important Concepts/Strategies
- **Leveraged Yield Farming**: Borrowing assets (e.g., via Aave flash loans) to amplify LP positions for 50-200%+ APY. High-reward for experienced traders but risks liquidation[1].
- **Delta-Neutral Yield Farming**: Hedging positions (e.g., via perps) to earn yield without directional risk, targeting 10-30% stable APY. Core 2026 strategy for risk-adjusted returns[2].
- **Restaking**: Restaking liquid-staked assets (e.g., LSTs like stETH) on EigenLayer for layered yields. Boosts ETH/Solana base staking to 20-100% APY[2][4].
- **Yield Tokenization**: Splitting yields into tradable PT/YT tokens (e.g., Pendle) for fixed-rate selling or speculation. Allows traders to trade future yields like derivatives[2].
- **Cross-Chain Yield Farming**: Bridging assets (e.g., via Thorchain) to chase highest APYs across chains. Unlocks 20-100% returns via ecosystem arbitrage[1].
- **Liquid Staking Derivatives (LSDs)**: Staking ETH/Solana via Lido/Jito for stTokens usable in farming. Enables yield stacking without lockups[4].
