# Trump Wins 2024 Election — Crypto Rallies Hard

## What Happened

On November 5, 2024, Donald Trump won the U.S. presidential election, defeating Vice President Kamala Harris. Trump had campaigned aggressively on pro-crypto policies, including promises to fire SEC Chair Gary Gensler, create a strategic Bitcoin reserve, and make the United States the "crypto capital of the world." The crypto market reacted with an explosive rally as traders priced in a dramatically friendlier regulatory environment. Crypto-focused Super PACs like Fairshake had spent over $130 million supporting pro-crypto candidates across both parties, resulting in a historically favorable Congress for the industry.

## Market Impact

- **BTC price**: Surged from ~$69,000 to above $75,000 on election night, continuing higher in subsequent days
- **[[coinbase]] stock**: Rallied approximately 30% in the days following the election
- **Altcoins**: Broad-based rally across the sector; DeFi tokens surged on deregulation hopes
- **Crypto-related stocks**: MicroStrategy, Marathon Digital, Riot Platforms all surged double digits
- **S&P 500**: Rallied on pro-business, tax-cut expectations
- **DXY (US Dollar)**: Strengthened initially on fiscal expansion expectations

## Why It Matters

The 2024 election represented a fundamental shift in U.S. crypto policy. Under the Biden administration and SEC Chair Gensler, the industry faced regulation by enforcement, multiple lawsuits, and hostility. Trump's election flipped the narrative entirely: a president openly supporting Bitcoin, a pro-crypto SEC chair appointment, and Congressional allies elected with crypto PAC support. This removed the single biggest overhang on the U.S. crypto industry — regulatory uncertainty.

## Aftermath

Gensler announced his resignation effective January 20, 2025. Trump appointed a pro-crypto SEC chair and began executive actions toward a strategic Bitcoin reserve. The regulatory shift catalyzed the final leg of the 2024 bull run, sending BTC through $100K in December. [[coinbase]] and other U.S. crypto companies saw their legal overhang removed. Pro-crypto Congressional candidates backed by Fairshake PAC won in key races across both parties. The broader [[2024-crypto-regulatory-shift]] accelerated dramatically post-election.

## Trading Lessons

- **Political catalysts can be asymmetric**: A Trump win was bullish for crypto in a way that was obvious beforehand, yet markets still repriced violently
- **Position for binary events with defined risk**: Election night volatility was extreme but directional for crypto
- **Regulatory overhang removal is powerful**: Years of accumulated fear unwind rapidly when the threat is removed
- **Crypto-equity correlation spikes on macro events**: [[coinbase]], mining stocks, and MicroStrategy moved as leveraged BTC proxies
- **The rally was just the beginning**: Election night was the starting gun for a multi-week melt-up, not the end of the move
- **Follow the PAC money**: Crypto industry political spending was a leading indicator of favorable policy outcomes
- **Altcoin beta to BTC amplifies on macro catalysts**: DeFi and [[coinbase]]-listed tokens outperformed BTC on a percentage basis during the post-election surge
- **Policy promises vs. execution**: Markets priced in promises immediately, creating risk if execution lagged expectations
