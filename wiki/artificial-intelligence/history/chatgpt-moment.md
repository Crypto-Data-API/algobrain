# The ChatGPT Moment (November 2022)

On November 30, 2022, [[openai|OpenAI]] released **ChatGPT** — a conversational interface to its GPT-3.5 model. It reached 100 million users in two months, making it the fastest-growing consumer application in history. This single product launch redefined public understanding of AI and set off the investment and innovation wave that transformed trading.

## Market Impact

- **NVIDIA** (NVDA) began its ascent — from roughly $150 (pre-split) in late 2022 to above $1,000 by mid-2024, when it executed a 10-for-1 stock split (June 2024). On a split-adjusted basis that is ~$15 to ~$100+ per share
- **AI-related stocks** (Microsoft, Google, AMD) saw massive re-ratings
- **Crypto AI tokens** launched a speculative cycle starting in late 2023
- **Venture capital** pivoted aggressively toward AI startups
- **Every industry** — including finance and trading — began exploring LLM applications

## Why It Changed Trading

Before ChatGPT, using AI for trading required ML engineering expertise, custom model training, and significant infrastructure. After ChatGPT, anyone could:

- Ask an AI to explain complex financial concepts
- Generate Python code for [[backtesting-overview|backtesting]]
- Analyze earnings reports through conversation
- Prototype trading ideas without coding skills

The barrier to entry for AI-assisted trading dropped from "quantitative finance PhD" to "anyone who can type a question."

## The Path to Trading Agents

ChatGPT (Nov 2022) → GPT-4 (Mar 2023) → Function calling (Jun 2023) → [[ai-trading-agents|Trading agents]] (2024) → [[ai-narrative-arc|Agent token mania]] (2025) → long-horizon agentic execution (2026)

Each step added capability: better reasoning, tool use, autonomous action, and — by 2026 — multi-step agents (managed agent platforms, computer use) that plan and execute whole workflows. The ChatGPT moment was the first domino.

## The Trading Lesson

The durable signal from the ChatGPT moment was not "trade the AI narrative tokens" — most of the late-2023/2025 crypto AI-agent speculation round-tripped. It was the **picks-and-shovels** insight: regardless of which model or application won, demand flowed to compute. NVDA, the hyperscalers, and power/datacenter infrastructure captured the most durable value. For traders, the lesson is to separate a genuine, broad technology shift (compute demand, productivity tooling) from the reflexive token-and-thematic-equity froth it spawns — the former compounds, the latter mean-reverts.
