# AI for Scientific Discovery

AI is accelerating scientific research across biology, chemistry, materials science, climate, mathematics, and physics. These breakthroughs have direct investment implications — faster drug discovery, new materials, energy efficiency, and compute demand. [[google-deepmind|DeepMind's]] AlphaFold (protein structure prediction) is the defining example: a single AI system solved a 50-year-old biology grand challenge.

## Breakthrough Applications

### Protein Structure Prediction (AlphaFold)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Problem** | Predict 3D protein structure from amino acid sequence |
| **Significance** | 50-year grand challenge in biology, fundamental to drug design |
| **Solution** | [[google-deepmind|DeepMind's]] AlphaFold (2020) — deep learning model |
| **Impact** | Predicted structures for 200M+ proteins (entire known protein universe) |
| **Nobel Prize** | 2024 Chemistry Nobel awarded to Demis Hassabis and John Jumper |
| **Trading relevance** | Accelerates drug discovery for every pharma/biotech company |

### Materials Discovery

| Application | AI Method | Impact |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| **Battery materials** | ML screens candidate compounds | Faster path to better EV batteries (affects TSLA, battery stocks) |
| **Semiconductor materials** | Generative models design new chips | Extends Moore's Law, affects [[nvidia-ai|NVIDIA]], INTC, AMD |
| **Superconductors** | AI-guided search for room-temperature superconductors | Would revolutionise energy and computing (speculative) |
| **Catalysts** | ML optimises industrial chemical processes | Energy efficiency, reduces emissions |

Google DeepMind's **GNoME** (2023) discovered 2.2 million new crystal structures — 800x more than the previous 200 years of human research.

### Climate & Weather

| Application | AI Method | Impact |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| **Weather forecasting** | [[foundation-models|Foundation models]] for weather (GraphCast, Pangu-Weather) | 10-day forecasts that beat physics-based models; commodity/energy trading signal |
| **Climate modelling** | ML accelerates climate simulation | Better climate risk assessment for long-term investing |
| **Carbon capture** | AI optimises capture processes | CleanTech investment thesis |
| **Fusion energy** | [[reinforcement-learning|RL]] controls plasma in tokamak reactors (DeepMind) | Long-term energy disruption |

**Trading angle**: AI weather models from DeepMind (GraphCast) and Huawei (Pangu-Weather) outperform traditional NWP models. Commodity traders (energy, agriculture) with access to better weather forecasts have an information edge.

### Mathematics & Theorem Proving

- **AlphaProof** (DeepMind, 2024): Solved International Mathematical Olympiad problems at silver medal level
- **FunSearch** (DeepMind, 2023): Discovered new mathematical constructions using LLMs + evolutionary search
- **Lean/Mathlib**: Formal proof verification where AI assists mathematicians

### Drug Discovery Pipeline

```
Target ID → Molecule Design → Synthesis → Preclinical → Clinical Trials → Approval
   AI         AI              Partial AI    AI              AI assist        Regulatory
```

AI impacts every stage but doesn't replace wet-lab work entirely. The acceleration is in reducing candidates from millions to hundreds computationally before synthesis.
