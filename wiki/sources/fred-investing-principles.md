A short document by [[fred-mcnaught|Fred McNaught]] listing specific investing rules he follows. This appears to be the start of a principles list that was not completed. The full investing philosophy is captured more comprehensively in [[fred-share-investing-guide]].

## Key Claims

1. [HIGH] "I never invest in the automotive industry because it is unpredictable and volatile."
2. [HIGH] "I never consider an investment purely based on tax reasons."

## Notes

These two principles are notable for what they exclude rather than include. The automotive avoidance aligns with Fred's preference for companies with predictable cash flows and defensible competitive positions. The tax principle reflects a focus on investment fundamentals over financial engineering — invest for the right reasons first, then optimise tax treatment.
