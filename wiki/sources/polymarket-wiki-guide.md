# Polymarket: A Comprehensive Wiki Guide

Compiled research document covering Polymarket's founding, technology stack, market categories, key milestones (2020-2026), trading strategies, notable traders, AI/LLM integration, controversies, competitive landscape, POLY token, and developer ecosystem. ## Key Claims

### Platform Facts
1. [HIGH] Polymarket founded in 2020 by Shayne Coplan, headquartered in New York City
2. [HIGH] World's largest decentralized prediction market platform as of 2026
3. [HIGH] $9 billion valuation after $2B investment from ICE (NYSE parent), October 2025
4. [HIGH] Previously raised $200M led by Founders Fund at $1B valuation, mid-2025
5. [HIGH] Binary Yes/No shares priced $0.01-$0.99, settling at $1 or $0 in USDC
6. [HIGH] Built on Polygon PoS (Layer-2), using ERC-1155 Conditional Token Framework
7. [HIGH] Hybrid CLOB: off-chain order matching, on-chain settlement
8. [HIGH] UMA Optimistic Oracle for decentralized resolution (2-hour challenge window, $750 bond)
9. [HIGH] Zero trading fees on transactions
10. [HIGH] All trades denominated in USDC (other assets auto-converted)
