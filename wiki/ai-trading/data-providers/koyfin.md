# Koyfin

## Overview

Financial data and charting platform that delivers institutional-quality fundamental analysis at a fraction of the cost of [[bloomberg-terminal]]. Koyfin is often called "Bloomberg for retail" or "Bloomberg-lite" -- it provides deep fundamental data, powerful screening, comparative charting, economic dashboards, and financial modeling tools through a clean web interface. The free tier is remarkably generous, covering more functionality than most paid platforms. The paid tier fills in the gaps: real-time data, advanced screening, extended history, and customizable dashboards.

## Pricing

- **Free**: generous -- fundamental data, basic charting, screening, economic data, limited history
- **Plus**: ~$25/mo -- extended history, more screening criteria, watchlist alerts
- **Pro**: ~$50/mo -- real-time data, advanced charting, full screening, Excel add-in, priority support
- **Enterprise**: custom -- team features, API, compliance tools
- Free tier alone makes Koyfin worth using for any stock investor

## What You Get (vs Free)

- Real-time quotes (free tier is 15-minute delayed)
- Extended fundamental history: 20+ years of financials vs. 5 years on free
- Advanced stock screener with 100+ fundamental criteria and custom formulas
- Excel add-in for pulling Koyfin data into spreadsheets (similar to Bloomberg BDH/BDP)
- Custom dashboard layouts with persistent watchlists and alert notifications
- Financial model templates and comparative analysis tools
- International stock coverage beyond US markets

## Alpha Edge

- **Bloomberg at 2% of the cost**: the core use case. For $50/mo you get 80% of what a $2,000/mo Bloomberg Terminal offers for equity fundamental analysis. Deep financial statements, valuation multiples, historical comparisons, and peer analysis
- **Comparative charting**: overlay any fundamental metric (P/E, revenue growth, margins) across multiple companies on a single chart. Instantly spot divergences and relative value opportunities
- **Economic dashboards**: macro data (GDP, CPI, employment, yield curves) presented in clean, interactive views for [[macro-trading]] context
- **Screening power**: screen the entire global equity universe on fundamental criteria that matter -- EV/EBITDA, free cash flow yield, margin trends, revenue acceleration -- without the cost of capital IQ or Bloomberg
- **Best value in financial data**: no other platform offers this depth of fundamental analysis at this price point. For [[value-investing]] and [[fundamental-analysis]], Koyfin is the optimal tool

## Key Features

- **Dashboard**: customizable multi-panel views combining charts, fundamentals, news, and watchlists
- **Screener**: advanced equity screener with fundamental, valuation, technical, and estimate criteria
- **Financial Analysis**: deep dive into income statements, balance sheets, cash flow, and key ratios
- **Charting**: multi-series comparative charts with fundamental overlays and annotations
- **Economic Data**: macro indicators, yield curves, and economic calendar
- **Estimates**: consensus analyst estimates, revisions, and earnings history
- **My Models**: basic financial modeling and custom calculation tools

## Who Uses It

- Financial advisors who need institutional-quality data without Bloomberg pricing
- Serious retail investors focused on [[fundamental-analysis]] and [[value-investing]]
- Small fund managers and independent analysts on tight budgets
- Students and academics learning financial analysis with professional-grade tools
- Anyone who has outgrown [[yahoo-finance]] but finds Bloomberg inaccessible
- Long-term investors who care about fundamentals over technical analysis

The honest answer for most stock investors: start with [[yahoo-finance]] for free basics, graduate to Koyfin for serious fundamental work, and only consider [[bloomberg-terminal]] if your firm is paying for it.
