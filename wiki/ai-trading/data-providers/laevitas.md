# Laevitas

## Overview

Crypto derivatives analytics platform providing professional-grade data on options, perpetual futures, and futures across all major exchanges. Laevitas fills a critical gap: while [[spotgamma]] covers equity options and [[glassnode]] covers on-chain fundamentals, Laevitas is the go-to source for crypto derivatives market structure. It tracks funding rates, open interest, options volatility surfaces, term structure, basis trades, and liquidation data across Deribit, Binance, OKX, Bybit, and other exchanges. If you trade crypto derivatives -- especially options or perpetual swaps -- this data is essential.

## Pricing

- **Free**: basic dashboards showing current funding rates, aggregate OI, and top-level derivatives data
- **Pro**: ~$100+/mo -- full historical data, granular analytics, API access, advanced dashboards
- **Enterprise**: custom pricing for institutional-grade feeds and integrations
- Free tier is functional for casual monitoring; Pro unlocks the depth needed for active trading

## What You Get (vs Free)

- Full historical data on funding rates, OI, volatility, and basis across all exchanges
- Options volatility surface: 3D visualization of implied volatility across strikes and expiries
- Granular term structure and basis data for identifying carry trade opportunities
- Cross-exchange comparison tools (funding rates differ across exchanges -- this matters for arb)
- Advanced liquidation analytics: historical liquidation cascades and current liquidation levels
- API access for programmatic integration into trading systems
- Custom dashboards and alert configurations

## Alpha Edge

- **Funding rate optimization**: perpetual futures funding rates vary significantly across exchanges. Laevitas shows real-time and historical funding across all venues, enabling traders to optimize their funding payments or collect funding on delta-neutral positions. This is a consistent income strategy for sophisticated crypto traders
- **Volatility surface for options pricing**: see the entire crypto options vol surface across strikes and expiries. Identify mispriced options, skew trades, and calendar spread opportunities on Deribit
- **Cross-exchange basis for arb**: spot-futures basis and cross-exchange spreads create arbitrage opportunities. Laevitas tracks these in real time, letting traders capture risk-free (or low-risk) basis profits
- **Open interest signals**: large OI buildups at specific strikes or price levels indicate where liquidation cascades or gamma squeezes may occur
- **Liquidation maps**: see where leveraged positions are concentrated and anticipate forced liquidation cascades that create violent price moves

## Key Features

- **Funding Rates Dashboard**: real-time and historical funding rates across all perpetual swap exchanges
- **Options Analytics**: vol surface, skew, term structure, put/call ratios, and OI by strike for crypto options
- **Open Interest Tracking**: aggregate and exchange-level OI for futures and perpetuals
- **Basis & Carry**: spot-futures basis and annualized carry rates across exchanges and expiries
- **Liquidation Data**: real-time and historical liquidation events with aggregated volume
- **Exchange Comparison**: side-by-side metrics across Deribit, Binance, OKX, Bybit, and more

## Who Uses It

- Crypto options traders on Deribit needing professional vol surface analytics
- Perpetual swap traders optimizing funding rate exposure and timing
- Basis/carry traders capturing spot-futures arbitrage across exchanges
- Crypto hedge funds monitoring derivatives market structure for risk management
- Delta-neutral market makers tracking funding and OI dynamics
- Anyone trading crypto with leverage who needs to understand where liquidation cascades will trigger

Think of Laevitas as the [[spotgamma]] equivalent for crypto derivatives -- essential market structure data that reveals the mechanics driving price action beyond simple supply and demand.
