# Nansen

## Overview

On-chain analytics platform built around one killer feature: wallet labeling. Nansen has identified and labeled over 250 million wallets belonging to funds, DAOs, venture capitalists, exchanges, whales, and known entities across Ethereum, Solana, BSC, and other chains. Raw on-chain data is publicly available and free -- but it is completely useless without knowing WHO is behind each address. Nansen solves this by mapping anonymous wallets to real-world entities, then building dashboards on top of that intelligence. Their Smart Money dashboard is the flagship product.

## Pricing

- **Lite**: ~$150/mo -- basic wallet labels, limited Smart Money alerts
- **Standard**: ~$500/mo -- full Smart Money dashboards, token analytics, NFT analytics
- **Enterprise**: $1,000+/mo -- API access, custom alerts, priority support, cross-chain data
- Free tier is extremely limited (basic portfolio tracking only)

## What You Get (vs Free)

- Wallet labels connecting addresses to known funds (a]16z, Paradigm, Alameda remnants, etc.)
- Smart Money dashboards showing what top-performing wallets are buying/selling right now
- Token God Mode -- comprehensive token analytics including holder composition, flow analysis
- NFT analytics: mint tracking, whale NFT purchases, wash trading detection
- Real-time alerts when labeled wallets make significant moves
- Cross-chain tracking across Ethereum, Solana, BSC, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base

## Alpha Edge

- **Smart Money front-running**: see what crypto venture funds and profitable wallets are accumulating before the market catches on. When multiple labeled "smart money" wallets converge on a token, it often precedes significant price moves
- **Exchange flow analysis**: large inflows to exchanges from whale wallets signal incoming sell pressure
- **VC unlock tracking**: monitor venture fund wallets approaching token unlock dates
- **DEX activity**: track which tokens smart money is swapping on [[uniswap]] and other DEXs in real time
- **NFT alpha**: identify which collections institutional wallets and known flippers are minting

## Key Features

- **Smart Money Dashboard**: aggregated view of top-performing wallet activity
- **Token God Mode**: one-page deep dive on any token's on-chain fundamentals
- **Wallet Profiler**: analyze any wallet's full history, PnL, and behavioral patterns
- **NFT Paradise**: NFT-specific analytics, floor price tracking, whale activity
- **Alerts Engine**: custom notifications for wallet movements, token flows, smart money activity
- **Query**: custom on-chain data queries for power users

## Who Uses It

- Crypto hedge funds and trading desks tracking institutional flows
- Venture capital firms doing due diligence on token holder composition
- DeFi researchers analyzing protocol usage and whale behavior
- NFT traders tracking smart money mints and accumulation
- Token projects monitoring their own holder base and whale activity
- Anyone serious about [[crypto-trading]] beyond price charts

Nansen's core moat is the wallet labeling database. Competitors like [[arkham-intelligence]] are building similar capabilities, but Nansen's label coverage and accuracy remain the benchmark.
