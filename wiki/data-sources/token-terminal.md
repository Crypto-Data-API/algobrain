Token Terminal is a crypto analytics platform that applies traditional financial metrics -- revenue, P/E ratios, price-to-sales, earnings, fees -- to blockchain protocols and decentralized applications. The platform is particularly valuable for evaluating whether [[yield-farming]] APYs are sustainable (funded by real protocol revenue) or unsustainable (funded primarily by token emissions that dilute holders).

## Key Features

### Protocol Financial Metrics
- **Revenue:** Total fees generated by a protocol, split between supply-side revenue (paid to LPs/stakers) and protocol revenue (retained by the treasury or token holders)
- **P/E and P/S ratios:** Traditional valuation multiples applied to crypto protocols, enabling cross-protocol comparison
- **Earnings:** Protocol revenue minus token incentive costs -- the true "bottom line" for a DeFi protocol
- **TVL-adjusted metrics:** Revenue per dollar of TVL, helping identify capital-efficient protocols

### DeFi Yield Analysis
- **Fee revenue vs. emissions:** Token Terminal distinguishes between yield funded by real trading fees and yield funded by token emissions. This is the single most important metric for evaluating farm sustainability
- **Protocol comparisons:** Side-by-side comparison of [[aave]] vs. [[compound]], [[uniswap]] vs. [[curve]], etc. on financial fundamentals
- **Historical trends:** Track how protocol revenue changes over time -- declining revenue with rising token emissions is a red flag

### Data Coverage
- Ethereum, Solana, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Polygon, Avalanche, and 20+ other chains
- 500+ protocols tracked including all major DeFi, NFT, and infrastructure projects
- Daily updated data going back to protocol launch

## Trading Relevance

- **Yield sustainability screening:** Before entering a [[defi-yield-farming|yield farm]], check Token Terminal to see if the protocol generates enough fee revenue to sustain its advertised APY. If 90% of yield comes from token emissions, expect APY to decline as emissions taper
- **Protocol fundamental analysis:** P/E ratios help identify undervalued or overvalued DeFi tokens relative to their revenue generation
- **Sector rotation signals:** Rising revenue across lending protocols ([[aave]], [[compound]]) but declining revenue in DEXs may signal shifts in DeFi activity and yield opportunities
- **Due diligence:** Rapid TVL growth with flat or declining revenue is a warning sign -- capital is chasing subsidized yields that will not last

## Pricing

- **Free tier ($0):** Public access to headline dashboards and key metrics, with limits on data export, raw/historical data, and API access
- **Pro tier (~$99/month as of mid-2026):** Full API access, historical/raw data, data exports, custom dashboards, and advanced filtering. Pricing drifts — verify on tokenterminal.com before committing

## Limitations

- Revenue data can lag by 24-48 hours
- Some newer or smaller protocols may have incomplete data
- Token emission costs are estimated and may not capture all forms of dilution
- Does not cover centralized exchange data or off-chain activity
