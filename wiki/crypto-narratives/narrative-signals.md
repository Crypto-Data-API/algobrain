    - `enable` **liquidation-fade** [assets dumped into the thin venue during the cascade, AFTER liquidation_volume_zscore peaks and rolls over] — venue wick is driven by forced selling into a vacuum; fade the over-extension once the cascade exhausts
    - `widen-stops` **market-making** [active quoting on the affected venue's stable pairs during the wick] — depth collapses and price gaps; widen inventory bands / pull quotes to avoid adverse fills, re-engage as depth returns
    - `reduce-size` **funding-carry** [perp positions using the wicking stablecoin as margin/collateral on the affected venue] — the wick can auto-liquidate over-collateralized borrowers via oracle/collateral mark-down — cut margin reliance on the coin during the event
    - `enable` **vol-breakout** [the broad market during the underlying liquidation cascade (BTC/ETH/large-cap)] — severe venue depegs coincide with market-wide cascades (Oct 2025, BTC ~-9%); the cascade itself is a tradeable vol event
    - `no-change` **directional-short** [the stablecoin itself] — do NOT short the stablecoin on the wick — it is fully backed and reverts to par; shorting the dislocation is negative-EV
- **Backtest gate:** `active = (per_venue_price_vs_aggregate_price_spread > 0.01) AND (aggregate_redeemable_price within 0.01 of 1.0) AND (collateralization_ratio_below_1 == FALSE) ; severe = active AND (liquidation_volume_zscore_market_wide > 2 OR amm_pool_imbalance_pct > 0.70 OR exchange_orderbook_depth_within_50bps in bottom decile)`
    - *Usage:* Use as an ON gate to ENABLE venue-isolated mean-reversion (buy-the-wick) on the dislocated venue with target = par, and to ENABLE liquidation-fade post-cascade. As a prior, P(revert-to-par)~high and P(down sustained)~0 for the redeemable coin — but treat the BROAD market during 'severe' as a bearish vol event (cascade). Position the wick arb only on venues where the on-chain redemption/aggregate path is confirmed liquid.
    - *Point-in-time:* Critical: the entry signal requires the AGGREGATE/redeemable price simultaneously available at decision time to confirm the wick is venue-isolated (not a true depeg). Do not use end-of-day reverted price to label the wick — use the intra-event per-venue vs aggregate spread. time_to_revert_to_peg is realized only after the fact; do not condition entry on it. Distinguish from algo-death-spiral via collateralization_ratio_below_1 == FALSE at event time — misclassifying a real insolvency wick (xUSD/deUSD) as a flash wick is the dangerous look-ahead failure.
