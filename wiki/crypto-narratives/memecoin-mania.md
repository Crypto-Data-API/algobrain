Memecoins are coins with no cash flows or utility whose price is driven entirely by attention, celebrity endorsement, and reflexive speculation. Three repeatable patterns recur: celebrity/influencer pumps on existing coins, brand-new "launch-day" tokens that go parabolic in hours then collapse 80-98% as insiders dump, and a broad "memecoin season" whose froth marks a late-cycle, risk-on top. For a trader, peak memecoin euphoria has historically been a contrarian sell signal for the entire crypto market, while individual launch tokens are almost always a loss for late buyers.

## How it moves price

All three archetypes share one engine: price is set by attention against a thin float, with no fundamental anchor. A high-follower endorsement or a viral launch concentrates a wall of retail FOMO buyers into a market with almost no real liquidity, so price goes vertical. The other side of the trade is consistent — early holders, the team/insider wallets, and sniper bots that pre-positioned (sometimes seconds before a public announcement) distribute into the retail bid. Because the catalyst is a one-off attention shock with no follow-through, once the news is priced and the spotlight moves on, liquidity vanishes and price mean-reverts hard. At the sector level, the same flow rotating *down* the risk curve into the lowest-quality assets signals an exhausted marginal buyer — dry powder being spent on pure gambling rather than BTC/large-caps — which is why peak memecoin froth has repeatedly preceded broad market tops.

## Celebrity / Influencer Pump on an Existing Memecoin

**Mechanism:** A high-follower individual (Elon Musk, Mark Cuban) tweets or publicly endorses an existing memecoin (DOGE, SHIB). Retail FOMO buyers chase the spike on near-zero float depth, driving a fast parabolic move. Early holders and the influencer's own implicit positioning distribute into the retail bid. Late buyers consistently lose because the catalyst has no fundamental follow-through; when the tweets stop, price mean-reverts. The "sell-the-event" dynamic is acute for scheduled, hyped events (e.g. the SNL appearance) — the run-up front-runs the event and the event itself triggers profit-taking.

- **Directional bias:** bullish (into the catalyst), then sharp reversal
- **Typical magnitude:** +20% to +600% on the named coin into the catalyst; -20% to -40% sell-the-news reversal within 24-48h
- **Typical lag:** immediate (minutes to hours after tweet/event)
- **Typical duration:** hours to a few days; scheduled-event pumps fade within 24h of the event
- **Recurrence:** very high during bull markets — Musk alone moved DOGE on a dozen+ occasions across Jan-May 2021 and again in 2024-2026
- **Affected scope:** the named coin, DOGE, SHIB, large-cap alts
- **Leading signals:** spike in social mentions / Google Trends for the coin name; scheduled celebrity media appearance announced in advance; price + volume run-up in days before a hyped event; influencer follower count vs coin float (thin float amplifies); Robinhood/exchange app outages from order volume

## Launch-Day Parabolic Token (Politician / Pump.fun / Celebrity Coin)

**Mechanism:** A brand-new token launches with almost all supply held by insiders/team and a thin public float. A loud endorsement or hype event (a politician's own announcement, a celebrity, or a viral pump.fun coin) drives a vertical move within minutes to hours as retail piles in. Insiders and sniper bots that bought pre-announcement sell into the retail bid. Late buyers almost always lose because insider distribution overwhelms demand once attention peaks — a near-pure wealth transfer from late retail to insiders/snipers (the LIBRA case showed ~86% of participants lost money). Bias is bullish only for the first minutes/hours and sharply bearish thereafter.

- **Directional bias:** bearish (after the first hours)
- **Typical magnitude:** +1000% to +10000% in the first hours, then -80% to -98% from peak within weeks
- **Typical lag:** the pump is immediate (minutes); the dump follows within hours to days
- **Typical duration:** peak within 24h; 80-98% collapse over days to weeks
- **Recurrence:** extremely high during froth windows — pump.fun launched thousands daily in late 2024 / early 2025; politician coins clustered Jan-Feb 2025
- **Affected scope:** the named coin
- **Leading signals:** insider/sniper wallets buying seconds-to-minutes before the public announcement; pct of supply held by team/top wallets; no liquidity lock / no vesting; concentrated holder distribution (top-10 wallet share); explosive first-hour volume with vertical price; official figure directly endorsing (legitimacy halo)

## Memecoin Season as a Late-Cycle Risk-On Top Signal

**Mechanism:** When speculative capital rotates down the risk curve into the lowest-quality assets, it signals an exhausted marginal buyer and dry powder being spent on pure gambling. Peak memecoin froth — record launchpad revenue, parabolic joke coins, politician/celebrity coins — has repeatedly coincided with or immediately preceded the cycle top. Smart money and early holders distribute large-cap and memecoin positions into euphoric retail; once froth peaks, the whole sector deflates 75-80% and capital consolidates back into BTC, dragging alts down harder than BTC. Direction is bearish for the forward 1-6 month return of the broad market once the froth-peak signal fires.

- **Directional bias:** bearish (broad market, forward 1-6 months)
- **Typical magnitude:** memecoin sector market cap -75% to -80% from cycle peak; broad market correction of -20% to -50%+
- **Typical lag:** froth peak leads the broad top by days to a few weeks
- **Typical duration:** sector drawdown plays out over months (-75% to -80% peak-to-trough in memecoin market cap)
- **Recurrence:** once per major cycle (2021, late-2024/early-2025); froth peaks recurred each bull phase
- **Affected scope:** BTC, large-cap alts, the whole memecoin sector, total-market-cap
- **Leading signals:** memecoin sector market cap at record + rising share of total crypto mcap; launchpad (pump.fun) daily revenue at record highs; politician/celebrity coins launching (peak novelty → exhaustion); joke coins (Fartcoin) at ATHs; memecoin share of total mcap above a high threshold; negative funding flips / BTC dominance starting to rise
