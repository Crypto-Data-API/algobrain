# Trading Courses

The hub for structured online courses, certifications, and learning paths. Courses offer what books cannot on their own — video lectures, coding exercises, graded projects, peer discussion, and sometimes mentorship. This section reviews the most valuable options, from university-affiliated specializations to platform-specific, deploy-tomorrow bootcamps.

Pick by goal: academic depth and credentialing, or hands-on systematic-trading skills you can apply to live code immediately.
