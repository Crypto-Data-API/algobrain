# Trading Books

Essential books on trading, investing, markets, and financial theory.

The best trading books distill decades of hard-won experience into a few hundred pages. This section reviews and summarizes the most impactful titles -- from practitioner classics like [[market-wizards]] (interviews with top traders) to quantitative references like [[advances-in-financial-ml]] (the ML-for-finance bible) to narrative non-fiction like [[the-man-who-solved-the-market]] (the Jim Simons / Renaissance Technologies story).

Each book page includes key takeaways, who should read it, and how to apply its lessons.
