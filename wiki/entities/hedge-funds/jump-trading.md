# Jump Trading

Jump Trading is a proprietary trading firm specializing in high-frequency and quantitative trading. Based in Chicago, it is one of the most technologically aggressive firms in the industry, known for pushing the boundaries of [[low-latency-trading]] with FPGA-based systems and microwave networks.

## Trading Approach

Jump Trading is a **proprietary firm**, meaning it trades its own capital rather than managing outside investor money. This distinction matters — they don't report AUM, don't have investors to answer to, and can take concentrated bets.

Their core competency is **speed**. Jump invests heavily in:

- **FPGA-based trading systems**: Custom hardware that processes market data and generates orders in sub-microsecond timeframes
- **Microwave communication networks**: Tower-to-tower links between Chicago (CME) and New Jersey (NYSE/NASDAQ) that transmit signals faster than fiber optic cables
- **[[co-location]]** at every major exchange globally
- **Custom silicon**: Pushing beyond FPGAs into application-specific designs

## Asset Classes

Jump trades across:

- **Futures**: Major presence on CME, Eurex, and other derivatives exchanges
- **Equities**: US and international stock market making
- **Cryptocurrency**: Through Jump Crypto, one of the largest crypto trading operations
- **Foreign exchange**: Major liquidity provider in spot and forwards

## Jump Crypto

Jump Crypto was the firm's cryptocurrency division, which became a major player in the digital asset ecosystem:

- **Market making**: Provided liquidity across dozens of crypto exchanges
- **DeFi participation**: Active in decentralized protocols, governance, and infrastructure
- **Wormhole incident**: Jump Crypto was deeply involved in the aftermath of the Wormhole bridge hack in February 2022, where approximately **$320 million** was stolen. Jump reportedly backstopped the losses to maintain the bridge's functionality.
- **Venture investing**: Funded crypto infrastructure projects and protocols

Jump Crypto's operations scaled back significantly following broader crypto industry turmoil and regulatory scrutiny.

## Culture

Jump maintains extreme secrecy about its strategies and technology. The firm:

- Rarely gives public talks or interviews
- Does not disclose financial results
- Recruits top engineering talent from systems programming, electrical engineering, and physics
- Operates as a flat, team-based organization
