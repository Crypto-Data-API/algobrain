Arca is a Los Angeles-based digital asset management firm founded in 2018 that builds institutional-grade investment products for the [[crypto-markets|crypto markets]], with a particular focus on yield-generating [[defi|DeFi]] strategies and tokenized securities. Unlike many crypto funds that embraced regulatory arbitrage, Arca has pursued a compliance-first approach, positioning itself at the intersection of traditional finance infrastructure and on-chain yield.

## Founding and Leadership

Arca was co-founded by CEO Rayne Steinberg (previously co-founder of WisdomTree Asset Management, where he helped build a sales organization responsible for ~$50 billion in ETF AUM), Jerald David, and Phil Liu. The traditional-finance pedigree of the founding team has shaped Arca's operational DNA: the firm built registered fund infrastructure, KYC/AML processes, and institutional custody relationships from day one. Co-founder Jeff Dorman, CFA, serves as Chief Investment Officer and is one of the most widely read commentators on [[defi|DeFi]] and digital asset credit markets, publishing weekly research ("That's Our Two Satoshis") that institutional allocators frequently cite.

## Fund Lineup

Arca operates several flagship strategies:

- **Arca Digital Assets Fund** — the flagship liquid token strategy investing across large-cap and mid-cap digital assets
- **Arca Endeavor Fund** — focused on [[defi|DeFi]] yield strategies including [[liquidity-provision|liquidity provision]], [[liquid-staking|liquid staking]], and structured yield products
- **Arca Digital Yield Fund** — launched August 2021 as the industry's first actively managed digital-asset income fund, co-managed by CIO Jeff Dorman and portfolio manager Hassan Bassiri, CFA
- **Arca NFT Fund** — one of the earliest institutional NFT-focused vehicles
- **ArCoin** — Arca's pioneering 2020 launch of the first SEC-registered fund issued as a token on Ethereum (U.S. Treasury Fund shares as ERC-1404 tokens), a landmark in tokenized securities

## Status 2025-2026

Arca remains an active digital asset manager as of 2025-2026, with Steinberg as CEO and Dorman as CIO. A notable late-2025 development was Arca's move into the **digital asset treasury (DAT)** trend: in October 2025, ENDRA Life Sciences announced a private placement to fund a digital-asset treasury strategy managed by Arca, with Jeff Dorman slated for the company's Digital Asset Advisory Board — an example of crypto fund managers becoming external managers for listed-company token treasuries. (Firm-wide AUM is not publicly disclosed; treat third-party AUM figures with caution.)

## Research and Industry Role

Arca's research arm publishes institutional-quality analysis covering [[defi|DeFi]] protocols, on-chain credit, token valuation frameworks, and market structure. The firm is frequently cited alongside peers like [[pantera|Pantera Capital]] as one of the institutional voices shaping professional crypto investing. Arca's regulatory-first approach and focus on yield distinguish it from pure directional crypto hedge funds.

## Trading Relevance

- **Tokenized securities pioneer**: ArCoin (2020) is the reference precedent for SEC-registered tokenized funds — the regulatory template behind the 2024-2026 tokenized money-market boom (BlackRock BUIDL, Franklin BENJI).
- **DeFi yield benchmarking**: Dorman's weekly research is a widely used read on digital-asset credit conditions and token-valuation frameworks.
- **DAT exposure**: Arca's management of listed-company digital asset treasuries (e.g., ENDRA, October 2025) makes it a participant in the equity-wrapped-crypto trade traders monitor for reflexive premium/discount dynamics.
