TattooTransform.ai is an AI tattoo design platform founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently paused under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

TattooTransform.ai applies artificial intelligence and machine learning to tattoo design, enabling users to visualize, transform, and create tattoo designs using AI. The platform leverages generative AI to help users explore tattoo concepts before committing to permanent ink.
