VentureAI Labs is an AI startup incubator founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]]. It serves as the umbrella for Sam's portfolio of AI-powered products and services, spanning data APIs, marketplaces, prediction platforms, and fintech tools.

## Overview

VentureAI Labs operates as an incubator and holding entity for a diverse portfolio of technology ventures. The portfolio is unified by a focus on artificial intelligence and automation, with projects ranging from financial data infrastructure to consumer-facing AI services. The incubator's tagline is "Building with AI at the speed of light."
