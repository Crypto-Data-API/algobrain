SportsPredictor.ai is an AI sports prediction platform concept founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently at concept stage under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

SportsPredictor.ai aims to apply machine learning and statistical modeling to sports outcome prediction. The platform would analyze historical data, team/player statistics, and other relevant factors to generate probabilistic forecasts for sporting events. It intersects with the broader [[prediction-markets|prediction markets]] ecosystem.
