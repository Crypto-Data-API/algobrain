Enovis Corporation (NYSE: ENOV) is a medical-technology company focused on orthopedic devices, spanning prevention/recovery (bracing, rehabilitation) and surgical reconstruction (joint implants). It is a small/mid-cap orthopedics pure-play created when Colfax Corporation split in 2022.

> Note: the ticker is **ENOV**, not "ENVX" (which is Enovix, a battery company — a common confusion). This page is the orthopedic medtech company.

## Business Overview

Enovis operates two reporting segments:

- **Prevention & Recovery (P&R)** — orthopedic bracing, supports, cold therapy, bone-growth stimulation, and rehabilitation products (the legacy DJO franchise). A steady, high-volume, cash-generative business.
- **Reconstructive (Recon)** — hip, knee, shoulder, and extremities implants plus surgical enabling technology, including the ARVIS augmented-reality surgical navigation system and other digital/AI-assisted surgery tools that underpin its "AI MedTech" positioning.

Enovis was formed in 2022 when Colfax Corporation separated its fabrication-technology business (spun off as ESAB) from its medical-technology business, which was renamed Enovis. Growth strategy centers on shifting mix toward the higher-margin, faster-growing Recon segment, helped by acquisitions such as Mathys (2024).
