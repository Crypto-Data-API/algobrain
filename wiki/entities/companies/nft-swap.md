NFTSwap.gg is an NFT trading and swapping platform concept founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently at concept stage under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

NFTSwap.gg aims to provide a platform for trading and swapping NFTs (non-fungible tokens) peer-to-peer. The .gg domain signals a gaming/community focus. The platform would complement Sam's broader crypto portfolio including [[crypto-data-api|CryptoDataApi]] and [[token-news|TokenNews]].
