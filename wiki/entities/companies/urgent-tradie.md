UrgentTradie.com is a marketplace concept for urgent tradesperson services, founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently at concept stage under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

UrgentTradie.com aims to connect people in Australia with available tradespeople for urgent jobs — plumbers, electricians, locksmiths, and other trades. The platform would match urgent service requests with nearby tradespeople who have immediate availability.
