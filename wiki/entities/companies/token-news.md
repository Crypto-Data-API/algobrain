TokenNews.ai is an AI-curated cryptocurrency and token news platform concept founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently at concept stage under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

TokenNews.ai aims to apply artificial intelligence to aggregate, curate, and deliver cryptocurrency and blockchain token news. The platform would filter signal from noise in the fast-moving crypto news landscape, building on Sam's experience with [[crypto-news-au|CryptoNews.com.au]] (exited) and complementing his [[crypto-data-api|CryptoDataApi]] data infrastructure.
