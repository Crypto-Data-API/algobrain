Rezolve AI Limited (NASDAQ: RZLV) is an AI-driven commerce-technology company whose "Brain Suite" platform powers conversational search, personalization, checkout and omni-channel engagement for retailers and brands — positioning it within the emerging [[agentic-commerce]] theme. Headquartered in London and listed on Nasdaq (via a 2024 SPAC combination), it is a small, highly speculative AI name that trades on revenue/ARR guidance, enterprise-adoption announcements and broad agentic-AI sentiment.

## Business Overview

Rezolve sells AI software to merchants aimed at converting browsing into purchases: natural-language product discovery, personalized recommendations, instant checkout and engagement tools, increasingly framed as "agentic commerce" (AI agents that complete transactions on a shopper's behalf). Revenue is reported as a mix of platform/subscription and is tracked closely against annual recurring revenue (ARR). The company is early-stage and its disclosures emphasize rapid ARR/guidance growth; sources are not always consistent on whether headline figures are realized revenue versus contracted ARR, so traders should treat guidance with caution.

## Product Suite (detail)

Rezolve markets its **Brain Suite** as a modular [[agentic-commerce]] stack for retailers and brands:

| Module | Function |
|---|---|
| **Brain Commerce** | Conversational/natural-language product discovery and search across a merchant's catalogue |
| **Brain Checkout** | Instant/embedded checkout to shorten the path from intent to purchase |
| **Brain Assistant** | AI shopping assistant / agent that can recommend and, in the agentic vision, transact on the shopper's behalf |
| **Brain Engage** | Omni-channel personalization, targeting and customer engagement |

The strategic positioning is "agentic commerce": rather than a human clicking through a funnel, an AI agent understands intent and completes the transaction. This places Rezolve in the same conceptual arena as much larger platforms (Shopify's AI features, Salesforce Commerce/Agentforce, Amazon's Rufus, Google's shopping AI). Rezolve's pitch is a merchant-neutral, deployable layer for brands that don't want to be locked into a megacap ecosystem — but its scale and balance sheet are a fraction of those competitors'.

A recurring analytical caution: Rezolve's headline numbers blend realized revenue and contracted/forecast ARR, and guidance has been revised upward aggressively. Distinguishing booked revenue from pipeline is essential before underwriting the growth story.

## Bull vs Bear Case

**Bull case**
- Direct exposure to the [[agentic-commerce]] theme — one of the most-hyped AI verticals — as a focused public pure-play.
- Aggressive ARR/guidance trajectory (raised 2026 guidance to ~$350M; commentary toward ~$500M ARR) implies a steep growth curve if realised.
- Merchant-neutral positioning could appeal to brands wary of megacap lock-in.
- High beta to agentic-AI sentiment; small float amplifies upside on good news.

**Bear case**
- Guidance is aggressive and the line between realized revenue and contracted/forecast ARR is blurry — execution and disclosure quality are open questions.
- Competes against Shopify, Salesforce, Amazon and Google, which are embedding the same agentic features into platforms merchants already use.
- SPAC provenance (2024 combination) often brings warrant overhang, redemption-driven small free float, and weak post-deal sponsorship.
- Cash burn and dilution risk; the equity is valued richly (≈$1.19B market cap) on a story that must keep compounding.
