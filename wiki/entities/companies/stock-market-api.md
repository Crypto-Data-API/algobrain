StockMarketApi.ai is a stock market data API with AI capabilities, founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently in alpha development under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

StockMarketApi.ai provides programmatic access to stock market data, enhanced with AI-powered analytics. The service is part of Sam's Data/API product cluster alongside [[crypto-data-api|CryptoDataApi]] and [[real-time-api|RealTimeApi]], offering equities-focused data infrastructure.
