Faraday Future Intelligent Electric Inc. (NASDAQ: FFAI) is a micro-cap Los Angeles-based electric-vehicle company that has pivoted toward an "EAI + Crypto" strategy — embodied [[artificial-intelligence|AI]] vehicles/robotics plus a crypto-treasury arm. For traders it is a pure speculation vehicle: minimal vehicle revenue, chronic dilution and reverse splits, but episodic 50%+ moves on crypto-treasury and meme-driven flows.

## Business / Product (Detail)

Founded in 2014 by Chinese entrepreneur Jia Yueting (LeEco), Faraday Future went public via SPAC in July 2021 (originally FFIE). Its product and strategy stack:

- **FF 91** — an ultra-luxury flagship EV that has delivered **only a handful of units since 2023**. It is a halo/technology-demonstration product, not a volume vehicle.
- **FX brand** — a planned mass-market lineup of lower-priced models, partly leveraging contract manufacturing, positioned as the **volume hope** that could in theory generate meaningful revenue. Execution and funding remain unproven.
- **"EAI + Crypto" strategy** — an "embodied AI" framing for the vehicle/robotics business paired with a crypto/digital-asset treasury arm (see below).

Vehicle revenue remains de minimis relative to operating losses; the company has survived on repeated equity raises, convertible financings, reverse stock splits, and Nasdaq-compliance maneuvers. Key financials, as of June 2026 (approximate): market cap around $0.1B; annual revenue in the low single-digit millions of USD against losses in the hundreds of millions — figures approximate; the company is not covered by the stockmarketapi fundamentals feed.

## 2025–2026 Developments

- **"EAI + Crypto" dual-flywheel strategy (2025):** Faraday announced a "Dual Flywheel & Dual Bridge" strategy pairing its embodied-AI vehicle business with a crypto/digital-asset arm, including a **C10 crypto treasury** (a basket-of-top-10-tokens treasury vehicle) — joining the 2025 wave of small caps adopting crypto-treasury strategies to attract speculative capital.
- **Crypto spin-off plan (September 2025):** announced intent to **spin off its crypto flywheel assets into a separately listed company**, attempting to crystallize value from the digital-asset arm.
- **Ongoing FX rollout:** continued prototype/early-production announcements for FX models, with execution and funding still unproven.
