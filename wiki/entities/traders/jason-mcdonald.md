# Jason McDonald

Jason McDonald is a Senior Trading Mentor at the [[itpm|Institute of Trading and Portfolio Management]] with 17 years of professional trading experience as a hedge fund portfolio manager and proprietary trader across major financial institutions. He remains listed as an active ITPM mentor as of June 2026, making him one of the longest-serving mentors at the firm alongside [[anton-kreil]].

## Overview

McDonald's career spans BZW, CSFB, MAKO Volatility Hedge Fund, Commerzbank, Lehman Brothers, and TD Bank. He managed portfolios ranging from $250 million to over $500 million in Pan European and global equities. He retired from institutional trading in 2009 and joined ITPM in 2013 as a senior mentor. In retirement he has also been contracted in corporate advisory roles across oil and gas, mining, port infrastructure, financial services, and aviation, helping companies structure capital for growth.

## Career History

### BZW (early 1990s)
- Started as research analyst in Japanese warrants
- Advanced to manage $500M Pan European Equities Proprietary trading book

### CSFB — Modal Capital
- Worked for CSFB's internal hedge fund Modal Capital with global mandate

### MAKO Volatility Hedge Fund
- Established and ran special situation risk arbitrage operations

### Commerzbank (from 2004)
- Joined as senior proprietary trader responsible for significant parts of a global multi-strategy, multi-billion Euro proprietary portfolio
- Became one of the largest Proprietary Traders on the Investment Bank side

### Lehman Brothers & TD Bank (2007-2009)
- Managed $250M global [[long-short-equity|long/short portfolios]] at both firms
- Retired in 2009

### ITPM (2013-present)
- Hired as Senior Trading Mentor in 2013 (announced via PR Newswire)
- One-on-one coaching through official Institute Mentoring Programme
- Guides graduates toward consistent profitability and track record development
- Capital allocation management across the Institute's global portfolio (Source: [[itpm-education-methodology-overview]])
- Still listed on ITPM's mentor roster with free and premium video content as of June 2026

## Trading Relevance

McDonald's background in proprietary trading and hedge fund management provides real-world context for ITPM's [[long-short-equity]] methodology. His experience spans multiple market cycles including the 2008 financial crisis, making his perspective on [[risk-management]] and portfolio construction particularly valuable.
