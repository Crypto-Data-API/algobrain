# Otherside

**Otherside** is the gamified metaverse platform developed by [[yuga-labs|Yuga Labs]], the company behind Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC). Land in Otherside is represented by **Otherdeed** NFTs on [[ethereum|Ethereum]], whose May 2022 mint was one of the largest NFT sales ever and a landmark event in on-chain market history. For traders, Otherside matters as the principal utility sink for **ApeCoin (APE)** and as a case study in NFT land speculation, gas-war market microstructure, and narrative-driven asset cycles.

---

## Overview

- **Otherdeed mint (2022-04-30 / May 2022)**: Yuga Labs sold 55,000 Otherdeed land plots at a flat 305 APE each, raising roughly **$317M in ApeCoin** — the largest NFT mint to date at the time. Demand was so extreme it congested Ethereum for hours and burned tens of millions of dollars in gas, prompting Yuga to apologize and float the idea of its own chain. A single rare Otherdeed later sold for a record **$1.5M** (The Block).
- **Asset structure**: 100,000 total Otherdeeds (55,000 public sale; the rest to BAYC/MAYC holders and Yuga/partners). Plots carry environment, sediment, resource, and "Koda" attributes that drive large intra-collection price dispersion — rarity-tier trading is the core Otherdeed strategy on [[opensea|OpenSea]], [[blur|Blur]], and [[magic-eden|Magic Eden]].
- **Tech demos**: "First Trip" (July 2022) and "Second Trip" (2023) demonstrated thousands of concurrent users; development then slowed through the 2023-2024 NFT bear market.

## 2025-2026: Restructuring and Renewed Focus

Under CEO **Daniel Alegre**, Yuga Labs completed a major restructuring in 2025 and explicitly refocused the company on **Otherside and BAYC**, divesting nearly everything else:

- **February 2025** — sold the Meebits IP.
- **May 2025** — transferred CryptoPunks stewardship to a digital-art nonprofit.
- **2025** — sold Moonbirds, Mythics, and Oddities to Orange Cap Games.
- **2025-2026 roadmap** — gaming experiences "Bathroom Blitz" and "Meet Me at the Clubhouse" (July 2025), **Voyager 2.0** plus expanded Otherdeed holder utility later in 2025, and a **"Resources" system in 2026** intended to introduce land production, crafting, and an open in-world economy.

As of mid-2026 the project is active and is effectively Yuga Labs' main product bet, alongside BAYC.

## Trading Relevance

- **ApeCoin demand driver**: Otherside is the primary intended utility for APE (land was priced in APE; in-world economy plans route through it). Otherside roadmap news is the dominant fundamental catalyst for APE price action.
- **NFT land as a speculative asset class**: Otherdeed is the most liquid metaverse-land collection; its floor and volume are a barometer for the entire "virtual land" narrative (alongside Decentraland/Sandbox land).
- **Event-driven setups**: mints, trips/demos, and roadmap announcements have historically produced sharp moves in APE, Otherdeed floors, and related BAYC-ecosystem assets — a recurring event-trading template.
- **Market-structure case study**: the 2022 mint gas war is a canonical example of priority-fee auctions and failed-transaction costs during hyped on-chain launches — relevant to anyone modeling execution in congested conditions.
- **Concentration risk signal**: Yuga's 2025 divestitures concentrated the company's success on Otherside execution; delivery (or failure) of the 2026 Resources economy is the key catalyst to monitor.
