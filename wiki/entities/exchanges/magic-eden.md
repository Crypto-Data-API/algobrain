Magic Eden is a multi-chain [[nft|NFT]] marketplace that launched in September 2021 as the dominant marketplace on [[solana|Solana]] before expanding to [[ethereum|Ethereum]], [[bitcoin|Bitcoin]] (Ordinals), Polygon, and other blockchains. The platform raised $130 million at a $1.6 billion valuation in June 2022 and became a key player in the Bitcoin Ordinals market during 2023.

## History

Magic Eden launched in September 2021 and quickly established itself as the leading NFT marketplace on the [[solana|Solana]] blockchain. While [[opensea|OpenSea]] and later [[blur|Blur]] battled for dominance on [[ethereum|Ethereum]], Magic Eden carved out a distinct niche by focusing on Solana's faster, cheaper transaction environment.

The platform played a central role in growing Solana's NFT ecosystem, serving as the primary marketplace for major Solana collections including DeGods, y00ts, and Mad Lads. Its lower fee structure (2% standard) compared to early [[opensea|OpenSea]] (2.5%) and Solana's minimal gas costs made it attractive to cost-conscious traders and collectors.

In June 2022, Magic Eden raised a $130 million Series B round at a $1.6 billion valuation, solidifying its position as a major player in the NFT marketplace space.

Magic Eden's most strategically significant move came with its expansion to Bitcoin Ordinals and BRC-20 tokens in 2023. As the Ordinals protocol brought NFT-like functionality to the [[bitcoin|Bitcoin]] blockchain, Magic Eden became the leading marketplace for this emerging asset class, differentiating itself from Ethereum-focused competitors.

The platform continued its multi-chain expansion by adding support for Ethereum, Polygon, and other chains, while also launching the Magic Eden Wallet and creator tools to build a more integrated ecosystem.

## Significance

Magic Eden's multi-chain strategy set it apart from competitors that focused primarily on a single blockchain. While [[opensea|OpenSea]] and [[blur|Blur]] competed for Ethereum dominance, Magic Eden built infrastructure across multiple chains, positioning itself to capture activity wherever NFT markets emerged.

The platform's early and aggressive move into Bitcoin Ordinals was particularly significant. By becoming the leading marketplace for Ordinals — the first widely adopted NFT-like standard on Bitcoin — Magic Eden established itself at the center of one of the most important developments in Bitcoin's history since the Lightning Network.

Magic Eden also played a foundational role in the Solana NFT ecosystem. The platform's marketplace infrastructure and community-building tools helped Solana develop into the second-largest NFT ecosystem after Ethereum, demonstrating that NFT culture could thrive beyond a single blockchain.
