X2Y2 was an Ethereum-focused NFT marketplace launched in January 2022 as an [[opensea]] alternative, distinguished by a liquidity-mining model tied to its $X2Y2 governance token and, later, an early decision to make creator royalties optional. The team announced the shutdown of the trading front-end and support channels on March 31, 2025, effective April 30, 2025, after a roughly 90% collapse in trading volume versus peak; the underlying smart contracts remain on-chain and functional, and the team announced a pivot toward crypto-AI products.

## History and founding

X2Y2 launched on Ethereum mainnet in January 2022, entering the market a few weeks after [[looksrare]] pioneered the "vampire attack" playbook of pulling [[opensea]] users with token rewards. The team operated pseudonymously from launch. Its initial pitch was a cheaper-fee alternative to OpenSea plus listing/trading rewards paid in the native $X2Y2 ERC-20 token.

During early 2022, X2Y2 captured a substantial share of Ethereum NFT wash-traded and organic volume, at times reported in the 20-30% range of Ethereum NFT marketplace volume before [[blur]]'s launch in late 2022 rapidly displaced both X2Y2 and LooksRare among professional traders. At peak in 2022, X2Y2 reportedly processed over $5.6B in Ethereum NFT transactions; by the final 12 months before its shutdown, total trading volume had fallen to roughly $53.6M, far behind Blur, OpenSea, and Immutable.

## What it did

- **Marketplace for ERC-721 and ERC-1155 NFTs on Ethereum**, with listings, bids, and collection offers.
- **$X2Y2 token and liquidity mining**: traders and listers earned $X2Y2 emissions; token holders could stake to capture a share of marketplace fees.
- **Optional royalties**: in August 2022 X2Y2 moved from enforced royalties to optional royalties, a decision that generated significant controversy among creators and was a precursor to a broader industry shift away from marketplace-enforced royalties. See [[nft-royalties]] for the wider debate.
- **Bulk listing, sweeping, and collection offer tools** aimed at power users.

## Trading relevance

X2Y2 is primarily of historical interest to traders in 2026:

- **Historical liquidity venue**: Backtests and on-chain analyses that cover 2022-2023 Ethereum NFT trading will include X2Y2 order flow and must distinguish organic volume from token-incentive-driven volume.
- **Case study in incentive decay**: X2Y2 illustrates how token-emissions-based marketplaces lose relevance once emissions taper and a more execution-focused competitor ([[blur]]) arrives. The pattern mirrors what later happened to [[looksrare]].
- **Royalty policy precedent**: X2Y2's move to optional royalties was a pivotal industry event; traders and creators pricing royalty risk today reference it.

## Current status

On March 31, 2025, the X2Y2 team (under pseudonymous founder "TP") publicly announced the shutdown of the trading platform effective April 30, 2025, citing the ~90% decline in volume versus peak and a strategic pivot to crypto-AI products. Per the team's public communication:

- The **web front-end and support channels were shut down on April 30, 2025**.
- The **deployed smart contracts remain live on Ethereum**, meaning existing orders, cancellations, and withdrawals can still be executed by anyone interacting with the contracts directly or through third-party interfaces.
- Users were urged to **revoke approvals and cancel outstanding listings/bids** before the front-end went offline, since managing them afterward requires direct contract interaction.
- The team signaled a **pivot to AI-related crypto products**, though that new venture is outside the scope of X2Y2 the NFT marketplace.

For traders, X2Y2 should be treated as an end-of-life venue since May 2025; routing new flow there carries operational risk even though the contracts technically continue to function.
