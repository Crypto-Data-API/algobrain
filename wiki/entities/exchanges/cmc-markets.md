CMC Markets is a global CFD and [[forex]] broker with a major Australian presence, offering both contract-for-difference (CFD) trading and traditional ASX share trading on a single platform. Listed on both the London Stock Exchange (LON: CMC) and the ASX (ASX: CMC), it is one of the few publicly traded brokers operating in Australia and brings institutional-grade charting and platform technology to retail traders.

## Overview

Founded in London in 1989 by Peter Cruddas, CMC Markets has grown into one of the world's largest CFD providers, regulated in multiple jurisdictions including the UK (FCA), Australia (ASIC), Germany (BaFin), and Singapore (MAS). The Australian operation, headquartered in Sydney, is significant — Australia is one of CMC's largest markets globally.

CMC's distinctive position in Australia is its ability to offer both CFD trading and traditional ASX share trading (with [[chess-sponsorship|CHESS sponsorship]]) on a single platform. This means a trader can hold long-term CHESS-sponsored ASX shares while also using CFDs for short-term leveraged trades across global markets — a combination few competitors offer.

## History

- **1989**: Founded in London by Peter Cruddas as "Currency Management Corporation"
- **1996**: Launched one of the world's first online forex trading platforms
- **2002**: Expanded into Australia
- **2007**: Launched the "Next Generation" trading platform
- **2016**: IPO on the London Stock Exchange (LON: CMC)
- **2019**: Listed on the ASX (ASX: CMC) via CDI (Chess Depositary Interest)
- **2021**: ASIC implemented new CFD regulations — CMC adapted with leverage limits and negative balance protection
- **2022–2024**: Continued platform development, expanded ASX share trading offering alongside core CFD business

## Key Features

- **Next Generation Platform**: CMC's proprietary platform featuring 80+ technical indicators, pattern recognition scanner, customisable chart layouts, and advanced order types — widely considered one of the best retail trading platforms globally
- **CFDs + Shares on One Platform**: Unique ability to trade CHESS-sponsored ASX shares alongside leveraged CFDs on the same platform
- **CHESS Sponsored**: ASX share trading is fully CHESS-sponsored with personal HIN
- **Massive CFD Range**: 10,000+ CFD instruments across shares, indices, forex, commodities, crypto, and treasuries
- **Mobile Trading**: Full-featured mobile app with the same charting and execution capabilities as desktop
- **Guaranteed Stop-Loss Orders (GSLOs)**: Available on CFDs for a premium — guarantees exit at specified price even through gaps
- **Price Improvement Technology**: CMC's execution engine frequently delivers price improvement on orders

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- **Powerful platform**: Next Generation platform with 80+ indicators, pattern recognition, and customisable layouts — best-in-class for technical analysis
- **CFDs + shares combined**: Unique ability to hold CHESS-sponsored shares and trade CFDs on the same platform
- **Competitive for larger trades**: 0.10% ASX brokerage is competitive at scale
- **Global market access via CFDs**: Trade virtually any global market without separate international accounts
- **Well-regulated**: ASIC + FCA + multiple jurisdictions — high regulatory standard
- **Publicly listed**: ASX and LSE listing provides transparency and financial accountability
- **Advanced order types**: Guaranteed stop-losses, conditional orders, trailing stops

### Cons
- **CFD risk is high**: ASIC requires disclosure that approximately 67% of retail CFD accounts lose money — CFDs are complex and leveraged products
- **Share trading fees higher than discount brokers**: $11 minimum is more than [[selfwealth]] ($9.50), [[stake]] ($3), or [[superhero]] ($2) for small trades
- **Inactivity fee on CFD account**: $15/month after 12 months of no trading
- **Learning curve**: The Next Generation platform is powerful but complex for beginners
- **Overnight funding costs on CFDs**: Holding CFD positions long-term incurs daily financing charges that erode returns

## Who It's Best For

- **Active traders** who want advanced charting and technical analysis tools
- **Traders wanting both CFDs and shares**: The combined platform is uniquely convenient
- **Forex traders**: Competitive spreads from 0.7 pips with a well-regulated broker
- **Larger trade sizes on ASX**: The 0.10% rate is attractive for trades above $11,000
- **Experienced traders** comfortable with [[leverage|leveraged products]] and [[risk-management|risk management]]
- **International market access seekers**: CFDs allow exposure to global markets without opening separate international brokerage accounts

> **Risk Warning**: CFDs are complex instruments and come with a high risk of losing money rapidly due to [[leverage]]. Approximately 67% of retail investor accounts lose money when trading CFDs with CMC Markets. You should consider whether you understand how CFDs work and whether you can afford the high risk of losing your money.
