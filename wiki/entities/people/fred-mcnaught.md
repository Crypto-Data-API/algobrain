Fred McNaught is a retired expert stock market investor based in Brisbane, Australia. He is the primary knowledge source behind [[alfred|Alfred]] — the trading wiki's AI investment analyst — and business mentor to [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], the creator of this wiki.

## Expertise

- **Fundamental stock analysis** — Deep expertise in company valuation, financial statement analysis, and the metrics framework that powers [[alfred-fundamental-analysis|Alfred's analysis methodology]]
- **Macro investing** — Broad understanding of economic cycles, interest rates, commodities, and how macro forces drive markets
- **Strategic business analysis** — Decades of hands-on experience running businesses, with expertise in evaluating competitive positioning, management quality, and business models
- **Self-managed superannuation (SMSF)** — Strong interest in self-managed super funds as a vehicle for tax-efficient investing within the Australian system
- **Tax optimisation** — Keen interest in legitimate tax minimisation strategies including [[franking-credits]], SMSF structures, and capital gains management

## Role in the Wiki

Fred is the domain expert whose investing knowledge, analytical frameworks, and decades of market experience form the foundation of [[alfred|Alfred's]] investment reports. The [[alfred-report-methodology|Alfred Report Methodology]] — including the verdict system, metrics selection, benchmarking approach, and qualitative analysis framework — originates from Fred's investing philosophy and process.
