## Role in the Wiki

Sam built the trading wiki as a comprehensive, LLM-maintained knowledge base covering all aspects of trading. He designed the three-layer architecture (Cloudflare R2 raw sources, wiki markdown, CLAUDE.md schema), the ingestion workflows, and the tooling that powers the system. Working with his mentor [[fred-mcnaught|Fred McNaught]], he created [[alfred|Alfred]] to encode Fred's decades of investing expertise into a structured, repeatable analysis framework.
