CryptoNews.com.au was an Australian cryptocurrency news publication founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]]. The venture has been exited.

## Overview

CryptoNews.com.au was an Australian-focused cryptocurrency news and media publication covering blockchain, crypto markets, and digital assets. It was one of Sam's earlier ventures, predating his current AI-focused portfolio. The experience in crypto media likely informed the concept for [[token-news|TokenNews.ai]], a planned AI-curated crypto news successor.
