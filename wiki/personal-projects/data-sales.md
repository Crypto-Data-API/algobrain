DataSales.ai is an AI data marketplace platform founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently in alpha development under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

DataSales.ai is a marketplace for buying and selling data, enhanced with AI capabilities. The platform sits within Sam's broader data infrastructure portfolio alongside [[crypto-data-api|CryptoDataApi]], [[stock-market-api|StockMarketApi]], and [[real-time-api|RealTimeApi]].
