jQuery4U.com was a jQuery and JavaScript developer resource founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]]. The venture has been exited.

## Overview

jQuery4U (jQuery for You) was a popular web development blog and resource site focused on jQuery tutorials, plugins, and JavaScript techniques. It was one of Sam's earliest ventures, reflecting his deep roots in web development spanning 20+ years. The site served the developer community during the height of jQuery's dominance as a JavaScript library.
