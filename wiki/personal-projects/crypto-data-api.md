CryptoDataApi.com is a cryptocurrency data API service founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently in alpha development under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

CryptoDataApi provides programmatic access to cryptocurrency market data. The service is part of Sam's Data/API product cluster alongside [[stock-market-api|StockMarketApi]] and [[real-time-api|RealTimeApi]], and complements his crypto-focused ventures including [[token-news|TokenNews]] and [[nft-swap|NFTSwap]].
