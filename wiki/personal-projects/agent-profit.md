AgentProfit.ai is an AI agent monetization platform concept founded by [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]], currently at concept stage under [[venture-ai-labs|VentureAI Labs]].

## Overview

AgentProfit.ai aims to provide infrastructure and tools for building, deploying, and monetizing AI agents. The platform sits at the intersection of the rapidly growing AI agent ecosystem and Sam's experience building [[alfred|Alfred]], the wiki's AI investment analyst. It would enable developers and businesses to create profitable AI agent services.
