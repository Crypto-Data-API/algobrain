Mentoring in trading and investing involves an experienced practitioner guiding a less experienced one through decision-making frameworks, risk management, and market analysis. The [[fred-mcnaught|Fred McNaught]] / [[sam-deering|Sam Deering]] relationship is the wiki's primary example — Fred's decades of investing experience are captured through 26 recorded sessions ([[fred-sam-sessions-overview]]) and codified as [[alfred|Alfred's]] operating philosophy. ITPM also emphasises mentoring as critical to developing professional traders.
