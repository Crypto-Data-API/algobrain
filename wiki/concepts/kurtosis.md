See [[fat-tails]] — kurtosis is the fourth statistical moment, measuring
tail-heaviness. **Excess kurtosis > 0 (leptokurtic)** is precisely what makes a
return distribution fat-tailed: more frequent extreme moves than the normal/
Gaussian model implies. See also [[skewness]] (the third moment / asymmetry) and
[[black-swan]].
