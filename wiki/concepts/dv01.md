See [[duration]] — DV01 (dollar value of a 01, a.k.a. PVBP/dollar duration) is
the change in a position's value for a 1 basis-point move in yield: DV01 ≈
modified duration × price × 0.0001. It is the standard unit for sizing
rate-hedge ratios and [[yield-curve]] trades.
