The variance risk premium (the gap between implied and realized variance, `E[IV²] − E[RV²]`) and the volatility risk premium (`E[IV] − E[RV]`) describe the same structural option-market edge and are covered together on the canonical page [[volatility-risk-premium]], including the variance-vs-volatility (convexity) distinction, the empirical record, why the premium exists, how to capture it, and the crash profile.
