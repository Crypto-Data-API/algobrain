# MiCA

**MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets)** is the EU's comprehensive crypto regulatory framework — licensing (CASPs), stablecoin reserve/redemption rules, and disclosure regimes phased in through 2024–2026; a primary regulatory catalyst for EU-exposed assets.
