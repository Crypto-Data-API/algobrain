See [[crowding-risk]] — when too many participants hold the same positions or
factor exposures, forced unwinds cascade (correlated deleveraging), turning a
consensus trade into a liquidity trap. See the Aug 2007 quant quake and factor
[[deleveraging]] dynamics.
