See [[stress-test]] — reverse stress testing inverts the exercise: instead of
applying a scenario and measuring the loss, you start from the failure outcome
(insolvency, a blown risk limit, ruin) and work backward to find the scenarios
and correlations that would cause it. See also [[scenario-analysis]] and
[[value-at-risk]].
