# Delegated Proof of Stake

**Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS)** is a consensus mechanism where token holders vote for a small set of validators/witnesses who produce blocks (EOS, TRON, early BNB Chain) — higher throughput, more concentrated validator sets.
