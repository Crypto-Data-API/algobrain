The **PUT Index** (ticker: PUT) is the Cboe S&P 500 PutWrite Index. For the full treatment — methodology, synthetic equivalence to BXM, the empirical PUT-vs-BXM gap, ETF proxies (PUTW), and portfolio use — see the canonical page **[[cboe-put-putwrite-index]]**.

> Disambiguation: "PUT" here means the *index*. For the *instrument* — the contract giving the right to sell — see [[put-option]].
