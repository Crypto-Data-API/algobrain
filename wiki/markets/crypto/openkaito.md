# OpenKaito

**OpenKaito** (SN5) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#923** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** decentralized AI-native web search and knowledge retrieval. Miners crawl and index the web; validators score retrieval quality. Complements broader search-as-retrieval-for-LLM-agents workflows.
- **Team:** Kaito AI (same team that built Kaito, the crypto-native social intelligence platform).
- **Trading relevance:** infrastructure bet on AI-native search as a category. The Kaito team has credibility from their parallel crypto-social product, which feeds into validator conviction on SN5.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-5.
