# ROACORE

**ROACORE** (ROA) is a Web3 ecosystem that transforms everyday behavior—walking, cultural experiences, and digital engagement—into data-driven value.
Through DOGDOG, MODA Gallery, and ROA STORE, user activity is analyzed by AI and returned as personalized rewards, consumable utility, and ecosystem participation benefits.
By linking real-world actions with on-chain assets, ROACORE creates a seamless cycle of engagement and value, enabling a new model of “Real Utility, Real Life.” It ranks **#2017** by market capitalization.
