## Overview

CrypGPT (CGPT) is an AI-driven cryptocurrency ecosystem designed to empower traders, investors, and communities. It integrates blockchain with advanced AI to deliver:
	•	AI Trading Tools &amp; Bots: Automated trading signals and portfolio management.
	•	Fraud &amp; Scam Detection: AI modules for security and trust.
	•	Crypto Payment Gateway: Simple, global, decentralized payment solutions.
	•	Compliance &amp; Transparency: KYC/AML tools, audits, and secure smart contracts.
	•	Community Growth: Staking, rewards, and an incentivized ecosystem.

With a focus on AI + Web3 synergy, CrypGPT brings accessibility, efficiency, and security to crypto users worldwide.
