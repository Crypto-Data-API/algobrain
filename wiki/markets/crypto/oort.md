## Overview

OORT aims to create a transparent, trustworthy, and inclusive AI ecosystem. By leveraging decentralized resources, OORT ensures open and community-involved AI development from data collection to model deployment. Their mission, "Cloud for Decentralized AI," utilizes global resources supported by a proprietary blockchain-based verification layer, ensuring transparency, security, and community participation at every stage of AI development, fostering a robust and equitable AI ecosystem.
