# Story

**Story** (ticker **IP**), also known as Story Protocol, is a purpose-built [[layer-1]] blockchain designed to bring intellectual property (IP) on-chain and make it programmable. It positions itself at the intersection of IP rights, [[real-world-assets|real-world assets (RWA)]], and AI, allowing creators to tokenize ideas, images, music, AI models, and NFTs while embedding usage terms, attribution, and royalty logic directly into smart contracts.

By making licensing and royalty agreements composable on-chain, Story aims to provide a transparent, decentralized layer for IP management — letting holders protect their work, collaborate, and monetize it in an increasingly AI-driven economy.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Story sits in the **IP / RWA / AI** narrative cluster. Its categories on CoinGecko include Artificial Intelligence (AI), Smart Contract Platform, Layer 1 (L1), Binance Alpha Spotlight, Made in USA, Story Ecosystem, and IP.

The core thesis: as generative AI commoditizes content creation, provenance, attribution, and programmable licensing of IP become economically valuable. Story aims to be the settlement layer for that — treating an idea, song, image, dataset, or model as a tokenized [[real-world-assets|real-world asset]] with enforceable, composable terms. This places it adjacent to both the RWA tokenization theme and the AI-x-crypto theme, two of the dominant 2025-2026 crypto narratives.

## Valuation Framing

*Qualitative — not a price target.* IP is priced as a small-cap [[layer-1]] narrative play rather than on protocol cash flows. The ~$113M market cap against a ~$327M FDV (~0.35) means roughly two-thirds of fully-diluted value is still locked, so a buyer today is paying a relatively low headline cap but inheriting meaningful future dilution from an uncapped, inflationary token. The bull framing is optionality: if programmable-IP licensing generates real on-chain volume, IP captures gas, staking and fee demand across an entire RWA-x-AI category, and the ~98% drawdown from a $14.78 ATH leaves enormous room for re-rating. The bear framing is that the valuation rests almost entirely on two narratives (AI + IP/RWA) with little proven licensing throughput, and that an inflationary L1 with no demonstrated fee sink struggles to absorb continued emissions. The +5% week is encouraging relative to peers but is a single data point in a long downtrend.

## Risks

- **Extreme dilution risk:** uncapped max supply plus a low MC/FDV ratio (~0.35) means continued emissions and unlocks can suppress price.
- **Narrative dependence:** valuation leans heavily on the AI + IP + RWA narratives staying in favor; rotation away from these themes hits IP disproportionately.
- **Adoption risk:** the IP-on-chain thesis is early and unproven at scale; real licensing volume and enforceable off-chain legal recognition remain open questions.
- **Bear-market beta:** with [[fear-and-greed-index|Fear & Greed]] at 23 (extreme fear) and an established bear-market regime, small-cap L1s like IP carry elevated downside and liquidity risk; the recent bounce can reverse with the tape.
- **Volatility / liquidity:** ~$113M market cap and ~$34M daily volume make IP a small-cap, high-volatility asset; liquidity is good for the tier but still prone to sharp moves and Upbit-driven KRW spikes.

## Overview

Data Network is a purpose-built layer 1 blockchain designed to facilitate registration of intellectual property (IP) of AI-related data to the blockchain and make it programmable.

Data Network tokenizes any type of IP; whether that’s an image, an AI model, a datapoint that trains a frontier model. By embedding usage terms, attribution, and royalty agreements directly into the blockchain, Story provides a transparent, decentralized solution for IP management for AI data. This enables AI data holders to protect their user's source contributions, collaborate with AI companies seamlessly, and unlock revenue opportunities in an AI-powered economy.
