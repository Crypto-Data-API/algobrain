## Overview

Altcoinist, The Trader In Your Pocket, is autonomous trading infrastructure for onchain markets. The platform combines execution, signal detection, and position management into one integrated system designed to reduce the manual effort required in active trading.

The execution engine routes trades across 100+ decentralized exchanges. Signal intelligence aggregates onchain activity, social data, and market indicators to identify tokens showing confluence, where multiple data sources point in the same direction. Position management provides 24/7 monitoring of open trades with alerts for scaling, profit-taking, and risk management.

These three layers are designed to function as a connected loop rather than standalone tools. Data from execution informs signal detection, signals guide position decisions, and position outcomes feed back into the system. This architecture supports the platform's long-term direction: an autonomous trading agent that can monitor markets, identify opportunities, and execute trades based on user-defined risk parameters.

Altcoinist currently operates as a Telegram-based trading interface, with planned expansion to additional interfaces and integration.
