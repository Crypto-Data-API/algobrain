# ShareX

**ShareX** (SHARE) is the Web3 infrastructure for the sharing economy,providing a standardized IRL onboarding toolkit and verifiable settlement layer to put shared and unattended devices, usage data, and cash flows on-chain, unlocking revenue-based RWAs at scale. It ranks **#1753** by market capitalization.
