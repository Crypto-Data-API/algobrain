## Overview

Ordz Games is the first gaming project on Bitcoin powered by Ordinals x Runes x DEPIN. Backers include Waterdrip Capital, Unisat, Satoshi Lab, CoinSummer, and Sora Venture.Ordz Games inscribed fully on-chain games on Bitcoin, and launched the world’s 1st web3 gaming device dubbed the BitBoy One. As reported by Decrypt, CoinDesk, and VentureBeat, the BitBoy One initial batch sold out within 2 minutes. With over 100,000 followers on socials and 120,000 players in the past 4 gaming seasons, Ordz Games has the largest gaming community on Bitcoin.
