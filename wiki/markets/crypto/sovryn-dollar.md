# Sovryn Dollar

**Sovryn Dollar** (DLLR) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#2341** by market capitalization. Bitcoin-backed dollar-pegged stablecoin, forked from Liquity, with RBTC (rootstock bitcoin peg) as the collateral. Currently overcollateralized 300% by bitcoin.
