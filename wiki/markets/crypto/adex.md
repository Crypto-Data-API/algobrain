## Overview

heyAura is a personal AI agent, helping users optimize their portfolio by generating yield, identifying opportunities, and automatically claiming airdrops. It gathers real-time data by analyzing onchain activity, assessing risk tolerance, and considering current market conditions and trends to deliver the most suitable suggestions. heyAura can even execute strategies on smart accounts by taking into account the user risk tolerance level.
