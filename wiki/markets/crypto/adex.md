## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

ADX is tradable on **Binance SPOT only** — there is no liquid perpetual venue for it, so leverage and short access are limited and this is a **spot-primary asset**. Perp funding, basis, and liquidation strategies do NOT apply. With no borrow/short rail and thin depth (24h volume is modest for a sub-$10M-cap token), execution should assume meaningful slippage on market orders. Position sizing must be conservative and long-biased or flat: use limit orders, scale entries/exits across the book, and avoid clip sizes that would move the ADXUSDT order book. Venue concentration on a single spot exchange also means all execution risk is tied to one book's depth.

### Applicable strategies

- [[dca-strategy]] — spot-only, long-biased structure with no short rail makes systematic accumulation the natural fit for a deep-drawdown ADX (over -98% from ATH).
- [[breakout-and-retest]] — a low-liquidity name that moves in sharp, narrative-driven bursts rewards waiting for a confirmed breakout and retest rather than chasing the initial spike.
- [[range-trading]] — for long stretches ADX drifts in a tight band (recent 24h range near $0.057–$0.061), suiting patient buy-low/sell-high inside defined support/resistance.
- [[rsi-mean-reversion]] — thin depth produces exaggerated intraday extremes that revert; fading oversold RSI on spot fits the no-leverage constraint.
- [[atr-trailing-stop]] — high per-trade volatility on a small-cap demands a volatility-scaled trailing stop to lock gains and cap downside without a hard leverage stop-out.
- [[narrative-trading]] — as an AI-agent/DeFAI token, ADX price is highly sensitive to AI and DeFi narrative rotations, making narrative timing a primary edge.

### Volatility & regime character

Micro-cap (rank ~1229, ~$8-9M market cap) with high reflexivity: moves are dominated by narrative flow (AI agents / DeFAI) and thin-book mechanics rather than fundamentals. High beta to broad crypto risk-on/risk-off and to the BTC/ETH majors, with amplified drawdowns and short-lived rallies typical of long-tail altcoins. As an Ethereum-based infra/DeFi token, it tends to track DeFi and AI-sector sentiment more than the BTC beta trade in isolation. Expect low baseline liquidity punctuated by episodic volume spikes on catalysts.

### Risk flags

- **Liquidity & venue concentration** — spot-only on Binance with no perp venue; single-book depth means wide spreads and slippage, and any Binance listing change is a concentrated risk.
- **Small-cap fragility** — sub-$10M market cap is prone to sharp, low-volume swings and is easily moved by a single large order.
- **Narrative dependence** — valuation leans on the AI-agent/DeFAI narrative; a sector rotation out of AI themes can compress price quickly.
- **Supply/tokenomics** — market cap near FDV (MC/FDV ~0.99) limits future dilution risk, but low circulating float still amplifies volatility.
- **No hedge rail** — absence of a liquid perpetual removes the ability to short or hedge exposure, so risk must be managed by sizing and stops on spot.
