## Overview

ZAI is an Onboarding AI Agent Swarm Framework for Billions, exploring ergonomic, lightweight multi-agent orchestration for crypto trading and mass adoption.
The ZAI platform integrates AI technology, blockchain data analysis, and community collaboration into an innovative framework, aiming to build a dynamically evolving open ecosystem driven by user behavior. By leveraging users’ learning patterns, transaction data, and community contributions, ZAI establishes an efficient training mechanism that empowers AI Agents to continuously enhance their intelligence. This enables the platform to deliver more precise, efficient, and personalized services to users, fostering intelligent iteration and sustainable growth within the ecosystem.
