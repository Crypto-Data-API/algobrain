# Turbo

**Turbo** (ticker **TURBO**) is an [[ethereum|Ethereum]]-based, frog-themed [[meme-coins|meme coin]] notable for being marketed as **the first memecoin created largely with AI** — its creator, artist Rhett Mankind, used [[ethereum|Ethereum]] tooling alongside ChatGPT/GPT-4 to design the token and brand on a small budget. It has no intrinsic utility beyond community, culture, and speculation, and trades as an [[erc-20|ERC-20]] on Ethereum (with a bridged presence on [[solana|Solana]]) under the ticker TURBO.

## Reflexive Valuation

TURBO has **no fundamentals** — no protocol revenue, fee capture, staking yield, or treasury. Its ~$61M market cap is a pure **attention price**: it rises and falls with virality, exchange listings, and where it sits in the [[meme-coin-cycle|meme rotation]], not with any underlying business. Because supply is fully circulating (cap = FDV, no unlocks), the cap moves one-for-one with sentiment. The only honest valuation lens is *relative attention vs. peer memes* — there is no discounted-cash-flow anchor and no intrinsic floor.

TURBO trades across spot CEXes, Ethereum DEXes, and an active perpetual market — meaning leverage and funding play a real role in its volatility.

- **Perps & funding/OI:** TURBO has a perpetual listing on [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]] and CEX derivatives venues. As a high-beta meme, funding can swing hard — strongly positive when retail crowds into longs during hype, sharply negative during capitulation — and liquidation cascades on leveraged OI drive its most violent moves.
- **DEX liquidity:** on-chain trading runs through [[uniswap|Uniswap]] V3 TURBO/WETH pools on Ethereum, so DEX traders pay gas plus slippage.
- **Liquidity note:** ~$6.8M daily volume against a ~$61M cap is roughly ~11% turnover — healthier than smaller BNB memes, supported by its broad CEX listings and active perp.

## Use Case / Narrative / Category

TURBO is a pure **meme coin**, with a hook that sets it apart in the category.

- **"AI-made meme" narrative:** its origin story — a memecoin conceived with the help of GPT-4 on a shoestring budget — gives it a durable, repeatable marketing angle that ties it to the AI hype cycle as well as the meme cycle.
- **Frog-themed community:** part of the broader frog/animal-meme lineage; value is sustained by community engagement, virality, and exchange listings rather than fundamentals.
- **Categories:** [[meme-coins|Meme]], AI Meme, Frog-Themed, [[ethereum|Ethereum Ecosystem]].

## Notable History

- **2023:** Launched (April 2023) by Rhett Mankind as an experiment in AI-assisted token creation, quickly going viral on the "first AI memecoin" framing.
- **2024:** Rode the meme-coin mania to its all-time high near $0.0143 (December 2024) before retracing with the broader meme sector.
- **2025–2026:** Expanded to [[solana|Solana]] via bridging and retained CEX/perp listings; trades around $0.00089, ~94% below its December 2024 ATH ($0.01432) but still ~14x above its September 2023 all-time low ($0.0000652) — a typical late-cycle meme profile.

## Risks

- **No fundamentals:** TURBO has no cash flows, utility, or yield. Its entire value rests on attention and sentiment, which evaporate quickly — especially in an **extreme-fear, established-bear** regime ([[crypto-fear-and-greed-index|F&G]] = 23) where meme coins are usually the first sold.
- **Near-total drawdown potential:** already ~94% below ATH; most memes that fade never reclaim prior highs, and a draw back toward the ATL is a realistic tail.
- **Reflexive volatility:** meme prices are reflexive and momentum-driven; rallies overshoot and reversals are abrupt and unforgiving.
- **Leverage/liquidation risk:** the active perp market means leveraged longs can be wiped out in cascading liquidations on small spot moves.
- **Narrative fatigue:** the "AI memecoin" hook differentiates TURBO, but novelty fades and newer memes constantly compete for the same speculative capital.
- **Rug / liquidity & concentration risk:** meme tokens often have concentrated holders; large wallets exiting can crater a thin order book, and on-chain holder distribution is not ingested here.

> *Risk note: pure meme coin with no intrinsic value, traded with leverage. Among the highest-risk assets in this group; size positions accordingly and assume extreme drawdown potential.*
