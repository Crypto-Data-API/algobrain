## Overview

Klever Coin (KLV) is the native utility token of the Klever Blockchain, designed to enhance user experience and drive the platform's functionality. Klever Blockchain offers low transaction fees, making it cost-effective for transfers and smart contract executions. 

Creators, Designers, Influencers and Entrepreneurs can utilize KLV to create and deploy decentralized applications (dApps) , tokens and stablecoins without code. 

Developers can deploy advanced Rust and WebAssembly based smart contracts to create advanced autonomous AI agents and machines, promoting innovation within the ecosystem.

Users can stake KLV to support network security and earn rewards, fostering active participation. 

Integrated across various Klever services, including the Klever Wallet and Klever Swap, KLV ensures a unified and efficient user experience. Its multifaceted utility and central role in the Klever ecosystem position KLV as a valuable asset for those engaging with blockchain technology.

Klever Blockchain's offer gasless token innovation eliminates this issue by allowing users to pay gas fees using the same token they are transacting. Token creators can decided how much will be the cost of each transaction.
