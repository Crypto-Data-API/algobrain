## Overview

Synapse Power is a U.S.-based AI infrastructure project focused on delivering high-performance GPU compute solutions for artificial intelligence, deep learning, scientific computing, advanced rendering, and real-time inference workloads. The project is designed to provide scalable, reliable, and transparent compute infrastructure, supporting developers, enterprises, and platforms that require consistent execution rather than speculative or virtualized services.
