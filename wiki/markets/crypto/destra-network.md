## Overview

Destra Network: Building the Web3 AI Stack

Destra is a decentralized AI-native network, purpose-built to run autonomous agents, decentralized compute, and real-world infrastructure. From inference to execution, Destra unifies the essential layers of Web3—AI, compute, privacy, and economics—into one composable ecosystem.

At the core lies $DSYNC, a deflationary utility token that powers and captures value from every product interaction across the network.
