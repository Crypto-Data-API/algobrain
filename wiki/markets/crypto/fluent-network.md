## Overview

Fluent is the blockchain for reputation-based apps that depend on long-term user behavior and contribution quality. Using Prints, Fluent’s reputation data layer, developers can integrate verifiable, cross-context reputation scores built from onchain activity, social signals, and contribution history directly into their apps. This enables features such as trust-gated access, believability-weighted governance, dynamic incentives that reward quality over volume, and more.
