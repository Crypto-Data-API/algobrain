# Creditcoin

**Creditcoin** (ticker **CTC**) is a Layer 1 EVM blockchain focused on **on-chain credit**: recording lending and borrowing history on a public ledger so credit can be built, verified, and ported across chains — with a focus on [[real-world-assets|real-world assets (RWAs)]], decentralized lending, and [[depin|DePIN]] use cases. The exchange-listed token, **CTC (G-CRE)**, is the [[ethereum|Ethereum]]-bridged, vesting version of Creditcoin's native token, letting Ethereum users participate in the native ecosystem. The project is in the DWF Labs portfolio and tagged "Made in USA."

## Technology / Protocol (RWA credit L1)

Creditcoin is a standalone **Layer 1 EVM blockchain** purpose-built for credit primitives rather than general DeFi:

| Property | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Layer 1, EVM-compatible |
| **Consensus** | Proof of Stake (uncapped emissions) |
| **Core function** | On-chain recording of loan origination, repayment, and credit history |
| **Bridged token** | CTC (G-CRE) on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] — vesting/bridged representation of native CTC |
| **Focus areas** | [[real-world-assets|RWA]] lending, emerging-market fintech credit, [[depin|DePIN]] |

The thesis is **portable, verifiable credit history**: by recording lending/borrowing on a public ledger, a borrower (or a fintech lender) can build a credit record that is auditable and reusable across chains and partners — particularly aimed at emerging-market lending where traditional credit bureaus are weak. The bridged CTC (G-CRE) lets [[ethereum|Ethereum]] users hold and trade exposure while the native token secures the L1.

## Valuation Framing

CTC's valuation hinges on a single unverified variable: **real loan-origination volume** through the network. As an infrastructure/RWA play, the bull case is that credit primitives scale with emerging-market fintech adoption; the bear case is that on-chain credit remains a thesis without throughput. With MC/FDV ~0.90 there is little dilution discount to apply, so the market cap is close to "fully valued" relative to current supply — meaning upside must come from adoption, not float mechanics, while uncapped emissions exert a persistent inflationary drag. The chart (down ~99% from 2021, hugging a 2026 all-time low) reflects the market's current skepticism.

## Overview

CTC (G-CRE) is the Ethereum-bridged version of Creditcoin's native token. CTC (G-CRE) is designed as a vesting token and is the token listed on centralized exchanges. This bridged version allows Ethereum users to participate in the native Creditcoin ecosystem, a layer 1 EVM blockchain that enables developers to build multi-chain applications for real use cases, such as decentralized lending, real-world assets (RWAs), and decentralized physical infrastructure networks (DePIN).
