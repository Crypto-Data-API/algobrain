# Hippius

**Hippius** (SN75) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#939** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** decentralized storage network on Bittensor. Miners store data and prove availability; validators issue challenges and score retrievability. Positions itself as Bittensor-native storage infrastructure, adjacent to Filecoin, Arweave, Sia.
- **Team:** Hippius (hippius.com).
- **Trading relevance:** storage-category exposure; competes with external storage networks for the same "decentralized data persistence" thesis.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-75.
