# PowerSnookerCoin

**PowerSnookerCoin** (PSC) is a utility token created to support the Power Snooker ecosystem, a modernised and dynamic version of snooker designed for digital audiences and interactive platforms. The token is used within the Power Snooker digital platform to access content, purchase merchandise, participate in fan-driven activities, and interact with players and events.
