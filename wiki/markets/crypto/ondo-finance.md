# Ondo

**Ondo** (ONDO) is the governance token of the Ondo DAO and ecosystem built by Ondo Finance, the market leader in tokenized [[real-world-assets|real-world assets]]: tokenized Treasuries (OUSG, [[ondo-us-dollar-yield|USDY]]) and, since 2025, **Ondo Global Markets — the dominant tokenized US stocks/ETFs platform with >70% market share and >$1B TVL (first ever for tokenized equities)**. For traders, ONDO is the flagship liquid proxy for the RWA/tokenization narrative — one of the few 2025–2026 sectors with verifiable fundamental growth metrics.

## Overview

The Ondo Foundation was founded in 2022 as an independently governed, nonprofit Cayman Islands Foundation Company with no beneficial owners. It assisted the launch of the Ondo DAO, which deployed and governs Flux Finance, the first lending protocol supporting tokenized securities as collateral. ONDO token holders govern the Ondo DAO and shape its mission to democratize access to institutional-grade finance.

The token launched in January 2024 via CoinList; the protocol's products span the tokenized-asset stack detailed below.

## Protocol / Technology

Ondo Finance is the market leader in tokenized [[real-world-assets|real-world assets (RWA)]] — wrapping yield-bearing, regulated, off-chain instruments (US Treasuries, money-market exposure, US equities) into on-chain tokens that settle on public blockchains. ONDO is the **governance token of the Ondo DAO**; it does not itself represent a claim on the underlying assets or their cash flows (a critical valuation caveat — see below). The product suite:

| Product | What it is | Audience | Scale (2026) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **OUSG** | Tokenized US Treasuries (originally fed by BlackRock's short-duration funds) | Qualified purchasers / institutions | Institutional Treasury exposure on-chain |
| **[[ondo-us-dollar-yield\|USDY]]** | Yield-bearing tokenized dollar note backed by short-term Treasuries + bank deposits | Non-US persons (retail-accessible) | ~$2.1B (June 2026) |
| **Ondo Global Markets (OGM)** | Tokenized US stocks & ETFs | Global non-US retail/institutional | >$1B TVL, 260+ assets, $18B cumulative volume |
| **Ondo Chain** | Permissioned EVM Layer-1 purpose-built for institutional tokenized assets | Institutions / issuers | Central to 2025–2026 roadmap |
| **Flux Finance** | Lending protocol accepting tokenized securities as collateral | DeFi / on-chain credit | First lending market for tokenized securities |

- **OUSG** is Ondo's institutional Treasury product, offering qualified purchasers tokenized exposure to short-duration US government debt with on-chain transferability and (intraday) liquidity.
- **[[ondo-us-dollar-yield|USDY]]** is the retail-facing yield-bearing dollar note for non-US persons, backed by short-term Treasuries and bank deposits. It had grown to roughly **$2.1B** by June 2026, making it one of the largest tokenized yield instruments in crypto.
- **Ondo Global Markets (OGM)** is the breakout product: a [[tokenized-stocks|tokenized US equities/ETF]] platform that **surpassed $1B TVL in June 2026 — the first tokenized-stocks platform ever to do so** — less than eight months after launch. It offers **260+ tokenized US stocks and ETFs across [[solana|Solana]], [[ethereum|Ethereum]] and BNB Chain**, distributed through [[binance|Binance]], Bitget, MetaMask and Blockchain.com, and holds **>70% market share** among tokenized-equity issuers (per RWA.xyz) with **>$18B cumulative trading volume**.
- **Ondo Chain** is a permissioned EVM Layer-1 designed for institutional tokenized assets — combining the openness of a public chain with permissioning/compliance controls institutions require. It was unveiled at the Ondo Summit in New York and is central to the 2025–2026 roadmap.
- **Flux Finance** is the on-chain lending protocol (deployed and governed by the Ondo DAO) that accepts tokenized securities as collateral — the first lending market to do so.

## Ecosystem & Use Cases

Ondo's positioning is as the **institutional-grade gateway to tokenized finance** — bridging regulated TradFi yield instruments to public-chain DeFi:

- **Tokenized yield for non-US retail** — [[ondo-us-dollar-yield|USDY]] lets non-US users hold a dollar instrument that earns Treasury yield while remaining composable in DeFi (collateral, LP, payments).
- **Institutional Treasury cash management** — OUSG offers qualified purchasers on-chain Treasury exposure with near-instant settlement vs. T+1 in traditional rails.
- **Global equity access** — OGM brings 260+ US stocks/ETFs to users who cannot easily access US brokerage, distributed via [[binance|Binance]], Bitget, MetaMask and Blockchain.com across [[solana|Solana]], [[ethereum|Ethereum]] and BNB Chain.
- **On-chain credit** — Flux Finance lets holders borrow against tokenized securities.
- **Compliance-first infrastructure** — Ondo Chain extends the stack with a permissioned EVM L1 tailored for issuers and institutions.

The throughline: ONDO is the most liquid public-market proxy for the entire **[[real-world-assets|RWA]] / tokenization** thesis, one of the few 2025–2026 crypto sectors showing verifiable fundamental growth.

## Valuation Framework

ONDO is unusual among altcoins in having **trackable fundamentals**, which makes a qualitative valuation lens possible:

- **Fundamental dashboard**: USDY supply (~$2.1B), OGM TVL (>$1B) and cumulative volume (>$18B), total RWA TVL (~$2.5B+) — all observable via RWA.xyz and Ondo reporting. This enables **divergence trades**: when protocol metrics grow but the token lags (as in early-mid 2026), the gap can mean-revert.
- **The key caveat**: the **ONDO governance token captures no direct fee value** from OUSG/USDY/OGM. Product revenue accrues to Ondo Finance / the foundation, not to token holders by default — so "fundamentals up" does not mechanically translate to "token up." Value accrual is governance-optionality and narrative premium, not cash flow.
- **Dilution drag**: MC/FDV ≈ 0.49 means fully-diluted value (~$3.5B) is roughly double the circulating market cap (~$1.71B); future unlocks weigh on price.
- **Drawdown context**: -83.6% from the Dec 2024 ATH of $2.14; the token is valued on a blend of sector-leadership scarcity, milestone optionality, and beta to the RWA narrative rather than on discounted cash flows.

## Trading Profile

**Venues & liquidity** — ONDO is a deep, liquid two-venue perp market. It trades on **[[binance|Binance]]** (ONDO/USDT spot plus a USD-margined perpetual) and on **[[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]]** (ONDO-PERP, up to ~40-50x leverage). Binance provides the primary CEX depth and price discovery for both spot and perp, while Hyperliquid offers transparent on-chain order-book depth and funding. Because ONDO is a top-~50 name (#43-#49 by cap) with meaningful open interest and multiple deep spot venues (Coinbase, Kraken, Upbit, Bitget, KuCoin, Uniswap V3), execution slippage is modest for typical sizes and the two-venue structure supports both cross-venue basis/funding work and confident sizing. The dual Binance + Hyperliquid footprint means spot legs can be hedged against either perp, and CEX-vs-DEX funding gaps are directly tradable.

**Applicable strategies**

- [[funding-rate-harvest]] — ONDO funding flips positive during RWA-narrative rallies and negative into unlock-driven selloffs, so harvesting the persistent funding side (long/short spot vs. perp) is a repeatable carry play on a liquid name.
- [[hl-vs-cex-funding-divergence]] — with active perps on both Binance and Hyperliquid, funding can diverge between the CEX and the on-chain venue, giving a clean two-venue funding-arbitrage leg.
- [[cash-and-carry]] — deep Binance/Coinbase spot plus USD-margined perps let you run a delta-neutral long-spot / short-perp carry to capture positive funding without directional risk.
- [[narrative-trading]] — ONDO is the most liquid pure-play proxy for the RWA/tokenization thesis; OGM TVL milestones and tokenized-securities headlines drive tradable narrative moves.
- [[token-unlock-supply-event]] — with MC/FDV ~0.49 and team/investor tranches vesting through ~2029, scheduled unlock cliffs are predictable, calendarable supply-pressure events to trade around.
- [[event-driven-trading]] — OGM TVL milestones, Ondo Chain progress, new exchange/chain distribution deals and regulatory rulings on tokenized securities are discrete catalysts to position into and fade on sell-the-news.

**Volatility & regime character** — ONDO is a high-beta DeFi / RWA-infrastructure altcoin. It carries strong beta to broad crypto risk appetite (BTC/ETH), but its distinguishing feature is a fundamental-driven overlay: it can decouple on RWA-narrative flows and OGM milestones even when majors are flat. Volatility is elevated and reflexive around unlock cliffs and narrative catalysts; in the current extreme-fear / Established Bear Market regime it has been relatively resilient for a high-beta alt, but it remains -80%+ off its ATH and moves hard in risk-off conditions.

**Risk flags**

- **Unlock / dilution overhang** — MC/FDV ~0.49; roughly half the supply is still locked, with vesting through ~2029. The Jan 2025 unlock cliff coincided with the ATH and the subsequent -88% drawdown. Dominant structural risk for longs and for any carry/basis position held into a cliff.
- **Narrative dependence** — much of the token's premium is RWA/tokenization narrative and milestone optionality rather than fee accrual (governance token captures no direct product revenue); narrative reversals hit price disproportionately.
- **Regulatory dependence** — OGM and tokenized securities exist because the US regulatory climate warmed in 2025-2026; an adverse tokenized-securities ruling is an existential product risk and a tail for the token.
- **Perp funding dislocations** — narrative rallies and unlock selloffs push funding to extremes and can crowd one side; monitor Binance and Hyperliquid funding/OI for squeeze and cascade risk before sizing leverage.
- **Political-headline sensitivity** — ONDO sits in the World Liberty Financial (Trump-affiliated) portfolio, adding headline and reputational two-way risk.
