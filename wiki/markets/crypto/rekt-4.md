## Overview

Rekt Brands Inc. encompasses several different verticals spanning art, culture, media, NFTs, and physical goods:

Rekt Drinks - Rekt Drinks is considered the official drink of web3.  It boasts one of the largest web2 product launches from a web3 brand with 222,456 drinks (flavoured sparkling water) sold out at public launch across 32 different countries in under 48 hours. 

Rektguy NFT collection - a FREE mint launched in 2022 with over $120M of trading volume and a cult-like community, including holders such as Snoop Dogg, Steve Aoki, Bassjackers and Gary Vaynerchuk.

Rekt Art - OSF is a former trader turned artist who has collaborated with Red Bull, Bybit, Ledger, Sotheby’s, Saatchi Gallery, House of Fine Art &amp; more.
Rekt Events - events held throughout the year including NYC, London, Paris, Miami, Lisbon, Dubai, Singapore, Las Vegas, Marfa &amp; Taipei.
Rekt Radio - a top crypto podcast produced by Rekt and Rug Radio, hosted by OSF, Mando and Keyboard Monkey. It features the biggest names in crypto including Raoul Pal, Beeple, Bitboy, Su Zhu, Ansem, RookieXBT, TraderMayne, Alex Wice, DCInvestor and more.

$REKT will be the token that underpins this entire ecosystem, staying true to the project’s memetic routes, while allowing for holders to gain exclusive access to parts of the ecosystem as well as significantly expanding its membership.

## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

Tradable on Binance SPOT only — no liquid perpetual venue, so leverage/short access is limited and this is a spot-primary asset. Perp funding/basis/liquidation strategies do NOT apply. With a single primary spot venue and thin depth typical of a sub-$60M-cap memecoin, execution should assume meaningful slippage: use limit orders, split large fills, and size positions to what spot liquidity can absorb. Absence of a deep perp market means directional exposure is effectively long-only (no easy borrow to short), and stops must be managed on spot rather than via funding-driven hedges.

### Applicable strategies

- [[momentum-investing]] — REKT is a high-beta memecoin that trends hard on narrative flows; riding confirmed upside momentum captures the reflexive pumps while avoiding buying into decay.
- [[breakout-trading]] — thin spot depth and memecoin reflexivity produce sharp range breaks on volume spikes; entering on a clean break of consolidation aligns with how these moves start.
- [[volatility-breakout]] — long stretches of low activity punctuated by violent expansions suit a volatility-expansion trigger rather than a fixed level.
- [[atr-trailing-stop]] — given wide intraday swings and gap risk on a single venue, an ATR-based trailing stop lets winners run while capping the frequent, fast reversals.
- [[dca-strategy]] — for accumulation-minded holders, spreading entries dampens the impact of extreme price swings and thin liquidity on any single fill.
- [[narrative-trading]] — REKT's value is tied to the Rekt Brands meme ecosystem (drinks, NFTs, events, radio); trading around narrative and community catalysts fits its driver set better than fundamentals.

### Volatility & regime character

Small-cap memecoin (rank ~424, sub-$60M cap) with very high beta and pronounced reflexivity. Price action is dominated by sentiment, community momentum, and broader risk appetite rather than cash flows or protocol usage. Expect strong positive correlation to BTC/ETH risk-on regimes and amplified drawdowns in risk-off phases; realized volatility is high with frequent large percentage swings on modest notional flow.

### Risk flags

- Liquidity/venue concentration: spot-primary with a single main venue; thin order books raise slippage and gap risk, and there is no liquid perp to hedge.
- Supply/emissions: extremely large fixed supply (420.69T) and micro unit price mean sentiment-driven repricing can be violent; monitor for concentrated holder distribution.
- Narrative dependence: valuation hinges on the Rekt Brands meme/community flywheel — waning attention can drive sustained declines independent of the broader market.
- Multi-chain footprint: contracts across several chains fragment liquidity and add bridge/contract-surface considerations for on-chain sizing.
