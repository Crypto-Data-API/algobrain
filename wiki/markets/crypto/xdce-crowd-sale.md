# XDC Network

**XDC Network** (XDC) is an enterprise-focused **hybrid Layer 1** blockchain (originally launched as XinFin in 2017–2019) optimized for international trade finance and [[real-world-assets|real-world asset (RWA)]] tokenization, using a delegated proof-of-stake consensus (XDPoS) with KYC'd masternode validators. For traders, XDC is one of the core liquid plays on the **RWA / institutional tokenization narrative**: a sub-$1B-cap asset whose price has historically responded to institutional partnership announcements (Archax, SBI, Animoca) rather than retail flows.

## Overview

XDC Network is an enterprise-ready hybrid Blockchain technology company optimized for international trade and finance. The XDC Network is powered by the native coin called XDC. The XDC protocol is architected to support smart contracts, 2000TPS, 2seconds transaction time, KYC to Masternodes (Validator Nodes). The XDC Chain (XinFin Digital Contract) uses XinFin Delegated Proof of Stake (XDPoS), with the intending to create a ‘highly-scalable, secure, permission, and commercial grade’ blockchain network. XinFin mainnet token XDC and also creates an opportunity to utilize the XinFin’s real-world use-cases such as TradeFinex.org, helps small and medium businesses or institutions originate their own financial requirements in a digital, fully structured manner so that they can distribute it to the bank or non-bank funders themselves using a common distribution standard.

---

## Protocol / Technology

XDC is an **enterprise-grade hybrid Layer 1**: a single network that runs a **public** state (anyone can transact and read) alongside **permissioned** features (KYC'd validators, optional private sub-networks/sidechains) so that regulated institutions can use the chain without exposing counterparties or commercially sensitive trade data.

- **Lineage** — launched as **XinFin** (XinFin Digital Contract) in **2017–2019**, later rebranded to **XDC Network**. The native token (originally "XDCe" as the ERC-20 crowd-sale instrument, hence the CoinGecko id `xdce-crowd-sale`) migrated to the XDC mainnet coin.
- **Consensus — XDPoS** ([XinFin Delegated [[proof-of-stake|Proof of Stake]]]): a delegated PoS where a bounded set of **masternode validators must be KYC'd**. This is the deliberate design trade — sacrificing permissionless validation for the identity/accountability layer that banks and trade-finance counterparties require. Institutional validators reportedly include **Deutsche Telekom, SBI Holdings, UOB Venture Management, HashKeyCloud, Republic, and Animoca Brands**.
- **EVM compatibility** — XDC is EVM-compatible, so Solidity contracts and Ethereum-style tooling port over; it positions itself as an enterprise-tuned alternative to public [[ethereum]] with lower, more predictable fees.
- **Performance claims** — ~**2,000 TPS** and ~**2-second block times**, marketed as suitable for high-volume settlement.
- **Standards alignment** — XDC emphasizes **ISO 20022** messaging alignment, the dominant standard for cross-border financial messaging, to slot into existing bank/trade-finance rails. This is the same regulated-finance positioning that anchors peers like [[quant-network|Quant]].
- **Trade-finance stack** — TradeFinex and related tooling let SMEs and institutions originate and distribute trade-finance instruments (invoices, receivables, letters of credit) on-chain in a structured, bank-distributable format.

- **No hard cap.** Unlike fixed-supply peers ([[quant-network|QNT]] 14.6M cap, [[bitcoin|BTC]] 21M cap), XDC has **no maximum supply** — emission is open-ended, and only ~52% of total supply is in circulation.
- **MC/FDV ≈ 0.52** — roughly **half** the total supply sits outside the float. This is a **structural dilution headwind**: as locked/non-circulating tokens enter the market, they apply continuous downward pressure that long holders must out-earn via adoption-driven demand. ⚠️ This is the single most important structural caution for the asset.
- **Demand side** — token utility is tied to gas/fees, masternode staking collateral, and ecosystem/RWA settlement. Whether that demand can absorb open-ended emission is the core long thesis question.

## Ecosystem & Use Cases

XDC's thesis is **international trade finance + [[real-world-assets|RWA]] tokenization**, an institution-first niche rather than retail DeFi.

- **Archax integration** — Archax, the UK's first FCA-regulated digital securities exchange, integrated with XDC Network, bringing tokenized money market funds from **BlackRock, Fidelity International, State Street and abrdn** onto the chain (verified June 2026).
- **Contour acquisition (Oct 2025)** — Contour Network, the trade-finance platform co-founded by **HSBC, Citi and Standard Chartered**, was acquired by XDC Ventures and integrated into the protocol — bringing an established bank-grade trade-finance network in-house.
- **VERT Capital (2025)** — ~**$1B** of Brazilian debt and receivables tokenization announced on XDC.
- **Animoca Brands validator (May 2026)** — Animoca Brands joined as an institutional masternode validator, alongside existing institutional validators Deutsche Telekom, HashKeyCloud, Republic, SBI Holdings and UOB Venture Management.
- **RWA traction** — by end-2025 XDC had surpassed ~**$717M** in tokenized RWAs (Trade Fi Network data) with ~$200M in on-chain stablecoin liquidity; UK fintechs moved over **£500M** of supply-chain assets onto XDC in early 2026 under the **UK Digital Trade Roadmap**.

## Valuation Framework (qualitative)

XDC has **no cash flows** to discount; valuation is narrative- and adoption-driven:

- **Adoption thesis** — the bull case is real trade-finance and tokenized-fund volume migrating on-chain. **RWA TVL** (~$717M end-2025, plus £500M UK supply-chain assets) is the closest thing to a fundamental gauge — track whether it compounds.
- **Dilution drag** — with **no hard cap** and **MC/FDV ≈ 0.52**, structural emission is a persistent valuation headwind; demand must out-grow supply expansion.
- **Drawdown context** — **-84.7% from the 2021 ATH** ($0.1928). The asset is deep in a multi-year bear and reprices mostly on partnership headlines, not continuous flow.
- **Peer relative value** — judged against [[ondo-finance|Ondo]] (RWA leader, trackable TVL) and [[quant-network|Quant]] (enterprise interop), XDC trades on a trade-finance niche with credible institutional logos but opaque conversion of announcements into recurring on-chain volume.
