## Overview

ZenChain is a Bitcoin-native EVM Layer 1, engineered from first principles to unify ecosystems, preserve security guarantees, and enable programmable utility across chains. It features native interoperability between Bitcoin and Ethereum, bridging non-programmable and programmable assets through trust-minimized infrastructure, smart contract-based rewards through ZenEarn, and validator-aligned LP staking.
