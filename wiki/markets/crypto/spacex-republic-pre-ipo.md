## Overview

SpaceX (Space Exploration Technologies Corp.) was founded by Elon Musk in 2002. The company focuses on reducing launch costs through reusable rockets and advancing the development of commercial spaceflight. Its flagship products include Falcon 9, Dragon, and Starship. Its long-term goal is to enable human missions to Mars and support multi-planetary life. preSPAX is a digital token issued by a regulated issuer, Republic, designed to track the economic performance of SpaceX after it goes public.
