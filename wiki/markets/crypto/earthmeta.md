## Overview

$EMT token is a utility token serving as the backbone of EarthMeta Metaverse. It is the EarthMeta ecosystem's primary currency, facilitating transactions, rewards, and governance participation.
EarthMeta is a Metaverse where the users can own a City and Govern it. Sell lands as NFTs, and create an economy in their city like a strategy game.
