# Bio Protocol

**Bio Protocol** (ticker **BIO**) is the curation, funding and liquidity protocol at the centre of the **Decentralized Science ([[desci|DeSci]])** movement. Its mission is to let global communities of patients, scientists and biotech professionals collectively fund, build and own tokenized biotech projects and intellectual property (IP). The BIO token is multi-chain ‚Äî issued on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] with deployments on [[solana|Solana]], [[base|Base]] and BNB Chain ‚Äî and is the reference asset for the on-chain "BioDAO" ecosystem.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Bio Protocol is the flagship of **DeSci** ‚Äî using crypto rails to fund early-stage biomedical research that struggles to attract traditional capital. The team previously created **Molecule** (a platform for tokenizing early-stage biomedicine IP via "IP-NFTs") and **VitaDAO** (the largest decentralized longevity-science community). BIO acts as a meta-layer: it curates and seeds individual "BioDAOs" (e.g. longevity, women's health, psychedelics research), provides liquidity, and gives holders broad exposure to the tokenized-science economy.

Category-wise BIO is the bellwether of the **[[desci|DeSci]]** narrative ‚Äî a niche, high-beta sub-sector of crypto distinct from the [[artificial-intelligence|AI]] and [[depin|DePIN]] cohorts, though it shares their early-stage, narrative-driven trading character. As the meta-layer that seeds and holds stakes in individual BioDAOs, BIO functions as a quasi-index of the on-chain science economy: it rises and falls with aggregate DeSci sentiment rather than any single research outcome.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

BIO defies cash-flow valuation: the protocol's worth is a leveraged bet on (1) the **DeSci narrative staying alive** and (2) tokenized biotech IP eventually producing fundable, value-accruing assets ‚Äî a decade-scale, low-base-rate outcome. With MC/FDV ~0.65, dilution is more contained than the AI cohort but still a ~1.17B-token overhang. The honest framing: BIO is a high-variance call option on a thesis that is intellectually compelling but commercially unproven, trading ~96% off its hype-peak ATH. Turnover that rivals a large fraction of market cap signals it remains a trading vehicle, not an accumulation asset, at present. Framing only, not advice.

## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

BIO is a genuine two-venue derivatives market. It trades **spot** on [[binance|Binance]] (a former Launchpool asset), [[kraken|Kraken]], Upbit (KRW), Bitget and KuCoin, and has a **USD-margined perpetual on Binance** alongside a **BIO-PERP on [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]]** (leverage up to ~40-50x). Having both a deep CEX book and an on-chain perp makes BIO one of the more liquid DeSci names: two independent price/funding sources support tighter execution, but as a ~#383-rank alt the book is still thin relative to majors. Practically, this means Hyperliquid vs Binance quotes can diverge during speculative bursts ‚Äî a spread that both aids CEX/DEX arbitrage and punishes oversized market orders. Size positions to the *thinner* of the two books, not the headline aggregate volume, since 24h turnover here is dominated by churn.

### Applicable strategies

- [[hl-vs-cex-funding-divergence]] ‚Äî BIO runs a perp on both Binance and Hyperliquid, so funding and mark can diverge between the two venues, creating a directly harvestable spread.
- [[funding-rate-harvest]] ‚Äî the two-venue perp market with volatile speculative flow lets a delta-neutral position collect funding when the crowd stampedes into leveraged longs on DeSci hype.
- [[crowded-long-funding-fade]] ‚Äî narrative-driven DeSci pumps routinely stack leveraged longs into positive funding, setting up a fade when crowding peaks.
- [[cash-and-carry]] ‚Äî with liquid spot on Binance/DEX and a perp on both venues, long-spot / short-perp carry is executable to monetise elevated funding without directional risk.
- [[narrative-trading]] ‚Äî BIO is the liquid bellwether of the DeSci theme; price is driven by aggregate narrative sentiment more than any single research outcome.
- [[liquidation-cascade-fade]] ‚Äî a thin, high-beta alt with perp leverage produces sharp liquidation flushes that overshoot and mean-revert, offering fade entries.

### Volatility & regime character

BIO is a **high-beta, narrative-driven infra/DeFi token** ‚Äî specifically the index-like proxy for the [[desci|DeSci]] sub-sector ‚Äî with memecoin-like reflexivity in its price action. It carries strong positive beta to broad crypto risk (BTC/ETH) on the downside: in an established [[bear-market|bear market]] it sells off with the rest of the risk curve, and its ~96%-off-ATH chart shows how punishing that beta is. On the upside it decouples to trade on DeSci-specific sentiment rather than BTC/ETH trend, giving it episodic, narrative-gated rallies rather than steady trend.

### Risk flags

- **Venue/liquidity concentration** ‚Äî real depth sits on Binance and Hyperliquid; a delisting, funding-cap change, or on-chain liquidity pull would sharply widen spreads for a #383-rank alt.
- **Dilution / emissions overhang** ‚Äî ~1.17B BIO (gap to the 3.32B max supply) remains to unlock; watch [[token-unlocks]] events for supply-driven pressure.
- **Narrative dependence** ‚Äî with no cash-flow anchor, price hinges on DeSci sentiment staying alive; attention and liquidity evaporate quickly when the theme fades.
- **Perp funding dislocations** ‚Äî thin two-venue perp liquidity means funding can swing violently and diverge between Binance and Hyperliquid during speculative churn; size and monitor funding on both venues before carrying leverage.
