# Bio Protocol

**Bio Protocol** (ticker **BIO**) is the curation, funding and liquidity protocol at the centre of the **Decentralized Science ([[desci|DeSci]])** movement. Its mission is to let global communities of patients, scientists and biotech professionals collectively fund, build and own tokenized biotech projects and intellectual property (IP). The BIO token is multi-chain — issued on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] with deployments on [[solana|Solana]], [[base|Base]] and BNB Chain — and is the reference asset for the on-chain "BioDAO" ecosystem.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Bio Protocol is the flagship of **DeSci** — using crypto rails to fund early-stage biomedical research that struggles to attract traditional capital. The team previously created **Molecule** (a platform for tokenizing early-stage biomedicine IP via "IP-NFTs") and **VitaDAO** (the largest decentralized longevity-science community). BIO acts as a meta-layer: it curates and seeds individual "BioDAOs" (e.g. longevity, women's health, psychedelics research), provides liquidity, and gives holders broad exposure to the tokenized-science economy.

Category-wise BIO is the bellwether of the **[[desci|DeSci]]** narrative — a niche, high-beta sub-sector of crypto distinct from the [[artificial-intelligence|AI]] and [[depin|DePIN]] cohorts, though it shares their early-stage, narrative-driven trading character. As the meta-layer that seeds and holds stakes in individual BioDAOs, BIO functions as a quasi-index of the on-chain science economy: it rises and falls with aggregate DeSci sentiment rather than any single research outcome.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

BIO defies cash-flow valuation: the protocol's worth is a leveraged bet on (1) the **DeSci narrative staying alive** and (2) tokenized biotech IP eventually producing fundable, value-accruing assets — a decade-scale, low-base-rate outcome. With MC/FDV ~0.65, dilution is more contained than the AI cohort but still a ~1.17B-token overhang. The honest framing: BIO is a high-variance call option on a thesis that is intellectually compelling but commercially unproven, trading ~96% off its hype-peak ATH. Turnover that rivals a large fraction of market cap signals it remains a trading vehicle, not an accumulation asset, at present. Framing only, not advice.

## Risks

- **Narrative-dependent and niche.** DeSci is a small, sentiment-driven corner of crypto; without continued narrative momentum, liquidity and attention evaporate quickly.
- **Extreme price decay.** A ~96% drawdown from ATH illustrates how punishing these early speculative names are; nothing guarantees the bottom is in.
- **Churn over conviction.** 24h volume equal to a large fraction of market cap signals rotational, speculative flow rather than durable demand.
- **Dilution overhang.** ~1.17B BIO still to unlock toward max supply. See [[token-unlocks]].
- **Execution risk in the underlying science.** The thesis ultimately rests on tokenized biotech actually producing fundable, value-accruing IP — a long, uncertain, regulation-heavy process.
- **Hype-cycle beta.** In an established [[bear-market|bear market]] (F&G 23), DeSci tokens sell off with the rest of the risk curve.

## Overview

BIO is a curation and liquidity protocol for Decentralized Science (DeSci). Our mission is to accelerate biotechnology by giving global communities of patients, scientists and biotech professionals the ability to collectively fund, build and own tokenized biotech projects and intellectual property (IP). 

The team behind BIO helped create Molecule, a tokenization platform for early-stage biomedicine, and VitaDAO, the largest decentralized community for longevity science. Building on these successes, BIO is designed to catalyze an onchain scientific economy through decentralized funding, incentives and liquidity.

The BIO token gives holders access to BIO's network of scientific communities and IP, enabling broad exposure to the DeSci economy.
