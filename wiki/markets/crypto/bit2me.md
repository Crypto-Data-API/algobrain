# Bit2Me

**Bit2Me** is one of Spain's largest and earliest regulated cryptocurrency [[exchange|exchanges]] and fintech platforms (founded 2014, headquartered in Madrid), offering buy/sell/store, a crypto-linked Mastercard debit card, IBAN accounts, and a suite of crypto-and-fiat financial services. **B2M** is its associated exchange-based [[centralized-exchange-tokens|utility token]], used to access fee discounts, rewards, and ecosystem benefits across Bit2Me's products. Bit2Me holds multiple registrations with Spain's central bank (Banco de España) and operates within the EU regulatory perimeter, which it markets as a key trust differentiator.

## Overview

Bit2Me is a full-stack crypto fintech: a [[centralized-exchange|centralized exchange]] combined with consumer banking-style features. Its product suite spans buying, selling, storing, and sending crypto and fiat; a Mastercard crypto debit card; IBAN accounts; peer-to-peer payments; staking; and OTC/institutional services. The company markets a "freedom formula" of cryptocurrencies + traditional money + a debit Mastercard cryptocard + shared IBAN + payments between friends + simple exchanges. A core part of its positioning is **regulatory compliance** within Spain and the EU, which it uses to differentiate from offshore venues.

---

## Token role

B2M is an **exchange utility token** — the on-chain loyalty and benefits layer of the Bit2Me platform. Typical functions for the token include:

- **Fee discounts** — reduced trading and service fees when paying with or holding B2M.
- **Rewards and cashback** — staking/holding tiers that unlock platform rewards and card benefits.
- **Ecosystem access** — gating premium features, launchpad allocations, and promotions across Bit2Me products.

B2M is deployed multi-chain ([[ethereum|Ethereum]], Polygon, and BNB Chain). It is a [[centralized-exchange-tokens|CEX token]]: its value is linked to the commercial success and trading volume of the Bit2Me exchange rather than to a decentralized protocol. At the earlier snapshot ~2.95B of a 5.00B max supply was circulating (MC/FDV ≈ 0.60), leaving meaningful future supply to be released.

### Value accrual & governance (B2M)

B2M follows the classic [[centralized-exchange-tokens|CEX-token]] value-capture playbook, which is **discount- and rewards-driven** rather than equity-like:

- **Fee discounts** — holding/paying in B2M reduces trading and service fees, creating utility demand that scales with the exchange's trading volume.
- **Staking / tier rewards** — holding tiers unlock card perks, cashback and platform rewards, locking float while staked.
- **Ecosystem gating** — premium features and launchpad allocations are gated by B2M, adding episodic demand.
- B2M is **not** a governance or revenue-share token: holders have no formal claim on Bit2Me's corporate cash flows. Value capture is whatever fraction of platform economics the company chooses to route through token utility — a key structural weakness versus equity in the operating company.

## Competitive Position

Within Spain and the EU, Bit2Me competes with global venues such as Binance, Coinbase, and Kraken as well as European players like Bitvavo. Its differentiation is local-market depth, Spanish/EU regulatory registration, and a banking-style feature set (card + IBAN). As an exchange-token, B2M's relevance is much smaller than the company itself: the token captures only a fraction of platform value through its discount/rewards utility, and it sits well below the leading CEX tokens (BNB, OKB, etc.) in scale and liquidity.

## Risks

- **Exchange-dependent value** — B2M's utility and demand are tied to Bit2Me's trading volume and commercial health; a downturn in the exchange's business directly weakens the token.
- **Thin liquidity** — historically low 24h volume (tens of thousands of USD) for a ~$17M-cap token implies wide spreads and slippage; CoinGecko found no major exchange listings beyond limited venues, concentrating liquidity risk.
- **Regulatory exposure (two-sided)** — EU/Spanish compliance is a selling point, but MiCA and evolving rules also raise compliance costs and could constrain token utility or distribution.
- **Dilution** — ~40% of max supply was uncirculated at the earlier snapshot, implying future unlock pressure.
- **Narrative/risk-off sensitivity** — exchange tokens fall with overall trading activity; under Extreme Fear (F&G 21) volumes and demand for utility tokens contract.
