## Overview

The Tharwa ecosystem is a pioneering multi-asset, RWA-tokenized hedge fund featuring thUSD, a high-yield stablecoin backed by gold, UAE real estate, oil, and U.S. Treasury bills, maintaining a 1:1 USD peg for stability and liquidity. It includes risk-on vaults with varied APYs, sthUSD for passive yield distribution, an OTC marketplace for thUSD trading, and the $TRWA token for revenue sharing and governance. AI-powered, Sharia-compliant, and built in the UAE, Tharwa scales globally, offering diverse investment opportunities.
