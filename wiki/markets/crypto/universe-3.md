## Overview

UNIVERSE is an Ethereum-based community participation token connected to an open-source ecosystem focused on digital culture, governance, reputation, and sovereign participation. The project combines blockchain infrastructure with public documentation, governance frameworks, educational resources, and community-driven development. The ecosystem includes MEME_OS, CODEX, DYOR, GitHub, VERSE, CHAT, Unstoppable Domain and other open-source tools designed to support transparent participation, digital identity, and decentralized coordination while encouraging responsible participation in Web3 communities.
