# SYMMIO

**SYMMIO** (SYMM) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#1499** by market capitalization. The Symmio Protocol - a trustless clearing house for derivatives.
Symmio is a trustless hybrid (combining on and offchain) clearing house acting as communication, settlement &amp; clearing layer for permissionless derivatives. At its core, Symmio is an intent-centric, meta-derivatives engine, with its first use case being a new type of hyper-efficient perps trading technology.

## Overview

The Symmio Protocol - a trustless clearing house for derivatives.
Symmio is a trustless hybrid (combining on and offchain) clearing house acting as communication, settlement &amp; clearing layer for permissionless derivatives. At its core, Symmio is an intent-centric, meta-derivatives engine, with its first use case being a new type of hyper-efficient perps trading technology. Solving the DeFi fragmentation problem by permissionlessly synthesizing exposure to virtually any established and yet-to-be-discovered assets."
