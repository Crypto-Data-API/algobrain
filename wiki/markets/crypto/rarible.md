## Protocol Overview

Rarible is a multi-chain [[nft|NFT]] marketplace launched in 2020 by Alexei Falin and Alex Salnikov (Moscow, Russia). The platform operates an open, permissionless listing model where any creator can mint and sell NFTs without an application or approval process, in contrast to curated platforms like [[superrare|SuperRare]].

In July 2020, Rarible introduced the $RARI governance token and pioneered **"marketplace mining"** — distributing $RARI tokens to active buyers and sellers as a reward for trading activity. This was one of the earliest applications of token incentives to bootstrap a marketplace, drawing parallels to DeFi yield farming. The mechanism successfully drove early adoption but also attracted wash trading, which inflated volume metrics. In 2021, Rarible raised **$14.2M Series A** led by Venrock and CoinFund.

## Multi-Chain Expansion

Rarible expanded beyond Ethereum to support multiple blockchains:
- **Ethereum**: Primary chain, ERC-721 and ERC-1155 standards
- **Flow**: Dapper Labs' blockchain, enabling access to the Flow NFT ecosystem
- **Tezos**: Popular with digital artists seeking lower gas fees
- **Polygon**: Near-zero gas fees for minting and trading

## Rarible Protocol

Beyond the consumer marketplace, Rarible developed an **open-source, blockchain-agnostic protocol** for building NFT applications providing indexing, order book functionality for NFT trading, lazy minting (creating NFTs without upfront gas costs until the first purchase), and royalty enforcement mechanisms.

## Business Model

- **Listing model**: Open/permissionless — any creator can mint and list without approval
- **Fees**: 2.5% service fee on sales (at launch; adjusted over time)
- **Governance**: $RARI token holders vote on protocol upgrades and fee structures
- **Curation**: None — contrast to [[superrare|SuperRare]]'s curated model

## Competitive Position

Rarible occupies the open/permissionless tier of the NFT marketplace landscape. Through 2024–2026 the broader NFT market remained far below its 2021–2022 peak, and Rarible has leaned on the **Rarible Protocol** (infrastructure / white-label marketplaces) and the $RARI governance token as differentiators rather than competing head-on for raw spot volume.

## Trading Relevance

Traders gain exposure only via the **$RARI** governance token (low-liquidity, high-volatility) or by trading the NFTs and collections listed on the platform. Its main relevance is as a barometer of NFT-market activity and as an early case study in **marketplace-mining tokenomics** — the wash-trading distortions $RARI incentives produced are a recurring lesson in how token rewards can inflate volume metrics.
