## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

WOD is tradable on **Binance SPOT only** — there is no liquid perpetual venue, so leverage and short access are limited and this is a **spot-primary** asset. Perp funding, basis, and liquidation strategies do **not** apply. With a small market cap (~#1842) and thin 24h volume, order books are shallow: execution should favor limit orders and patient scaling, position sizing must account for slippage and wide spreads, and single-venue concentration means Binance availability (or a delisting) directly governs tradability and exit liquidity.

### Applicable strategies

- [[breakout-and-retest]] — low float and reflexive gaming/metaverse narrative can drive sharp breakouts; waiting for a retest filters false moves in thin liquidity.
- [[volatility-breakout]] — WOD compresses then expands violently on news; volatility-triggered entries capture directional bursts without predicting them.
- [[atr-trailing-stop]] — wide, erratic swings near ATL make ATR-based trailing stops essential for locking gains and capping downside on a small-cap.
- [[news-trading]] — as a GameFi/Epic Games launch token, price is highly sensitive to game milestones, partnership, and listing headlines.
- [[dca-strategy]] — for spot-primary holders with a long-horizon thesis, scaling entries dollar-cost-averages through the current deep drawdown and low liquidity.
- [[range-trading]] — extended sideways basing near the all-time low offers defined support/resistance zones for spot range plays.

### Volatility & regime character

Small-cap GameFi/metaverse token on BNB Chain with high idiosyncratic volatility and strong reflexivity to gaming and NFT narratives. Beta to BTC/ETH is elevated in risk-on phases but returns are dominated by project-specific catalysts (game releases, listings, unlocks) rather than broad market direction. Currently ~97% below ATH and hovering near all-time lows, indicating a depressed, low-momentum regime.

### Risk flags

- **Liquidity/venue concentration** — Binance-spot-only with thin volume; poor exit liquidity and gap risk in stress.
- **Emissions/unlocks** — circulating supply (~554M) is roughly half of max supply (1B), so future unlocks/emissions are a persistent dilution and supply-overhang risk.
- **Narrative dependence** — valuation hinges on GameFi/metaverse adoption and game traction; sentiment reversals hit hard.
- **Drawdown regime** — trading near all-time lows with steep 30d/1y declines signals weak demand and heightened downside continuation risk.
