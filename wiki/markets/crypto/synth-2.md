# Synth

**Synth** (SN50) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#897** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** synthetic financial time-series data generation. Miners produce synthetic market data (price, order flow, option chains) that quant model developers can use for training, augmentation, or stress-testing without leaking proprietary data. Validators score statistical realism (distributional fit, autocorrelation, tail behavior) vs held-out real data.
- **Team:** Mode Network / Synthetic Minds.
- **Trading relevance:** **directly useful for quants** — synthetic financial data has a real market value (firms pay for it) and SN50 is the clearest "data-as-product" subnet on Bittensor.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-50.
