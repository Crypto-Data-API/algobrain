## Overview

The native token of Wingbits, the world's first rewards-based decentralized flight tracking network. WINGS powers a global DePIN ecosystem where contributors earn rewards for collecting ADS-B aviation data, creating the world's largest virtual map of the sky while transforming how flight data is gathered, verified, and shared.
