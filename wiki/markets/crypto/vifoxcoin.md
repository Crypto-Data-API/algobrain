## Overview

ViFoxCoin (VFX) is a Web3 utility and reward token powering the ViFox ecosystem across education, trading, fintech, and media. Built on BNB Smart Chain, VFX enables learn-to-earn rewards, micro-transactions, staking, and cross-platform engagement. Originating from ViFox College’s educational model, it now supports multi-industry partners who integrate VFX for missions, incentives, and user activation. Through transparent on-chain automation, ViFoxCoin connects participation with measurable value in a scalable, community-driven ecosystem.
