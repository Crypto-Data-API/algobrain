## Overview

PUPS (Bitcoin) is one of the first bitcoin memes, created by the Bitcoin Puppets NFT (Ordinals) community. The pups mission is to spread world peace through their positive vibes and art.

Initially launched as a BRC20 token on Bitcoin's first token standard in April 2023, it has since transitioned to the "Runes" standard on Bitcoin.
