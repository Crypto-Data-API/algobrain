## Overview

ZEGG is an AI-powered meme coin born at the intersection of artificial intelligence and internet culture. More than just a token, ZEGG is a dynamic character with a sarcastic, chaotic personality that engages the community through AI-generated content and real-time interaction. Governed by its holders through the ZEGG DAO, it features a suite of AI tools for meme creation, a reward-driven meme ecosystem, and token utility for premium access, tipping, and battles.
