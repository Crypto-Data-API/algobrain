# Tether Gold

**Tether Gold** (XAUT) is [[tether|Tether's]] gold-backed token: each XAU₮ represents one troy ounce of physical gold on a specific London Good Delivery bar held in Swiss vaults. It is the largest or second-largest tokenized-gold product (alongside Paxos' PAXG) and the main on-chain proxy for the 2024–2026 gold bull market — its market value topped **$3.3B in Q1 2026** after a 36% quarterly jump in bullion reserves, before settling around **$2.6B as of June 2026 (approximate)** as gold pulled back from highs.

## Overview

Each XAU₮ token represents ownership of one troy fine ounce of physical gold on a specific gold bar. XAU₮ is available as an ERC-20 token on Ethereum (historically also TRC-20 on TRON) and can be transferred to any on-chain address. Tether Gold charges zero custody fees and has direct control over physical gold storage in a Switzerland vault. Holders can look up the serial number of their allocated bar.

### 2025–2026 developments

- **Gold supercycle**: spot gold rallied from ~$2,600/oz (early 2025) through ~$4,000+ in late 2025 and spiked **above $5,100/oz in 2026** amid central-bank buying (~80 t/month per World Gold Council), geopolitical tension and inflation hedging. XAUT printed its ATH **$5,504.62 on 2026-01-29** alongside the metal.
- **Q1 2026** — Tether reported XAUT market value **above $3.3B**, backed by ~707,747 oz, after physical reserves jumped **36% in the quarter** — Tether aggressively minted to meet demand for on-chain gold.
- **June 2026** — gold consolidated below highs (XAUT ~$4,300–4,700); XAUT remains ~-15–20% off ATH but +~55% y/y.
- Tether has folded XAUT into its broader commodities/RWA push (alongside its T-bill holdings and aUSD₮ initiatives), and XAUT is increasingly accepted as collateral on offshore derivatives venues.

## Trading Relevance

- **On-chain gold proxy**: XAUT tracks spot [[gold]] near-1:1 — it is the cleanest way to express a gold view inside crypto rails, and trades 24/7 unlike COMEX/spot. Premium/discount to spot gold is a stress indicator (premiums spike when crypto-native demand for hard assets surges).
- **Where it trades**: spot on Binance (XAUT/USDT), Kraken, Bitget, KuCoin, Crypto.com, and Uniswap v3 vs USDT on Ethereum. 24h volume runs in the hundreds of millions of dollars ($180–460M range observed Q2 2026) — deep enough for size.
- **Narrative basket**: tokenized gold (XAUT, PAXG) sits in the **RWA / debasement-hedge basket**; it bids when the "[[bitcoin]] + gold debasement trade" narrative runs, and is the defensive leg crypto traders rotate into during risk-off without leaving stablecoin rails.
- **Catalysts**: central-bank gold purchases, real-yield moves, USD debasement headlines, Tether attestations (quarterly reserve reports), and any regulatory action on Tether group.
- **Risks**: issuer/custody trust in Tether (attestation, not full audit); redemption is restricted to large lots via Tether directly; TRON-leg deprecation.

## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

XAUT is tradable on [[binance]] as **spot (XAUT/USDT)** plus a **USD-margined perpetual** exposing [[funding-rate|funding]], [[open-interest]] and [[liquidations]] data — Binance is the primary leveraged venue. It is **NOT on [[hyperliquid]]**, so the on-chain perp order flow and liquidation prints that inform many crypto-native setups are unavailable here; leveraged execution, funding harvesting and cascade-fade signals all concentrate on Binance. Because it is a tokenized-gold RWA rather than a mainstream large-cap, perp depth and open interest are thinner than BTC/ETH, so leverage and clip sizing should stay conservative — funding can swing on a small book, and slippage on stops widens fast. Spot liquidity is spread across Binance, Kraken, Bitget, KuCoin and Uniswap v3, which supports cross-venue basis work but reinforces that Binance is where derivatives-driven strategies must be executed.

### Applicable strategies

- [[cash-and-carry]] — long XAUT spot vs. short the Binance USD-M perp to lock the basis; clean carry when the perp trades at a premium to on-chain gold.
- [[basis-trading]] — trade the perp-vs-spot spread on XAUT, which can dislocate when crypto-native demand for a hard-asset proxy spikes above physical gold pricing.
- [[funding-rate-harvest]] — collect funding on the Binance perp during gold-bull euphoria, when crowded longs push funding persistently positive on a relatively thin book.
- [[cross-exchange-arbitrage]] — arb XAUT price and its premium/discount to spot gold across Binance, Kraken, Bitget, KuCoin and Uniswap, where the RWA peg can diverge venue-to-venue.
- [[narrative-trading]] — position XAUT as the RWA / debasement-hedge leg that bids on central-bank buying, real-yield and USD-debasement headlines.
- [[liquidation-cascade-fade]] — fade forced-selling flushes on the Binance perp when a thin OI book overshoots gold's actual move.

### Volatility & regime character

Mid-cap RWA (rank ~#37) whose price is a near-1:1 proxy for spot [[gold]], not for crypto beta. Realized volatility tracks bullion, so XAUT is far calmer than typical altcoins and is largely **decorrelated from BTC/ETH** day-to-day — it behaves as a defensive, risk-off rotation asset inside crypto rails. Reflexivity is low (no memecoin dynamics), but the perp can show short bursts of premium/funding stress when the "debasement trade" narrative runs hot or when leveraged longs crowd in during a gold breakout.

### Risk flags

- **Venue concentration**: leveraged trading lives almost entirely on Binance; no Hyperliquid perp, so venue/regulatory risk on a single CEX is concentrated.
- **Issuer/custody trust**: backed by Tether-group attestations (not a full audit); redemption is restricted to large lots via Tether directly.
- **Thin derivatives depth**: perp OI is small relative to majors — funding and liquidations can whip on modest flow; size accordingly.
- **Narrative/macro dependence**: price is driven by gold macro (real yields, central-bank buying); a gold reversal drags XAUT regardless of crypto conditions.
- **Chain/regulatory**: TRON-leg deprecation and any regulatory action against the Tether group are tail risks.
