# Tether Gold

**Tether Gold** (XAUT) is [[tether|Tether's]] gold-backed token: each XAU₮ represents one troy ounce of physical gold on a specific London Good Delivery bar held in Swiss vaults. It is the largest or second-largest tokenized-gold product (alongside Paxos' PAXG) and the main on-chain proxy for the 2024–2026 gold bull market — its market value topped **$3.3B in Q1 2026** after a 36% quarterly jump in bullion reserves, before settling around **$2.6B as of June 2026 (approximate)** as gold pulled back from highs.

## Overview

Each XAU₮ token represents ownership of one troy fine ounce of physical gold on a specific gold bar. XAU₮ is available as an ERC-20 token on Ethereum (historically also TRC-20 on TRON) and can be transferred to any on-chain address. Tether Gold charges zero custody fees and has direct control over physical gold storage in a Switzerland vault. Holders can look up the serial number of their allocated bar.

### 2025–2026 developments

- **Gold supercycle**: spot gold rallied from ~$2,600/oz (early 2025) through ~$4,000+ in late 2025 and spiked **above $5,100/oz in 2026** amid central-bank buying (~80 t/month per World Gold Council), geopolitical tension and inflation hedging. XAUT printed its ATH **$5,504.62 on 2026-01-29** alongside the metal.
- **Q1 2026** — Tether reported XAUT market value **above $3.3B**, backed by ~707,747 oz, after physical reserves jumped **36% in the quarter** — Tether aggressively minted to meet demand for on-chain gold.
- **June 2026** — gold consolidated below highs (XAUT ~$4,300–4,700); XAUT remains ~-15–20% off ATH but +~55% y/y.
- Tether has folded XAUT into its broader commodities/RWA push (alongside its T-bill holdings and aUSD₮ initiatives), and XAUT is increasingly accepted as collateral on offshore derivatives venues.

## Trading Relevance

- **On-chain gold proxy**: XAUT tracks spot [[gold]] near-1:1 — it is the cleanest way to express a gold view inside crypto rails, and trades 24/7 unlike COMEX/spot. Premium/discount to spot gold is a stress indicator (premiums spike when crypto-native demand for hard assets surges).
- **Where it trades**: spot on Binance (XAUT/USDT), Kraken, Bitget, KuCoin, Crypto.com, and Uniswap v3 vs USDT on Ethereum. 24h volume runs in the hundreds of millions of dollars ($180–460M range observed Q2 2026) — deep enough for size.
- **Narrative basket**: tokenized gold (XAUT, PAXG) sits in the **RWA / debasement-hedge basket**; it bids when the "[[bitcoin]] + gold debasement trade" narrative runs, and is the defensive leg crypto traders rotate into during risk-off without leaving stablecoin rails.
- **Catalysts**: central-bank gold purchases, real-yield moves, USD debasement headlines, Tether attestations (quarterly reserve reports), and any regulatory action on Tether group.
- **Risks**: issuer/custody trust in Tether (attestation, not full audit); redemption is restricted to large lots via Tether directly; TRON-leg deprecation.
