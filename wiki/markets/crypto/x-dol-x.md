## Overview

XDOL Digital Dollar (XDOL) is an Ethereum-based ERC-20 digital asset created to provide accessible digital dollar exposure with a focus on transparency, utility, and sustainable ecosystem development. The project is designed to support real-world use cases such as payments, transfers, and participation in decentralized finance through public liquidity pools and verifiable on-chain activity. XDOL combines a fixed supply model with open blockchain transparency, aiming to build long-term market presence through structured growth rather than short-term speculation. By prioritizing accessibility, liquidity, and practical integration, XDOL seeks to establish a reliable digital asset framework for users interacting with global blockchain markets.
