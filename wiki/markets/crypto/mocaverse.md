# Moca Network

**Moca Network** (ticker **MOCA**) is building chain-agnostic **digital identity infrastructure** for the open internet — one universal account for a user's assets, identity, and reputation across multiple ecosystems. It is backed by [[animoca-brands]], whose portfolio of 540+ companies and 700M+ addressable users underpins Moca's distribution thesis. MOCA is the utility/governance token, issued by the Moca Foundation, with deployments on [[ethereum]] and [[base]] (Base). **Mocaverse** is the flagship consumer platform built on Moca's identity stack.

## Technology / Protocol (Moca Network identity / gaming)

Moca Network is **identity and reputation infrastructure**, not a chain of its own — it deploys as token contracts and an SDK across existing chains ([[ethereum|Ethereum]] and [[base|Base]]):

| Component | Function |
|---|---|
| **Moca ID** | A universal, chain-agnostic account carrying a user's identity, assets, and reputation |
| **AIR Kit** | An SDK letting Web2 and Web3 apps embed a Moca account / shared identity layer |
| **Mocaverse** | Flagship consumer platform adopting AIR Kit — identity- and reputation-gated experiences and rewards (Mocana, MocaDrop, MocaList) |
| **MOCA token** | Pays fees for transactions, data generation, storage, and verification (for users and AI agents); used for DAO governance |

The pitch is to give any app a "Telegram-mini-app-like" embedded blockchain layer keyed to a shared **Moca ID**, so users carry identity and reputation across apps and chains. Partners cited include SK Planet and OneFootball. Issuance is by the Moca Foundation, with the token distributed in part via a CoinList launchpad; backers include OKX Ventures and Outlier Ventures.

MOCA is a bet on **converting distribution into usage**: the [[animoca-brands]] portfolio (540+ companies, 700M+ users) is the asset, and the question is whether AIR Kit / Moca ID turns that reach into actual on-chain accounts and fee-generating activity — historically a high bar for "consumer Web3." With MC/FDV ~0.48, FDV (~$87M) implies roughly double the spot valuation, so the dilution discount is real and unlock cadence matters. Absent usage metrics, MOCA trades as a high-beta, narrative-driven micro-cap; the ~98% drawdown from ATH prices in deep skepticism about near-term adoption.

## Overview

Moca Network is building the chain-agnostic digital identity infrastructure for the open internet - one universal account for your assets, identity, and reputation across multiple ecosystems. Moca Network has direct (Animoca Brands) access to a portfolio of 540+ companies to tap into 700M+ addressable users.

AIR Kit, as the key product built by Moca Network, enables Web2 and Web3 projects to create a universal embedded account for users to own and use assets, identity, and reputation data, while getting access to a suite of DeFi and consumer services. AIR Kit empowers all large user base apps to create a Telegram mini-app like ecosystem within their own apps with embedded blockchain features, while ensuring these users can use the same account, identity, reputation across all consumer apps on any chains adopting the AIR Kit.

Mocaverse, one of the key consumer platforms adopting the AIR Kit, alongside SK Planet, One Football, and more partners, provides curated experiences and rewards to users based on their identity and reputation through Moca ID and products like Mocana, MocaDrop and MocaList.

$MOCA is the utility token that powers the Moca Network - used as fees for transactions, data generation, storage, verification for users and AI agents, and for DAO governance. It is issued by the Moca Foundation.

Learn more about Moca Network at https://moca.network/
