## Overview

What is the project about? 
MNFT, utility, and governance token granting our community the opportunity to benefit from the variety of upcoming token utilization and incentivize active participation in the MongolNFT marketplace, DAO, Streaming services, Gaming, and DeFi projects. 

What makes your project unique? 
The MNFT token is a utility and governance token audited by Certik. MNFT is distinguished from other cryptocurrencies by the opportunities given to our community, to benefit from the variety of token utilizations and incentivize active participation in the projects under MongolNFT. 

As an MNFT token holder, you will be able to do the following: 
~Buy and Sell NFTs on the MongolNFT platform, 
~An investment tool within the DAO concept, 
~Access pass to the MongolNFT Streaming Platform, 
~Access to MongolNFT Gaming, 
~Accepted as a payment method in more than 100,000 businesses, 
~Third-party integration.
