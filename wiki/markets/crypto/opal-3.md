## Overview

(OPAL) is a next-generation decentralized exchange (DEX) on Ethereum, designed to bring privacy, fairness, and revenue-sharing to perpetual trading. Using AI-driven sealed order execution, Opal ensures trades remain hidden until settlement, protecting users from front-running, predatory bots, and unfair advantages—even from the exchange itself. 100% of platform revenue is distributed to OPAL holders in ETH, establishing real token utility and passive income. With a focus on scalability, verifiable on-chain proofs, and non-custodial ownership, Opal represents a fairer, more transparent model for DeFi trading.
