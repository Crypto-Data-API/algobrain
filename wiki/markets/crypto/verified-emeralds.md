## Overview

VEREM is a Real World Asset (RWA) blockchain project that tokenizes GIA-certified emerald gemstones, bridging the physical gemstone market with decentralized finance. Each VEREM token is backed by real emeralds that undergo internationally recognized GIA certification and are securely held under audited custody, ensuring transparency, authenticity, and verifiable on-chain backing.

The project operates with a strong focus on regulatory alignment, following compliance standards under ADGM (Abu Dhabi Global Market) frameworks, reinforcing institutional-grade credibility and governance. VEREM combines responsible sourcing, third-party certification, and blockchain transparency to create a trusted digital representation of physical gemstones.

Through its decentralized ecosystem, VEREM enables token holders to participate in governance via a global DAO while gaining exposure to a historically valuable asset class. The project’s mission is to democratize access to certified gemstones by merging real-world asset value with secure, compliant, and transparent blockchain infrastructure.
