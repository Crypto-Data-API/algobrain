## Overview

The qiibee foundation is building the decentralized payment infrastructure for the $500bn rewards economy, fueled by the $QBX.

The vision is to position loyalty currencies as one of the top three global payment methods.

The qiibee foundation offers a decentralized payment infrastructure - known as the qiibee Rewards Chain - that enables businesses and consumers to trade loyalty currencies and enrich them with more liquidity.

The $QBX is used as the gas to trigger transactions connected to creating, earning, spending and exchanging loyalty tokens and loyalty NFTs on the qiibee Rewards Chain.
