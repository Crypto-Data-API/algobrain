# JUST

**JUST** (JST) is the governance token of the JUST [[defi|DeFi]] suite on [[tron|Tron]] — chiefly **JustLend DAO** (Tron's dominant lending market, plus staked-TRX products) and historically the USDJ/USDD stablecoin system — launched in 2020 within [[justin-sun|Justin Sun]]'s ecosystem. For traders, JST became interesting in 2025 when JustLend DAO began funding aggressive revenue-backed **buyback-and-burn** deflation, roughly doubling the token over 2025 and making it one of the cleaner "real yield / value accrual" plays in an otherwise legacy ecosystem.

## Protocol / Product

JUST launched in 2020 as [[tron|Tron]]'s flagship DeFi platform. It is a suite, not a single product:

- **JustLend DAO** — the largest money market on Tron: supply/borrow lending, plus **staked-TRX (sTRX)** products and Stake-and-Mine (SBM) yield. This is the protocol's core revenue engine and the source of buyback funding.
- **USDJ (legacy, sunset)** — the original CDP/collateralized stablecoin. Justin Sun confirmed near-complete liquidation in 2025: >95% of supply redeemed at a premium under a code-enforced phased shutdown ("USDJ Sunset Plan", extended 2025-09-30), leaving only ~$200K circulating.
- **USDD-adjacent** — JST/JustLend connect to the [[stablecoins|USDD]] stablecoin ecosystem; USDD multi-chain profits above $10M help fund JST burns.
- **JST token** — governance and utility across the suite.

The platform sits squarely inside the Tron / Justin Sun orbit, which is both a liquidity tailwind (large retail base, especially [[stablecoins|USDT]] on Tron) and a concentration/governance risk (see Risks).

## Tokenomics & Supply

JST flipped from inflationary genesis distribution to **revenue-funded deflation** in 2025 — the dominant narrative driver.

### Buyback-and-burn program (2025–2026)

- JustLend DAO governance approved a revenue-funded JST buyback-and-burn: ~$17.7M of protocol revenue burned **560M JST (~5.66% of supply)** in initial rounds, with a further **~$41M earmarked for phased burns through 2026**, funded by JustLend net income and USDD multi-chain profits above $10M. One reported single burn event destroyed **525M JST**.
- **Cumulative deflation**: over **1.35B JST (~13.7% of supply)** burned via the program by early 2026; circulating supply has since fallen further to ~8.54B (2026-06-20).

- **JustLend DAO** — lend/borrow on Tron; sTRX staking; SBM yield. Largest Tron money market.
- **USDD ecosystem** — JST tied to the USDD stablecoin's profitability (burn funding).

Investment framing: *JST = a deflating governance claim on JustLend/USDD protocol revenue — a "fee-switch / real-yield" trade in Tron DeFi.*

## Valuation Framework

- **Real-yield / fee-switch proxy** — JST is closest to a "value-accrual" token: protocol revenue (JustLend net income, USDD profits >$10M) funds buyback-and-burns, so JST can be valued on a *burn-yield* basis — annualised burn ÷ market cap as an implied buyback yield.
- **Tron-activity beta** — demand scales with JustLend TVL and USDD usage; rising Tron DeFi activity grows revenue and therefore burns.
- **Comparable set** — closer to [[maker|MKR/SKY]] and [[aave|AAVE]] fee-switch dynamics than to exchange tokens; the trade is "real revenue funds deflation," not "exchange volume funds discounts."
- **Governance-policy risk discount** — the burn is a DAO policy that can be amended, so the implied yield should be discounted for policy reversibility and Sun-entity influence.

## Risks

- **Centralization / Justin Sun risk** — JST sits inside a Sun-controlled ecosystem; entity influence over governance, treasury, and the burn program is a structural concentration risk, and Sun headlines drive volatility in both directions.
- **Governance-policy reversibility** — the buyback-and-burn is a DAO policy, not a smart-contract guarantee; it can be slowed or amended, which would deflate the core thesis.
- **Tron-ecosystem concentration** — JST's fortunes are tied to Tron activity, USDD health, and Tron's regulatory standing; a shock to any of these transmits directly.
- **Stablecoin legacy** — USDJ is sunset, reducing risk, but USDD-adjacency means USDD instability could impair burn funding.
- **Bear-market regime** — Fear & Greed 23; despite recent relative strength, JST remains -57% from its 2021 ATH.
