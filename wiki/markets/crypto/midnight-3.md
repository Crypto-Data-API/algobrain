# Midnight

**Midnight** (ticker **NIGHT**) is a privacy-first Layer 1 blockchain — a [[cardano|Cardano]] "partner chain" — incubated by Input Output Global (IOG, the Cardano developer) and built by Shielded Technologies. It uses [[zero-knowledge-proofs|zero-knowledge proofs]] and "selective disclosure" to combine public verifiability with confidential data handling, a "rational privacy" pitch aimed at regulated enterprises and dApp developers rather than anonymity maximalists. NIGHT is the freely tradable governance/value token; a second non-transferable resource called **DUST** (generated by holding NIGHT) pays for shielded transactions, so fee payment itself does not leak metadata. For traders, NIGHT is the flagship of the 2025–26 ZK-privacy narrative and the subject of one of the largest airdrop distributions in crypto history — the "Glacier Drop", claimable by holders of eight chains including [[bitcoin|BTC]], [[cardano|ADA]], [[ethereum|ETH]] and [[solana|SOL]] — which makes airdrop-unlock supply dynamics central to its price behaviour.

## Overview

Midnight is a privacy-first blockchain that blends public verifiability with confidential data handling, using selective disclosure and ZK proofs so apps can keep data confidential while still proving compliance. Key milestones:

- **Token genesis / Glacier Drop (2025)** — NIGHT was distributed via the "Glacier Drop", a multi-phase claim open to holders of BTC, ADA, ETH, SOL, XRP, BNB, AVAX and BAT. Phase 1 saw **3.5B NIGHT claimed by ~170k addresses**; the phase-2 "Scavenger Mine" distributed a further **1B NIGHT to ~8M addresses** — distribution reached **8M+ wallets** in total.
- **Redemption timeline** — the Redemption Portal opened before 2025-12-10; a "thawing period" runs 2025-12-10 → 2026-12-04, followed by a 90-day grace period. Unclaimed/unredeemed tranches are a standing supply-overhang consideration.
- **Mainnet (2026)** — mainnet went live around March 2026, with a stable-mainnet launch and governance upgrade announced 2026-05-20 and bridge/alpha integration updates in June 2026.
- **Listings** — NIGHT listed at launch on [[binance|Binance]], [[kraken|Kraken]], OKX and Bitpanda; also trades on KuCoin.

## Architecture & How It Works

Midnight's core design goal is to make confidentiality a first-class feature of a public blockchain instead of an afterthought, so that businesses can run on-chain logic without exposing customer data, balances, or trade flow while still being able to *prove* compliance when required.

### Zero-knowledge proofs & selective disclosure

- **ZK at the protocol level.** Transactions are validated against [[zero-knowledge-proofs|zero-knowledge proofs]] rather than fully public state. A user can prove a statement is true (e.g. "this transfer is valid and I am over a KYC threshold") without revealing the underlying data. Midnight's smart-contract framework, branded **Compact**, lets developers write circuits that mix public and private state.
- **Selective ("rational") disclosure.** Rather than the all-or-nothing anonymity of [[monero|Monero]], Midnight lets a party reveal *specific* facts to *specific* counterparties (an auditor, a regulator, a counterparty) while keeping everything else shielded. This is the "rational privacy" thesis: privacy that a regulated enterprise can actually adopt. It positions NIGHT as a compliance-friendly alternative within the [[privacy-coins|privacy-coin]] category.
- **Public verifiability + confidential data.** Consensus and proof verification are public, so the chain is auditable in aggregate, but the *contents* of shielded transactions remain confidential.

### Cardano partner-chain model

Midnight is built as a **[[cardano|Cardano]] partner chain** — an independent Layer 1 with its own consensus and token, secured in coordination with the Cardano ecosystem and the IOG technology stack rather than as a Cardano sidechain or rollup. This ties Midnight's developer mindshare and validator base to Cardano while letting it run its own privacy-specialised execution environment. The eight-chain Glacier Drop deliberately spread NIGHT across the largest L1 communities to bootstrap a broad holder base from day one.

### Dual-token design: NIGHT + DUST

Midnight separates *value/governance* from *fee payment* using two assets — a deliberate privacy choice, because paying gas in a transparent fee token would otherwise re-expose who is transacting:

| Token | Transferable? | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **NIGHT** | Yes | Tradable governance and value token; staking/holding it generates DUST |
| **DUST** | No (non-transferable, decaying) | Renewable resource that pays for shielded transactions; regenerates over time from held NIGHT and decays if unused |

Because DUST is generated by *holding* NIGHT and cannot be bought or transferred, the cost of using the network is decoupled from a visible, buyable gas market — fee payment does not leak a metadata trail the way a normal gas token would. The model also creates a structural reason to hold (rather than only trade) NIGHT.

### Distribution: the Glacier Drop & thaw schedule

The majority of NIGHT entered circulation through the **Glacier Drop**, a multi-phase airdrop rather than a sale:

- **Phase 1 (Glacier Drop)** — open to holders of BTC, ADA, ETH, SOL, XRP, BNB, AVAX and BAT; ~**3.5B NIGHT claimed by ~170k addresses**.
- **Phase 2 (Scavenger Mine)** — distributed a further **~1B NIGHT to ~8M addresses**, taking total distribution to **8M+ wallets**.
- **Redemption / thawing** — the Redemption Portal opened before 2025-12-10; a **"thawing period" runs 2025-12-10 → 2026-12-04**, followed by a **90-day grace period**. Claimed NIGHT is released gradually over the thaw rather than all at once.

**Supply overhang.** Because so much supply is airdropped and unlocks on a thaw schedule, NIGHT carries a structural drip-feed of new sellable tokens through 2026-12-04 (plus the grace window). With MC/FDV ~0.69 at the April 2026 snapshot, roughly a third of fully-diluted supply was still to enter free float. Redemption-portal claim stats are therefore a leading indicator of potential sell pressure (see [[token-unlocks]] and the [[#Risks]] section).

## Value Accrual & Governance

- **Governance.** NIGHT is the governance token of the network; holders are intended to participate in protocol decisions (parameter changes, upgrades). The 2026-05-20 stable-mainnet release included a governance upgrade.
- **Utility-by-holding.** The clearest value-accrual channel is the DUST mechanic: holding NIGHT continuously regenerates the resource needed to transact privately, so network usage creates a reason to *hold* rather than only trade. This is closer to a "staked-for-bandwidth" model than a fee-burn token.
- **No fee-burn / buyback disclosed.** Value accrual is via governance rights and DUST generation rather than a [[fee-burn|burn]] or buyback at the snapshot date; demand depends on shielded-dApp adoption driving the need to hold NIGHT.

## Risks

- **Supply overhang / airdrop-dump dynamics.** With 8M+ wallets receiving NIGHT for free and a thaw schedule running to 2026-12-04 (plus 90-day grace), latent sell pressure is structural. Recipients with zero cost basis sell into strength; rallies can be capped until the float is more fully distributed. This is the single most important trader-facing risk.
- **Regulatory / privacy-coin risk.** Despite the compliance-friendly framing, NIGHT is categorised with [[privacy-coins|privacy coins]]. Exchange policy, the EU AMLR regime (privacy-asset restrictions due July 2027) and shifting regulator attitudes are a two-sided risk — a tailwind versus opaque coins, but still listing/jurisdiction risk.
- **Execution / roadmap risk.** Value ultimately depends on real shielded-dApp adoption driving demand to hold NIGHT (for DUST). Mainnet is young (~Mar 2026); the bull case requires IOG to deliver an enterprise developer ecosystem against the usual L1 execution risk.
- **Liquidity risk.** Mid-cap depth that thins quickly in risk-off conditions; no confirmed major perp market to backstop spot liquidity.
- **Macro / regime risk.** As of **2026-06-24** the market is in **Extreme Fear** (Fear & Greed 22; market-health 28/100, BEARISH) and a long-horizon **Established Bear Market**. High-beta, narrative-driven, supply-overhang assets like NIGHT are especially exposed to risk-off de-rating in this regime.

## Trading Playbook (bear / Extreme-Fear regime)

- **Default stance: cautious / underweight.** In an Established Bear Market with Extreme Fear (F&G 22, 2026-06-24), a high-beta L1 with a live airdrop-thaw overhang is a low-conviction long. Respect the trend; don't fight a downtrend on narrative alone.
- **Wait for float clarity.** The thaw runs to 2026-12-04 — much of the overhang resolves only as that schedule completes. Position sizing should assume continued latent supply until claim/redemption stats show the airdrop is largely distributed.
- **Catalyst-driven, not buy-and-hold.** If trading it, anchor to discrete catalysts (Binance perp/margin add, major dApp/enterprise announcement, governance milestone) and define invalidation against the April-2026 ATL/ATH band; size for reflexive liquidity that vanishes in drawdowns.
- **Relative-value angle.** NIGHT can outperform [[monero|Monero]]/Zcash *relatively* on opaque-privacy-coin crackdown headlines — a pairs/relative idea rather than an outright directional bet in this regime.
- **Risk controls.** Hard stops (thin liquidity gaps fast), no leverage on a sub-top-50 privacy name in a bear tape, and treat the ~$29M-volume profile as a position-size constraint. See [[risk-management]] and [[position-sizing]].

## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

NIGHT is tradable on [[binance|Binance]] — both spot (NIGHT/USDT) and a USD-margined perpetual (with [[funding-rate|funding]], [[open-interest|open interest]] and [[liquidations]]). It is **not** listed on Hyperliquid, so Binance is the primary leveraged venue and the reference point for perp funding, OI and liquidation data. This single-venue concentration for leverage means execution and sizing hinge on Binance depth: with only mid-cap spot liquidity behind the perp (volume is thin versus top-50 names and reflexive in drawdowns), leverage should be modest and orders scaled to avoid slippage. Cross-exchange spot depth (Kraken, OKX, KuCoin, Bitpanda) supports the cash-and-carry/basis leg, but the perp-vs-spot basis and funding will be dominated by Binance flow. Wide funding swings and cascade-driven liquidations are likely given the thin, narrative-driven float, so account for gap risk when sizing.

### Applicable strategies

- [[funding-rate-harvest]] — collect Binance perp funding when NIGHT's crowded, narrative-driven positioning pushes funding persistently positive or negative.
- [[cash-and-carry]] — hedge long spot against the Binance USD-M perp to monetise basis/funding on a supply-overhang token where directional risk is unattractive.
- [[liquidation-cascade-fade]] — fade forced-liquidation flushes on a thin, reflexive float where cascades overshoot before mean-reverting.
- [[oi-confirmed-trend]] — use Binance open-interest expansion to confirm whether a NIGHT move is real positioning or a low-conviction wick.
- [[narrative-trading]] — trade the ZK-privacy / Cardano-ecosystem narrative and regulator-crackdown relative tailwinds that drive NIGHT's largest moves.
- [[token-unlock-supply-event]] — position around Glacier Drop thaw/redemption milestones that inject sellable supply through 2026-12-04.

### Volatility & regime character

Small/mid-cap (rank ~#104) high-beta L1 with strong narrative reflexivity — moves sharply on ZK-privacy and [[cardano|Cardano]]-ecosystem headlines and correlates broadly to BTC/ETH risk appetite while amplifying it on the downside. As an infra/privacy token with a massive airdrop float (8M+ wallets, zero cost basis) and an active thaw schedule, realised volatility is elevated and depth thins fast in risk-off tape, giving it memecoin-like reflexivity despite an infrastructure thesis.

### Risk flags

- **Venue/liquidity concentration** — Binance is the sole significant leveraged venue and spot depth is thin; slippage and gap risk are material.
- **Unlocks/emissions** — Glacier Drop thaw runs to 2026-12-04 (plus 90-day grace); latent airdrop supply is a persistent overhang and the dominant trader-facing risk.
- **Narrative dependence** — price is driven by privacy/ZK and Cardano-ecosystem narrative rather than cash flows; sentiment reversals are abrupt.
- **Regulatory** — categorised with [[privacy-coins|privacy coins]]; EU AMLR privacy-asset restrictions (due July 2027) and shifting exchange policy pose listing/jurisdiction risk.
