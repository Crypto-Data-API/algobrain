## Overview

Flash Trade is a decentralized perpetual exchange built on Solana that offers asset backed high leverage trading with 0 slippage on crypto assets, forex, metals and stocks.
Users can either trade the exotic perpetuals on various market all completely onchain in a permissionless way. Along with trading users can also provide liquidity for other traders to earn stable yield on various pools with varying yields.
