# Moo Deng

**Moo Deng** (ticker **MOODENG**) is a [[solana|Solana]]-based [[meme-coin]] inspired by the viral pygmy hippo of the same name that became an internet sensation in 2024. It launched via [[pump-fun|Pump.fun]] and is categorised as a zoo-/animal-themed Solana meme. Like most memes it has no protocol utility — its value is entirely community- and narrative-driven.

## Market Structure & Derivatives

**Spot (CEX):** Listed on Kraken (MOODENG/USD), Upbit (MOODENG/KRW — a notable South Korean retail venue), Bitget, KuCoin and Crypto.com.

**Spot (on-chain):** Native trading on [[solana|Solana]] DEXs, principally Orca and Raydium against SOL/USDC.

**Derivatives:** MOODENG trades as a perpetual — **MOODENG-PERP** on [[hyperliquid]] — and on several CEX derivatives desks. Meme [[perpetual-futures|perps]] like this routinely see swings in the [[funding-rate]] and [[open-interest]] as leveraged longs/shorts crowd in; extreme funding is a classic precursor to liquidation cascades, especially during Extreme-Fear conditions.

**Liquidity:** ~$8.4M of daily volume on a ~$39M cap (~21%/day turnover) is active for a small meme, but the Upbit KRW listing means a meaningful share of flow is Korean-retail-driven — a venue concentration that can amplify both rallies and washouts. Real depth is thinner than market cap implies.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Moo Deng is a pure **attention/narrative asset**. The "Moo Deng" pygmy hippo at Thailand's Khao Kheow Open Zoo went viral on TikTok and X in 2024, and the token rode that cultural moment. CoinGecko tags it across **Solana Ecosystem, Meme, Solana Meme, Pump.fun Ecosystem, Zoo-Themed, Binance Alpha Spotlight and TikTok Meme**. Its thesis is liquidity + virality: it captured a real cultural meme, secured tier-1/tier-2 exchange listings, and now persists on brand recognition and an active trading community rather than any product roadmap.

---

## Valuation Framing

[[meme-coin|Meme coins]] have **no fundamentals, no cash flows, and no intrinsic value** — they are valued reflexively on attention. For MOODENG:

- **Attention is the only "fundamental":** value tracks the residual cultural relevance of the Moo Deng hippo meme plus the size of the active trading community. The viral moment has already peaked, so the question is how much durable mindshare remains.
- **Reflexive price action:** with ~$8M daily volume and no DCF anchor, price is driven by momentum and sentiment — rallies attract attention and buyers; drawdowns feed on themselves. The muted -1.7% week reflects low conviction in either direction during Extreme Fear.
- **Fair-launch structure:** the full 1B supply is in circulation (MC/FDV ≈ 1.00), so there is no unlock overhang to discount — but equally no team/VC backing to defend the price.
- **Meme-cycle context:** down ~94% from ATH, MOODENG fits the classic [[meme-coin-cycle|meme-coin cycle]]: euphoric viral launch, parabolic peak, then a long retrace as attention rotates to newer memes. Survival in the top ~530 by cap is itself a (weak) signal of staying power relative to the thousands of memes that go to zero.

## Risks

- **Extreme volatility & drawdown** — Down ~94% from ATH; meme assets routinely round-trip large moves.
- **Narrative decay** — Value depends on a cultural moment that has already peaked; attention is the only "fundamental."
- **Insider / holder concentration** — Pump.fun-style launches can leave early snipers and large wallets controlling significant float (on-chain distribution not yet ingested here).

- **Leverage risk** — Active [[perpetual-futures|perps]] with potentially extreme [[funding-rate|funding]] magnify liquidation risk.

## Overview

MOODENG（ETH） is a meme coin on the Ethereum blockchain inspired by a real-life pygmy hippopotamus named Moodeng from Thailand, which became an internet sensation. MOODENG（ETH）is a completely community take over project. Vitalik adopted MOODENG and became its 'foster father', affectionately known as the 'Father of MOODENG'. Vitalik holds 7.13% of the tokens and has interacted with and built the token project. The community's goal is to become the new king of MEME, linking Web2 and Web3.
