## Overview

NAWS is a no-code platform for users to sell Web2, Web3 content, and DePIN vouchers. The P2P Crypto Payment system uses DEX AI aggregators for secure, low-cost payments, allowing users to choose tokens for transactions. Foundations and projects can onboard their tokens to grow their ecosystems. NAWS Quest rewards user activity with points convertible to tokens. NAWS tokens are used for content purchases, reducing transaction fees and subscriptions. As market share grows, token supply decreases.
