## Architecture / What it does

BUILDon is primarily a **narrative/community token** rather than an infrastructure project. Its identity rests on (a) the BNB Chain meme/community ecosystem and (b) association with the **World Liberty Financial (WLFI)** portfolio theme — a politically branded DeFi venture that has driven speculative interest in tokens it is perceived to hold or favour.

There is no verified protocol or product utility recorded in this wiki beyond the token itself. Treat the project as a sentiment-driven asset; any "AI"/build tooling implied by branding is self-reported and unverified.

### Token mechanics

BUILDon is a standard BEP-20 token on [[bnb|BNB Smart Chain]] (contract `0x6bdcce4a559076e37755a78ce0c06214e59e4444`) with a fixed 1.0B supply, fully in circulation. There is no staking module, emissions schedule, or protocol revenue recorded here — the token is a pure spot-demand instrument. Its two reflexive drivers are:

1. **The WLFI "portfolio" narrative** — speculation that World Liberty Financial holds or favours B; this is an *attention* mechanism, not a contractual right, so it can reprice abruptly on a single headline.
2. **Binance Alpha Spotlight** — inclusion in Binance's early-stage discovery surface, which periodically injects retail attention and liquidity.

Because both drivers are attention-based, B trades like a high-beta political-meme coin: large, fast moves on narrative shifts, no fundamental floor.
