## Overview

NX8 is a tokenized index product that tracks leading blockchains where stablecoins move, perps trade, sats are stacked, and memes go viral. NX8 is designed as both a core portfolio building block and a utility asset for DeFi. NX8 is brought to market by in partnership with and built on the regulated methodology developed by GMCI.

NX8 as an index product is composed of BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, TRON, HYPE, AVAX, and SUI.
