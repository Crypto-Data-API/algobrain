# Qubic

**Qubic** (ticker **QUBIC**) is a decentralized [[layer-1|Layer-1]] network that pairs a feeless, instant-finality design with a **"Useful Proof of Work" (uPoW)** model that channels miners' computational power toward **AI training** instead of wasting it. Qubic runs its own L1 (no parent chain) and ties its mining directly to its **Aigarth** initiative, a stated effort to work toward artificial general intelligence. QUBIC sits in the [[artificial-intelligence|crypto-AI]] / useful-[[proof-of-work|PoW]] / [[depin|decentralized-compute]] cohort.

## Overview

Qubic is a decentralized Layer-1 built for feeless transactions and instant (sub-second) finality, designed to run directly on bare-metal hardware to maximize performance. Its distinguishing feature is **Useful Proof of Work (uPoW)**: rather than burning energy on arbitrary hashing, miners' compute is directed toward AI training.

- **Aigarth initiative** — the core of Qubic's mission, channeling miner compute via uPoW toward AI training with the stated ambition of working toward AGI, framed as a community-powered, decentralized AI effort.
- **Bare-metal architecture** — running without a traditional OS to maximize throughput.
- **Feeless + instant finality** — zero-fee transactions and sub-second confirmation aimed at real-time applications.
- **Smart contracts** — the project advertises very high smart-contract / transfer throughput.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

QUBIC is a **high-beta AI-narrative micro-cap trading at extreme distress** (~96% below ATH, ~10% above a freshly-set ATL). Key framing points:

- **Narrative-priced, not cash-flow-priced.** There is no burn, buyback, or revenue link; the token is a pure bet on the uPoW-for-AI thesis converting into real, valuable compute output — which remains unproven.
- **Emission headwind.** With MC/FDV ~0.69 and miners continuously selling fresh QUBIC to fund hardware/energy, the token faces built-in sell pressure that demand must absorb just to hold flat.
- **Theme correlation.** QUBIC is tightly coupled to the broader [[ai-narrative-arc|AI-narrative cycle]]; in an established bear market it underperforms, but it offers outsized upside if AI sentiment re-ignites.
- **Execution discount.** Bold claims ("world's fastest smart contracts", "AGI") are unverified; the market is pricing heavy skepticism.

The honest read: a **lottery-ticket-style AI/PoW speculation** — deep drawdown, emission overhang, thin liquidity, and a thesis that lives or dies on deliverables.

## Risks

- **Extraordinary claims.** "AGI by [a target year]", "world's fastest smart contracts", and uPoW-for-AI are bold, largely unverified claims; treat marketing language with heavy skepticism and judge by deliverables.
- **Near all-time lows.** Price is hovering just above a freshly-set ATL with a ~96% drawdown from peak — momentum and trend are weak.
- **Thin liquidity / venue concentration.** ~$1.4M daily volume and limited CEX pairs make for wide spreads and difficult exits.
- **Quantum/AGI narrative beta.** Heavily exposed to the [[ai-narrative-arc|AI-narrative cycle]]; in an established [[bear-market|bear market]] (F&G 23) speculative AI microcaps underperform.
- **Emission overhang.** ~31% of FDV is still un-emitted and miners must sell to cover costs — persistent structural sell pressure.
- **Useful-PoW execution risk.** Directing mining toward genuinely useful AI work is technically hard and unproven at scale; the value of the resulting "AI" is itself uncertain.
- **Denomination optics.** The trillions-supply, sub-microcent price can attract uninformed "it's cheap" buyers and add volatility.
