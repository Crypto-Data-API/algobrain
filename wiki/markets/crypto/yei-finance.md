## Overview

Yei introduces Clovis, a cross-chain clearing, execution, and settlement layer that unifies fragmented liquidity across DeFi. 

By combining a global Clearing Layer with lightweight vaults on connected chains, Clovis consolidates capital that is currently split across lending markets, DEXs, and bridges, enabling a single pool of liquidity to power lending, swapping, and bridging while generating stacked yield from every deposit.
