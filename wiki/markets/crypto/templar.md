# τemplar

**τemplar** (SN3) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#767** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** fully-decentralized LLM pretraining at scale. Miners contribute compute and gradients to a shared training run. Notable for the Covenant-72B pretraining run in 2025, one of the first credibly-decentralized frontier-scale model trainings.
- **Team:** Templar Foundation; part of the "Covenant AI" cluster alongside [[w-ai-parked|Basilica]] (SN39) and [[patrol-tao-com|Grail]] (SN81).
- **Trading relevance:** high-conviction "decentralized training is real" bet. Alpha-3 performed strongly post-Covenant-72B on validator conviction that distributed pretraining had crossed a capability threshold.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-3. See [[dtao]].
