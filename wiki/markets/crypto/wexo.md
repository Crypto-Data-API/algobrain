## Overview

Bridge Between Crypto &amp; the Real World Economy

WEXO is a utility token designed as a real-world asset (RWA) that seamlessly connects the cryptocurrency ecosystem with everyday consumer spending and commerce. By tying token value to real economic activity — primarily through cashback, loyalty rewards, and transaction-based incentives generated from daily purchases — WEXO creates a sustainable bridge between digital assets and traditional fiat-based consumption.

The WEXO token connects cryptocurrency with everyday consumer spending through cashback, deflationary burns, and tiered staking — creating sustainable value from real economic activity.

The First Real-World Asset Token
Crypto has promised to change the world for over a decade — but for most people, it still lives in a separate universe from their daily lives. Groceries, fuel, travel, rent — none of it touches their portfolio.

The WEXO token was built to fix that. It is an independent utility token — not a product, not an app — whose value is directly anchored to real economic activity. 

Currency of the Super App
Every interaction inside the WEXO ecosystem runs on WEXO token. Hold it to unlock higher cashback tiers. Stake it to earn yield. Use WEXO Fuel to amplify your rewards by 10%.

The token is not issued by the app — it lives on Ethereum. The only way to acquire it is to buy it on a public exchange.

A Deflationary Model Unique in the World
Most tokens inflate over time. WEXO does the opposite — it shrinks. Every real-world transaction across the Super App ecosystem adds buying pressure on WEXO while simultaneously removing tokens from circulation forever.

Combined with the Linear Burn and staking lockups, WEXO's circulating supply is on an accelerating downward curve — tied directly to adoption, not hype.
