## Overview

Open Loot is a Web3 gaming distribution platform and marketplace designed to address challenges with crypto games, such as growth, UX, scalability, payments, and compliance. It manages all the infrastructure, crypto, and non-crypto item sales and rentals for Big Time— the leading Web3 game.

The $OL token is designed to capture value from the platform by offering exclusive benefits to Open Loot users. Whether you're seeking better deals on the Open Loot marketplace or aiming to collect additional rewards while playing your favorite games, $OL delivers substantial utility.
