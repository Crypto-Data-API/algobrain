## Overview

The official token of the Mansory car manufacturer.
Mansory is a renowned luxury car modification company based in Brand, Germany, founded in 1989 by Kourosh Mansory. They specialize in creating high-end, customized vehicles that are often limited edition and one-of-a-kind. Mansory's services range from interior and exterior design to performance upgrades, resulting in truly unique driving experiences.
