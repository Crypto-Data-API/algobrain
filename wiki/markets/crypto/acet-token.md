# Acet

**Acet** (ACT) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#2380** by market capitalization. ACT (Acet) is DeFansFi Token. It is initiated on the concept of “Zero Initial Supply”; in the belief that to be a digital asset, the initial amount of supply should start at “0”, and the amount of the token should only be generated based on the holders’ demand.
