## Overview

AKEDO is a Multi-Agent AI framework built for Autonomous Content Creation and Intelligent Collaboration. It empowers creators to design engaging games in just 2 minutes using simple natural language prompts.

Powered by large language models (LLMs), AKEDO’s modular framework is designed for scalability and flexibility, enabling creators to turn their ideas into reality with ease.
