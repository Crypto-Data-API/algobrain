## Overview

Origin Dollar (OUSD) is a yield-bearing stablecoin that earns while it's still in your wallet. OUSD is 100% backed by USDC, with collateral deployed into DeFi protocols including Morpho and Curve to generate yield. The protocol automatically moves capital across strategies to optimize returns for holders. Yield is distributed to OUSD holders automatically via daily rebase. Built by the team at Origin Protocol (OGN).
