## Overview

NATIX Network is a decentralized multi-camera data network that crowdsources real-world driving footage to power Physical AI, including autonomous driving and robotics. Built on Solana and operating across three continents, the network combines token incentives, computer vision, and proprietary edge infrastructure to collect, anonymize, and validate multi-camera street-level data at a scale and geographic diversity that traditional centralized fleets cannot match. Its flagship hardware product, VX360, taps into Tesla vehicles' built-in multi-camera systems to capture surrounding footage, which contributors can earn $NATIX tokens for. The data supports use cases ranging from world model training and end-to-end autonomous driving development to HD mapping and infrastructure monitoring.
