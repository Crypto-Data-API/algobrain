# Autonomi

**Autonomi** (ANT) is an autonomous [[decentralized-storage]] network where data is stored securely across many everyday devices, with privacy as the default. It is the rebranded continuation of the long-running **Safe Network** project built by **MaidSafe** — one of the oldest projects in the space, with roots predating Bitcoin. The network was renamed **Autonomi** in 2024–2025, and the token migrated from the legacy **SNT / MaidSafeCoin (Omni-layer)** lineage to **ANT**, an [[arbitrum|Arbitrum]]-based ERC-20.

## History & Rebrand

Autonomi is the modern identity of the **Safe Network**, the decentralized data project developed by Scotland-based **MaidSafe** since the mid-2000s (its SAFE — "Secure Access For Everyone" — vision dates to ~2006, with the MaidSafeCoin crowdsale in 2014, making it one of the earliest blockchain-era projects). In **2024–2025** the project rebranded to **Autonomi**, launched its network with payments routed through [[arbitrum|Arbitrum One]], and migrated holders from the legacy **MaidSafeCoin / SNT** tokens to the new **ANT** ERC-20. This long history is noted honestly here: the project has been "coming soon" for many years, which is both a credibility marker (persistence) and a risk flag (repeated delays).

---

## Architecture

Autonomi is a peer-to-peer [[decentralized-storage]] and data network ([[depin]]):

- **Distributed storage** — Data is encrypted, chunked, and spread across a global network of nodes running on everyday devices; there is no central server or single point of control. Nodes validate and serve one another autonomously.
- **Self-encryption & privacy-by-default** — Files are split and **self-encrypted** end-to-end before storage, so no node sees usable plaintext; the project describes the encryption as quantum-secure.
- **Permanent, pay-once storage** — Users pay a **one-time upload fee** (in ANT) rather than recurring subscriptions; uploaded data is intended to persist permanently with no ongoing fees.
- **Node rewards** — Node operators earn ANT for contributing storage and bandwidth, aligning the [[depin]] supply side with token incentives.
- **Arbitrum settlement** — Payments and token accounting run on [[arbitrum|Arbitrum One]] (Layer 2), keeping transaction costs low while the data layer remains off-chain in the node network.

---

## What the ANT Token Does

- **Storage payments** — ANT pays the one-time upload fee to store data on the network.
- **Node rewards** — Operators earn ANT for providing storage/bandwidth and serving data.
- **Network medium of exchange** — ANT is the unit of account for the network's data economy.

Note the supply profile: circulating supply is a fraction of the ~1.2B total/max supply, so future emissions to node operators (and remaining migration) are a valuation consideration.

---

## Distinguishing Features

- **Pay-once, permanent storage** — Contrasts with subscription/recurring models common in [[decentralized-storage]] (e.g., Filecoin, Arweave's endowment model differs again).
- **Privacy-by-default, self-encrypting** — No accounts or plaintext exposure at the node level.
- **Deep heritage** — Among the oldest decentralized-data efforts, predating most competitors.

---

## Competitive Position

Autonomi competes directly with the established [[decentralized-storage]] cohort. Its closest economic analogue is Arweave (both target permanence via a one-time payment), while Filecoin and Storj use recurring storage-deal economics.

| Network | Storage model | Payment | Privacy default | Settlement | Contrast vs Autonomi |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Autonomi (ANT)** | Permanent, autonomous node mesh on everyday devices | One-time upload fee | Self-encrypting, no plaintext at node | [[arbitrum|Arbitrum One]] | Permanence + privacy-by-default + deepest heritage; long delivery history |
| **Filecoin (FIL)** | Storage deals between clients and providers | Recurring deal payments | Optional, deal-dependent | Filecoin | Far larger ecosystem; recurring (not pay-once) economics |
| **Arweave (AR)** | Permanent ("permaweb") | One-time, endowment-funded | No | Arweave | Closest permanence analogue; endowment model vs node-reward model |
| **Storj / Sia** | Distributed cloud storage | Recurring usage | Encrypted shards | Storj/Sia chains | Conventional cloud-replacement economics, not permanence |

Autonomi's narrowest differentiator is the **combination** of permanence, privacy-by-default self-encryption, and a node mesh on consumer hardware — but it enters a market where Filecoin and Arweave already have far larger adoption and tooling.

## How & Where It Trades

ANT liquidity is thin. CoinGecko reported no major centralized-exchange listings in the snapshot data, and on-chain volume is modest (~$190k/24h at the April snapshot) — among the least liquid names in this DePIN cohort. Trading is concentrated on smaller venues and on-chain via [[arbitrum|Arbitrum One]], where the ANT ERC-20 settles. The consequences are practical: wide spreads, high slippage on size, and outsized percentage moves (the +19.7% weekly rally noted in the snapshot is illustrative). There is no meaningful ANT derivatives market. The large gap between circulating (~326M) and total/max supply (1.2B) — MC/FDV ≈ 0.27 — means node-reward emissions and remaining migration are a persistent overhang.

## Narrative & Catalysts

Autonomi sits in the **decentralized-storage / [[depin|DePIN]]** narrative, adjacent to the AI-data theme (its own framing ties permanent storage to an AI/robotics future). In the current tape (2026-06-22, Fear & Greed 21 / Extreme Fear, established bear market) DePIN small-caps are high-beta. ANT-specific catalysts:
- **Network maturation / launch milestones** — after a famously long development history, credible progress on mainnet stability, node growth, and real stored-data volume is the key re-rating driver.
- **Listing catalysts** — given thin liquidity, any tier-1 exchange listing would be an outsized volume/price event.
- **Storage-narrative rotation** — moves in Filecoin/Arweave and the broad DePIN basket spill into ANT.
- **Token-migration completion** — finishing the legacy MaidSafeCoin/SNT → ANT migration removes uncertainty but can also release supply.

## Trading Playbook

> Educational framing only — not financial advice. ANT is an illiquid, high-beta small-cap.

- **Liquidity first** — ANT's defining trading characteristic is thinness. Always use limit orders; market orders of even modest size will move price materially. Position sizes that work for a Binance-listed token do not work here.
- **Regime awareness** — at Fear & Greed 21 (Extreme Fear) in an established bear market, illiquid DePIN small-caps can gap sharply both ways; the +19.7% weekly move illustrates that volatility cuts in both directions.
- **Emission overhang** — with MC/FDV ≈ 0.27, factor in node-reward emissions and remaining migration supply; rallies into unlock/migration events often fade.
- **Catalyst-driven** — ANT's asymmetric upside is tied to delivery milestones and listings; its asymmetric downside is "another delay." Trade the news, not the perpetual narrative.
- **Invalidation** — repeated mainnet slippage or stalled node growth undermines the core thesis; treat a fresh delay as structural, not a dip to buy.

## Risks

- **Execution / delay history** — Extremely long development timeline; the network has repeatedly missed earlier launch expectations, a recurring credibility concern.
- **Adoption risk** — Permanent pay-once economics depend on real usage and durable node participation to keep data available long-term.
- **Competition** — Faces Filecoin, Arweave, Storj, Sia, and others in [[decentralized-storage]].
- **Liquidity & volatility** — Modest market cap and uneven listings; the +19.7% weekly move illustrates sharp volatility in both directions.
- **Emission overhang** — Large gap between circulating and total supply can pressure price as tokens are distributed.
- All figures other than the dated market snapshot are qualitative; node counts and storage capacity are not independently verified here.

## Overview

Autonomi is a decentralised network where data is stored securely across millions of everyday devices, with privacy the default, not the exception. 

The Network is a fully decentralized ecosystem for data storage and retrieval. It does not require any single entity or service control as nodes are able to validate and engage (or ignore) one another without intervention.
Unlike edge computing and others promoting private, decentralized hosting, The Autonomi Network is autonomous, permanent and has zero subscriptions or recurring fees. To use Autonomi a one-time upload fee is paid to The Network - the uploaded data is then quantum securely encrypted end to end.

With Autonomi, everyday internet users, content creators and app developers will have access to a truly decentralized infrastructure layer - they’re going to be able to build, protect and deploy amazing things. With AI and Robotics changing our world fast, Autonomi will ensure each and everyone of us can be in control of our own knowledge, thoughts and actions.
