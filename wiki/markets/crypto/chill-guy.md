## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

CHILLGUY is a **perp-first** asset. It trades on **Hyperliquid** as **CHILLGUY-PERP** with leverage up to roughly **40-50x**, but it is **not listed on Binance**. Spot access is limited and offshore (smaller CEXs plus Solana DEXs like Orca), so real directional flow and price discovery concentrate on the Hyperliquid perp rather than spot. Book depth is thin relative to majors and consistent with a sub-$15M-cap memecoin, so large orders move price and slippage is meaningful. The venue concentration means execution should assume the HL perp is the reference market: size positions modestly, favor limit/passive fills, and expect the HL mark and funding to lead any offshore spot quotes.

### Applicable strategies

- [[crowded-long-funding-fade]] — memecoin rallies here draw crowded leveraged longs on the HL perp; persistently positive funding flags an over-owned book to fade.
- [[liquidation-cascade-fade]] — thin depth plus high leverage make CHILLGUY prone to sharp liquidation flushes that overshoot and snap back, offering fade entries.
- [[funding-rate-harvest]] — elevated, volatile perp funding on a low-cap meme can be systematically collected when a spot/DEX hedge is available.
- [[oi-price-exhaustion]] — watch HL open interest against price: OI spiking into a stalling meme move often precedes a squeeze in the crowded direction.
- [[meme-coin-cycle]] — CHILLGUY is a pure narrative/meme token, so its behavior tracks the broader meme risk-on/risk-off cycle rather than fundamentals.
- [[breakout-and-retest]] — with price discovery on one venue, HL perp breakouts from range and their retests give clean, mechanical entries.

### Volatility & regime character

CHILLGUY is a **high-beta Solana memecoin** driven by reflexivity and social/TikTok narrative rather than fundamentals. Realized volatility is high, moves are sharp and headline-sensitive, and the token sits ~98% below its 2024 ATH — a deep drawdown regime typical of post-hype meme assets. Correlation to BTC/ETH is loose and asymmetric: it tends to amplify broad risk-on rallies and get hit hard in risk-off/de-grossing, while diverging on idiosyncratic meme flows. Beta is high in absolute terms but unstable.

### Risk flags

- **Venue concentration** — perp flow sits on a single venue (Hyperliquid); an HL outage, delisting, or leverage/param change directly impairs tradability and hedging.
- **Thin liquidity** — low market cap and shallow books mean high slippage, gap risk, and vulnerability to liquidation cascades.
- **Narrative dependence** — value is tied entirely to meme/social momentum; fading attention can drive persistent, non-mean-reverting decline.
- **Perp funding dislocations** — funding on a low-cap meme perp can swing violently and diverge from any offshore spot, so carry/hedge assumptions must be re-checked often.
- **Limited spot hedge** — no Binance listing and offshore-only spot make cash-and-carry or clean delta hedging harder and more basis-risky than for majors.
