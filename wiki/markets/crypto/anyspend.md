## Overview

AnySpend is a next-generation fiat on-ramp and swap platform built for the onchain economy. AnySpend sits at the center of a growing consumer and gaming ecosystem on Base. The platform connects traditional payment rails (ACH, card, and local bank transfers) directly to onchain liquidity via Caldera’s metalayer routing infrastructure, allowing users to instantly purchase, swap, or fund wallets across chains with a single transaction.
