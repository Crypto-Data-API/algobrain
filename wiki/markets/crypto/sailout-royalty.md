## Overview

LiquidRoyalty is a berachain-based RWA project that tokenize real world cashflow into royalty tokens that pay daily stablecoins.

Cashflow are intercepted from e-commerce businesses, on-ramped to berachain, then token holders are airdropped USDe on a daily basis, just by holding.

LiquidRoyalty has curated a pipeline of $200mil cashflow by partnering with our TradFi partners, and SAIL.r is the first business to be tokenized with over $15mil in annual revenue - so far SAIL.r has paid over $200k in royalties on-chain since launching in Dec 2025.
