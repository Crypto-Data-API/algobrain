# Dog (Bitcoin)

**Dog (Bitcoin)** (ticker **DOG**, full name "Dog Go To The Moon") is a fully decentralized [[meme-coins|memecoin]] **native to the [[bitcoin|Bitcoin]] blockchain**, issued as a [[bitcoin-runes|Bitcoin Runes]] token and stored in UTXOs like BTC itself. It is the flagship meme of the Bitcoin Ordinals/Runes ecosystem, distributed via a **100% airdrop fair launch** with no team allocation. It ranks **#394** by market capitalization.

## Technology & Protocol

DOG is a **[[bitcoin-runes|Bitcoin Runes]]** token — part of the **Runes fungible-token standard** that launched at the April-2024 Bitcoin halving as a UTXO-native alternative to BRC-20. Unlike Ethereum/Solana memes that live in smart contracts, DOG balances are **etched directly into Bitcoin UTXOs**, secured by Bitcoin's [[proof-of-work]] consensus and transferred in ordinary Bitcoin transactions. Practical implications:

- **No smart contract, no admin keys** — the token is as immutable as the rune etching; there is no contract to upgrade or rug.
- **Bitcoin block times (~10 min) and fees** make native transfers slower and chunkier than EVM/Solana DEX swaps; trading clears via PSBTs on Runes marketplaces.
- **Bridged wrappers** on Solana and Starknet exist for faster, cheaper trading at the cost of bridge/custody risk.

## Narrative & Category

DOG is a pure **community/narrative-driven [[meme-coins|memecoin]]**, but its distinguishing narrative is being **the meme OF Bitcoin** — native to BTC L1 via [[bitcoin-runes|Runes]], not an Ethereum or Solana token. Its thesis is *"onboarding millions to Bitcoin"* through meme culture and a maximally fair, fully decentralized distribution. The self-organized "DOG Army" produces art, apps, merchandise, meetups, and PFP collections under the CC0 license, with no paid promotion. As the standout survivor of the Runes [[meme-coin-cycle|meme cycle]], it carries the "Bitcoin's dog coin" mantle. Category tags: Bitcoin Ecosystem, Runes, Bitcoin Meme, Dog-Themed.

## Valuation Framing

DOG has **no fundamentals** — no cash flows, treasury, or utility — so its value is **purely reflexive**: price *is* the product, and demand is a self-referential function of attention, the strength of the [[bitcoin-runes|Runes]] narrative, and Bitcoin-cycle beta. Framing points:

- **Reflexivity:** higher price → more attention → more buyers, and vice versa; there is no fundamental floor to arrest a slide, which is why DOG sits near its ATL.
- **Relative-meme valuation:** the only meaningful "valuation" is comparative — DOG's market cap vs. other dog/Bitcoin memes — and Schelling-point status as *the* Bitcoin meme.
- **Fully diluted (MC/FDV = 1.00):** no dilution risk, so the entire valuation is a live float — clean, but also fully exposed to sentiment.

## Risks

- **Severe illiquidity:** ~$469K/24h volume is very thin for a $61M-cap token; exits move price hard and slippage is high.
- **Extreme volatility:** down ~94% from ATH and hovering just above all-time lows.
- **No fundamental floor (reflexivity):** pure meme — no utility, cash flows, or treasury to support price.
- **Runes infrastructure friction:** native trading depends on Bitcoin fees/block times and less-mature Runes marketplaces; bridged versions add bridge/custody risk.
- **Holder concentration / dump risk:** despite the fair airdrop, early Ordinals holders can still coordinate sells.
- **Regime sensitivity:** high-beta to sentiment; near-ATL in an extreme-fear / bear regime (Fear & Greed = 23 on 2026-06-21).
- **Survivorship bias:** most Runes/Ordinals memes have faded; DOG's status as the survivor is not predictive of future returns.
