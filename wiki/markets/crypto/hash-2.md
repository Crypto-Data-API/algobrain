# Provenance Blockchain

**Provenance Blockchain** (HASH) is a sovereign Cosmos-SDK proof-of-stake Layer 1 launched in 2018 by Figure Technologies (the blockchain lender founded by SoFi co-founder Mike Cagney), purpose-built for regulated financial assets — mortgages, HELOCs, lending, and tokenized funds. For traders, HASH is the purest **RWA-infrastructure proxy with a listed-equity sister asset**: Figure Technology Solutions (Nasdaq: FIGR, IPO Sep 2025) runs most of its loan volume on Provenance, making HASH a thin-liquidity satellite trade around FIGR news.

## Overview

HASH is the native token of Provenance Blockchain, a sovereign L1 public, proof-of-stake blockchain built using the Cosmos SDK. Launched in 2018, Provenance Blockchain modernizes and democratizes financial markets by bringing clarity, access, and equity to participants across the global financial ecosystem. Our platform addresses the challenges of legacy financial infrastructure by providing a transparent, efficient, and accessible alternative for financial institutions and users alike.

HASH pays all fees on-chain and carries governance rights; its value is driven by usage of the chain's financial-asset workflows (mortgage origination, HELOC securitization, tokenized funds) rather than retail DeFi activity.

### Architecture & value-accrual

Provenance is a **Cosmos-SDK sovereign Layer 1** using Tendermint/CometBFT proof-of-stake consensus. Because it is its own chain (not an app on a shared L1), HASH plays the standard Cosmos triple role: (1) **gas/fee token** for all on-chain transactions, (2) **staking/security token** delegated to validators to secure the network (with the usual [[slashing]]/[[staking|staking]] mechanics), and (3) **governance token** for protocol parameters. Value accrual is therefore tied to *on-chain financial activity* — the more mortgage origination, HELOC securitization, and tokenized-fund settlement flows through Provenance, the more fees and staking demand support HASH. The distinguishing feature versus generic L1s is that the demand driver is **regulated financial-asset workflows run primarily by Figure**, not retail DeFi or memecoins.

## 2025–2026 Developments

- **Figure IPO (Sep 2025)** — Figure Technology Solutions, the blockchain lender founded by Mike Cagney that built and runs its lending stack on Provenance, priced its Nasdaq IPO at **$25/share on 2025-09-10** and began trading as **FIGR on 2025-09-11**, raising **~$787.5M** at a **~$5.3B valuation**. ## Trading Relevance

- **Narrative basket**: [[real-world-assets|RWA]] / regulated-tokenization basket, alongside [[xdce-crowd-sale|XDC]] and ONDO. HASH is the crypto-beta leg of a pair with **FIGR equity** — the cleaner expression of Figure fundamentals trades on Nasdaq.
- **Liquidity warning**: CEX listings are sparse and spot volume is extremely thin (April 2026 snapshot showed only ~$3.5K daily CEX volume; most float sits with insiders/stakers). Slippage and exit risk dominate any position sizing; this is effectively an illiquid venture-style token.
- **Event profile**: moves on Figure earnings/announcements, RWA-sector flows, and US regulatory news on tokenized securities.
- **Structural caution**: MC/FDV ≈ 0.57 with 100B max supply — emission overhang.

## Trading Playbook (bear / Extreme-Fear regime)

In the 2026-06-24 backdrop (Fear & Greed 22, established bear market, BTC ~$62.6K and ~18% below its 200-day MA), HASH is an **illiquid, event-driven RWA proxy**:

- **Pair-trade framing.** The cleanest fundamental expression of Figure is **FIGR equity on Nasdaq**, which is far more liquid and regulated. HASH is the high-beta crypto leg — useful for amplified exposure to the RWA/Figure narrative, but it carries crypto-beta risk on top of Figure's fundamentals.
- **Liquidity dominates sizing.** Spot CEX volume has been extremely thin (an April 2026 snapshot showed ~$3.5K/day) with most float held by insiders/stakers. Slippage and exit risk are the primary constraints; position as if you may not be able to exit near mark.
- **Catalyst-driven, with a real precedent.** HASH's defining move was the **Figure IPO (Sep 2025)**, which drove the ATH then fully retraced (-82% to the 2026-03-30 ATL). Expect outsized moves around Figure earnings/announcements and US tokenized-securities regulation, and treat the IPO round-trip as the template: narrative spikes can reverse hard.
- **Dilution and bear beta.** ~43B of 100B max supply is non-circulating (MC/FDV ≈ 0.57). In a continued risk-off regime, an illiquid, dilution-heavy, narrative token should be assumed to underperform majors.
