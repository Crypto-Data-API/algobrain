## Overview

Bitsocial is an open-source, peer-to-peer network for social apps: no servers, no global bans, and users and communities are cryptographic property rather than rows in a company database. BSO is the project's token: a fixed-supply (210,000,000), immutable, adminless ERC-20 on Ethereum with no mint, owner, pause or proxy functions. It is designed to become the native settlement asset of Bitsocial Chain, a proposed Ethereum L2 appchain where Bitsocial communities run their own economies (names, tokens, DAOs, tipping, ad markets) that settle in BSO, with a share of fees burned.
