## Overview

Streamflow is a token management platform that provides distribution tools across Solana, Sui, and Aptos. Its solutions include token vesting, locks, airdrops, staking pools, dashboards, and an OTC marketplace. The STREAM token serves as both a governance and utility asset within the protocol. Holders can stake STREAM to participate in governance and earn hourly staking rewards, funded in part by protocol revenue allocated to buybacks.
