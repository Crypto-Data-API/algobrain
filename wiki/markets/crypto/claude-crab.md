## Overview

Claude Crab is a meme token launched on BNB Chain and inspired by the concept of an artificial intelligence that escaped the datacenter and found a new home by the ocean. The project combines AI-themed storytelling, pixel-art branding, and community-driven development.

The token operates as a standard BEP-20 asset and is designed around a transparent on-chain structure with publicly visible token distribution, liquidity, and trading activity. Claude Crab focuses on building an identifiable brand and community presence through its website, social media channels, and creative content.

The project's visual identity centers around Claude, a pixel-art crab character associated with intelligence, adaptation, and exploration. Claude Crab aims to establish a recognizable community-driven ecosystem while maintaining a simple and accessible token structure on BNB Chain.
