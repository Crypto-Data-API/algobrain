## Overview

AlphaBlock AI is a complete ecosystem built to track, understand, and act on on chain activity. It starts with an information layer that monitors whales, labels smart wallets, reads trends, and delivers real time alerts. On top of this sits an insights layer that converts raw data into clear guidance for traders. The final layer is execution, where users can act on the data through advanced tools and trading features powered by AI. The goal is to give traders a single ecosystem that covers discovery, analysis, and action.
