# edgeX

**edgeX** (EDGE) is an orderbook-based decentralized perpetuals exchange ("CEX-grade perp DEX") offering 24/7 perps across crypto, commodities, equities/tokenized stocks and RWA, settled on its own EDGE Chain. It became one of the largest perp DEXs by volume during the 2025 points season and launched the EDGE token on 31 March 2026 — making it a core name in the post-[[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]] [[perpetual-futures|perp]]-DEX narrative basket.

## Overview

edgeX is a 24/7 decentralized trading layer for global assets, offering perpetuals across commodities, equities, and crypto, alongside spot markets. Powered by its own EDGE Chain, edgeX is designed to deliver capital-efficient, self-custodied and 24/7 trading for users globally. Backed by Circle and integrated with native USDC, edgeX is building next-generation trading infrastructure that connects global liquidity with onchain settlement across multiple asset classes.

The matching engine claims up to 200,000 orders/second with sub-10ms latency — explicitly competing on CEX-like execution (the same pitch as [[hyperliquid]] and [[lighter]]), with a mobile-first front end.

## Major News & Events (2025–2026)

- **November 2025** — monthly trading volume hit an all-time high of **$167.2B**, making edgeX the ~4th-largest perp DEX with roughly **14% market share**.
- **Pre-TGE (Q1 2026)** — points season drove edgeX to #2 perp DEX by volume at peak, processing **over $3B in 24h volume**.
- **31 March 2026** — **EDGE TGE**: 1B supply, 25% community airdrop fully unlocked at launch. crypto.news flagged transparency concerns around team/investor allocations ahead of launch.
- **Early 2026** — EDGE Chain migrated to an Arbitrum-based L2 as the settlement home for all edgeX products.
- **Post-TGE** — token ran from an ATL of $0.4938 (31 Mar) to an ATH of $1.17 (3 Apr 2026); platform daily volumes held above ~$200M.

## Valuation Framing (Qualitative)

- **MC/FDV 0.35** — heavy unlock overhang (~65% of supply unminted); the largest valuation headwind, compounded by an airdrop-heavy float prone to early sell pressure.
- **Volume durability is the key question** — the November-2025 $167.2B monthly volume and ~14% share were achieved during a points-incentivized period; the bull case requires that post-incentive volume (and fee revenue accruing to EDGE) holds, the classic perp-DEX make-or-break.
- **Fee-to-token accrual** — watch for announced mechanisms tying platform fees to EDGE value; without clear accrual the token is a governance/airdrop claim rather than a cash-flow asset.
- **~-73.7% from ATH, near ATL** — EDGE has round-tripped most of its post-TGE move; the +21.6% daily snapshot pop is volatility off lows, not evidence of a re-rate.

This is framing, not a price target; the wiki holds no fair-value model for EDGE.
