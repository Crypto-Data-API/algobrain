## Overview

LOUZI is an AI-powered digital finance ecosystem focused on automated trading strategies, tokenized markets and on-chain yield generation.

The project combines AI-driven analytics, algorithmic trading systems and premium subscription services to generate ecosystem revenue across crypto and tokenized asset markets. A portion of the generated revenue is allocated toward token buybacks, liquidity expansion and long-term ecosystem growth.

LOUZI also introduces a staking mechanism with a target annual yield ranging from 12% to 25%, depending on market conditions and ecosystem performance. The platform is designed around transparency, sustainable token economics and community-driven development.

The LOUZI token is used as the core utility asset of the ecosystem, powering staking access, premium features and participation within the platform’s AI finance infrastructure.
