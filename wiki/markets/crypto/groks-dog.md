## Overview

BOLT ($BOLT) is a community-driven memecoin built on the Solana blockchain using the SPL token standard. The token is directly associated with StarBoltSprint, also known as Bolt, an official xAI-created fictional character introduced in March 2025. StarBoltSprint is described as a cosmic canine companion to Grok, participating in space-themed adventures, interactive storytelling, and AI-driven simulations as part of xAI's creative initiatives.
