## Overview

SCOR is an on-chain sports engagement protocol that transforms both fan participation and professional sports IP into programmable digital assets. On the fan side, SCOR-ID is a permanent, soulbound on-chain identity that records engagement, skill, and verified fandom across platforms. On the IP side, SCOR enables licensed teams, leagues, and athletes to deploy programmable sports IP that powers games, collectibles, and competitive experiences.

Over 2,000 professional athletes, teams, and leagues have opted in, validating SCOR as core infrastructure for licensed sports engagement. Through skill-based gameplay, progression mechanics, and sustainable token economics, SCOR is designed for long-term utility, cross-chain expansion, and rewards driven by genuine participation.
