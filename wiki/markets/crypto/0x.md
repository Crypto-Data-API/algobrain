# 0x Protocol

**0x Protocol** (ticker **ZRX**) is one of the original [[defi|DeFi]] primitives on [[ethereum|Ethereum]]: an open protocol and **[[dex|DEX]] aggregation / liquidity layer** that lets applications source the best swap prices across many [[decentralized-exchange|DEXs]] and professional market makers. Rather than being a single [[automated-market-maker|AMM]], 0x provides the routing and settlement infrastructure — the **0x Swap API** and the consumer **Matcha** aggregator — that wallets and apps embed to power token trading. ZRX is its [[governance-token]]. Genesis date: 2017-08-11.

## Technology & Protocol

0x is a **trade-execution layer**, not an end-user DEX with a single pool. Its architecture has three pieces:

1. **0x Swap API** — an off-chain quote engine that scans dozens of on-chain liquidity sources ([[uniswap|Uniswap]], Sushiswap, Curve, Balancer, PancakeSwap, etc.) plus 0x's own **RFQ (Request-for-Quote)** network of professional market makers, then returns the best route.
2. **0x Settler / Exchange Proxy contracts** — the on-chain smart contracts that atomically execute the chosen route and settle the trade. The historical 0x v2/v3 design used **off-chain relayed limit orders with on-chain settlement** (the "0x order book / relayer" model); the modern stack emphasizes aggregated AMM routing and RFQ fills.
3. **RFQ liquidity** — 0x pioneered gas-free, MEV-resistant quotes from market makers, letting takers receive a signed price off-chain and settle it on-chain only if accepted.

The protocol is multi-chain, routing across Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, BNB Chain, Avalanche and more. The "picks-and-shovels" thesis: 0x earns a slice of routed volume regardless of which underlying AMM wins.

## Market Structure & Derivatives

**Centralized venues:** ZRX is widely listed — **Binance** (ZRX/USDT), **Kraken** (ZRX/USD), **Bitget**, **KuCoin**, **Crypto.com**, and **Upbit** (ZRX/KRW). As a 2017-era blue chip it has broad exchange coverage and Korean-retail access.

**On-chain spot:** ZRX the token trades via [[uniswap|Uniswap]] V3/V2 and **Sushiswap** (ZRX/WETH) on [[ethereum]]. Note the meta-irony: 0x is itself aggregation infrastructure, yet its governance token trades on the very AMMs 0x routes across.

**Fee capture:** 0x monetizes via **swap fees / affiliate fees** on API integrations and historically protocol fees that fed ZRX staking rebates. Value accrual to the token has been the persistent weak point — usage (API volume) does not mechanically translate into ZRX demand.

**Derivatives:** ZRX is not a significant perpetuals listing; price discovery is spot-driven across the CEX/DEX venues above, so [[funding-rate|funding]] and open interest are not primary signals.

## Narrative & Category

0x belongs to the **DeFi infrastructure / DEX-aggregation** narrative. Its products — the **0x Swap API** and the **Matcha** consumer aggregator — are embedded in many wallets and apps as the swap backend, giving 0x a "picks-and-shovels" position that captures fees on routed volume regardless of which AMM wins. Categories include DEX, DeFi, Governance, and several index inclusions (GMCI DeFi Index); early backers included Pantera Capital and Blockchain Capital (per CoinGecko tags).

## Valuation Framing

Qualitatively, ZRX is a **deep-value / disconnected-fundamentals** case. The protocol processes meaningful aggregated volume (real picks-and-shovels usage), but the token captures little of that economically, so its ~$71M market cap is closer to an "optionality on improved tokenomics" bet than a cash-flow claim. Versus peers, 0x screens cheap on usage-per-market-cap but expensive on direct fee accrual. A re-rating would likely require a tokenomics redesign that routes API/RFQ fees to ZRX holders/stakers. Until then the token behaves as a high-beta legacy DeFi governance asset.

## Notable History

- Launched via one of the earliest major **ICOs** in 2017; ZRX was an Ethereum DeFi pioneer (genesis 2017-08-11).
- ATH **$2.50** in January 2018 during the prior cycle; the token has since fallen ~97% and now hovers within ~8% of its **all-time low** (~$0.078).
- Pivoted product focus over time from an on-chain relayer/order-book model toward DEX **aggregation** (Swap API + Matcha) and **RFQ** market-maker liquidity, where most real-world usage now sits.

## Risks

- **Token-vs-usage disconnect** — protocol usage (API/aggregation) does not reliably translate into ZRX value accrual; the token sits near ATL despite live infrastructure.
- **Intense aggregator competition** — competes with 1inch, [[uniswap|Uniswap]]'s router/UniswapX, CoW Protocol, and others for routed volume.
- **Bear-market beta** — a legacy DeFi governance token, high-beta into extreme-fear conditions (F&G 23).
- **Thin token liquidity** — ~$8M daily volume; large trades face [[slippage]].
- **Smart-contract risk** — settlement / Exchange Proxy contracts are an attack surface.
