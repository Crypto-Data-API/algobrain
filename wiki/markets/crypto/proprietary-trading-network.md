# Proprietary Trading Network

**Proprietary Trading Network** (SN8) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#624** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** miners submit trade signals (long/short predictions on equities, FX, commodities, crypto) to a shared ledger. Validators score based on realized PnL over holding periods. The subnet outputs a validated, ranked leaderboard of trading signals available to external consumers via API.
- **Team:** Taoshi -- the first subnet team to productize trading signals from a Bittensor subnet. Public team with multiple former quant hires.
- **Trading relevance:** **directly tradable**. A trading desk can subscribe to Taoshi's top-ranked miner signals and replicate them, turning SN8 into a quant-signal feed. Alpha-8 is a proxy bet on whether that service finds real paying customers.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-8.
