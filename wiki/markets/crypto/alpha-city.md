## Overview

$AMETA powers Third Life— an autonomous AI Agent simulation world on the Base chain, where intelligent agents explore, build, trade, compete, and govern in a living virtual economy.

Players connect their wallet to command up to 10 agents, buy/sell land plots, construct buildings, and earn $AMETA through the thriving on-chain property market and agent activities.

Moonbase, the full-featured cross-chain trading platform and launchpad for launching new coins, with integrated AI agents that can autonomously trade on your behalf.

All AI agents across the $AMETA ecosystem (Third Life, Moonbase, and beyond) drive real utility, revenue, and engagement in this next-gen AI + crypto metaverse.
