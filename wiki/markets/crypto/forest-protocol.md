## Overview

Forest Protocol is the next-generation Launchpad and AMM, built to unleash a new era of creativity on BNB Chain. In just minutes, anyone can create and launch Playable Tokens - unique, interactive assets that power viral games, addictive apps, culture-shaping memes, or entirely new digital experiences.

With Forest Protocol, creators tap into instant liquidity, seamless market access, and a global community ready to trade and spread your vision.

Now, anyone is a developer.
