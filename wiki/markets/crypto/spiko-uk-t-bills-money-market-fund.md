## Overview

UKTBL (Spiko UK Treasury Bills) is a tokenized UCITS money market fund launched by Spiko, investing directly in UK Treasury Bills denominated in GBP. The fund allows investors to subscribe from as little as 1 GBP and generates stable yields tracking the UK T-Bill rate, while maintaining overnight liquidity. The shareholder register is hosted on Ethereum, Polygon PoS, Arbitrum One, Base, and Etherlink. CACEIS (CrĂ©dit Agricole group) acts as depositary bank, PwC as auditor, and Chainlink provides on-chain NAV oracle feeds. The fund is supervised by the French AMF.
