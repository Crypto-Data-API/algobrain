## Overview

Golden Dragon is a closed digital ecosystem designed to bring together a wide array of tools and services, offering users an integrated platform. The project aims to create a high-tech environment where individuals can access resources necessary for their personal and professional development. Furthermore, Golden Dragon focuses on establishing a modern economic system built on transparency and advanced technologies to support its user base in achieving their digital ecosystem goals.
