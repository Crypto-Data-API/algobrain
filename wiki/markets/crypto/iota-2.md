# iota

**iota** (SN9) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#644** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** distributed LLM pretraining on Bittensor. Originally launched as the "Pretraining" subnet — the first training-focused subnet on the network — later rebranded to IOTA as Macrocosmos evolved the subnet's scope.
- **Team:** Macrocosmos (iota.macrocosmos.ai).
- **Trading relevance:** historically the "longest-running training" subnet. Competes with SN3 [[templar|Templar]], SN29 [[coldint|Coldint]], and SN38 [[distributed-training-subnet|Distributed Training]] for share of the training-infrastructure thesis.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-9.
