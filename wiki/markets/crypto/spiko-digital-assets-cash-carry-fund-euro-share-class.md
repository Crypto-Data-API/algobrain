## Overview

- Spiko Cash &amp; Carry is a systematic fund that allocates each month to whichever opportunity offers the highest net return between a classic crypto cash-and-carry trade on CME futures, or short-duration U.S. Treasury bills. The fund seeks to lock in predictable, market-neutral returns that exceed the prevailing risk-free rate. Investor returns directly reflect the performance of a transparent, rules-based strategy index, offering institutional-grade exposure to a market-neutral cash-and-carry approach without taking directional crypto or interest-rate risk.
