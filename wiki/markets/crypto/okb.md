# OKB

**OKB** is the native utility token of [[okx|OKX]], one of the largest crypto exchanges by volume, and the gas token of OKX's X Layer L2. For traders it is the canonical 2025 case study in **supply-shock tokenomics**: in August 2025 OKX burned 52% of the supply in one stroke, hard-capping OKB at 21 million tokens (deliberately mirroring [[bitcoin|Bitcoin]]'s cap) and tripling the price within days. It belongs to the [[centralized-exchange|centralized-exchange]] (CEX) token basket alongside [[binance-coin|BNB]], [[bitget-token|BGB]], and [[kucoin-shares|KCS]].

## Protocol / Product

OKB is the platform token of **[[okx|OKX]]** (formerly OKEx), a top-tier global crypto exchange offering spot, margin, perpetual/futures, options, and earn products, plus a self-custody [[wallet|Web3 wallet]] and DeFi access. OKB is the connective utility asset across that stack and, since 2025, the gas asset of OKX's own L2.

### What OKB does

- **Trading-fee discounts** — tiered fee reductions for holders, the core sink that anchors demand to OKX trading volume.
- **Launchpad / Jumpstart access** — OKB holdings gate allocations to new token launches on OKX, a recurring demand pulse.
- **X Layer gas** — OKB is the native gas token of [[x-layer|X Layer]], OKX's Ethereum-aligned L2 (a Polygon CDK-based zkEVM lineage). Network usage burns/consumes OKB as gas, creating a usage-linked sink independent of exchange fees.
- **Ecosystem utility** — staking, payments, and discounts across OKX products.

### X Layer

Alongside the August 2025 burn, OKX upgraded **X Layer** (raising throughput toward ~5,000 TPS and cutting gas to negligible levels) and announced the full transition of OKB from Ethereum to X Layer, making OKB the network's native gas asset. The post-burn bull thesis hinges on X Layer adoption: with supply fixed at 21M, the only durable demand growth comes from (a) OKX trading-fee sink and (b) X Layer transaction demand. See [[layer-2|Layer 2]] and [[x-layer]].

## Tokenomics & Supply

OKB launched in 2018 on ERC-20 with an original supply of 1 billion, of which 600 million was allocated to customers for community building and marketing (no ICO or public fundraising; remainder locked 1–3 years). The old model ran a **quarterly buy-and-burn** funded from OKX revenue.

### The August 2025 burn (supply shock)

On 13–14 August 2025, OKX permanently burned **65.26 million OKB worth ~$7.6 billion** — cutting circulating supply by 52% and capping total supply at **21 million** (a deliberate psychological echo of Bitcoin's max supply). The burn was confirmed via on-chain audits and **ended the quarterly buy-and-burn program**: supply is now immutable. OKB spiked **+200% to ~$142** on the news, with 24h volume up ~13,000% to $723M, and ran to an all-time high of **$228.74 on 5 October 2025**.

## Market Structure & Derivatives

- **Where it trades**: liquidity is overwhelmingly concentrated on **OKX itself**; off-OKX venues are thin (Kraken OKB/USD, [[uniswap|Uniswap]] v2/v3 on Ethereum). This **venue-concentration** means OKB price action largely reflects OKX-platform flows and is hard to short at size off-platform — a structural risk shared with [[kucoin-shares|KCS]] and, to a lesser degree, [[bitget-token|BGB]].
- **24h volume** at the 2026-06-20 snapshot was ~$22.9M — thin relative to a $1.6B market cap (turnover <2%), characteristic of a token held largely by exchange users rather than actively traded.
- **Derivatives**: OKB perps/futures exist primarily on OKX itself; off-platform perp coverage is limited. Open interest and funding are not deep enough off-OKX to support institutional-scale hedging — another consequence of the single-venue structure.

## Valuation Framework

Exchange tokens have no cash-flow claim, but they can be valued **qualitatively as a proxy for exchange economics**:

- **Exchange-volume proxy** — OKB demand scales with OKX trading-fee revenue (via discounts + launchpad gating). Rising retail/derivatives volume on OKX tightens the float.
- **Buyback proxy → now demand proxy** — pre-2025, OKB was valued partly on the expected NPV of quarterly buy-and-burns. Post-burn, that lever is gone; the token reprices purely on demand against a frozen 21M supply.
- **X Layer usage proxy** — incremental demand from L2 gas consumption; adoption metrics (TVL, daily transactions, active addresses) are the forward valuation drivers.
- **Relative**: compare OKB market cap / OKX volume to [[binance-coin|BNB]]/Binance volume and [[bitget-token|BGB]]/Bitget volume to gauge whether OKB is rich or cheap within the basket.

A useful sanity check: with supply fixed, **price is the only adjustment variable** — there is no buyback to "absorb" weakness, so OKB tends to be higher-beta to OKX flow sentiment than buyback-cushioned peers.

## Trading Playbook

- **Basket rotation** — OKB trades within the CEX-token basket. Long OKB / short a weaker peer (e.g., a token mid-dilution like [[bitget-token|BGB]]) expresses a view on relative exchange momentum with less directional crypto-beta.
- **Supply-shock case study** — the Aug 2025 burn is the cleanest recent natural experiment in supply-shock repricing: +200% on a 52% supply cut, then a ~67% retrace from the October top, illustrating that **supply shocks rerate but don't permanently defy flow**.
- **Catalyst calendar** — X Layer adoption metrics, OKX business milestones (e.g., US expansion), broader CEX-token rotation, and overall exchange-volume trends.
- **Bear-market context** — in the current extreme-fear regime, thin off-venue liquidity makes OKB prone to gappy moves; size accordingly.
- **Counterparty caveat** — because liquidity lives on OKX, OKB is only as accessible as OKX itself; treat platform/regulatory risk as a position risk, not just a headline.

## Risks

- **Exchange counterparty risk** — OKB's value and liquidity are inseparable from OKX. Any solvency, security, or operational failure at OKX directly impairs the token. See [[exchange-collapses]] for the tail scenario.
- **Regulatory risk** — OKX has faced regulatory scrutiny across jurisdictions; enforcement, license loss, or a forced market exit would hit OKB demand and the fee-discount utility. (See [[kucoin-shares|KCS]]'s 2025 DOJ plea as the template for how exchange-regulatory shocks transmit to the token.)
- **Venue-concentration risk** — thin off-OKX liquidity means price discovery and risk transfer depend on a single platform; hard to hedge or exit at size off-venue.
- **No buyback cushion** — post-burn, there is no buy-and-burn to support price in drawdowns; the token is fully exposed to demand swings (higher downside beta than buyback-cushioned peers).
- **Adoption risk** — the post-burn bull case rests on X Layer usage; weak L2 adoption leaves OKB reliant solely on OKX fee-driven demand.
- **Bear-market regime** — at Fear & Greed 23 in an established bear market, CEX-token premia compress; OKB has already round-tripped most of its 2025 supply-shock gains.
