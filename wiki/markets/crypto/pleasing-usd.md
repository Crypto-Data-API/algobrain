## Overview

Pleasing Golden is an RWA platform that transforms precious metals into liquid, yield-generating tokens accessible to anyone, anywhere. Pleasing Golden’s vision is to make gold ownership open, liquid, and collaborative. Through tokenization and a suite of&nbsp;liquidity-sharing programs—including&nbsp;DeFi liquidity leasing&nbsp;and&nbsp;Tokenization-as-a-Service—the brand transforms slow, closed markets into dynamic, programmable assets that can circulate instantly among builders, traders, and holders.

PUSD&nbsp;is Pleasing Golden’s synthetic stablecoin connecting on-chain liquidity with the physical gold ecosystem. Backed by a&nbsp;hybrid reserve of USDT collateral and tokenized metal exposure, PUSD enables real-time financing and settlement throughout the network.

The model connects:

- Depositors&nbsp;— deposit USDT, receive PUSD, and stake it into sPUSD for yield.
- Investors&nbsp;— traders or asset managers seeking stable liquidity with gold-linked returns.
- Operators — metals participants using PUSD to unlock working capital and accelerate settlement.
