# Corn

**Corn** (CORN) is a high-performance, low-cost network built for stablecoin payments, settlements, and financial applications at global scale.

Powered by USDT0, Corn enables instant, zero-fee transfers that move value anywhere, anytime, for free.

Built on Arbitrum Orbit, Corn delivers the scalability, interoperability, and efficiency needed to support real-world stablecoin adoption, connecting people, businesses, and applications through a shared layer of digital money that simply works. It ranks **#752** by market capitalization.
