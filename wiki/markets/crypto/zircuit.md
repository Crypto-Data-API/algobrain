## Overview

Zircuit Finance is an institutional cross-chain yield aggregator offering high rates with maximum security and transparency. Zircuit Finance onboards capital from multiple networks and deploys it across a growing set of yield strategies, giving users access to stable APR without relying on a single settlement layer. Built on security-first principles, Zircuit makes secure, accessible yield available across the onchain economy.
