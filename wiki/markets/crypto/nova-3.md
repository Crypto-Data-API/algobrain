# NOVA

**NOVA** (SN68) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#824** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** small-molecule drug discovery. Miners submit candidate molecules optimized for binding affinity to target proteins; validators score against in-silico docking and ADMET benchmarks. Part of the "AI for science" thesis on Bittensor.
- **Team:** Metanova Labs (metanova-labs.ai).
- **Trading relevance:** niche science-AI exposure. Unusual subnet because the output has potential real-world pharmaceutical value, but the monetization path is long and uncertain.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-68.
