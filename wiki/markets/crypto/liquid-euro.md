## Overview

weEUR is a yield-bearing ERC-20 token, representing exposure to ether.fi's Liquid EURO strategy. It automatically compounds EUR-denominated yield onchain, giving holders seamless access to institutional-grade euro yield without active management. Built on Ethereum, weEUR is fully backed, redeemable, and priced via a real-time onchain oracle, making it a transparent and composable building block for DeFi.
