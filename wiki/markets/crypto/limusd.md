# limUSD

**limUSD** (LIMUSD) is the USD expression of Hyperliquid's Native Yield: a single, yield-bearing token that continuously captures yield wherever it is most efficiently produced across Hyperliquid's yield sources.

It dynamically allocates capital to get the best risk-adjusted yield across funding rates, staking, money market yield and upcoming new primitives. It ranks **#2022** by market capitalization.
