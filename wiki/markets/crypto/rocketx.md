## Overview

RocketX is the most advanced hybrid CEX &amp; DEX aggregator that makes it easy to compare prices across 250+ exchanges to help traders with best prices with every Swap.
For dapps &amp; institutions, we act as a middleware by simplifying access to global crypto liquidity via a single API for in-app crypto swaps with best prices and 1-click cross chain capabilities.
