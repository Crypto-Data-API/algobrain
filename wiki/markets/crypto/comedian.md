# Comedian

**Comedian** (ticker **BAN**) is a [[solana|Solana]]-based [[meme-coin|meme coin]] inspired by Maurizio Cattelan's "Comedian" artwork — the banana duct-taped to a wall — which is the source of both its ticker (BAN, for "banana") and its branding. It launched out of the **[[pump-fun|Pump.fun]]** Solana meme ecosystem and is a pure narrative/community token with no protocol revenue or treasury. It trades as a small-cap Solana meme whose price is driven entirely by attention and meme rotation — a **reflexive** asset where price and narrative reinforce each other on the way up and unwind together on the way down.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

BAN is a **narrative/community meme** with no utility beyond speculation. Its hook is art-world virality — Maurizio Cattelan's duct-taped banana "Comedian" became a globally recognized image (and famously sold at auction for millions, then eaten) — repackaged as a Solana token. It belongs to the [[pump-fun|Pump.fun]] meme cohort and the broader [[memecoin-mania|memecoin-mania]] / [[meme-coins]] category, where value is a pure function of attention, social momentum, and meme rotation rather than fundamentals or cash flow.

## Risks

> Meme coins are among the highest-risk assets in crypto. BAN is reflexive and attention-dependent; treat the items below as the dominant consideration, not a footnote.

- **Extreme volatility:** As a pure meme, BAN routinely moves several percent per day and far more over longer windows; the ~82% drawdown from ATH shows the downside.
- **Reflexivity:** Price and narrative feed each other. Rising price draws attention and buyers; falling price kills the narrative and accelerates exits. There is no fundamental anchor to arrest a slide.
- **Thin-to-moderate liquidity:** Despite decent turnover, exits can still be costly in fast markets, and depth is far below large caps.
- **Insider / holder concentration:** Pump.fun launches frequently carry concentrated early-buyer or sniper holdings; on-chain distribution is not yet ingested here and should be checked before sizing — concentrated holders can dump into any rally.
- **Zero intrinsic value:** No revenue, product, or treasury — the price floor is attention, not fundamentals.
- **Bear-market backdrop:** Extreme Fear ([[fear-and-greed-index|index ~23]]) and an Established Bear Market regime make low-cap memes especially vulnerable to risk-off selling and liquidity withdrawal.
