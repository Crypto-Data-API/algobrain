## Overview

The future of global payments

Oobit lets anyone pay in-store or online with stablecoins using any wallet, fully agnostic from Phantom, MetaMask to Trust Wallet to Binance Wallet. It delivers a simple Apple Pay–style experience while running globally compliant issuing operations in 100+ countries, connecting the world’s wallets to real-world spending with unmatched accessibility, innovation, and reliability.
