## Overview

mGLOBAL is an ERC-20 liquid yield token that auto-compounds returns from a diversified portfolio of global fixed income instruments onchain. Price accrues continuously and is tracked via a real-time onchain oracle. Backed by investment-grade bonds and managed by professional asset managers, it offers seamless exposure to global yield as a composable building block in the Liquid Yield Tokens ecosystem.
