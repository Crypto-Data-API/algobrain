## Overview

mROX is a product in the Midas ecosystem of onchain investment products. Midas describes its products as tokenized financial instruments with clearly defined investor rights, ongoing onchain transparency, and compatibility with DeFi applications. Under the Midas product framework, each mToken tracks an underlying reference portfolio, and its value changes in line with the net asset value of that portfolio rather than functioning as a stablecoin or a DeFi vault. mROX was announced in March 2026 as part of Midas’ expanding suite of tokenized investment products.
