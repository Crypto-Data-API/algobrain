## Overview

Wen is a community coin based on a fractional NFT. The NFT refers to @weremeow’s poem 'A Love Letter to Wen Bros'.
The NFT is fractionalized into a trillion pieces that trade like normal Solana tokens. Each Wen token equals proportional ownership in Meow's poem.
