## Overview

Minima is an embedded, edge-native blockchain infrastructure designed so that every device, phones, sensors, vehicles, industrial machines and chips, can run a full validating and constructing node. Rather than relying on central validators or cloud infrastructure, it distributes consensus and data integrity directly across hardware at the source of activity. This creates a highly decentralised network where trust is generated at the edge rather than imposed from above.

Beyond its core protocol, Minima is evolving into a broader trust and data integrity layer through Integritas, enabling verifiable data capture, audit trails and AI decision provenance. Combined with quantum-resilient cryptographic design and support for machine-to-machine coordination, Minima is positioned as foundational infrastructure for a world of autonomous systems, decentralized physical networks (DePIN), and real-world data-driven economies.
