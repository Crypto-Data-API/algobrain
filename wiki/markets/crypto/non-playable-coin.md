# Non-Playable Coin

**Non-Playable Coin** (ticker **NPC**) is a multichain [[meme-coin]] / NFT-hybrid inspired by the "non-playable character" (NPC) internet meme that gained popularity in 2018. Its motto is *"I support the current coin,"* a self-aware homage to the meme. NPC originated on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] as an ERC-20 and has since expanded to [[base|Base]], [[solana|Solana]] and [[bnb]] (BNB Chain).

---

## Overview

**What is the project about?**
Non-Playable Coin (NPC) is a meme coin and NFT-hybrid for all 8+ billion humans on Earth. "I support the current coin" is the motto, a homage to the meme that first gained popularity in 2018.

**What makes the project unique?**
Inspired by the non-playable character meme, NPC leans into the joke by creating an ERC-20 token whose total supply equals the total human population as recorded on July 26, 2023. Each token is also convertible into an NFT at a 1:1 rate — i.e. a token with JPEG metadata designed to be traded both on decentralized exchanges like Uniswap (as an ERC-20) and on NFT marketplaces (as an ERC-1155).

This design — an NFT that can also be traded as an ERC-20 — is described by the project as a **meme fungible token (MFT)**. An MFT links two often-separated liquidity markets (fungible token markets and NFT markets), while also letting far more people own "a part of the culture." On the project's dApp, users can switch between the two formats: "respawn" as an NFT or "transform" into a meme coin. The most liquid form for trading is the ERC-20.

**History:** The project began by experimenting with NFT AMMs (e.g. Sudoswap) and the under-used ERC-1155 standard, aiming to show that communities can scale to millions using ERC-1155 while unlocking liquidity by making such tokens fungible as an ERC-20.

## Origin & Narrative

NPC draws on the **"NPC"/Wojak meme lineage** — the 4chan-rooted "non-playable character" gag that went mainstream around 2018, depicting people as scripted background characters. The token leans fully into the joke: its motto *"I support the current coin"* parodies the "I support the current thing" NPC meme, and its supply was deliberately set to the recorded global human population (so "everyone" can own a piece). The technical twist (the MFT / ERC-20 ↔ ERC-1155 hybrid) layers a novelty hook on top of a durable internet meme. As with all memes there is **no cash-flow utility**; value is reflexive and attention-driven.

## Market Structure & Derivatives

### Spot venues (centralized)

Listed on **Kraken** (NPC/USD), **Bitget**, and **KuCoin**.

### On-chain / DEX

Native trading on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] via **Uniswap V2/V3** against WETH, with additional [[liquidity]] across its [[base|Base]], [[solana|Solana]] and [[bnb]] (BNB Chain) deployments. Because NPC is an MFT, it can also change hands on NFT marketplaces in its ERC-1155 form, though the **ERC-20 form is by far the more liquid**.

NPC's derivatives footprint is thinner than the most-traded memes; trading is primarily spot/DEX. ## Valuation Framing

NPC has **no fundamental valuation** — no cash flows or discountable stream, and the MFT/NFT hybrid mechanic does not create one. Memes are **reflexive and sentiment-driven**: price is a self-referential function of attention and liquidity. The only meaningful anchors are *relative* (market cap vs peers, MC/FDV, liquidity), which describe positioning rather than intrinsic worth. Any price target is a sentiment bet.

## Risks

NPC carries the full risk profile of a utility-free [[meme-coin]]. **These risks are severe and can result in near-total loss:**

- **Extreme volatility & drawdown potential** — down ~92% from ATH; large daily swings (e.g. +3.61% on the snapshot date) cut both ways, and further moves toward zero are possible.
- **Narrative decay** — value rests on the staying power of the NPC/Wojak meme rather than any cash-flow utility.
- **Reflexivity** — attention drives price drives attention; the loop accelerates declines as readily as rallies.
- **Liquidity fragmentation / rug risk** — multichain + dual-standard (ERC-20/ERC-1155) design splits liquidity across venues and standards, which can worsen slippage; multichain meme tokens carry bridge and pool-drain exposure.
- **Insider / holder concentration** — large early/insider wallets may dominate float (on-chain distribution not yet ingested here).
- **Leverage risk** — where [[perpetual-futures|perps]] exist, extreme [[funding-rate|funding]] can drive liquidation cascades.
- **Macro / regime risk** — the snapshot date sat in Extreme Fear (index ~23) within an established bear market, an unfavorable regime for memes.

Speculative asset; apply strict [[risk-management]] and treat capital as fully at risk.
