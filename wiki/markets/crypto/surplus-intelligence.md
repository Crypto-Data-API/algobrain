## Overview

Surplus Intelligence is a free market for AI inference on Base.

Inference is becoming a core cost of software. Agents, coding tools, and automated workflows will call models constantly. That creates a demand for a cheaper, more liquid, more programmable market for inference.

Surplus turns unused capacity into revenue, and turns inference into a market-priced resource.

Oil has NYMEX. Equities have Nasdaq. Inference has Surplus.
