# Vaulta

**Vaulta** (ticker **A**) is the rebranded continuation of **EOS** — a high-throughput [[layer-1]] blockchain (Antelope/EOSIO lineage) repositioned in 2025 as a "Web3 banking" network for decentralized financial services. It pitches one-second finality, on-chain RAM-based memory, and native [[bitcoin]] integration via exSat, aiming to serve as a financial operating system spanning consumer payments, Bitcoin-native yield, and tokenized assets. The token migrated from the EOS ticker to **A**.

## Technology & Consensus

Vaulta runs on the **Antelope (formerly EOSIO)** technology stack, a delegated [[proof-of-stake]] (DPoS) [[layer-1]] known for fast block times, one-second finality, fee-less transactions for end users, and an on-chain **RAM** resource market for state/memory. The rebrand layers a "Web3 banking" product thesis on top: **native [[bitcoin]] integration via exSat** (bringing BTC liquidity and yield on-chain), tokenized real-world assets, and consumer payment rails — positioning the chain as a finance-focused operating system.

---

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

The narrative is a **legacy L1 reinvention into Web3 banking / institutional DeFi**. After years as EOS — once one of the largest ICOs ever — the project rebranded to Vaulta to escape the EOS overhang and target Bitcoin-native yield, tokenized assets ([[real-world-assets|RWAs]]), and payments. CoinGecko tags: Layer 1 (L1) and World Liberty Financial Portfolio. It competes with other finance-oriented L1s and with Bitcoin-DeFi bridges.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

- **MC/FDV 0.79:** ~79% of the fixed 2.1B supply circulates; remaining dilution is bounded (~21%) and capped, a structural positive — but the FDV of ~$138M still sits ~28% above the live $108M cap.
- **Rebrand discount / overhang:** EOS's history (one of the largest ICOs ever, ~$4B raised by Block.one, followed by years of underdelivery) leaves a deep brand-skepticism discount. The Vaulta rebrand is an explicit attempt to re-rate by shedding the EOS association; the market has not yet granted that re-rating (-92% from the post-rebrand high).
- **Execution-gated thesis:** the "Web3 banking" value case depends on *measurable* exSat BTC-yield TVL, tokenized-asset issuance, and payment volume — none of which can be confirmed from price data. Until those metrics show traction, A trades as legacy-L1 beta with a finance narrative.
- **Liquidity premium:** at ~$8.7M/day on a ~$108M cap (~8% turnover), A is by far the most liquid and tradeable name in this cohort, which makes it the cleanest vehicle here for expressing a view but does not by itself imply fundamental demand.
