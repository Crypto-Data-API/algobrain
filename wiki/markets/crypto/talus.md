## Overview

Talus is an AI infrastructure network that provides an on-chain execution layer for autonomous and intelligent agents. The project develops Nexus, a decentralized automation protocol, to enable AI agents to execute complex workflows in a verifiable manner on-chain, fostering a transparent and trustless environment for AI and consumer use cases. Ultimately, Talus is building the core infrastructure for decentralized AI agents, allowing developers to launch revenue-generating agents. By leveraging the Sui network to bridge off-chain AI compute with on-chain execution, Talus establishes the foundational layer for the AI and Information Economy.
