# Mysterium

**Mysterium** (MYST) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#1928** by market capitalization. Launched on 02/03/2022 by a team based in the United States, Mystery is a community token that rewards its holders with massive draws while making its token deflationary by burning tokens used to purchase tickets to the draw.
