## Overview

Solstice is the Yield Layer, a protocol bringing institutional-grade yield products on-chain for DeFi composability. Most on-chain yield has been leveraged on volatile assets. Institutional yield strategies, like delta-neutral funding capture, tokenized corporate credit, and sovereign-rate exposure, have historically sat behind compliance perimeters that public chains couldn't cross. Solstice closes that gap. The protocol wraps licensed off-chain strategies in a standard on-chain container, so the yield they produce flows across the DeFi ecosystem as composable tokens. Built for institutions. Open to everyone.
