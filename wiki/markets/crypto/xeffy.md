## Overview

Xeffy is a project building an RWA hybrid platform that integrates institutional grade multi strategy yield vaults with RWA asset infrastructure. By combining institutional grade vault strategies with dedicated on chain RWA infrastructure, Xeffy has built a comprehensive asset management solution and RWA ecosystem.  The product lineup is built around four pillars: a Vault (institutional-grade yield), RWA (tokenized real-world assets), Payment, and a Stablecoin. These are progressively unified into one total Xeffy App, a SuperApp that houses these dApps. Every product routes a portion of its real revenue into XEF buyback-and-burn, so ecosystem usage structurally reduces XEF supply over time.
