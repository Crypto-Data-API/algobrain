## Overview

Edel Finance is the global lending network for tokenized stocks. It is the next generation of securities lending infrastructure, built to be transparent, efficient, and scalable. By accepting tokenized equities from issuers such as Ondo and xStocks as collateral, Edel brings securities lending on-chain. It allows users to earn real yield by renting out their stocks and enables traders to build long-term long or short positions without relying on perpetual exchanges. Edel unlocks a new era of on-chain market efficiency where equities can move, earn, and be borrowed with the same freedom as crypto.
