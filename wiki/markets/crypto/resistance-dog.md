## Overview

Resistance Dog ($REDO), is a symbolic representation of the fight against censorship and freedom through digital resistance. The token pays homage to the creation of Pavel Durov, founder of TON - The Open Network and Telegram.

$REDO intends to create, and has begun creating a robust decentralized community that is at the forefront of the battle for digital resistance, with TON - The Open Network, Telegram and the millions of individuals fighting for the same cause.
