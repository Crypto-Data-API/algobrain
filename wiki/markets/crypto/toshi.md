# Toshi

**Toshi** (ticker **TOSHI**) is a cat-themed [[meme-coin]] native to [[base]], Coinbase's [[ethereum]] Layer-2. Named after Coinbase CEO Brian Armstrong's cat, it is one of the original and best-known memes on Base and has positioned itself as a community/launchpad brand within the Base ecosystem. It is a community-driven token without inherent utility.

## Origin & Narrative

TOSHI is named after **Brian Armstrong's cat**, tying it directly to the Coinbase/[[base]] brand identity — a provenance that made it one of the earliest and most recognizable cat memes on the chain when Base launched in 2023. Its narrative blends Base-ecosystem tribalism, the durable "cat vs frog" meme rivalry (it competes for Base attention with [[based-brett|BRETT]]), and a community/launchpad angle the project has cultivated over time. As with all memes, there is **no protocol revenue or utility**; value is attention-driven and reflexive.

## Category Context: The Meme-Coin Cycle

TOSHI is a **community / narrative [[meme-coin]]** and one of the foundational cat memes of the [[base]] ecosystem. Its arc fits the [[meme-coin-cycle]]: an extremely low 2023 base (~1.9e-8), a 2025 spike to ~$0.00226 during the Base meme cycle, and a sharp correction into the bear market (~95% off ATH). It is the cat counterpart to Base's frog flagship [[based-brett|BRETT]]. CoinGecko categories: Base Ecosystem, Base Meme, Cat-Themed, Launchpad, and Meme.

## Risks

TOSHI carries the full risk profile of a utility-free [[meme-coin]]. **These risks are severe and can result in near-total loss:**

- **Extreme volatility & drawdown potential** — high-beta to the meme/risk cycle; already ~95% below ATH, and Korean (Upbit) flow can add idiosyncratic spikes in either direction.
- **Narrative dependence** — value rests on continued Base-brand attention with no cash flows to support it.
- **Reflexivity** — attention drives price drives attention; the loop accelerates declines just as it fuels rallies.
- **Insider / whale concentration** — despite full circulation, large early wallets can dominate price action.
- **Liquidity / leverage / rug risk** — thinner derivatives presence limits hedging; spot depth can deteriorate during de-risking; on-chain meme pools carry drain/rug exposure.
- **Macro / regime risk** — the snapshot date sat in "extreme fear" (index ~23) within an established bear market, an unfavorable regime for memes.

Speculative asset; apply strict [[risk-management]] and treat capital as fully at risk.
