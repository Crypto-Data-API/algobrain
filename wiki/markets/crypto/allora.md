# Allora

**Allora** (ticker **ALLO**) is the native token of the Allora Network, a decentralized machine-intelligence network that coordinates many specialized AI models to produce continuously self-improving inferences (predictions) for applications, agents, and DeFi systems. The protocol's token contract is deployed on [[ethereum|Ethereum]], with bridged representations on [[base|Base]] and BNB Chain, and ALLO sits within the broader [[artificial-intelligence|crypto-AI]] / [[depin|decentralized-infrastructure]] narrative alongside peers like [[bittensor|Bittensor]].

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Allora is a **decentralized intelligence network**. Rather than a single model, it runs a marketplace of "workers" (model operators) whose forecasts are weighted and combined by a "reputer/consumer" mechanism that rewards models for being right *and* for being differentiated — a self-improving ensemble. Target consumers are on-chain applications that need a live signal: price/volatility forecasts for DeFi, risk inputs, and inputs for autonomous [[ai-agent-tokens|AI agents]].

Category-wise ALLO clusters with the **crypto + AI** cohort ([[artificial-intelligence]], [[decentralized-ai]]) and the [[depin|DePIN]]-adjacent "decentralized inference" sub-narrative. Its closest conceptual competitor is [[bittensor|Bittensor]], which also pays a network of model operators; Allora differentiates on the specific incentive design around forecast accuracy and context-awareness, and on producing **live, consumable inferences** (price/volatility forecasts) rather than generic subnet outputs. A natural consumer base is autonomous [[ai-agents|AI agents]] that need a continuously updated, trust-minimized signal.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

ALLO is an **early-stage infrastructure bet** priced largely on narrative beta. There is no mature fee base to anchor a fundamental valuation; the asset trades as a call option on "decentralized inference becomes real demand." The two numbers that dominate the thesis: (1) **MC/FDV ~0.30** — a persistent structural overhang as ~765M tokens unlock, which caps upside unless demand growth outruns emissions; and (2) **real consumer pull** — paid forecast consumption by DeFi protocols and agents, which is the only thing that converts the network from speculation to cash-flow infrastructure. Bull case: Allora becomes the go-to inference oracle for on-chain AI. Bear case: emissions dilute a network with thin real usage in a hostile macro. Framing only, not advice.

## Risks

- **Heavy dilution ahead.** Circulating supply is only ~30% of max; the remaining ~765M ALLO will release over time, a persistent structural headwind. See [[token-unlocks]].
- **Early / speculative AI name.** Allora is pre-mass-adoption infrastructure. The thesis depends on real demand for decentralized inference, which is unproven at scale.
- **Narrative beta.** Price is dominated by the broad [[ai-narrative-arc|AI-narrative cycle]] and overall crypto risk appetite — in an established [[bear-market|bear market]] (F&G 23) these names sell off hard regardless of fundamentals.
- **Liquidity & venue concentration.** Most depth sits on a few CEX pairs; large orders move the book and exit liquidity can vanish in a risk-off flush.
- **Competition.** [[bittensor|Bittensor]] and other decentralized-AI networks compete for the same developer and capital mindshare.
