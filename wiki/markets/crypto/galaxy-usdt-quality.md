## Overview

Galaxy USDT Quality (gUSDTq) is an ERC-20 token deployed on the Ethereum blockchain, representing a reward-bearing position in a USDT lending vault built on Morpho Blue. Morpho Blue is a permissionless lending protocol that enables the creation of isolated, overcollateralized credit markets. The gUSDTq token is curated by Galaxy, a global digital asset firm, and accrues value as deposited USDT earns interest through Galaxy's curated lending market allocations on Morpho.
