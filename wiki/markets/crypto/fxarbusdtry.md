## Overview

FXArbUSDTRY is a tokenized market-making fund designed to provide investors with delta-neutral trading exposure through algorithmic strategies. Focused on arbitrage opportunities in the Turkish crypto market — particularly USD/TRY FX spread arbitrage — FXArbUSDTRY leverages advanced trading models to ensure 24/7 liquidity and consistent returns with minimized risk. The fund capitalizes on inefficiencies between USD stablecoins (such as USDT) and USD/TRY FX rates, offering institutional-grade investment access with full transparency and security.
