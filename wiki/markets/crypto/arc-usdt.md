# ARC/USDT (disambiguation)

**This page is a disambiguation note.** **ARC/USDT** is a crypto trading pair — the **ARC altcoin** quoted against the [[tether-limited|USDT]] stablecoin, traded on venues such as Binance Futures. It is **unrelated** to the [[arc-strategy|ARC (Area-Range-Candle) trading method]] and to the [[architects-ai|Architects AI / ARC-AI]] strategy vendor. The three-letter overlap ("ARC") is purely coincidental and is a common source of confusion.

## The ARC/USDT crypto pair

ARC/USDT denotes a perpetual/spot market for the ARC crypto asset priced in USDT. Exchange-authored commentary (e.g., a Binance Square post dated 2025-08-06) has discussed ARC/USDT trade ideas in conventional momentum-and-indicator language — for example, a ~19% intraday surge with RSI, MACD, and Parabolic SAR readings, and explicit entry, target, and stop levels on a 15-minute to 1-hour timeframe (Source: gap-finder Perplexity research 2026-06-19).

Although such commentary superficially resembles the [[arc-strategy|ARC method's]] treatment of range and momentum, it is ordinary technical-analysis content about a token that merely shares the "ARC" ticker — not an application of the Area-Range-Candle framework. The wiki does not maintain detailed coverage of the ARC token's fundamentals; this page exists only to prevent confusion.
