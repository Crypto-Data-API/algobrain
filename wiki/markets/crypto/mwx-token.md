## Overview

MWX Token (MWXT) powers MWX, The First Global Decentralized Agentic AI Marketplace for Ready-To-Use Business Solutions. Built to help businesses cut costs and increase efficiency, MWX delivers 25× faster output at a fraction of the price — with no scattered tools, no complex integration, and just one powerful suite.

MWXT is the native utility token used for: * Discounts – Pay marketplace fees in MWXT to receive instant discounts. * Burn – 20% of every commission is automatically burned at transaction time, reducing supply. * Buy-Backs – Quarterly profit buy-backs create additional deflationary pressure. * Stake – Stake MWXT to unlock higher API limits and receive fee rebates. * Regulation – Participate in governance by voting on treasury allocations and future listings via the MWX DAO. * Affiliate &amp; Reward – Earn MWXT through affiliate referrals, active community participation, and builder grants that drive ecosystem growth.
