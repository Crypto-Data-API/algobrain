## Overview

Striker Token (STKRI) is a TRC-20 token built on the TRON blockchain, designed to provide a secure and transparent way to manage digital transactions. The project focuses on simplifying blockchain-based payments and record-keeping, offering real-time transaction visibility, tamper-proof records, and advanced security measures. Striker Token aims to empower individuals and businesses with reliable tools for financial management while maintaining accessibility and ease of use for all users.
