## Overview

Neural ($NEURAL). Build Worlds. Own Them.

Neural is the infrastructure layer for games and virtual worlds. $NEURAL powers the full stack: the SPARQ game engine, the Neural Launchpad, and the Trinity revenue model that flows ecosystem activity back to holders.

Game development used to require years of specialized training. Neural is changing that. Building for the 100%, not the 1% of developers with studios behind them.

SPARQ Engine
Game engine built from scratch in C++. Real-time multiplayer editing, cross-platform deployment across PC, web, and mobile, and a 1000+ asset library. Anyone can build and ship worlds without a studio. 

Neural Launchpad
Launchpad for game and world tokens built on SPARQ, powered by Uniswap V4 hooks. Permissionless bonding curves with contract-level guardrails, pro-grade presales, up to 85% creator fee share, and automated $NEURAL buybacks from every graduated token. No-rug by default: immutable settings, burned LP, milestone-based vesting.

Trinity Revenue Model
Every transaction flows back to the ecosystem.
30% burn. Permanent supply reduction from real revenue.
35% staker yield. Real yield, not emissions.
35% operations and treasury.

Ecosystem
SPARQ Engine, Neural Launchpad, Staking 2.0 with real yield from Launchpad revenue, and the $NEURAL Tokenization Vehicle for creators who want to build directly on $NEURAL with a flat 70/30 split.
