# DeXe

**DeXe** (DEXE) is a DAO-infrastructure protocol: a no-code "DAO Studio" built on 60+ modular smart contracts that lets teams deploy and govern DAOs with advanced mechanics (delegation, expert sub-DAOs, validator layers) on Ethereum and BNB Chain. For traders, DEXE was one of the standout momentum names of 2026 — up roughly **+363% YTD by 2026-05-20** (BeInCrypto ranked it the second-best-performing investable asset of the year to that date), riding a [[dao|DAO]]-governance narrative revival.

## Overview

DeXe launched in 2020 as a decentralized social-trading platform and pivoted into **DAO infrastructure**. DeXe Protocol provides an infrastructure layer that lets anyone create and manage a DAO without writing code, via the **DeXe DAO Studio** (dexe.io), built from 60+ modular smart contracts. Its governance design targets the known failure modes of token voting — whale dominance and sub-10% participation — with delegation, expert sub-DAOs and validator layers. (Sources: Phemex Academy; Crypto News Navigator, 2026.)

### DEXE token utility (original design, preserved from earlier draft)

1. Programmed redemption and burning of tokens funded by a percentage of traders'/protocol compensation.
2. Control over the DeXe treasury; farming rewards from social-trading/ecosystem activity.
3. Governance voting: burn-schedule parameters, farming reward accrual rates, and burn-date/reward calculations.
4. Insurance staking for users' deposits.

## Valuation Framing (Qualitative)

- **MC/FDV ~0.48** — about half of supply is non-circulating; the burn mechanic must outpace treasury/team distribution for the structure to be net-deflationary.
- **Momentum, not fundamentals** — DEXE's 2026 move was a narrative/momentum re-rating (DAO-governance revival), not a fundamental cash-flow re-rate; it carries the full retrace risk of a single-narrative momentum trade (-17% in the snapshot week).
- **TVL anchor** — the ~$1.7B TVL across DeXe-built DAOs is the most concrete adoption metric, but TVL-to-mcap is a weak valuation tether for an infra/tooling token.
- **Reflexive burn kicker** — protocol activity → buyback/burn → supply narrative; reflexive on the way up *and* down, since burns scale with fees and fees scale with activity.

This is framing, not a price target; the wiki holds no fair-value model for DEXE.
