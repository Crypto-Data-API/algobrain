## Overview

Dual is the tokenization engine for real world assets.

Build programmable tokens for anything, from currencies and loyalty points to real estate, treasuries, and physical products. Production-ready enterprise-grade infrastructure that scales from simple assets to complex use cases.

DUAL is the utility token that powers the ecosystem. Businesses, creatives, and developers fund their projects by depositing $DUAL or using USD, which is converted into $DUAL via the open market.
