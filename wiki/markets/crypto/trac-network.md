## Overview

Trac Network is a groundbreaking local-first Layer 1 and true peer-to-peer Crypto, enabling business &amp; consumer-grade applications without compromises. It is fast (1-2s finality per TX)  and extendible (apps).

- Validators verify transactions and earn TNK as rewards
- App deployers earn TNK for every transaction on-top

Our goal is to empower content creators, developers, and data providers with the tools to manage and monetize their data assets effectively.
