## Overview

$CWU is the native token of Commonwealth Union, a blockchain-powered economic network connecting 56 nations, 2.6 billion people, and a $19 trillion economy. Built on Solana, CWU enables seamless participation across a distributed ecosystem of media, commerce, and financial infrastructure spanning Commonwealth markets. 

The token powers cross-border settlement, access to network services, and coordination among 150 million SMEs and 100 million diaspora professionals. CWU tokenizes real-world economic opportunity by providing a digital layer for transaction settlement, governance participation, and access to planned verticals including education, visa services, and hospitality. 

Designed to facilitate institutional-grade cross-border commerce while maintaining accessibility for individual participants, CWU represents the infrastructure layer for a globally interconnected economy grounded in Commonwealth cooperation and real-world utility.
