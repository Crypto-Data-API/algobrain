## Overview

Wise Monkey ($MONKY), a memecoin inspired by the globally recognized “Three Wise Monkeys” proverb: See No Evil, Hear No Evil, Speak No Evil. Wise Monkey is supported by Floki and ApeCoin, building on BNBchain. Binance, OKX, Kucoin, Gate support its airdrop with 2.3M+ airdrop recipients.  $MONKY embodies the wisdom and collective power of the community. 

The “Three Wise Monkeys” is widely regarded as one of the earliest examples of a meme, a cultural symbol that has transcended centuries to convey universal values of “See no evil, hear no evil, speak no evil.” Its simplicity and visual clarity have made it a cultural touchstone, evolving from ancient Japanese carvings to modern adaptations in global media.
