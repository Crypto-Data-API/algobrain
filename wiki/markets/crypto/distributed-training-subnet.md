# Distributed Training (SN38)

**Distributed Training** (SN38) is a Bittensor subnet focused on coordinating large-scale distributed model training across miner nodes. Complementary to the other training-focused subnets (SN3 [[templar|Templar]], SN9 [[iota-2|IOTA]], SN29 [[coldint|Coldint]]) with a different specific flavor of training coordination.

## How It Works

Miners participate in a shared training run, contributing compute and receiving gradients or partial updates. Validators verify that miners are doing genuine work (not just returning noise) and score contribution quality.

## Trading Relevance

Training-infrastructure exposure. Alpha-38 competes with the other training subnets for emission share in the broader "decentralized training is real" narrative bucket.
