# BitMind

**BitMind** (SN34) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#806** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** deepfake and synthetic-media detection. Miners serve classifiers that identify AI-generated images, video, and audio; validators score detection accuracy on held-out sets.
- **Team:** BitMind.
- **Trading relevance:** infrastructure for trust/safety layers. Relevant as a category as regulatory pressure on AI-generated content grows, but not directly tradable as a signal.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-34.
