# Official FO

**Official FO** (FO) is a [[solana|Solana]] memecoin launched via fo.meme that briefly reached the top 150 by market cap in early 2026 before collapsing roughly 90% in market capitalization by June 2026. It is a cautionary example of mid-cap memecoin reflexivity: thin DEX-only liquidity, opaque provenance, and a large supply/valuation revision.

> **Status note (2026-06-10):** This page remains `draft`. The token's origin story, team, and the reason for the dramatic rank collapse (≈#141 → ≈#706 between April and June 2026) could not be verified from credible sources. CoinGecko's listed circulating supply was also revised from ~997M (April 2026) to ~129M (June 2026), which mechanically explains most of the market-cap drop — but no official explanation was found. Treat all fundamentals here as low-confidence.

## Overview

Official FO is categorized by CoinGecko as Solana Ecosystem / Meme / Solana Meme. Public information about the team and launch is minimal; the project communicates via fo.meme, Twitter (@Fo_Coin) and a large Telegram group (~64,500 members at April 2026).

Timeline of what is verifiable:
- **2025-09-28** — All-time high $0.83.
- **2026-03-27** — Local low $0.2627.
- **April → June 2026** — Listed market cap fell from ~$268M to ~$25M, driven primarily by CoinGecko's circulating-supply revision (997M → 129M) plus a ~28% price decline to ~$0.19. The MC/FDV ratio went from a reported 1.00 to ~0.13, implying ~87% of supply is now considered non-circulating.
- **June 2026** — Rugcheck.xyz flags that the contract creator retains authority to change token metadata, modify fees, and restrict minting — a material holder risk.

## Trading Relevance

- **Venues**: DEX-only; primary liquidity is the FO/USDT pool on Meteora (Solana), ~$170K daily volume as of June 2026. No notable CEX or perp listings found. Effectively untradeable in size.
- **Lessons for the memecoin basket** ([[narrative-trading]]): reported market-cap rank is only as good as the supply data behind it — a supply restatement can vaporize a "top-150" classification overnight; and tokens whose creators retain mint/metadata authority carry unhedgeable contract risk.
- **Catalysts**: none identified.
