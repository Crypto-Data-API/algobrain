# Moonbirds

**Moonbirds** is one of the best-known profile-picture (**PFP**) [[nft]] collections — 10,000 pixel-art owl avatars originally minted on Ethereum in April 2022 by **Proof** (the collective behind Kevin Rose's PROOF Collective), and later acquired by **Yuga Labs** (the team behind Bored Ape Yacht Club). This page tracks the **BIRB** token, a [[solana]]-ecosystem token associated with the Moonbirds brand and distributed in part via airdrop to NFT holders — i.e., it is a *token* tied to the Moonbirds IP, distinct from the underlying ERC-721 NFT collection itself. CoinGecko classifies BIRB as an NFT-linked, Solana-ecosystem, meme, and "Airdropped Tokens by NFT Projects" asset, and it ranks **#816** by market capitalization.

## Overview

The Moonbirds **NFT collection** launched in April 2022 to one of the largest opening weeks in PFP history. Its two signature features were **nesting** (a non-custodial staking mechanic where holders "nest" their owls to accrue benefits without transferring custody) and a tiered-utility model gating access to the PROOF community. The collection — and the affiliated *Oddities* derivative set — became a blue-chip name on Ethereum before **Yuga Labs** acquired the Moonbirds and PROOF IP in early 2024, folding it into its broader NFT portfolio.

### What the BIRB token is

The **BIRB** token tracked on this page is a separate, fungible token associated with the Moonbirds brand, living in the [[solana]] ecosystem and partly distributed by airdrop to NFT-project participants. It is best understood as a **brand/community token** riding the Moonbirds name rather than a claim on the NFT collection's royalties or treasury. Its value drivers are largely speculative and meme-driven: attention on the Moonbirds / Yuga brand, [[solana]] ecosystem sentiment, and airdrop-farming dynamics. Holders of BIRB do not, by virtue of the token, own the underlying owl NFTs.

> **Subject note:** Do not conflate the BIRB token with the Moonbirds ERC-721 NFTs. The NFTs trade on NFT marketplaces (priced in ETH), whereas BIRB is a Solana-based fungible token with its own market cap and price shown above.

## Architecture / how it works

There are two distinct assets under the Moonbirds umbrella, and the value linkage between them is purely brand-based, not contractual:

**1. The Moonbirds NFT collection (the underlying IP).** A 10,000-piece ERC-721 PFP set on [[ethereum|Ethereum]], created by **PROOF** (Kevin Rose's collective). Its defining mechanic was **nesting** — a non-custodial "staking" model where holders mark an owl as nested to accrue status/benefits without transferring custody, a design that reduced sell-side liquidity and signaled long-term holding. A tiered-utility model gated access to the PROOF community, and the *Oddities* derivative set extended the ecosystem. **Yuga Labs** (BAYC) acquired the Moonbirds and PROOF IP in early 2024.

**2. The BIRB token (this page).** A [[solana|Solana]] SPL token (contract `G7vQWurMkMMm2dU3iZpXYFTHT9Biio4F4gZCrwFpKNwG`), partly airdropped to NFT-project participants. BIRB is a fungible brand/community token; it confers **no ownership of the owls, no nesting rights, and no claim on Yuga's treasury or royalties**. Its value is reflexive — driven by Moonbirds/Yuga brand attention, Solana-meme sentiment, and airdrop-farming flows.

The critical architecture point for traders: the two assets can decouple completely. The NFT floor (in ETH on NFT marketplaces) and the BIRB price (in USD on Solana DEXs/CEXs) respond to overlapping but not identical demand, and BIRB carries an additional ~71% unlock overhang (see Tokenomics) that the fixed-supply NFT collection does not.

### Value accrual / utility

BIRB has no cash-flow rights and no claim on the NFT collection. The only value-accrual channels are reflexive: (1) **brand attention** on Moonbirds/Yuga; (2) **Solana-meme rotation** when SOL-ecosystem speculation is hot; and (3) **airdrop-farming loops** that temporarily concentrate demand. With only ~29% of the 1B max supply circulating, future unlocks are a persistent headwind that must be absorbed by fresh demand — a structurally bearish setup absent a strong brand catalyst.

## How & where it trades

**The BIRB token** trades on Solana DEXs (native SPL liquidity) and a set of centralized venues: Upbit (BIRB/KRW — Korean retail exposure), Bitget (BIRB/USDT), KuCoin (BIRB/USDT), and Crypto.com Exchange (BIRB/USD), with Binance Alpha Spotlight attention. Reported 24h volume (~$5.76M at the earlier snapshot) against a sub-$25M cap is thin and concentrated; budget for [[slippage]] and gap risk on size. No verified perpetual-futures market is recorded, so directional exposure is effectively spot-only.

**The Moonbirds NFTs** (a separate asset) trade on Ethereum NFT marketplaces — [[opensea|OpenSea]], Blur, and Magic Eden — priced in ETH. Their floor responds to PFP/blue-chip NFT demand and Yuga brand stewardship rather than to the BIRB token's price.

## Trading playbook

BIRB is a thin, reflexive NFT-linked Solana meme; in the current Extreme-Fear/Bottoming tape it is a low-conviction speculative name:

- **Don't buy the NFT thesis through BIRB:** the token gives no exposure to the owls or Yuga treasury. If the thesis is Moonbirds brand recovery, the cleaner expression is the NFTs themselves on [[opensea|OpenSea]]/Blur/Magic Eden, not BIRB.
- **Respect the unlock overhang:** ~71% of supply is uncirculated; rallies into unlock events tend to fade. Discount upside targets accordingly.
- **Liquidity discipline:** sub-$25M cap, thin volume — use limits, scale orders, and assume wide spreads. No perp means no cheap hedge.
- **Catalyst, not carry:** trade around discrete Yuga/Moonbirds brand or Solana-meme rotations rather than holding through the bear tape.

This is not investment advice; BIRB is a speculative brand/meme token.

## Risks

- **Meme / brand-token dynamics** — BIRB derives value from association with the Moonbirds brand, not from cash flows or NFT ownership; price is highly sentiment-driven and prone to sharp reversals (note the >70% drawdown from its 2026 ATH).
- **Subject-confusion risk for buyers** — investors may mistakenly believe BIRB confers rights to the Moonbirds NFTs or Yuga treasury; it does not.
- **Airdrop-farming overhang** — tokens distributed via airdrop to NFT projects often face concentrated early selling as recipients monetize "free" allocations.
- **High FDV vs. circulating** — only ~285M of a 1B max supply circulates (MC/FDV ≈ 0.29), leaving substantial future unlock / dilution overhang.
- **Low liquidity** — a sub-$25M-cap meme/brand token is thinly traded; large orders move price materially. See [[liquidity]] and [[slippage]].
- **Brand / IP risk** — value is hostage to how Yuga Labs manages (or neglects) the Moonbirds IP; any brand fatigue or strategic pivot can drag the token.
