## Overview

Shelter of Exiles (SoE) is a Web3 gaming ecosystem that combines AI-driven mechanics, dynamic NFTs (dNFTs), and a player-driven economy.

The game allows players to own, trade, and interact with evolving in-game assets.
AI Agents assist in optimizing certain mechanics, while $SOEX serves as the native utility token, used for transactions within the ecosystem.

SoE continues to expand, introducing new mechanics, NFT integrations, and AI-powered features to enhance long-term engagement and sustainability.
