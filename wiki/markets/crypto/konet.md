# KONET

**KONET** (KONET) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#1258** by market capitalization. Mainnet upgrade adopts Proof of Stake for enhanced efficiency and security, featuring advanced mechanisms such as EIP-1559 for fee burning and PoSDAO for decentralized governance, fostering stable participation in staking for network contributors.
