# Onyxcoin

**Onyxcoin** (ticker **XCN**) is the native and governance token of the Onyx protocol (the rebranded "Chain" project), an infrastructure effort building multi-asset, scalable, cryptographically secure ledgers. XCN is native to the Onyx XCN Ledger and Onyx Goliath blockchains and is also issued as an [[ethereum]] ERC-20 used for staking and governance. The project is backed by Pantera Capital and is one of the larger-cap names in this set, sitting in the top ~200 by market capitalization.

## Architecture & Technology

Onyx descends from the **"Chain" protocol** (Chain.com), an enterprise-grade ledger technology that originally targeted banks and financial institutions building permissioned, multi-asset ledgers. The modern Onyx incarnation re-positions that lineage toward public, token-incentivised infrastructure.

**Two chains, one token.** XCN is the native and governance asset of two related ledgers:
- **Onyx XCN Ledger** — a ledger optimised for issuing and settling many asset types with cryptographic security.
- **Onyx Goliath** — positioned as a higher-throughput chain for financial applications.

**ERC-20 governance/staking layer.** Crucially, the asset most traders hold is the **ERC-20 on [[ethereum]]**, used for staking and governance, with bridged representations on **Base** and **BNB Chain**. The chain self-describes as [[layer-1|Layer 1]] / Smart Contract Platform infrastructure using a [[proof-of-stake]]-style staking model. This multi-chain footprint gives XCN broader exchange and DeFi reach than most peers in this cohort, at the cost of liquidity fragmentation across venues.

The thesis is **financial-grade ledger infrastructure**: prioritise security, scalability, and usability for financial applications, with XCN as the unified staking/governance unit across the stack. Product traction beyond the token is hard to verify from market data alone — a recurring critique of long-lived "enterprise ledger" projects.

### Value Accrual & Governance

- **Staking** — XCN is staked (as an ERC-20) to earn emissions and participate in network security; staking rewards add to issuance over time and are part of why the max supply (~68.89B) exceeds current total/circulating supply.
- **Governance** — XCN holders direct protocol governance; the token is the unit of voting weight across the Onyx ecosystem.
- **Demand driver** — value ultimately depends on adoption of the Onyx ledgers for real asset issuance/settlement. With venture backing from **Pantera Capital** and a multi-chain presence, XCN has more distribution than peers, but the dilution path (MC/FDV 0.72) is a structural counterweight.
