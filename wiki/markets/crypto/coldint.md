# Coldint

**Coldint** (SN29) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#849** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** distributed LLM fine-tuning. Miners run specialized fine-tuning jobs on base models; validators score the resulting model's performance on held-out benchmarks.
- **Team:** Coldint.
- **Trading relevance:** niche training-subnet exposure; smaller than SN3 [[templar|Templar]] and SN9 [[iota-2|IOTA]].
- **Alpha token:** alpha-29.
