## Overview

What is iMe Lab?
iMe is an ecosystem with the Telegram-based messenger, Crypto Wallet, and DeFi tools and its native utility token LIME. The app allows sending crypto in a few clicks, making crypto transactions fast, secure and hassle-free.
 
Any user can send cryptocurrencies they hold in their iMe wallet to any of their contacts via their Telegram handles, in a matter of seconds. With robust DeFi capabilities integrated into the messenger (Uniswap, BNB Chain, Ethereum, PancakeSwap, Polygon), iMe is on a mission to make DeFi more accessible to a wider population.
 
iMe is the first one to add Binance functionality into the messaging ecosystem to swap cryptocurrency on Binance, make deposits to Binance accounts from iMe Wallet, send request payments and make zero-fees transactions, and many more. iMe facilitates mass adoption of crypto!

LIME token by iMe Lab

LIME token (ERC20, BEP20, Polygon) is a utility token of the iMe Lab project offering a wide range of utilities that are linked to monetization facilities and paid functionality operation in iMe:
 
Creation of NFT-Avatars and NFT-Neurobots;
NFT publication in the Marketplace;
NFT promotion in the Marketplace;
Publication of groups, channels, and chat-bots in the Catalog;
Promotion in the Catalog;
CryptoBoxes creation;
Gas payment in blockchain networks in LIME tokens;
DAO-Votings creation.
LIME Donation feature in Telegram groups and channels.
 
And other opportunities:
 
Access to the functionalities of partner projects in iMe
DAO principle utility. Lime token holders will have a say in the development of the ecosystem and the right to suggest or influence the decision-making process depending on their stake.
Staking opportunities. This feature will allow users to stake LIME and earn a certain APR percentage according to their stake part and period.
Earning rewards in LIME tokens for the execution of tasks on the iMe tester platform that will be implemented in the app.
