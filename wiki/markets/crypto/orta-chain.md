## Overview

Orta Chain is a blockchain-based financial and accounting infrastructure designed to support real-world business operations rather than consumer-facing applications. The project provides a crypto-native framework that allows platforms and enterprises to integrate blockchain technology into existing off-chain workflows, including digital asset management, peer-to-peer value transfers, subscription and payment processes, and crypto-related accounting records. Orta Chain is structured as an underlying service layer that enables transparent, auditable, and automated handling of blockchain-based financial activity while remaining compatible with traditional business systems. The project has completed its Initial Vestra Offering (IVO) through the Brolyz launchpad, demonstrating early market validation and platform adoption. Orta Chain’s focus is on delivering infrastructure that supports practical business use cases and scalable integration, rather than speculative or consumer-oriented blockchain functionality.
