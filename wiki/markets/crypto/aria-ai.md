## Overview

AriaAI is a next-generation game development and publishing experiment inspired by Disney-style immersive worlds and AI technology, designed with its own IP related gameplay at its core. It represents a major leap forward in bringing Web2-quality game design and publishing standards into the Web3 era.

 With the integration of AI, ARIA is evolving into a living, adaptive game world — featuring intelligent NPCs, personalized storytelling, AI-generated content, and dynamic gameplay that grows with its players.
