# Official Trump

**Official Trump** (TRUMP) is a [[solana|Solana]]-based [[meme-coin|memecoin]] launched on 2025-01-17, days before Donald Trump's second inauguration, and officially affiliated with Trump-linked entities (CIC Digital LLC and Fight Fight Fight LLC, which hold 80% of supply). It is the defining asset of the **PolitiFi** sector — a case study in event-driven memecoin mania, insider-heavy supply, and multi-year unlock overhang. From a $73.43 all-time high in January 2025 it has drawn down more than 97%, making it one of the most-cited examples of retail value destruction of the 2025 cycle.

## Overview

TRUMP is a politics-themed digital coin built on the Solana blockchain that uses the brand of Donald Trump to foster a community-driven ecosystem. As a community-focused asset, it is designed to bridge political support with web ownership, operating within the PolitiFi sector to blend political movements with decentralized finance tools. The project's main value proposition lies in its official ties to Donald Trump's media and business brands, which allows it to provide unique physical utility such as invitations to gala dinners and meetings at Mar-a-Lago.

The token serves as the primary currency for the officially licensed Trump Billionaires Club mobile and web game, where it is used for in-game purchases and unlocking features. By leveraging the Solana blockchain, the project ensures that these in-game actions and token transfers remain fast and cost-effective. The ecosystem remains stable through a programmatic release schedule that introduces new tokens slowly to prevent supply shocks, alongside the use of specialized market makers to maintain consistent liquidity for traders.

Holders can also participate in liquidity sharing by providing their tokens to automated systems to facilitate trading for others, often earning rewards or prizes in return. Beyond digital utility, holding a specific amount of the token is a requirement for attending exclusive real-world events, including VIP parties at Mar-a-Lago. The project is managed by the Trump Meme Team and is linked with Trump Media.

## Protocol & Project — Political Memecoin with Insider Supply

TRUMP is a **political memecoin (PolitiFi)** — there is no protocol, no novel technology, and no cash-flow business; it is an SPL token on [[solana|Solana]] whose entire value proposition is the Trump brand plus episodic real-world perks. The defining structural facts are about *ownership and supply*, not technology:

- **Issuers** — CIC Digital LLC and Fight Fight Fight LLC, Trump-affiliated entities, control **80% of the 1B supply**. This is one of the most concentrated insider distributions of any top-150 token.
- **Perk machine** — value is engineered through *announced, dated events*: the May 2025 "top-220 holders gala dinner," Mar-a-Lago VIP access, and the "Trump Billionaires Club" web/mobile game where TRUMP is the in-game currency. These create buying contests and tradeable catalysts.
- **Vesting** — insider allocations vest over ~3 years (through ~2028) on a programmatic release schedule, the issuers' stated rationale being to "prevent supply shocks." In practice the tranches are a persistent structural sell-flow.
- **Affiliations** — linked to Trump Media; the project is managed by the "Trump Meme Team." Conflict-of-interest criticism (sitting president profiting from a token marketed to supporters) is a recurring headline risk.

The honest framing: TRUMP is a *case study* in insider-heavy, event-driven memecoin design — high attention and liquidity, but with a built-in multi-year dilution headwind that structurally favors fading rallies.

- **Spot venues** — Binance (TRUMP/USDT), Kraken, Upbit (TRUMP/KRW), Bitget, KuCoin, Crypto.com; Orca on Solana. ~$59.1M 24h volume (2026-06-20) — deep for a sub-$500M token, reflecting heavy speculative interest and broad CEX access.
- **Perpetuals** — **TRUMP-PERP on [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]]** and Binance/Bybit/OKX futures. TRUMP is one of the few sub-$1B tokens with enough perp depth to absorb large short positions — important because the structural unlock overhang makes it a popular *short*. As a [[perpetual-futures|perp]], funding swings violently: persistently *negative* funding (shorts crowding the unlock thesis) is common, which itself creates short-squeeze fuel on any positive headline (pardons, crypto policy).
- **Funding/short-squeeze dynamic** — when negative funding gets extreme, a Trump-administration headline can trigger a sharp squeeze; conversely, positive-funding euphoria around perk announcements (gala dinners) historically fades. The asymmetry: violent up-squeezes vs grinding unlock-driven downside.
- **Basis trade** — short perp / long spot is structurally unattractive here because spot carries the unlock overhang; most flow is directional or funding-driven.

## Valuation Framework (qualitative)

TRUMP is a pure narrative/attention asset with a *dominant dilution overlay*:

1. **Attention / political beta** — the core driver: Trump news cycles, administration crypto policy, and perk announcements move the token. It is the PolitiFi bellwether.
2. **Liquidity** — top-tier for its size; the liquid proxy for "Trump trade" risk appetite in crypto.
3. **Dilution discount (the decisive factor)** — MC/FDV ≈ 0.24 with ~76% locked and vesting to ~2028 means the *float* market cap understates true supply by ~4x. Any rally must absorb continuous ~900k/day unlocks. This structurally caps upside and is why the page treats fading rallies as the base case.
4. **Insider-concentration risk** — 80% issuer-held supply is a permanent overhang and conflict-of-interest headline magnet.

Net: "high-attention political memecoin with violent two-way headline risk, structurally weighed down by extreme insider concentration and a multi-year unlock schedule — the textbook case of FDV >> market cap dilution risk."
