## Overview

Garden is the fastest Bitcoin bridge, enabling cross-chain Bitcoin swaps in as little as 30 seconds. It operates on an intents-based architecture with trustless settlements, ensuring zero custody risk for users. Using peer-to-peer design and Hashed Timelock Contracts (HTLCs), Garden eliminates the need for intermediaries. Supporting multiple chains, it has already facilitated over $1 billion in swaps, offering a secure and efficient solution for decentralized interoperability.
