## Overview

The mission of Nest is to build a global, permissionless, on-chain economy where anyone can earn from real-world yields. As the flagship staking protocol of the Plume Network RWAfi ecosystem, Nest offers a fully permissionless product enabling users to stake stablecoins and access institutional-grade APYs derived from real-world assets. These vaults are backed by regulated originators and custodians, audited smart contracts, and transparent on-chain mechanisms, bringing previously inaccessible yields into the DeFi ecosystem.
