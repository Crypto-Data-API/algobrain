# BetTensor (SN30)

**BetTensor** (SN30) is a Bittensor subnet for AI-powered prediction markets and sports betting. Miners submit calibrated probability forecasts on sports, political, and market events; validators score miners on realized outcomes. The team layers a consumer-facing betting / prediction-market product on top of the subnet's forecasts.

## How It Works

The subnet operates a public market-making layer where miner forecasts are aggregated into market odds. Users can participate in markets against these odds. Validator scoring uses standard proper scoring rules (Brier, log-loss). Top miners earn emission share; losing miners dilute out.

## Trading Relevance

**Directly tradable** -- consume the SN30 consensus forecast as input to external sports-betting or prediction-market positions. Overlaps with SN6 [[infinite-games-subnet|Infinite Games]] (broader prediction markets), SN41 [[sportstensor]] (sports-specific data), and SN44 [[score]] (sports scoring / vision). Each subnet has a different reward function, so outputs are not perfectly redundant.
