## Overview

TheEXCCcoin was created in 2017 to provide finance in support of an idea to build a decentralised exchange platform. The platform was developed to offer services to semi-professional cryptocurrency traders. The Exchange Coin is one of the rare cryptocurrencies with a value reflected in the features of real products. It is similar to the traditional market and stock exchange setting.
