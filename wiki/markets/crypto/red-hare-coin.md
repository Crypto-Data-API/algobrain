## Overview

About Red Hare (Red Hare)Red Hare is an on chain digital asset issued based on the FLAP (Fair Launch Protocol) butterfly platform mechanism and deployed on BNB Chain. The project is designed around the principles of public offering, transparency mechanism, and market-oriented operation, and the relevant processes are executed on the chain and verifiable.This project does not have pre-sales, private placements, or team shares, nor does it have any form of token distribution arrangement. The token is open to the market through an on chain liquidity pool from its issuance, and all participants must obtain it through open market transactions. There is no mechanism for priority participation or differentiated acquisition.Red Hare adopts a market-oriented operation mode, without setting price ranges or distribution ratios. The token price and liquidity are formed by market supply and demand relationships.
