# Monad

**Monad** (ticker **MON**) is a high-performance, fully EVM-compatible [[layer-1]] blockchain built around **parallel execution**, targeting 10,000 TPS with sub-second finality. Founded by ex-Jump Trading HFT engineers Keone Hon and James Hunsaker, it raised a $225M Series A from Paradigm in April 2024 (~$3B valuation) — among the largest crypto venture rounds of its cycle — and launched mainnet plus the MON token on **November 24, 2025**. For traders, MON is the flagship "parallel-EVM" launch trade of 2025-26 and a textbook **low-float / high-FDV** structure (≈11% circulating at launch).

## Overview

Monad is a high-performance Layer 1 that processes multiple transactions simultaneously through **optimistic parallel execution**, rather than sequentially like Ethereum. It separates consensus from execution (deferred execution), letting validators agree on transaction ordering first and execute afterwards, and pairs this with **MonadDB**, a custom state database optimized for SSDs. The result targets ~10,000 TPS and sub-second finality on consumer-grade hardware.

Monad offers complete bytecode-level EVM compatibility: applications built for Ethereum run without modification, users connect with MetaMask, and developers retain Ethereum's tooling while gaining throughput. This positions Monad directly against [[solana|Solana]] (performance) and Ethereum L2s (EVM compatibility) simultaneously.

### Launch and 2025-2026 developments

- **November 2025** — Tokenomics revealed Nov 10; **mainnet launched November 24, 2025**, with MON airdropped to claimer wallets the same day. 76,021 unique wallets claimed across five eligibility groups (Monad Community, Onchain Users, Crypto Community, Builders, Crypto Contributors); the airdrop totaled roughly $105M in value.
- **Coinbase public sale** — 7.5% of supply sold via Coinbase's Token Platform at **$0.025 per MON** — a notable revival of the regulated public-sale (ICO-like) model in the US.
- **Post-launch** — MON hit an ATH of ~$0.0488 (Nov 26, 2025) days after launch, then sold off to ~$0.016 (Feb 6, 2026) as airdrop supply and launch hype unwound — a classic post-TGE decay path — before recovering into Q2 2026. Foundation focus for 2026: staking participation, developer funding, and DeFi infrastructure buildout. (Source: CoinDesk, Monad announcements; verified via web search 2026-06-10.)

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

Monad is the highest-quality-funded new-L1 launch of the cycle (~$244M raised, Paradigm-led) and the cleanest liquid proxy for the **parallel-EVM** thesis. The bull case is execution: full EVM bytecode compatibility *plus* genuine throughput could pull both [[ethereum]]-L2 and [[solana]] activity, and a ~$243M circulating cap is modest for a chain of its profile. The bear case is dominated by structure and adoption: **MC/FDV ≈ 0.12** means today's price is set against ~12% of supply, with a multi-year vesting cliff schedule (team + investors ~47% of supply); spot already trades below the $0.025 public-sale price; and the project carries acute **"ghost chain"** risk if app/TVL traction stalls against MegaETH, Sei, and Ethereum's own scaling. A high-beta sentiment bellwether for new-chain risk appetite. Not investment advice.
