## Overview

United States Water Reserve ($USWR) is a satirical, narrative-driven memecoin on Solana that tokenizes the world's most valuable liquid asset: clean water. As AI data centers and tech giants consume billions of gallons of water, $USWR serves as the ultimate cultural receipt for the people. Inspired by the "smart money" quietly acquiring water rights across the globe, $USWR is a community-owned movement. "Control the reserve. Control the future.

" Featuring a fully verified fair launch with a fixed 1 billion supply, mint authority revoked, zero taxes, and 100% burned liquidity, $USWR empowers the community to ride the wave of the AI boom through decentralized meme culture.
