# WhiteBIT Coin

**WhiteBIT Coin** (ticker **WBT**) is the [[exchange-tokens|exchange token]] of WhiteBIT, the Ukrainian-founded CEX that styles itself the largest in Europe (8M+ exchange users; ~35M across the wider "W Group" ecosystem as of 2025–2026). It is an ERC-20 on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] with a [[tron|Tron]] deployment. WBT screens as a top-20 asset by market cap, but traders should treat the rank with caution: float is limited, volume is thin relative to the headline cap, and price discovery happens mostly on WhiteBIT's own venue.

## Overview

What is WhiteBIT Token?

WBT is a utility token of the largest European cryptocurrency exchange, WhiteBIT. The platform was established in 2018 and has already become one of the leading crypto exchanges with 3+ mln users worldwide. WhiteBIT’s goal is to contribute to the mass adoption and popularization of blockchain technologies by implementing the most effective trading and staking tools on the most convenient terms. The most popular and efficient trading orders for spot and margin trading, up to 20x leverage for margin and perpetual Bitcoin futures trading, unique passive income tools, a referral program, and the lowest trading fees on the market are only part of the functionality available on WhiteBIT. Discover the full potential of the platform at the link: https://whitebit.com/.

WBT is a full-fledged product of the whole WhiteBIT ecosystem that is integrated into all WhiteBIT services, making it easier for the users to interact on the platform. WBT is a solid base for dozens of blockchain startups, new projects, and fruitful trading. Moreover, WhiteBIT Token is a reliable asset that can be used for staking on its native platform, which provides numerous perks to its holders and owners. 

Key advantages:

increased referral interest rate (up to 50%);
decreased trading fees; 
free daily ERC-20/ETH withdrawals;
free daily AML checks; 
integration with the current and future products of WhiteBIT and much more.

WBT tokenomics

The token supply is limited to 400 000 000 WBT, with no new tokens to be created in the future. 200M of this amount is treasury tokens that are backing the total amount and will be unlocked within the next three months after the launch. 

Trading competitions, airdrops, bounties, beneficial holding programs, and access to the token from other exchange platforms are part of activities associated with the WBT cryptocurrency available on WhiteBIT.

> *Note (June 2026): the "3+ mln users" figure above is the project's original 2022-era description; WhiteBIT reported 8M+ registered exchange users and ~35M across the W Group ecosystem in 2025–2026 marketing materials.*

## Tokenomics & Supply

| **Fully Diluted Valuation** | ~$20.9B |
| **Market Cap / FDV Ratio** | ~0.30 |

**Deflationary burn model.** WBT supply is capped at **400M tokens with no new issuance**, and is shrunk over time by **weekly burns of 33% of trading fees + 5% of service income** (historically on the order of several million WBT removed per year). This is a stronger structural burn than the discretionary buyback used by [[mx-token|MX]], and closer in spirit to [[binance-coin|BNB]]'s burn program.

**Treasury overhang.** A large block of treasury tokens (originally ~200M, designed to back the supply) sits between the ~118M circulating float and the ~294M total / 400M max. The low ~0.30 MC/FDV ratio reflects this gap; how and when treasury tokens enter circulation is the key dilution variable and a recurring transparency concern.

> *Note: an earlier April-2026 snapshot reported ~213M circulating / ~320M total with MC/FDV ≈ 0.67. The 2026-06-20 CoinGecko figures above (118.24M circulating / 294.14M total) supersede them; treat the lower float as current and verify directly on WhiteBIT/CoinGecko before relying on either.*

## Trading Relevance

- **Narrative basket:** CEX tokens ([[leo-token|LEO]], BNB, OKB, BGB). These trade on exchange volume growth, burn cadence, and listing/expansion headlines rather than crypto beta alone.
- **Liquidity reality check:** ~$38.8M reported daily volume against a ~$6.19B cap (2026-06-20 snapshot) and the bulk of trading on WhiteBIT itself. Until the March 2026 Kraken listing, there was effectively no independent venue. Treat WBT rank as inflated for screening purposes; position sizes that look small vs market cap can still be large vs real float.
- **Catalysts:** further tier-1 listings, burn reports, Nova card volume milestones, European MiCA-era licensing news, and Ukrainian/EU regulatory developments.
- **Risk:** exchange tokens carry issuer concentration risk — WBT value is a claim on WhiteBIT's continued operation (compare FTT in the [[ftx]] collapse).
