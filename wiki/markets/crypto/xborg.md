## Overview

XBorg is building the AI infrastructure for personalised experiences.

Initially focused on the gaming and esports industry, XBorg has recently expanded its focus to include additional verticals, including: Gaming, Events, Sports, Music, Streaming, Film, Fashion and Lifestyle. XBorg now unlocks AI for the consumer brand market via its flagship products:

**FanBase Copilot (NEW)** - An AI agent that automatically creates contextual engagement campaigns. It monitors everything consumer brands and creators do (streams, tournaments, social posts, announcements) and activates personalised engagement across all platforms like Twitch, YouTube, Discord, Web and more.

**FanBase Apps** - Turnkey loyalty platforms for consumer brands and creators. Where fans can earn, engage, and unlock rewards in environments you fully control. This remains our premium offering for organisations with the audience and resources to support dedicated platforms.

Together, these products redefine how consumer brands and creators connect with their audiences, turning the fan economy into something more intelligent, scalable, and human.
