# lium

**lium** (SN51) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#523** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** decentralized GPU compute marketplace — miners rent out GPU capacity to AI workloads; validators verify availability and job completion. Directly competes with SN64 [[chutes|Chutes]] and SN39 [[w-ai-parked|Basilica]] for the compute-infrastructure thesis.
- **Team:** Celium / Lium (originally launched as "Lium"; community often uses "Celium").
- **Trading relevance:** one of the larger alpha market caps on Bittensor. High off-chain revenue visibility drives validator conviction and emission share. Frequently the top-performing alpha in compute-narrative cycles.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-51.
