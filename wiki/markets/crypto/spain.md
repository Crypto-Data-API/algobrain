## Overview

Royal Spanish Football Federation (Spain)
The Spanish national football team have won the UEFA European Championship a record four times, securing victories in 1964, 2008, 2012, and 2024. Spain have also been crowned World Champions once, winning the FIFA® World Cup in 2010.
La Roja, as they are well known for the vibrant red shirts they play with, are celebrated worldwide for their highly technical, possession-based style of play. They consistently remain a top-tier competitor in the FIFA® Men’s football rankings.
