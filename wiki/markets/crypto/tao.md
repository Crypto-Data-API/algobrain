# TAO (Bittensor)

**TAO** (ticker: TAO) is the native token of **Bittensor**, a decentralized, Substrate-based Layer 1 network for artificial intelligence and machine learning. Bittensor creates an open marketplace where AI models compete to provide intelligence, with miners running ML models that are scored and rewarded with TAO tokens by validators across specialized [[bittensor-subnets|subnets]]. It sits at the intersection of crypto and AI -- two of the most prominent investment narratives of the mid-2020s -- and is widely regarded as the leading "AI x crypto" / [[defai]] token.

## How It Works

Bittensor's architecture consists of **subnets** -- independent networks each focused on a specific AI task. Within each subnet:
- **Miners** run machine learning models and serve inference requests
- **Validators** evaluate miner outputs for quality and assign scores
- **TAO emissions** are distributed based on validator scores, incentivizing the best-performing AI models

The subnet model allows the network to scale across many AI verticals simultaneously.

## Risks

- **Narrative concentration** — TAO trades as a leveraged bet on the decentralized-AI thesis; a cooling of the broader AI trade (or a sell-off in AI equities like NVDA) can drag it down disproportionately.
- **Liquidity & volatility** — lower liquidity than top-10 assets means larger slippage and violent moves; perp funding/OI on [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]] can drive liquidation-driven wicks.
- **Tokenomics dilution** — with circulating supply well below the 21M cap, ongoing emissions and the deferred MC/FDV gap create structural sell pressure until emissions taper.
- **Subnet quality / verifiability** — the "Proof of Intelligence" scoring relies on validators judging model output; gaming, collusion, or low-value subnets can dilute the network's real economic output.
- **Macro / regime risk** — as a higher-beta altcoin, TAO is acutely exposed to the prevailing **Established Bear Market** and extreme-fear conditions as of 2026-06-20.

## Overview

Bittensor is an open-source protocol that powers a decentralized, blockchain-based machine learning network. Machine learning models train collaboratively and are rewarded in TAO according to the informational value they offer the collective. TAO also grants external access, allowing users to extract information from the network while tuning its activities to their needs.

Ultimately, BitTensors vision is to create a pure market for artificial intelligence, an incentivized arena in which consumers and producers of this valuable commodity can interact in a trustless, open and transparent context.

Bittensor enables:

-A novel, optimized strategy for the development and distribution of artificial intelligence technology by leveraging the possibilities of a distributed ledger. Specifically, its facilitation of open access/ownership, decentralized governance, and the ability to harness globally-distributed resources of computing power and innovation within an incentivized framework.

-An open-source repository of machine intelligence, accessible to anyone, anywhere, thus creating the conditions for open and permission-less innovation on a global internet scale.

-Distribution of rewards and network ownership to users in direct proportion to the value they have added.
