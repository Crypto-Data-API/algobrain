# affine

**affine** (SN120) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#518** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** reasoning and evaluation benchmark subnet. Miners submit models / reasoning traces; validators score on reasoning benchmarks (math, logic, chain-of-thought). Functions as a subnet-native evaluation layer.
- **Team:** Affine.
- **Trading relevance:** evaluation infrastructure is understated-important in AI — if Affine becomes a canonical on-chain benchmark, alpha-120 has structural demand from anyone building evaluation pipelines.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-120.
