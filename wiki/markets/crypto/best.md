## Overview

Best Wallet is a non-custodial, multi-chain crypto wallet built on Fireblocks MPC-CMP technology. It supports thousands of assets across major networks and integrates a DEX aggregator, presale access portal, multi-wallet management, and cross-chain swaps. The project includes three interconnected products: Best Wallet, Best DEX, and Best Card. Best Wallet enables buying, swapping, storing, and selling digital assets within a mobile-first interface and provides direct access to vetted presales through its Upcoming Tokens feature. The wallet is designed to improve accessibility, security, and interoperability for everyday crypto users. The ecosystem operates on the Ethereum network and is powered by the ERC-20 token $BEST, which provides utilities such as reduced ecosystem fees, access to presales, staking rewards, and participation in governance decisions
