## Overview

NEXST is an AI-driven entertainment infrastructure that tokenizes the full spectrum of fan experiences. It introduces Fan Continuity, where every interaction—attending concerts, engaging with AI, or purchasing merchandise—creates persistent digital rights that accumulate and unlock greater value over time. By bridging traditional entertainment with Web3, NEXST transforms one-off moments into ongoing participation, ownership, and long-term fan engagement within a unified digital ecosystem.
