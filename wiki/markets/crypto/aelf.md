# aelf

**aelf** (ticker **ELF**) is an **AI-focused Layer 1 blockchain** originally launched in 2017, built on the C# programming language with a multi-layered, side-chain architecture aimed at efficiency and scalability. Headquartered out of Singapore, aelf markets itself as an AI-enhanced [[layer-1]] that pairs a main chain with modular side chains and, more recently, [[layer-2|Layer 2]] ZK-rollup tooling, while positioning AI integration as its differentiating narrative. ELF is the network's native token, used for fees, governance, and [[staking]]. It is a small-cap asset ranked **#432** by market capitalization as of this snapshot.

## Technology & Consensus

aelf is built in **C#** and uses a **main-chain + side-chain** architecture so that applications can run on dedicated side chains to avoid congesting the main chain — an early take on horizontal scaling and modularity. Consensus is a delegated/AEDPoS (aelf Delegated Proof of Stake) model in which token holders vote for block producers, aligning it with the [[proof-of-stake]] family. More recently aelf has emphasized **AI integration** and **ZK-rollup** Layer 2 tooling as part of its roadmap. (See [[layer-1]], [[proof-of-stake]].)

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

- **MC/FDV ~0.82:** ~82% of the 1B max supply circulates, so remaining dilution (~18%) is limited and bounded by the hard cap — a structural positive versus low-float 2024–2025 launches with large unlock cliffs ahead.
- **No earnings anchor:** like all infra L1s, ELF has no cash-flow valuation. The bull case rests on the "AI L1" narrative converting into real developer activity; the bear case is that aelf is a long-lived but low-traction chain whose AI rebrand is more marketing than usage.
- **Long-cycle underperformer:** ELF has been ~97% below its 2018 ATH across multiple cycles, never reclaiming prior-cycle highs. This is the profile of a chain that the market has repeatedly declined to re-rate — a caution flag that the AI narrative may need a genuinely differentiated product to break the pattern.
- **Liquidity discount:** sub-$1M daily volume on a ~$53M cap means the quoted price is fragile; any re-rating would need volume to follow, and large orders move the market.

## Overview

aelf, an AI-enhanced Layer 1 blockchain network, leverages the robust C# programming language for efficiency and scalability across its sophisticated multi-layered architecture. Founded in 2017 with its global hub in Singapore, aelf is a pioneer in the industry, leading Asia in evolving blockchain with state-of-the-art AI integration and modular Layer 2 ZK Rollup technology, ensuring an efficient, low-cost, and highly secure platform that is both developer and end-user friendly. Aligned with its progressive vision, aelf is committed to fostering innovation within its ecosystem and advancing Web3 and AI technology adoption. 

The story of aelf began on 10 December 2017, when aelf's vision and plans were introduced to global investors at a Coindesk conference. aelf successfully completed its fund-raising significantly ahead of schedule, having secured investments from notable institutions such as Arrington Capital, Draper Dragon, Galaxy Digital etc. aelf Testnet was successfully launched in 2018, followed by another successful launch of the Mainnet in 2020. aelf is currently operated and managed by a team of highly experienced Web3 veterans led by its founder and CEO, Auric, who bring a wealth of expertise, innovation and drive to the company. 

For more information about aelf, please refer to https://www.aelf.com and our Whitepaper V2.0 (https://docs.aelf.com/resources/whitepaper-2/), or visit us on X (https://x.com/aelfblockchain) for the latest updates.
