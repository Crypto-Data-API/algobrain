# Velo

**Velo** (ticker **VELO**, CoinGecko id `velo`) is the native token of the **Velo Protocol**, a blockchain-based financial settlement and payments network for digital credit issuance and borderless asset transfer between businesses. Velo Labs builds a federated credit exchange that lets partners issue collateral-backed digital credits and settle value transfers, originally anchored on the [[stellar|Stellar]] network (Stellar Consensus Protocol) with a bridged deployment on [[bnb|BNB Chain]]. The project is associated with the [[dwf-labs|DWF Labs]] portfolio and the [[real-world-assets|Real World Assets (RWA)]] / payments category.

> **Disambiguation:** this page documents **Velo Protocol** (Stellar-based settlement, id `velo`). It is *not* [[velodrome-finance|Velodrome Finance]], a separate [[base|Base]]/Optimism DEX whose token *also* trades under the ticker VELO (CoinGecko id `velodrome-finance`). The two are unrelated.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Velo sits in the **payments / settlement / RWA / DeFi** category. Core functions:

- **Digital credit issuance** — partners mint collateral-backed digital credits corresponding to fiat currencies
- **Borderless settlement** — value transfer between federated business partners using the Stellar Consensus Protocol
- **Real-world assets (RWA)** — tokenized fiat credits as the bridge between TradFi settlement and on-chain rails
- **Cross-chain** — Stellar-native with BNB Chain availability

The narrative centers on a B2B / remittance-and-settlement use case rather than retail speculation: Velo aims to be settlement infrastructure for partners moving value across borders.

## Risks

- **Adoption risk** — value depends on real-world partner adoption of the federated credit network; B2B settlement adoption is slow and hard to verify on-chain.
- **Counterparty / DWF association** — DWF Labs portfolio association brings market-making and concentration considerations.
- **Uncapped supply** — no fixed max supply means long-run dilution depends on protocol policy.
- **Liquidity risk** — mid-single-digit-million daily volume can thin out quickly in stressed markets.
- **Ticker confusion** — VELO shares its ticker with [[velodrome-finance|Velodrome Finance]] (id `velodrome-finance`), an unrelated DEX; verify the contract/id before trading to avoid acquiring the wrong asset.
- **Macro / regime risk** — with Fear & Greed at 23 in an established bear market, low-cap RWA/payments tokens are exposed to sharp drawdowns.
