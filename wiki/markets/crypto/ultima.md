# Ultima

**Ultima** (ULTIMA) is the infrastructure/utility token of the Ultima ecosystem, a self-described payments and DeFi ecosystem. It is issued as a BEP-20 token on **BNB Chain** (per the contract address; project marketing also references a "Smart Blockchain"). With a tiny 100,000-unit max supply, ULTIMA trades at a very high per-unit price (>$2,000), and its history has drawn frequent scrutiny over its multi-level / high-yield marketing model.

## Technology / What It Does

Ultima markets itself as a payments-focused crypto ecosystem:

- **Payments token** — pitched as a digital currency for daily transactions, with claims of high throughput (marketing cites ~2,000 TPS and ~3-second blocks).
- **DeFi-U / staking** — a staking product paying rewards in ULTIMA tokens (see [[staking]]).
- **Ecosystem products** — marketing references a marketplace plus planned crypto debit card, exchange, and travel/crowdfunding platforms.

In practice the on-chain token is a BNB Chain BEP-20 (contract `0x5668...a8b8`); the "Smart Blockchain" framing is project marketing and should be treated cautiously. **Caution:** Ultima has been widely flagged by third parties as exhibiting characteristics of a multi-level-marketing (MLM) / referral-driven high-yield scheme. CoinGecko sentiment on this asset has read 0% positive. Treat all capability and user-count claims (e.g., "2.8M users in 120 countries") as unverified marketing.
