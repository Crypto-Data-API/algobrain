## Overview

inALPHA is a token representing ownership in a diversified yield vault within the Nest ecosystem. The vault aggregates a range of real-world assets, including tokenized instruments from AAA-rated firms and asset managers, as well as private credit and trade financing yields from accredited partners. The vault uses the BoringVault architecture, with an Accountant contract providing transparent, on-chain pricing. Designed for high liquidity, inALPHA allows users to earn balanced, dollar-denominated yields through a secure, blockchain-based vehicle.
