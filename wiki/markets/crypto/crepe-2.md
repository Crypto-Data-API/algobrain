## Overview

Feel the power of decentralization. Copytrading, Swap, Liquid Pools. Anything related to DeFi. $CREPE is also meme.

Crypto users can swap tokens within #BNBChain. Users can add liquid to Pools. Also do Staking.

$CREPE also scans popular high-profit wallets to create a list and allows you to copytrade he profit transactions of most profitable traders. With a successful presale raising 80 BNB, Crepe has garnered early traction and community trust. Built on Polygon and BNB Smart Chain, it introduces the Planetary Augmented Asset Makerspace (PaaM™), a powerful system that lets users create customizable, diversified asset baskets including crypto, RWAs like real estate and commodities, and other financial instruments—all within an intuitive interface.
