## Overview

HyperLend is a decentralized lending protocol on Hyperliquid EVM designed to be the credit layer of Hyperliquid. It lets users and integrators supply assets to earn interest and borrow against collateral to unlock liquidity, leverage, and capital efficiency across trading and onchain finance. HyperLend is built to turn idle balances into productive credit, powering use cases like margin financing, structured strategies, treasury management, and ecosystem native liquidity for builders. Over time, the goal is a unified onchain credit stack that supports permissionless DeFi and institution grade workflows on the same settlement rails - transparent, composable, and risk-parameterized credit that can scale with the Hyperliquid economy.
