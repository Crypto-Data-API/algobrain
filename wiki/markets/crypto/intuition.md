## Overview

Intuition is the L2 for trusted data, and the world's first token-curated graph—allowing people and machines to get the information they need, when they need it, from the sources they trust. With Intuition, you can put your 'intuition' to work—transforming your thoughts, opinions, and experiences into fuel for personalized AI, recommendations, and ads that truly understand you. You fully control your digital identity and data—the most valuable resources of the digital age—enabling you to fully leverage their value while unlocking and democratizing trillions in economic potential. Bring your social graphs, preferences, and 'intuition' with you wherever you go. No more rebuilding your profile and connections from scratch on every new platform—create and curate a universal, portable identity and context that allows applications and AI across the web to understand you instantly. And as you contribute, you’re not just improving your own experience—you’re enhancing the web for everyone who follows and trusts your perspective.
