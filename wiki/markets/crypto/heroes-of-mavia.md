## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

MAVIA is a **PERP-FIRST** asset. It trades on **Hyperliquid** as **MAVIA-PERP** with leverage up to ~40-50x, but it is **NOT listed on Binance**, and spot access is limited to smaller/offshore venues (KuCoin, Crypto.com, thin Uniswap V3 liquidity on Ethereum/Base). As a result, price discovery and speculative flow concentrate on the Hyperliquid perp rather than on fragmented spot books. Given the sub-$10M market cap and ~$1.3M daily volume, on-book depth is thin and slippage-prone: large orders will move the mark, so size should be scaled down and execution favors passive limit orders or scaling in/out rather than aggressive market fills. The absence of a deep CEX spot market means there is no easy, low-cost spot leg for classic delivery-based basis or cash-and-carry structures — funding and OI on the HL perp are the primary tradable signals.

### Applicable strategies

- [[crowded-long-funding-fade]] — as a low-cap GameFi token far below its ATH, MAVIA-PERP is prone to speculative long crowding; persistently positive funding flags over-leveraged longs to fade.
- [[oi-confirmed-trend]] — with flow concentrated on one perp venue, rising open interest that confirms directional moves is a cleaner trend signal here than on multi-venue majors.
- [[oi-price-exhaustion]] — spiking OI into a price extreme on a thin book often marks a local exhaustion point ripe for reversal on MAVIA.
- [[liquidation-cascade-fade]] — high leverage (~40-50x) on a low-liquidity perp makes MAVIA susceptible to sharp liquidation cascades that overshoot, offering mean-reversion entries.
- [[funding-rate-harvest]] — when funding is elevated on the HL perp, a delta-managed position can harvest carry, mindful that a genuine spot hedge is hard to source.
- [[breakout-and-retest]] — deep in price discovery near multi-year lows, clean breakouts from established ranges that hold on retest give structured momentum entries.

### Volatility & regime character

MAVIA is a **low-cap, high-beta GameFi/NFT altcoin** with pronounced reflexivity: down ~99.75% from its 2024 ATH and near all-time lows, it exhibits large percentage swings on modest flow. It broadly tracks BTC/ETH risk sentiment with amplified beta — it rallies harder in alt/GameFi risk-on phases and bleeds faster in risk-off — while also carrying idiosyncratic, narrative-driven moves tied to game updates and GameFi sector rotation. Expect volatility regimes to cluster around news catalysts and broad-market liquidity shifts.

### Risk flags

- **Liquidity & venue concentration** — thin spot books and no Binance listing mean tradable exposure is heavily concentrated on the single Hyperliquid perp; a venue outage or de-listing would sharply impair exit liquidity.
- **Low float / supply** — circulating supply (~247M) is very close to max supply (250M) with a Market Cap/FDV ratio near 0.99, so incremental unlock overhang is small, but the tiny market cap makes the price easy to move.
- **Narrative dependence** — as a GameFi token, valuation is highly sensitive to game engagement, sector sentiment, and the broader NFT/gaming narrative rather than fundamentals.
- **Perp funding dislocations** — on a thin single-venue perp, funding can swing to extremes and stay dislocated; crowded positioning can trigger outsized liquidation cascades against leveraged traders.
