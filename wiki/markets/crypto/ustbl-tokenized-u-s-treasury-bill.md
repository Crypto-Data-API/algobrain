## Overview

USTBL is a regulated digital asset that gives exposure to the yield of short-term U.S. Treasury Bills. 
Designed to deliver stability and dollar-denominated returns directly on the blockchain, it combines the safety of Treasuries with the efficiency of digital finance. 

Each token is issued and settled on the Liquid Network, a Bitcoin-based layer designed for secure, confidential, and high-speed transactions. 
Each issuance is backed by real U.S. Treasury securities, securely held and verified monthly by Grant Thornton, one of the world’s leading independent audit firms.USTBL also holds an “A” rating from Particula, an international rating agency specializing in digital assets. 

The yield is embedded within the token itself, accruing automatically over time — no staking or additional actions required. 
USTBL offers a low-risk way to preserve value in dollars and can also serve as collateral within future regulated DeFi ecosystems.
