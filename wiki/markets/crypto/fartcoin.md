# FARTCOIN

**FARTCOIN** (ticker **FARTCOIN**) is a [[meme-coin|meme coin]] on the [[solana|Solana]] blockchain (SPL token) with no fundamental utility or protocol behind it. It traces its origin to the AI agent experiment "Terminal of Truths" and gained outsized traction among crypto traders. It is especially well known among [[perpetual-futures|perpetual futures]] traders for its **persistently elevated [[funding-rate|funding rates]]** on [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]], making it a textbook case study in meme coin market microstructure. It currently ranks **#232** by market capitalization.

## Reflexive Valuation

FARTCOIN has **no cash flows, no protocol, and no treasury** — there is nothing to value on a fundamental basis. Its ~$122M market cap is entirely a **reflexive attention price**: rising price → more social mentions and perp volume → more buyers → higher price, and the loop unwinds violently in reverse. Because the supply is fully circulating (cap ≈ FDV), the cap *is* the price times 1B tokens with no hidden float — so the number moves directly with sentiment. The only defensible framing is *relative* (FARTCOIN's attention/cap vs. peer memes like [[pepe|PEPE]], [[turbo|TURBO]], and [[cheems-token|CHEEMS]]), never an intrinsic "fair value."

## How & Where It Trades

**Spot (DEX):** FARTCOIN trades on Solana DEXs, principally via [[jupiter|Jupiter]] routing into Raydium and Orca pools paired against wrapped SOL.

**Spot (CEX):** Listed on Kraken (FARTCOIN/USD), Bitget, KuCoin, and Crypto.com, among others.

**Derivatives / Perps:** FARTCOIN is one of the most-watched meme perps. It trades as **FARTCOIN-PERP** on [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]] and as USDT-margined perps on several CEXs. Its defining feature is **persistently positive [[funding-rate|funding]]** — long-heavy retail positioning has at times pushed funding to extreme levels, making it a popular target for funding-rate [[arbitrage]] (short perp + long spot). The same crowded-long structure makes it prone to sharp liquidation cascades when momentum reverses; open interest expands fast on attention and unwinds violently on drawdowns.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

FARTCOIN is a pure **community/narrative-driven [[meme-coin]]** with no protocol or utility. Its narrative hook is its AI-agent origin story (the "Terminal of Truths" / Infinite Backrooms experiment), which gave it a distinctive lore relative to generic dog/frog memes. It functions in practice as a **barometer for meme-coin speculation appetite** — when it pumps, broad meme risk appetite is elevated; when it bleeds, it signals risk-off across [[crypto-markets]].

---

## Why Traders Watch FARTCOIN

- **Elevated funding rates** — persistent positive funding on [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]] (long-heavy positioning) makes it a target for funding-rate [[arbitrage]].
- **Meme coin volatility** — daily moves of 20–50% have been common, creating momentum opportunities and tail risk.
- **Liquidity mismatch** — thin order books relative to speculative interest mean extreme price impact; [[whale-trade|whale]] flows cause heavy slippage.
- **Narrative trading** — it trades on attention and reflexive loops, not fundamentals.

---

## Notable History

- **Mid–late 2024:** Spawned from the AI "Terminal of Truths" experiment; launched via pump.fun. ATL of $0.0200 on 2024-10-30.
- **January 2025:** Ran to an all-time high of **$2.48 (2025-01-19)**, becoming a flagship "AI meme."
- **2025–2026:** Multi-stage drawdown to roughly $0.12 by 2026-06-21 (~95% below ATH), tracking the broader bear regime, while still holding ~6x above its 2024 ATL.

---

## Risks

- **Extreme volatility:** Down ~95% from ATH; 20–50% daily moves are normal. Size positions accordingly.
- **No fundamental floor:** Zero utility, cash flows, or treasury — nothing backstops the price; can trend toward zero like most memes.
- **Liquidity / slippage:** Thin books and pools relative to interest; large flows move the market hard.
- **Insider / holder concentration:** pump.fun-style launches and concentrated early holders create coordinated-dump risk.
- **Crowded perps:** Persistently high [[funding-rate|funding]] and open interest make it prone to violent liquidation cascades.
- **Regime sensitivity:** High-beta to sentiment; underperforms badly in extreme-fear / bear regimes ([[crypto-fear-and-greed-index|Fear & Greed]] = 23 on 2026-06-21).
- **Near-total drawdown / survivorship bias:** Already ~95% below ATH. Most meme coins lose 90%+ and disappear; FARTCOIN's survival to date is not predictive of a floor, and a draw back toward the ATL is a live tail risk.
