# Masa (SN42)

**Masa** (SN42) is the current home of the Masa Network's real-time decentralized data subnet on Bittensor. Miners source data from social and web sources (X/Twitter, TikTok, Discord, web pages) and submit structured records; validators score freshness, coverage, and quality. Masa's broader protocol vision is a user-owned data layer where individuals contribute their own data for rewards.

## How It Works

SN42 is the data-production arm of the broader Masa protocol. The subnet combines:

- Miner-run scrapers for public social data
- User-contributed data (via Masa Network's consumer app, where users can share data for rewards)
- Validator scoring on timeliness and coverage

## Trading Relevance

Alternative-data feed for sentiment-based and event-driven strategies. Partially overlaps with [[data-universe-subnet|SN13 Data Universe]] (Macrocosmos's scraping subnet) but has a different production model and broader data coverage. Both subnets benefit from Twitter/X API pricing squeezing out older alt-data vendors.

## History

Masa originally ran on **SN6** before migrating to SN42. SN6 was subsequently taken over by the [[infinite-games-subnet|Infinite Games]] team for prediction markets.
