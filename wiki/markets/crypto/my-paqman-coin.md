## Overview

My Pacman Coin (symbol: $MPC) is a token inspired by and is a tribute to the legendary Filipino 8-Division World Champion in boxing who is equally loved because he's simply a good guy.

He is adored because of his legendary generosity, dedication to public service, and pure heart.

MPC runs on the Xode Blockchain, the Philippines' first homegrown blockchain in the Polkadot network.

MPC is an amalgamation of Filipino grit and resilience, with digital innovation that celebrates the Legacy of Goodness of the boxing icon.
