## Overview

tokenforge is a Hamburg-based SaaS provider founded in 2021 that delivers regulated infrastructure for tokenizing real-world assets in Europe. Its TokenSuite platform is used by financial institutions and regulated issuers across more than 40 deployed platforms, enabling compliant onboarding, issuance, distribution, and lifecycle management under MiCAR, MiFID II, and Germany’s eWpG. TokenSuite provides a modular framework with integrated custody, KYC/KYB, registry functions, and digital subscription workflows. tokenforge is a member of the MiCAR Crypto Alliance and participates in the European Blockchain Sandbox, supporting the development of compliant digital-asset standards and institutional adoption across multiple jurisdictions.
