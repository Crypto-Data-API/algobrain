Built on Sonic, a high-speed, EVM-compatible Layer 1 optimized for DeFi, SODAX eliminates the user’s need for bridges, wrapped tokens, or network switching by managing it in the backend.

## Overview

SODAX is a cross-chain DeFi platform enabling seamless swaps, lending, and borrowing across 12+ EVM and non-EVM blockchains.
