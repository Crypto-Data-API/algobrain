## Overview

Liquid Mercury's strategy is to connect institutional trading infrastructure with a tokenized real-world asset marketplace. MERC is designed primarily for Liquid Mercury's Mercury RWA marketplace and related ecosystem services. 

MERC provides access to selected institutional platform tools, eligible fee benefits for selected services, and marketplace participation features.  Future staking functionality will be introduced in Q4 2026 with a rollout on base network scheduled for Q1 2027.
