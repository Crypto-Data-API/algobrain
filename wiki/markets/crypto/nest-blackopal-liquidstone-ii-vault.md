## Overview

Nest is building a global, permissionless on-chain economy where anyone can access and earn from real-world yields without traditional barriers. As the flagship staking protocol of the Plume RWAfi ecosystem, Nest provides a seamless, transparent, and fully permissionless way for users to earn institutional-grade APYs sourced from high-quality real-world assets. By bridging regulated off-chain yield opportunities to decentralized infrastructure, Nest empowers users and developers alike to participate in a more open, efficient, and globally accessible financial system.
