# Mask Network

**Mask Network** (ticker **MASK**) is the token of Dimension's social/web3 gateway product that overlays decentralized features — encrypted messaging, crypto payments, and Web3 dapp access — onto existing Web2 social platforms such as X (Twitter). It is an [[ethereum|Ethereum]]-native ERC-20 with bridges to BNB Chain, Polygon and Energi. MASK positions itself as the bridge that connects mainstream Web2 internet users into Web3, emphasizing user ownership of data, attention, and content.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Mask Network's product is a **browser-extension overlay** that injects Web3 functionality directly into Web2 social feeds: users can send encrypted posts, tip/pay in crypto, trade tokens, and access decentralized storage from inside platforms like X. The thesis is that mass Web3 adoption arrives not by replacing social networks but by augmenting them in place.

Beyond the consumer app, Dimension (the team behind Mask) has incubated SocialFi and DAO tooling. MASK serves as the governance and utility token across this stack. Notable backers per CoinGecko categorization include Animoca Brands and DWF Labs. The token also appears under the Alameda Research portfolio tag — a legacy association from the pre-collapse FTX/Alameda era — which is a historical, not active, relationship.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

MASK is a **fully-circulating, no-dilution consumer/SocialFi token** (MC = FDV, 100M cap), so its price is a pure read on demand for the Web2-to-Web3 overlay narrative — there is no emission tailwind or overhang to model. With the token at a 2026 all-time low ~99% below its 2021 peak, the market is pricing in a **failed-narrative scenario**: the original "inject Web3 into social feeds" thesis no longer commands a premium. A re-rating requires a genuine new catalyst (product traction, a fresh SocialFi cycle, or platform integrations), not just supply mechanics. Absent that, MASK trades as a low-float, high-beta thematic option on the episodic SocialFi/decentralized-social narrative.

## Overview

Mask Network is the core product of Dimension, which is positioned to become the bridge that connects internet users from Web 2.0 to Web 3.0. The foundational technology of Mask Network is a peer-to-peer encrypted messaging application, with new functions continuously being created around this foundation. We at Mask Network are strong believers in the ownership economy. People should own what they produce, people should own their data, their attention and the virtual space they choose to contribute to.

Mask Network integrates decentralized social messaging, borderless payment network, and decentralized file storage and sharing to provide a safe and convenient portal for users to jump right into the continent of decentralized finance and then the new world of Web 3.0.
