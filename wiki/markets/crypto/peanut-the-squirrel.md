# Peanut the Squirrel

**Peanut the Squirrel** (ticker **PNUT**) is a [[meme-coin]] on [[solana]], launched via the Pump.fun launchpad. It commemorates "Peanut," a pet squirrel whose seizure and euthanization by authorities in late 2024 became a viral news story, fueling one of the highest-profile memorial meme runs of that cycle. The token has no inherent utility; it is a narrative-driven speculative asset.

## Origin & Narrative

PNUT is a **memorial meme**. The real squirrel "Peanut" (P'Nut), a long-running social-media pet, was seized and euthanized by New York state authorities in late 2024, sparking viral outrage and broad political commentary. Within hours a Pump.fun token capitalized on the story, and PNUT became one of the defining memorial/news-driven runs of the 2024 cycle. Its appeal is purely cultural — the emotional resonance and virality of the Peanut story — with **no protocol, revenue, or utility** behind it. Memorial memes characteristically front-load attention and fade as the news recedes.

## Category Context: The Meme-Coin Cycle

PNUT is a **memorial / narrative [[meme-coin]]** and a clean example of the [[meme-coin-cycle]]: an explosive news-driven launch to $2.44, a Binance listing at peak attention, and a sustained ~98% decline as the story aged. Memorial and news-driven memes are especially attention-perishable. CoinGecko categories: Solana Ecosystem, Solana Meme, Pump.fun Ecosystem, Zoo-Themed, and Memorial-Themed.

## Valuation Framing

PNUT has **no fundamental valuation** — no cash flows or discountable stream. Memorial memes are **reflexive and sentiment-driven** to an extreme: price tracks the half-life of a news story plus residual community attention. The only anchors are *relative* (market cap vs peers, liquidity, MC/FDV). Treat any price target as a bet on attention persistence, not valuation.

## Risks

PNUT carries the full risk profile of a utility-free [[meme-coin]]. **These risks are severe and can result in near-total loss:**

- **Extreme volatility & drawdown potential** — capable of multi-hundred-percent moves and equally sharp collapses; already ~98% below ATH, with the all-time low only ~38% below the current price.
- **Pure narrative dependence** — once the memorial story faded from headlines, sustained demand became hard to source; attention can evaporate entirely.
- **Reflexivity** — the same attention loop that lifted PNUT to $2.44 amplified the decline.
- **Insider / early-buyer concentration** — Pump.fun launches frequently concentrate supply in a handful of early wallets, enabling coordinated dumps.
- **Liquidity / leverage / rug risk** — perp funding and OI can spike; thin spot depth amplifies liquidation cascades; Pump.fun memes carry elevated rug/illiquidity risk.
- **Macro / regime risk** — the snapshot date sat in "extreme fear" (index ~23) and an established bear market, a regime in which high-beta memes typically underperform.

Treat as a high-risk speculative position; apply strict [[risk-management]] and treat capital as fully at risk.
