## Overview

EURØP is a euro-denominated stablecoin that is secure, transparent, MiCA compliant and redeemable 1:1 for euro. It is issued by France’s licensed stablecoin issuer and regulated by the French banking regulator ACPR.

Every EURØP is 100% backed by euro. The cash reserves backing EURØP are held in government-supervised EU banks such as Société Générale and audited by a leading global audit firm, KPMG.

EURØP is bringing the euro back to the forefront as financial services transition on-chain. It offers everyone in Europe who is using digital assets or moving their financial services on-chain an ideal alternative to USD-based stablecoins. EURØP enables global euro users to transact in their native currency without the risks of foreign exchange and conversion fees.

EURØP is your go-to choice for DeFi, payments, on-chain FX, lending, and borrowing. Thanks to its stability pegged to the euro, it also serves as an ideal safe haven during market volatility.
