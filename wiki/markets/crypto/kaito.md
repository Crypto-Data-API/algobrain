# KAITO

**KAITO** (ticker **KAITO**) is the native token of Kaito AI, an AI-powered crypto information platform and the leading project in the **InfoFi** ("information finance") category. Kaito indexes and ranks crypto content — research, social signals, and "mindshare" — and rewards creators for attention via its "Yaps" system, turning attention into a measurable, monetizable asset. The token is issued on **[[base]]** (Coinbase's L2, an **[[ethereum]]** rollup) and is central to a token-incentivized attention economy that brands and protocols use to bootstrap distribution.

## Technology & Protocol

Kaito is an **AI / InfoFi attention engine**, not a chain. Its stack:

- **Data ingestion & ranking** — AI models continuously crawl crypto Twitter/X, research, news, and on-chain signals, then score "mindshare" (relative attention) across projects, narratives, and contributors.
- **Kaito Yaps** — a public scoring system that quantifies each contributor's "yapping" (high-signal posting) and attributes a numerical attention score, which gates token and partner-token rewards. This makes attention a measurable, tradable input.
- **Yapper campaigns / leaderboards** — protocols pay (in KAITO and partner incentives) to run mindshare campaigns; contributors compete on leaderboards for rewards, giving Kaito a marketing/distribution layer that plugs the whole ecosystem.
- **Analytics dashboards** — narrative tracking, mindshare timelines, and attention-flow tools used by funds and marketers.

The core innovation is **financializing attention** — turning a previously soft metric (engagement) into a quantified, incentivized, on-chain-settled primitive that can route token rewards to humans and, increasingly, [[ai-agents|AI agents]].

## Valuation Framing

KAITO is best framed as an **attention/marketing-network token** rather than a cash-flow asset:

- **Campaign-spend proxy** — value scales with aggregate budgets protocols route through Yapper campaigns; KAITO is leveraged to crypto-wide marketing/distribution spend.
- **InfoFi category bet** — KAITO is the category leader, so it functions as the index expression of the InfoFi/attention-mining narrative; reflexive and meta-sensitive.
- **Float-adjusted caution** — at MC/FDV ~0.24, the ~$475M FDV vs ~$115M cap means current price embeds optimism about demand absorbing future unlocks. Drawdown is deep (~84% below ATH), so the asset is priced as a high-beta narrative play, not a value name.
