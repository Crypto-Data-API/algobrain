# Memecoin

**Memecoin** (ticker **MEME**) is an [[ethereum|Ethereum]]-based token from the **Memeland** project, the Web3/NFT studio created by the media brand 9GAG. It launched in late 2023 and is the native token of the Memeland ecosystem (home to NFT collections such as *Captainz* and *Potatoz*). It is a [[meme-coin]] backed by an established internet-culture brand rather than an anonymous fair launch.

## Market Structure & Derivatives

**Spot (CEX):** Listed on Binance (MEME/USDT — MEME was a Binance Launchpool asset, giving it strong day-one liquidity), Kraken (MEME/USD), Bitget and KuCoin.

**Spot (on-chain):** Native trading on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] DEXs, principally Uniswap V3 against WETH.

**Derivatives:** MEME trades as a perpetual — **MEME-PERP** on [[hyperliquid]] — and on major CEX derivatives desks. As with other meme [[perpetual-futures|perps]], monitor the [[funding-rate]] and [[open-interest]]; crowded leveraged positioning can drive extreme funding and liquidation cascades, particularly during the Extreme-Fear regime in effect on 2026-06-21.

**Liquidity:** ~$10.4M of daily volume against a ~$35M cap is unusually high turnover for a sub-$40M meme, partly a legacy of its Binance Launchpool / tier-1 listing footprint. As always, headline volume overstates true book depth.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

MEME is **community- and brand-driven**, sitting at the intersection of memes and NFTs. CoinGecko categorises it as **NFT, Meme, Ethereum Ecosystem, Binance Launchpool, YZi Labs (formerly Binance Labs) Portfolio, GMCI Meme Index and GMCI Index**. Its differentiator versus anonymous memes is institutional/brand backing: it emerged from Memeland (9GAG) and counts YZi Labs / Binance-affiliated backing among its supporters, and is a constituent of the GMCI Meme Index. Value still ultimately rests on the strength and engagement of the Memeland community and the meme sector's overall risk appetite.

## Valuation Framing

Like all [[meme-coin|meme coins]], MEME has **no cash flows and no intrinsic value**; it is valued reflexively on attention and narrative. The brand/NFT backing softens but does not remove this:

- **Brand as a (soft) anchor:** unlike an anonymous fair launch, MEME's value is partly tethered to the ongoing relevance of the Memeland/9GAG brand and its NFT collections (*Captainz*, *Potatoz*). This provides a narrative floor that pure memes lack — but brands fade, and the token has still retraced ~99% from ATH.
- **Reflexivity remains dominant:** day-to-day price is driven by meme-sector risk appetite and momentum, not by Memeland fundamentals. Index inclusion (GMCI Meme) adds passive demand but ties MEME to broad meme-sector flows.
- **Supply overhang:** with MC/FDV ≈ 0.92, ~8% of max supply is still to enter circulation — a modest dilution headwind absent from fully-circulated memes like [[moo-deng|Moo Deng]] or [[purr-2|Purr]].
- **Meme-cycle context:** the ~99% drawdown is a textbook [[meme-coin-cycle|meme-coin cycle]] outcome; the recent ATL (May 2026) suggests the brand backing has not insulated MEME from the broader meme washout.

## Risks

- **Extreme drawdown** — Down ~99% from ATH; a stark reminder of meme-sector mean reversion.
- **Brand dependency** — Value is tethered to the continued relevance of the Memeland/9GAG brand and its NFT collections.
- **Supply overhang** — ~8% of max supply is not yet circulating; future unlocks/emissions can add sell pressure.
- **Insider / holder concentration** — Early stakers, team and backers may hold concentrated positions (on-chain distribution not yet ingested here).
- **Volatility & leverage risk** — Active [[perpetual-futures|perp]] markets with potentially extreme [[funding-rate|funding]] magnify liquidation risk.
