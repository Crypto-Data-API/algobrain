## Overview

POTS Money is a modular decentralized finance protocol built on algorithmic stablecoins and collateralized liquidity. Starting with IBS — an algorithmic non-stable coin issued through collateralized debt mechanisms — POTS Money constructs a comprehensive DeFi ecosystem. The protocol integrates decentralized payment systems, prediction market lending dynamics, auction-based issuance mechanisms, and AI-powered tools to enable users to rapidly build customized yield strategies within the POTS DAO ecosystem.

IBS is the protocol's native algorithmic token with unlimited supply, algorithmically minted based on user bonding and staking participation across the ecosystem.
