## Overview

Zylo is not a single product and not just a token.It is an economic layer designed to connect multiple digital experiences into one continuous system of progress.
Most platforms treat users as separate roles: a gamer here, a trader there. Each product resets context, value, and history. Zylo is built on a different assumption — one user, many behaviors, one economy.
Winhin Zylo, progress is not limited to a single app or activity. Whether a user plays games, participates in tournaments, uses utility services, or engages with financial tools, their actions contribute to a shared economic and progression layer. The form of interaction may change, but the user’s value inside the system does not reset.
Zylo is the single utility token of the Zylo ecosystem.It is not a standalone product and is not tied to a single use case.Zylo acts as an infrastructure-level economic layer that connects multiple products and user activities under one shared system of rules, access, and incentives.
The Zylo ecosystem follows the principle: one user → multiple contexts → one economy A user may interact with different products (games, trading, services), but remains within a single economic environment.
