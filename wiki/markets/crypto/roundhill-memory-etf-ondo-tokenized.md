## Overview

DRAMon is the Ondo Tokenized version of the Roundhill Memory ETF, giving tokenholders economic exposure similar to holding DRAM and reinvesting any dividends. Ondo tokenized stocks enable non-US retail and institutional users around the world to instantly mint and redeem tokenized U.S. stocks and ETFs, 24 hours a day, five days a week with full access to traditional exchange liquidity.
