## Overview

IBITon is the Ondo Tokenized version of the iShares Bitcoin Trust ETF, giving tokenholders economic exposure similar to holding IBIT and reinvesting any dividends. Ondo tokenized stocks enable non-US retail and institutional users around the world to instantly mint and redeem tokenized U.S. stocks and ETFs, 24 hours a day, five days a week with full access to traditional exchange liquidity. Additional restrictions apply. Learn more at ondo.finance/global-markets.
