## Overview

infiniFi is a borrow short lend long asset-liability management system that uses fractional reserve principles from traditional banking to amplify returns on any asset starting with bleuchip defi assets and eventually spreading out to RWA.  infiniFi balances the liquidity and risk preferences of any flavor of user to produce the most optimized outcomes from all depositors.  Whether a user wants to stay liquid while earning top of market liquid returns or earn the highest returns in defi by locking infiniFi is the right choice.
