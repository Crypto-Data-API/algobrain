## Overview

CL8Y is a deflationary memecoin designed to fuel open-source development, DeFi automation, and financial sovereignty. Operating on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and Terra Classic (USTC), CL8Y integrates with top DeFi protocols for automated buy pressure and burns. With zero buy fees, a fixed 3,000,000 supply, and a tapering sell burn, CL8Y enforces scarcity while supporting liquidity. No presales, no private allocations—only permissionless adoption.
