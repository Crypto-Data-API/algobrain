# Bitway

**Bitway** (ticker **BTW**) is the native utility and governance token of the **Bitway** DeFi ecosystem, issued on [[bnb|BNB Chain]] (with an Ethereum deployment) and featured as a **Binance Alpha Spotlight** project. BTW powers network operations, staking and governance, and aligns incentives across Bitway's yield, payment and financing products. It is a [[defi|DeFi]] / [[yield-farming|yield-farming]] governance token.

**Supply / valuation note:** BTW carries a **severe dilution overhang**. Circulating supply (~2.20B) is only ~22% of the 10.0B max; market cap (~$313M) is roughly one-fifth of FDV (~$1.42B), so ~7.8B BTW (~78%) is still to enter circulation. Note the extreme recent move — **+48% in 24h, +61% in 7d**, with an ATH set just one day prior (2026-06-20) and price already -26% off it. That combination of a high-FDV/low-float token, an enormous +99.7M 24h volume spike, and a fresh ATH followed by a pullback signals a highly speculative, momentum-driven episode — treat the price as unstable.

## Risks

- **Dilution.** ~78% of supply not yet circulating; future unlocks/emissions are a major overhang against a ~$1.4B FDV.
- **Speculative volatility.** A +48%/24h, +61%/7d spike with a one-day-old ATH and immediate -26% pullback indicates momentum/speculative risk; sharp reversals are likely.
- **Liquidity risk.** The huge 24h volume appears event-driven; baseline depth is probably much lower, raising slippage and exit risk once the spike fades.
- **Sentiment flag.** Previously recorded 0% positive community sentiment on CoinGecko.
- **Execution risk.** Value depends on the ecosystem's yield/payment/financing products gaining real usage, not just token speculation.
- **Macro backdrop.** As of 2026-06-21 crypto Fear & Greed reads 23 ("Established Bear Market"); a high-FDV, low-float token rallying into a bear tape is especially prone to violent mean reversion.
