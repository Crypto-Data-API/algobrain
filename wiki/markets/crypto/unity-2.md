## Overview

YieldPoint is a capital management platform that simplifies access to advanced DeFi strategies. Its flagship product, Unity (UTY), is a digital synthetic dollar. UTY is engineered to unlock liquidity and capital efficiency within different blockchain ecosystems by allowing users to maintain their existing capital exposure while accessing sustainable yield through delta-neutral and other trading strategies.
