## Overview

$NORMIE is a community-driven meme coin on Solana. Launched in early July 2026 on Pump.fun, it was created in the spirit of the Ansem ecosystem to make meme coins accessible to everyday users (“normies”).Key facts:Fixed supply of 1 billion tokens
80% initially allocated to Ansem’s wallet to reward holders
Completed community takeover on July 7, 2026 — now 100% community-owned and operated
Trades on PumpSwap with no complex utilities or roadmap
Focuses on accessibility, meme culture, and community participation on Solana

The project is simple, fair, and run entirely by the community.
