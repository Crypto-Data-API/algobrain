# Cheems Token

**Cheems Token** (ticker **CHEEMS**) is a community-driven [[meme-coin]] on the [[bnb|BNB Chain]] (Binance Smart Chain, BEP-20). Its mascot is "Cheems," the small, helpless Shiba Inu drawn from the long-running Doge/Cheems internet meme. The project markets itself as a pure-narrative, no-utility meme — the contract is renounced, there is no team tax, no team reserves, and no built-in revenue stream — positioning it as a "homage to the Lord of Memes" rather than a product. It is a small-cap, fully meme-driven asset whose price is governed almost entirely by sentiment and BNB-Chain [[meme-coin-cycle|meme rotation]].

## Reflexive Valuation

CHEEMS has **no fundamentals** — no cash flows, no protocol revenue, no treasury, no yield. Its market cap is purely a function of *attention*: the number of holders, the intensity of social activity, and where it sits in the BNB-Chain [[meme-coin-cycle|meme rotation]]. This makes its valuation **reflexive** — rising price draws attention, attention draws buyers, buyers raise price, and the loop runs in reverse just as violently on the way down. There is no discounted-cash-flow anchor and no "fair value": the only honest framing is *relative attention vs. peer memes*, not intrinsic worth. The ~$108M cap should be read as a sentiment gauge, not a balance-sheet-backed number.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

CHEEMS is explicitly a **narrative/community asset**, not a utility token. The team frames it as "pure meme" — a tribute to survivors of past crypto dumps, rugs, and the broader Doge meme lineage — and stresses that it has renounced contract control, charges no tax, and holds no reserves. Stated "use cases" are limited to swapping, speculation, and participation in any BNB-Chain ecosystem projects that choose to integrate it. Value here is a function of attention and meme rotation, in the same family as [[memecoin-mania|memecoin mania]] cycles rather than cash-flow-backed protocols.

## Risks

- **Extreme volatility:** As a pure meme with no cash flows, CHEEMS can swing double-digit percentages in a single week (down ~10% over 7 days here) and far more over longer windows. Treat it as among the highest-risk holdings in any book.
- **Near-total drawdown potential:** The token is ~75% below its 2025 ATH and historically traded near zero (ATL ~$0.0000000057). Most BNB-Chain memes that fade never recover; a draw toward the ATL is a realistic tail.
- **Rug / illiquidity risk:** With ~1% daily turnover relative to market cap, exits can be costly and price is sensitive to a few large sellers. Although the contract is renounced (limiting classic mint/tax rugs), liquidity can still be pulled and on-chain holder distribution is not ingested here — concentration cannot be ruled out and should be checked before sizing.
- **Zero intrinsic value:** No revenue, no tax, no reserves by design — there is no fundamental floor; the price depends entirely on continued attention.
- **Bear-market backdrop:** With market-wide Extreme Fear ([[crypto-fear-and-greed-index|index]] 23) and an Established Bear Market regime, low-cap memes are typically among the first assets sold in risk-off conditions.
