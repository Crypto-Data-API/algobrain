# Apex

**Apex** (SN1) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#928** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** decentralized LLM inference — miners serve text completions, validators score quality. The longest-running and historically flagship text-generation subnet on [[bittensor|Bittensor]].
- **Team:** Macrocosmos contributed heavily to early development; now a distributed contributor set.
- **Trading relevance:** alpha-1 is one of the most-traded Bittensor alphas and acts as a benchmark for the "generic LLM capability" bucket. Rotation in and out of alpha-1 is a proxy for the decentralized-text-gen vs specialist-subnet trade.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-1, traded via Subtensor bonding curve. See [[dtao]].
