## Overview

NuNet enables a global, decentralized computing framework that connects diverse devices — from personal laptops and edge nodes to data centers — into a unified, peer-to-peer network. The platform allows anyone to contribute or utilize GPU and CPU resources securely and efficiently, facilitating distributed AI and data processing at scale. By enabling direct interaction between resource providers and users through blockchain-based micropayments, NuNet creates a transparent and open infrastructure for running computational workloads without centralized intermediaries.
