## Overview

Elephant Money TRUNK is a cross-chain deflationary utility token of the Elephant Money protocol that is used as collateral in various DeFi ecosystems. Elephant Money Trumpet is a store of value backed by TRUNK that only goes up in value. Trumpet has zero transfer or dev fees, a low 5% fee is charged on minting and redemption. EMT grows in value linearly and is fully backed by the TRUNK deposited with zero reliance on third party liquidity. Bridge TRUNK to Solana and borrow USDC to maximize your gains.
