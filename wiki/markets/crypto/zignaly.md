# ZIGChain (ZIG)

**ZIGChain** (ticker **ZIG**, CoinGecko id `zignaly`) is the token that grew out of **Zignaly**, a social/copy-trading platform, and has since rebranded toward a purpose-built Layer-1 "wealth-generation" blockchain. Its pitch is a wealth-management module that lets retail users co-invest alongside professional fund managers in [[real-world-assets|real-world assets]] (RWAs) while supporting institutional adoption and compliance. The ZIG token powers governance, transaction validation and network incentives. It is a multi-chain asset with its origins on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] and deployments across BNB Chain, Polygon, [[solana|Solana]] and Injective.

## Use Case / Narrative / Category

CoinGecko tags ZIG under **AI, Smart Contract Platform, Layer 1, BNB Chain / Solana / Polygon / Ethereum / Injective Ecosystems, DWF Labs Portfolio and OKX Ventures Portfolio.** The narrative is **"democratized wealth management on-chain"** — pairing retail capital with professional managers and tokenized [[real-world-assets|RWAs]] — layered onto a [[layer-1|Layer-1]] + AI positioning. It originated as **Zignaly**, a social/copy-trading marketplace where users could mirror the trades of vetted traders; the L1 pivot aims to give that ecosystem its own settlement layer. The asset therefore rides the RWA, AI and L1 narratives simultaneously, which cuts both ways: more upside optionality, but also more dependence on hype cycles.

## Valuation Framing

*Qualitative — not a price target.* ZIG is priced as a small-cap, narrative-driven [[layer-1]] at ~$71M market cap and ~$98M FDV. Two features shape the read. First, the high ~0.72 float means most of the dilution is already done — a structurally cleaner setup than the heavily-locked launches elsewhere in this loop, so future-unlock risk is comparatively modest. Second, and offsetting that, the project is mid-pivot: it is transitioning from a working social/copy-trading product (Zignaly, with real users) into an L1 + RWA wealth-management platform whose token utility (gas, governance, validation) is still maturing. The valuation therefore rests on execution of that pivot rather than on established L1 fee revenue, and ZIG's association with market-maker/VC backers (DWF Labs, OKX Ventures) adds both liquidity-support and concentrated-selling considerations. The thin ~$2M daily volume means price can gap on relatively small flow. Net: ZIG is best understood as a low-float-risk but high-execution-risk bet on a copy-trading franchise successfully becoming its own chain.

## Risks

- **Liquidity risk** — ~$2.1M/day on a ~$71M cap is thin; expect slippage and gap-down risk in extreme fear ([[fear-and-greed-index|Fear & Greed]] = 23).
- **Dilution overhang** — MC/FDV ~0.72 means future unlocks can pressure price.
- **Execution/transition risk** — pivot from copy-trading app to L1 + RWA wealth platform is ambitious and unproven at scale.
- **Multi-chain fragmentation** — liquidity split across five chains weakens any single venue's depth.
- **Backer-distribution risk** — market-maker/VC-linked tokens (DWF Labs) can see concentrated selling.
- **Narrative beta** — heavily exposed to AI / RWA / L1 sentiment swings.
