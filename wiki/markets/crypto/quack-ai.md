## Overview

Quack AI is the governance layer for the Agent Economy, where AI agents, users, and applications interact under verifiable rules. Its x402 standard enables sign-to-pay, gasless transactions across blockchains, supported by a facilitator relay system, policy engine, and governance intelligence modules. Quack AI aims to make automated on-chain actions secure, compliant, and transparent for all participants.
