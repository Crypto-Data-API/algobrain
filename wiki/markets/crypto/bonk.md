# Bonk

**Bonk** (BONK) is the original dog-themed memecoin on [[solana|Solana]], airdropped on Christmas Day 2022 to revive the post-FTX Solana community, and since evolved into a full ecosystem: the [[letsbonk|LetsBonk.fun]] launchpad, [[bonkbot|BonkBot]] Telegram trading bot, BonkSwap DEX, and BonkDAO governance. For traders it is a benchmark **Solana-meme basket** asset with deep spot and perp liquidity, distinctive revenue-funded burn mechanics, and — unusually for a memecoin — corporate treasury (Safety Shot) and US ETF-filing catalysts.

## Overview

Bonk is a dog-themed memecoin on the Solana blockchain, launched on Christmas Day 2022 to revitalize the Solana community following the FTX collapse. Created by an anonymous team of 22 Solana developers and builders, Bonk distributed 50% of its total 100 trillion token supply via airdrop to community members including NFT collectors, developers, and artists. 

The project positions itself as "the first Solana dog coin for the people, by the people," explicitly rejecting venture capital backing in favor of grassroots community ownership. What sets Bonk apart from other memecoins is its combination of fair launch mechanics, extensive ecosystem integration, and active deflationary strategy. 

Built on Solana, BONK benefits from fast transaction speeds and low fees that make it practical for everyday use. Native products include LetsBonk.fun (a memecoin launchpad that briefly became Solana's market leader in 2025, surpassing Pump.fun with over 78% market share), BonkSwap (a decentralized exchange) and BonkBot (a Telegram trading bot with built-in burn mechanics). 

BONK is available across 10 blockchain networks and listed on major exchanges including Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, and Robinhood. Governance is handled by BonkDAO, which holds 15% of the token supply and makes decisions through on-chain voting. 

The community has organized several major burn events, most notably the December 2024 BURNmas campaign that destroyed 1.69 trillion tokens worth over $50 million. In September 2025, Nasdaq-listed Safety Shot, Inc. formed BONK Holdings LLC and acquired over 2.5% of the circulating supply (valued at $55-63 million), making BONK one of the first memecoins to attract formal corporate treasury adoption. Core contributor Nom was appointed to Safety Shot's board, and the company holds a 10% revenue share in LetsBonk.fun.

As with all memecoins, BONK is a highly speculative asset with substantial volatility. Its value is primarily driven by community sentiment, social media activity, and ecosystem product revenue rather than cash-flow fundamentals.

## 2025–2026 Developments

- **LetsBonk.fun flips Pump.fun (July 2025)** — the BONK-ecosystem launchpad overtook [[pump-fun-vs-letsbonk-vs-believe-vs-moonshot-vs-heaven|Pump.fun]] as Solana's largest memecoin launchpad, peaking at **~84% market share** (vs Pump.fun's 12%) in late July 2025 with **$30M+ monthly revenue**, ~50% of which was used to buy and burn BONK (CoinDesk, Dropstab). Dominance proved temporary: by August 2025 Pump.fun rebounded to ~74% share while LetsBonk graduations collapsed ~98%.
- **Burn program** — beyond the Dec 2024 BURNmas (1.69T BONK, >$50M), launchpad and BonkBot revenue continued funding ongoing burns through 2025, making BONK structurally deflationary at the margin.
- **Safety Shot treasury (Sep 2025)** — Nasdaq-listed Safety Shot, Inc. formed BONK Holdings LLC, acquired >2.5% of circulating supply ($55–63M), took a 10% revenue share in LetsBonk.fun, and seated BONK core contributor "Nom" on its board — among the first corporate memecoin treasuries.
- **ETF filings (2025)** — Tuttle Capital set 2025-07-16 as earliest launch for its 2x leveraged BONK ETF; a **REX-Osprey BONK ETF** was filed via ETF Opportunities Trust (SEC 485BXT filings, Sep–Dec 2025), and Tuttle filed a "Bonk Income Blast" covered-call-style ETF — putting BONK in the first cohort of memecoins with US ETF wrappers in registration.
- **Price** — ATH $0.00005825 (2024-11-20); by spring 2026 BONK traded ~90% below ATH around $0.0000057, with the meme sector broadly out of favor.

## Protocol & Project — BONK and the Solana Meme Ecosystem

BONK began as a pure [[meme-coin|memecoin]] but is unusual in having grown a self-reinforcing product flywheel on [[solana|Solana]]:

- **The token** — an SPL [[meme-coin|memecoin]] with a fair-launch airdrop (no VC allocation), 50% of the original 100T supply distributed to the Solana community on 2022-12-25. It is now bridged to 10+ chains.
- **[[letsbonk|LetsBonk.fun]]** — the BONK ecosystem's memecoin launchpad (the direct rival to [[pump-fun|Pump.fun]]). It briefly became Solana's #1 launchpad in July 2025 (~84% issuance share). Critically, **~50% of launchpad revenue is used to buy and burn BONK**, tying token demand to Solana memecoin issuance volume — a structural value-capture loop most memecoins lack.
- **[[bonkbot|BonkBot]]** — a Telegram trading bot for sniping/trading Solana tokens, with built-in BONK burn mechanics on its fee flow.
- **BonkSwap** — the ecosystem's own DEX.
- **BonkDAO** — on-chain governance, holding ~15% of supply.

This "meme + launchpad + bot + DEX + DAO" stack is why BONK trades less like a one-line joke and more like a *Solana meme-economy index* — its fundamentals (such as they are) are launchpad market share and burn rate.

## Market Structure & Derivatives

- **Spot venues** — deep liquidity on Binance (BONK/USDT), Coinbase, Kraken, Upbit (BONK/KRW), Bitget, KuCoin, Crypto.com, Robinhood, plus Orca/Raydium on-chain. BONK is one of very few memecoins with both Coinbase *and* Upbit listings, broadening US-retail and Korea-retail access.
- **Perpetuals** — among the most liquid memecoin perps: BONK-PERP on Binance, Bybit, OKX and [[hyperliquid|Hyperliquid]]. As a [[perpetual-futures|perpetual]] market, BONK funding swings hard with sentiment — extended periods of strongly positive funding during meme-FOMO (longs paying) and sharply negative funding during capitulation. Open interest is healthy enough to support both directional and basis trades, but funding is *expensive* relative to majors and can dominate carry.
- **Funding/basis trade** — collect funding by going long spot / short perp when funding is persistently positive; in the current Extreme-Fear regime funding is muted-to-negative, favoring the opposite (or no) carry.

## Valuation Framework (qualitative)

A memecoin has no cash flows, so value BONK on the *three meme drivers* plus its one quasi-fundamental:

1. **Narrative / attention** — Solana ecosystem sentiment, social volume, "dog coin" cultural relevance. BONK is the flagship Solana dog coin, so it captures Solana-meme mindshare.
2. **Liquidity / reflexivity** — depth across CEX + DEX makes BONK the *liquid proxy leg* for Solana-meme basket trades; flows beget flows.
3. **Distribution** — fair-launch + Coinbase/Upbit/Robinhood breadth = unusually wide holder base for a memecoin.
4. **Quasi-fundamental (the differentiator)** — launchpad/bot **revenue → buyback-and-burn**. This gives BONK a (loose) "fee-token" valuation overlay absent in WIF/POPCAT: rising LetsBonk share and burn rate are bullish; collapsing share (as in Aug 2025) is bearish.

Net: BONK is "Solana-meme beta with a deflationary burn engine and institutional optionality (treasury + ETFs)."
