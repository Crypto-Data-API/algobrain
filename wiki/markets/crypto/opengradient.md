## Overview

OpenGradient is the Network for Open Intelligence — a decentralized infrastructure network designed to host, inference, and verify AI models at scale. Rather than functioning as a standalone blockchain, OpenGradient operates as a specialized AI coprocessor: it enables applications, blockchains, and autonomous agents to outsource computationally-heavy AI tasks to a dedicated network of GPU and Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) nodes. The validator set verifies every computational proof (TEE attestation or zkML proof) at the consensus level before it is committed on-chain, enabling trustless usage of AI from any network or application.

## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

OPG is tradable on **Binance** — both spot (OPG/USDT) and a USD-margined perpetual, giving access to funding rates, open interest, and liquidation data. It is **not** listed on Hyperliquid, so Binance is the primary (and effectively sole) leveraged venue for the token. This concentration means perp liquidity, funding, and OI signals should be read almost entirely off Binance, and cross-venue perp arbitrage is not available. With a small ~$21M cap and thin order books relative to majors, leveraged positions can move price and trigger cascades quickly — size conservatively, use limit entries, and expect wider effective spreads and slippage on larger clips. Spot exposure is also available on Upbit (KRW), which can add Korea-session volatility and premium/discount dynamics.

### Applicable strategies

- [[funding-rate-harvest]] — a Binance-only perp means funding on OPG can run persistently one-sided; collect the funding leg while hedging spot.
- [[oi-confirmed-trend]] — with all leverage concentrated on one Binance perp, OI changes cleanly confirm or reject directional moves in this small-cap AI token.
- [[liquidation-cascade-fade]] — thin books plus single-venue leverage make OPG prone to sharp liquidation wicks that mean-revert; fade the flush.
- [[narrative-trading]] — OPG rides the AI-infrastructure / decentralized-inference narrative and Binance Alpha spotlight flows; trade the theme rotations.
- [[breakout-and-retest]] — low float and a wide ATH-to-current gap produce clean range breaks; enter on the retest to filter false moves.
- [[volatility-targeting]] — sizing to realized vol keeps risk bounded on a reflexive small-cap that can swing double digits intraday.

### Volatility & regime character

Small-cap (~#794) AI-infrastructure / DeFi-adjacent token with high beta to BTC/ETH risk regimes and amplified reflexivity typical of low-float names. Price sits well below its 2026-04 ATH and near recent ATL, indicating a downtrend/accumulation regime with elevated realized volatility. Moves tend to be narrative- and liquidity-driven rather than fundamentals-driven, so expect sharp expansions on AI-sector rotations and equally sharp mean-reversions when flows fade.

### Risk flags

- **Venue concentration:** leveraged exposure lives almost entirely on Binance; a listing change, delisting, or Binance-specific event would sharply impair liquidity and price discovery.
- **Low float / emissions:** only ~19% of the 1.00B max supply circulates (MC/FDV ~0.19), so future unlocks and emissions are a significant overhang.
- **Narrative dependence:** valuation leans on the AI-infrastructure/decentralized-inference story; sentiment reversals can compress the token quickly.
- **Liquidity/slippage:** thin books relative to majors make large orders costly and cascades more likely; Korea-session (Upbit) flows can add premium/discount volatility.
