## Overview

Strike Robot is a full-stack robotics project building humanoid systems for real-world industrial operations. At its core is SR Agentic, an agentic AI platform that enables robots to perceive, reason, and act autonomously in complex environments such as factories and high-risk facilities.

The system combines perception (vision models like YOLOv11/DINOv3), decision-making (ReAct-based reasoning), and physical control (locomotion + intervention) to allow robots not only to monitor but to actively respond to hazards.

Strike Robot also develops the SR Platform, a simulation-to-real training infrastructure that allows enterprises to train and deploy robots without building their own robotics stack. The goal is to power Physical AI BPOs where robots can replace or assist human labor in dangerous environments, improving safety, efficiency, and scalability.
