# Zebec Network

**Zebec Network** (ZBCN, base chain [[solana|Solana]]) is the native token of a [[solana|Solana]]-based payments and "money-streaming" protocol. Zebec's integrated products — real-time payroll, continuous on-chain payment streams, a card/merchant layer, and a networked **DePIN** component — aim to let employers, merchants and individuals move value continuously rather than in discrete batched transactions, reducing reliance on traditional banking intermediaries. ZBCN is a high-supply, low-unit-price token (sub-cent), and the protocol is positioned in the [[real-world-assets|RWA]], **payments** and **DePIN** narratives.

## Use Case / Narrative / Category

CoinGecko categorises ZBCN under **DeFi, Solana Ecosystem, Real World Assets (RWA), Payment Solutions, DePIN, Coinbase Ventures Portfolio, Alameda Research Portfolio, Circle Ventures Portfolio, OKX Ventures Portfolio, RWA Protocol, and "Made in USA."** Its core differentiator is **continuous/streaming payments** — paying salaries, subscriptions, and vendors by the second rather than per pay-period — built on Solana for low fees and fast settlement.
