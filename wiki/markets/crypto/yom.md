## Overview

YOM is pioneering the first DePIN for cloud gaming, delivering high-fidelity games, white-label experiences, and next-gen entertainment at scale. By leveraging a distributed network of nodes, YOM provides global, low-latency cloud gaming to any device at near-zero costs. Node operators earn passive revenue by contributing their machine cycles, yielding up to 480% APY per license via a deflationary token. By removing the need for expensive hardware and traditional platform gatekeepers, YOM dramatically expands the reach for developers, allowing them to build and launch faster than ever.
