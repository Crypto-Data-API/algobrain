## Overview

æternity is a scalable, decentralized blockchain platform designed to enable fast and secure smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps). It utilizes a unique hybrid consensus model combining Proof-of-Work (PoW) for security and Nakamoto Consensus for scalability. The platform is equipped with innovative features such as state channels for off-chain scaling, a highly efficient Oracle system, and naming services, making it suitable for diverse real-world applications.
