# Railgun

**Railgun** (ticker **RAIL**) is a [[privacy-coins|privacy]] infrastructure protocol that brings **zk-SNARK privacy to on-chain activity** on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] and other EVM chains. Built as a smart-contract system — with no bridge or layer-2 — it lets users trade, send, and interact with [[defi|DeFi]] privately while keeping funds fully on-chain. RAIL is the protocol's governance and utility token. It currently ranks **#214** by market capitalization.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

RAILGUN is **privacy infrastructure**, not a privacy coin in the Monero/Zcash sense. Rather than being its own private chain, it is a **smart-contract privacy layer** on top of existing EVM chains: users deposit into RAILGUN's shielded pool and can then trade, send, and interact with DeFi protocols from a private balance using **zk-SNARK** proofs ([[zero-knowledge-proofs|zero-knowledge proofs]]), without withdrawing and without relying on a bridge or external L2. This makes it a tool for on-chain privacy and [[mev|MEV]] protection. It is currently deployed on Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, and Arbitrum. Category tags: Privacy, Privacy Infrastructure, Zero Knowledge (ZK), MEV Protection, Governance.

A distinguishing design choice is RAILGUN's **"Private Proofs of Innocence" (PPOI)** system, which lets users cryptographically prove their funds did not originate from known illicit sources without revealing transaction details — an attempt to thread the needle between user privacy and the [[regulation|compliance]] pressure that has crippled mixer-style protocols. This is a structural differentiator from a pure-mixer model and the core of RAILGUN's "compliant privacy" pitch.

## Peer Comparison

RAIL belongs to the [[privacy-coins|privacy]] cohort but is *infrastructure* (a shielded smart-contract layer), not a standalone private chain. Framing against the category:

| Token | Type | Privacy model | Trading character |
|---|---|---|---|
| **RAILGUN (RAIL)** | EVM privacy layer | zk-SNARK shielded pools on existing chains | Thin float, high regulatory beta, ZK-narrative driven |
| [[monero\|Monero (XMR)]] | Native privacy L1 | Ring signatures / stealth addresses (always-on) | Large, established; heavy delisting pressure |
| [[zcash\|Zcash (ZEC)]] | Native privacy L1 | zk-SNARKs (optional shielding) | Established; original zk-SNARK pioneer |

RAILGUN's edge over native privacy coins is **composability** — users keep [[defi|DeFi]] access from inside the shielded pool rather than living on a separate private chain. The trade-off is smart-contract risk and dependence on the host chains' liquidity. Versus [[monero|Monero]]/[[zcash|Zcash]] it is far smaller and more thinly traded, so it behaves more like a high-beta ZK-narrative call option than a store-of-value privacy asset.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

RAIL is hard to value on cash flows — fee accrual to the token is limited and the thesis is overwhelmingly a bet on **demand for compliant on-chain privacy**. Bullish case: privacy becomes a first-class requirement for institutional and power-user DeFi, PPOI satisfies regulators, and RAILGUN captures shielded-pool TVL as the EVM-native standard. Bearish case: regulatory hostility forces continued CEX delistings, liquidity thins further (already ~$226K/24h), and the token decays as a narrative trade. The asymmetry is real but so is the tail risk — privacy tooling sits closest to the regulatory line of any crypto category. This is framing, not advice.

## Risks

- **Regulatory / compliance risk:** Privacy protocols face the heaviest regulatory headline risk in crypto (sanctions, delistings, exchange compliance pressure). This is the dominant idiosyncratic risk for RAIL.
- **Future dilution:** Max supply (100M) is ~74% above current circulating (57.5M); further emissions could weigh on price.
- **Thin liquidity:** ~$226K/24h volume means high slippage and gap risk on entries/exits.
- **Smart-contract risk:** A zk-SNARK shielded-pool contract is a high-value target; a bug or exploit would be severe.
- **Narrative dependence:** Token value is tied to demand for on-chain privacy and the ZK narrative, which is cyclical.
- **Regime sensitivity:** Down ~56% from ATH; underperforming into an extreme-fear / bear regime ([[fear-and-greed-index|Fear & Greed]] = 23 on 2026-06-21).
