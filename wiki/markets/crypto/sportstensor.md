# Sportstensor (SN41)

**Sportstensor** (SN41) is a Bittensor subnet specialized in sports prediction and analytics. Miners submit predictions for specific sports markets (game outcomes, player props, live-in-game markets); validators score against realized results. The team targets the sports-betting alpha use case directly, positioning the subnet as an alternative data and signal feed for sports bettors.

## How It Works

The subnet covers major North American and European sports (NFL, NBA, MLB, Premier League, etc.). Miners submit structured predictions; validators fetch official game results and score using proper scoring rules. High-performing miners earn emission share; losers dilute.

## Trading Relevance

**Directly consumable for sports bettors.** A professional sports-bettor / sportsbook can subscribe to SN41 as an independent signal source and compare against in-house models. Sister subnet to [[score]] (SN44, sports-vision/analytics) and partially overlapping with [[bettensor]] (SN30) and [[infinite-games-subnet|Infinite Games]] (SN6).
