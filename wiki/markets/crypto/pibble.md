## Overview

‘Generative AI Content Hub, PIBBLE 2.0’
 
‘PIBBLE 2.0’ is a special service that combines two groundbreaking technologies, an AI chatbot and an image generator, to revolutionize the world of creativity and emotional expression.
 
It's not just conversation to AI, user can draw and write while having a conversation with AI, and upload creations to the PIBBLE app and share them with friends on social media.
 
In this sequence of actions, Pibble create a vibrant and inclusive community where participants can express themselves, connect with like-minded others, and actively engage with the content or services offered within the ecosystem.
