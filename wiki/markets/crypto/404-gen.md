# 404—GEN

**404—GEN** (SN17) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#801** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** text-to-3D generation. Miners generate 3D assets from text prompts; validators score quality, geometry, and texture fidelity. Outputs feed into games, virtual worlds, and AR/VR applications.
- **Team:** 404 Collective / 404.xyz.
- **Trading relevance:** niche creative-AI exposure. Small TAM relative to compute or trading subnets.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-17.
