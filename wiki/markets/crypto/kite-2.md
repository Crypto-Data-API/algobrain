# Kite

**Kite** (ticker **KITE**) is an EVM-compatible Layer 1 blockchain purpose-built so that autonomous [[ai-agents|AI agents]] can transact, hold identity, and make payments on-chain with cryptographic safety and native **x402** compatibility. Its thesis is that the emerging "agent economy" needs payment rails and verifiable identity that traditional card networks and human-centric KYC cannot serve, and KITE is the gas/utility/staking token of that network. It sits squarely in the [[ai-agent-tokens|AI-agent token]] and [[decentralized-ai|decentralized-AI]] narratives.

## Valuation Framing (qualitative)

KITE is a **forward-looking infrastructure bet on the agent economy, priced at a ~$1.67B FDV on largely unproven usage**. The valuation embeds the [[ai-agent-tokens]] / [[decentralized-ai]] narrative — one of the most hyped 2025–2026 themes — rather than demonstrated on-chain agent transaction volume. Two structural headwinds compound: (1) only ~23% of supply circulates, so unlocks add persistent sell pressure; (2) the thesis depends on autonomous agents actually transacting at scale via x402, which is still emerging. Reflexivity cuts both ways: KITE rallied on narrative and is now down ~48% from ATH and ~14% on the week as the narrative cools into an extreme-fear tape. A durable re-rate would need measurable, recurring agent payment throughput on the chain — not just listings or partnerships. Qualitative framing only — not a price target.

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Kite's pitch is infrastructure for the **agentic economy**: AI agents that can autonomously pay for compute, data, APIs, and services need (1) a verifiable identity layer, (2) programmable spending controls/guardrails, and (3) low-friction stablecoin-style payments. Native **x402** support (the HTTP 402 "Payment Required" pattern revived for machine-to-machine payments) is its headline feature. The token's narrative fits the broader [[ai-agent-tokens]] and [[decentralized-ai]] themes — one of the most hyped 2025–2026 crypto narratives, and correspondingly one of the most reflexive on the way down.

## Risks

- **Unlock / dilution overhang (high):** MC/FDV ~0.23 means most supply is still locked; scheduled vesting is a structural headwind.
- **Hype-cycle / narrative risk:** AI-agent tokens are sentiment-driven and high-beta; a cooling of the AI narrative or a broader risk-off (already underway per the [[crypto-fear-and-greed-index|Fear & Greed]] reading of 23) hits these names disproportionately.
- **Unproven adoption:** The agent-payments thesis is largely forward-looking; real, sustained on-chain agent transaction volume is unverified. Valuation currently prices a narrative, not demonstrated usage.
- **Liquidity / volatility:** Free-float liquidity is thinner than headline cap implies; sharp drawdowns (~-48% from ATH) underline the volatility.
- **Competition:** Multiple chains and middleware projects are chasing the same agent-economy / x402 niche.
