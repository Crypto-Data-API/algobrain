# Score

**Score** (SN44) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#522** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** sports AI analytics and computer vision — the Score Vision team applies ML to sports video and event data. Miners produce predictions / scoring analytics; validators evaluate accuracy against ground truth.
- **Team:** Score Vision (scorevision.io).
- **Trading relevance:** **directly tradable** via sports-betting markets. Overlaps with SN41 [[sportstensor]]; traders can consume either/both subnets as inputs to sports-betting strategies.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-44.
