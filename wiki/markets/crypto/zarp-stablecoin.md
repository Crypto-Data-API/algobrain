# ZARP Stablecoin

**ZARP Stablecoin** (ZARP) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#1637** by market capitalization. ZARP is a collateralised stablecoin pegged to the price of the South African Rand with treasury managed by Old Mutual Wealth.
