# Ribbita by Virtuals

**Ribbita by Virtuals** (ticker **TIBBIR**) is an AI-themed token in the [[virtuals-protocol|Virtuals Protocol]] ecosystem on the [[base|Base]] chain. CoinGecko classifies it across the *Meme*, *AI Meme*, *Base Ecosystem*, *Virtuals Protocol Ecosystem* and *DeFAI* ([[defai|decentralized AI finance]]) categories — placing it squarely in the [[ai-agent-tokens|AI-agent/DeFAI meta]] rather than the infrastructure tier. The project markets itself as a stealth-launched effort to improve efficiency and security across the crypto and AI sectors, and is informally associated in community discussion with the fintech VC firm Ribbit Capital and with participation in an "Agentic Finance" hackathon.

## Architecture / how it works

Public technical detail is thin by design — the project was stealth-launched and the linked "website" is its [[virtuals-protocol|Virtuals]] launch page (`app.virtuals.io/virtuals/18820`) rather than a standalone product site. TIBBIR is best understood as a **token launched through the Virtuals Protocol agent-launchpad framework**, which mints and bonds AI-agent tokens on Base. Community framing positions it within "DeFAI" — agents that automate or assist on-chain financial actions — and around an agentic-finance hackathon narrative tied to Ribbit Capital. As of this snapshot the wiki has no verified evidence of a live, differentiated product beyond the token and its narrative; treat the AI utility as aspirational.

### How the Virtuals launchpad shapes the token

Virtuals Protocol is an agent-tokenization platform on [[base|Base]]: every project launched through it follows a standardized lifecycle that is more important to TIBBIR's mechanics than any bespoke technology:

1. **Bonding curve / launch** — the token is minted and seeded with bonded liquidity on a Virtuals-managed curve; early price discovery happens on-chain rather than via a CEX listing.
2. **Graduation to a DEX pool** — once a token "graduates," liquidity migrates to a standard Base DEX (Uniswap-style) pool paired against the [[virtuals-protocol|VIRTUAL]] token or WETH, so VIRTUAL's own price feeds reflexively into TIBBIR.
3. **Agent association** — Virtuals tokens are nominally tied to an AI agent; in TIBBIR's case the agent/product surface is unverified, so the token trades as a **narrative/meme claim on the Virtuals + DeFAI meta**, not on agent revenue.

The practical consequence: TIBBIR's beta is dominated by (a) [[virtuals-protocol|Virtuals Protocol]] ecosystem flows and (b) the broader [[ai-agents|AI-agent meta]], with idiosyncratic Ribbit Capital / agentic-finance narrative layered on top.

## Tokenomics & supply

- **Supply:** ~999.9M circulating / ~999.9M total / 1.00B max — fully (or near-fully) distributed.
- **FDV ≈ MC ($114.75M):** minimal future dilution risk, which removes one of the standard bear-case mechanics for low-float AI tokens.
- **Standard Virtuals launch mechanics** apply (bonded liquidity at launch); holders are exposed to the token's price, not to protocol cash flows.

### Value accrual / utility

There is no verified mechanism by which TIBBIR captures cash flow. Value accrues purely through narrative and attention: demand rotates in when the Virtuals/DeFAI meta is hot or the Ribbit-Capital/agentic-finance story gains traction, and out when attention fades. The one genuine structural positive is the **clean float** — with MC ≈ FDV, there is no scheduled unlock to suppress price, so TIBBIR avoids the slow-bleed dilution that drags low-float peers. The flip side is there is also no "scarcity unlock" catalyst; price is entirely demand-driven.

## Trading playbook

TIBBIR is a reflexive AI-meme whose realistic edge is event-driven, not buy-and-hold, especially in the current Extreme-Fear/Bottoming tape:

- **Trade the meta, not the coin:** TIBBIR is highly correlated to [[virtuals-protocol|VIRTUAL]] and the AI-agent narrative — confirm the meta is bid before taking idiosyncratic risk; in a risk-off tape the whole basket bleeds together.
- **Liquidity-first sizing:** at <1% daily turnover, a market order can move price several percent. Use limit orders, scale in/out, and assume wide spreads.
- **Catalyst fade vs follow-through:** stealth/opaque tokens spike on rumor (e.g., Ribbit-Capital chatter) and often round-trip; prefer fading unverified-narrative spikes over chasing them.
- **No hedge:** no recorded perp market, so directional risk cannot be hedged cheaply — treat any position as one-directional spot exposure.

This is not investment advice; TIBBIR is a speculative AI-meme token.
