# ChangeNOW

**ChangeNOW** is a **non-custodial instant crypto-exchange / swap service** that lets users convert between hundreds of assets without registration or holding funds on the platform — orders are routed and settled programmatically rather than held in user accounts. **NOW** is the native utility token of the ChangeNOW ecosystem, used for cashback, staking, fee discounts, and payments across the platform's product suite (the swap service, NOW Wallet, NOWPayments, and NOW Nodes).

## Overview

**ChangeNOW** is a non-custodial **instant exchange / swap aggregator**: a user selects a "from" and "to" asset, sends funds to a generated address, and receives the converted asset at the destination address. Because the service does not require account registration or custody of user balances between trades, it differs from a traditional centralized order-book exchange — it functions more like a routing and settlement layer over liquidity sources. Supported assets number in the hundreds across many chains.

The broader ecosystem includes related products that the **NOW** token plugs into:

- **NOW Wallet** — a multi-asset crypto wallet
- **NOWPayments** — a crypto payment-processing/gateway service for merchants
- **NOW Nodes** — blockchain node/RPC infrastructure access
- **ChangeNOW Pro** — a trading/staking surface for NOW holders

### Token role (NOW)

NOW is the ecosystem's utility token. Its documented uses include:

- **[[Staking]]** via ChangeNOW Pro to earn rewards (the project has advertised an APY of roughly **~6.25%**, with weekly reward accrual)
- **Cashback** on swaps performed via ChangeNOW / NOW Wallet
- **Payment / fee** utility across NOWPayments, NOW Nodes, AML-check services, and partner perks
- **Distribution** as rewards through airdrops and community campaigns

The project has historically described **periodic (quarterly) token burns** intended to reduce supply over time. NOW is issued as a token on Binance Chain / BNB Chain and [[ethereum]] rather than running its own [[layer-1]]; security therefore inherits from those host chains rather than a dedicated consensus network.

> *Note: APY, burn cadence, and product specifics are project-stated and may change; treat them as descriptive of the design rather than guaranteed current terms.*

### Competitive position

ChangeNOW competes in the instant-swap / non-custodial exchange niche alongside services such as SimpleSwap, StealthEX, FixedFloat, and Changelly, and indirectly with on-chain [[decentralized-exchange]] aggregators (e.g., 1inch) and CEX "convert" features. Its differentiators are no-registration UX, broad asset coverage, and a bundled product suite (wallet, payments, nodes) that the NOW token spans.

Most direct competitors run **without a token**, so NOW's investment case is distinctive: it is one of the few instant-swap services where holders can attach a tokenized claim (cashback, fee discounts, staking, burns) to platform activity. The flip side is that the value link is indirect and project-controlled — see [[centralized-exchange-tokens]] for the general pattern.

### Value accrual & governance (NOW)

NOW's value-capture mechanics are **fee/discount- and burn-oriented** rather than governance-driven:

- **Fee discounts / cashback** route a share of swap economics back to holders, creating utility demand proportional to platform volume.
- **Staking** locks float (advertised ~6.25% APY) and removes sell-side supply while paid.
- **Periodic burns** (project-stated, historically quarterly) are the main deflationary sink — if real and sustained, they tighten supply as usage grows. Verify burn execution on-chain rather than relying on project framing.
- There is **no strong on-chain governance role** documented for NOW; it is primarily a loyalty/utility asset, not a protocol-control token.

## Risks

- **Regulatory / AML exposure:** Non-custodial instant-swap services that minimize KYC are a recurring focus of regulators and chain-analytics scrutiny; tightening rules could affect availability or volumes.
- **Revenue concentration:** Token value is tied to ChangeNOW platform usage; a downturn in swap volume or competition from CEX "convert" features and DEX aggregators pressures the thesis.
- **Off-chain trust:** As an instant-exchange operator, settlement quality and rates depend on the company and its liquidity partners — a different trust model than a fully on-chain [[decentralized-exchange]].
- **Small-cap liquidity:** Rank #660 (~$28.9M); thin depth means slippage and gap risk (see [[risk-management]]), amplified in the current "Extreme Fear" regime.
- **Project-stated terms can change:** Advertised staking APY (~6.25%) and burn cadence are not guarantees.
