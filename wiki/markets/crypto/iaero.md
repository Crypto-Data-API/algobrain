## Overview

The iAero Protocol is a decentralized staking and rewards infrastructure built on Base. It enables users to lock and stake iAERO and LIQ to earn yield sourced from protocol revenue, DEX incentives, and on-chain activity. The system includes a Permalock Vault, epoch-based reward distribution, bribe harvesting, and automated reward conversion. All contracts are open-source and fully on-chain, with transparent reporting, token economics, and weekly distribution data.
