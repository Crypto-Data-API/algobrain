# Cryptocurrency

**Cryptocurrency** refers to digital or virtual currencies that use cryptographic techniques for security and operate on decentralized blockchain networks. [[bitcoin]], launched in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto, was the first cryptocurrency. The ecosystem has since expanded to include thousands of tokens spanning smart contract platforms ([[ethereum]]), stablecoins, [[defi]] protocols, [[nft]] marketplaces, and more.

Cryptocurrency markets trade 24/7 across centralized exchanges (Binance, Coinbase) and [[decentralized-exchanges]] (Uniswap, dYdX), with total market capitalization that has ranged from under $100 billion to over $3 trillion.

For the full overview, see [[crypto-markets]].
