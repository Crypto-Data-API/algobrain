# River

**River** (ticker **RIVER**) is a **chain-abstraction stablecoin / [[defi|DeFi]]** protocol — formerly associated with Satoshi Protocol — building an "omni-CDP" system that lets users post collateral on one chain and mint its stablecoin **satUSD** on another, without bridging the underlying assets. RIVER is the governance/utility token; it is deployed across [[ethereum|Ethereum]], Base, and [[bnb|BNB Chain]] at the same address. (Note: this is the omni-CDP stablecoin "River" — not an unrelated project sharing the name.)

## Technology / what it does

River's core innovation is the **omni-CDP** (collateralized debt position) module. In a conventional CDP (e.g., MakerDAO), a user locks collateral and mints a stablecoin on the same chain. River's design lets a user collateralize assets on Chain A and mint **satUSD** on Chain B, abstracting away the bridging step. The goal is cross-chain collateral, yield, and liquidity that behave natively across ecosystems — a "chain-abstraction" approach to stablecoin issuance and leverage. The GitHub org (Satoshi-Protocol) reflects its lineage.

satUSD is the protocol's omni-CDP stablecoin; RIVER captures governance and value-accrual for the system.
