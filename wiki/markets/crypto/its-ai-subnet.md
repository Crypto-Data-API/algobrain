# It's AI (SN32)

**It's AI** (SN32) is a Bittensor subnet for **AI-generated text detection** -- miners submit classifiers that identify whether a passage was written by a human or generated by an LLM; validators score accuracy on labeled datasets.

## How It Works

The subnet runs classifiers against a rotating benchmark of AI-generated and human-written text, across domains (academic, journalistic, casual). Validators score precision and recall, penalizing both false positives and false negatives.

## Trading Relevance

Indirect. Text-authenticity detection is a moderation and trust/safety primitive — an adjacent concept to SN34 [[bitmind|BitMind]] (which does deepfake detection for images/video). Not directly a trading signal. Alpha-32 is a bet on whether AI-detection becomes a mainstream B2B service and whether SN32 captures a share of that market.
