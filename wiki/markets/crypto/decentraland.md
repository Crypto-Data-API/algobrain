# Decentraland

**Decentraland** (MANA) is a browser-based, DAO-governed virtual world — one of the original blockchain [[metaverse|metaverses]] — where users buy, build on, and trade virtual LAND parcels as [[nft|NFTs]]. **MANA** is its ERC-20 currency on [[ethereum]] (bridged to [[polygon]]), used to buy LAND, wearables, and names, and to vote in the Decentraland DAO. Launched in 2017, it is the long-standing peer of [[the-sandbox|The Sandbox]] (SAND) as a reference "metaverse / play-to-earn" token and is part of the [[animoca-brands|Animoca Brands]]-adjacent metaverse cluster.

## Tokenomics & Supply

MANA has **no hard max supply** (it is uncapped), with ~1.94B circulating and ~2.19B total (MC/FDV ~0.88). Originally MANA was burned to mint LAND (deflationary pressure), and the Decentraland DAO controls a large treasury and the issuance of new wearables/names. MANA is used to: purchase LAND and estates, buy wearables/emotes/names in the marketplace, and vote in the **Decentraland DAO**, which governs the protocol, LAND auctions, and grants. Two NFT classes anchor the economy — **LAND** (fixed grid of parcels) and **wearables/Names** (collectibles).

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

Decentraland is a charter member of the **[[metaverse]] / NFT / [[gamefi|GameFi]] / play-to-earn** category and is run by one of crypto's earliest fully on-chain DAOs. The product is a 3D virtual world accessible in-browser: users explore, build experiences on their LAND, attend events (concerts, fashion weeks, conferences), and trade NFTs. Its narrative is the **purest "own a piece of the metaverse" thesis** — LAND scarcity plus DAO governance — and it rose alongside [[the-sandbox|SAND]] during the 2021 metaverse mania. The bear case is that concurrent-user counts have remained low relative to the valuation of LAND, and the metaverse narrative has cooled sharply, leaving MANA dependent on a renewed hype cycle.

## Valuation Framing

MANA defies cash-flow valuation; it is best framed qualitatively:

- **LAND-scarcity thesis** — the bull case is that a fixed grid of LAND parcels is genuinely scarce digital real estate, so MANA (the only currency to buy LAND) accrues value as the world is built out and used. The bear case is "digital ghost town": LAND market caps have historically dwarfed actual concurrent-user activity.
- **Narrative-beta** — MANA's price is dominated by the metaverse hype cycle, not protocol fundamentals. It re-rates violently up in risk-on/narrative phases (2021; the +7% 2026-06-20 flare) and bleeds in between. It is effectively a leveraged call option on the metaverse narrative returning.
- **DAO treasury & uncapped supply** — the Decentraland DAO controls a large treasury and the issuance of new wearables/names, adding governance-driven supply/demand variables outside a simple tokenomics model.

## Notable History

- **2017** — ICO raises ~$24M; genesis date 2017-09-06.
- **2020 (Feb)** — Decentraland goes live to the public; LAND becomes tradable NFTs.
- **2021 (Nov)** — All-time high near **$5.85** during the metaverse mania; Decentraland hosts high-profile virtual events.
- **2022 (Feb)** — Hosts "Metaverse Fashion Week"; LAND speculation peaks.
- **2022-2024** — Long decline; reports of very low daily active users weigh on sentiment as the NFT/metaverse market deflates.
- **2026 (Jun)** — Trades around **$0.0706** (rank ~#218), ~98.8% below ATH, amid extreme fear and an established bear market; a +7% 24h metaverse-narrative flare in the 2026-06-20 snapshot.

## Risks

- **Narrative dependence** — value is tightly coupled to the metaverse/NFT hype cycle; weak organic usage between cycles.
- **Low engagement vs valuation** — persistently low concurrent users relative to LAND market caps invite "ghost town" criticism.
- **Bear-market beta** — small-cap, high-beta; can underperform [[bitcoin]]/[[ethereum]] in extreme-fear regimes.
- **Regulatory** — MANA carries an "Alleged SEC Securities" tag, reflecting US regulatory overhang.
- **Uncapped supply / DAO discretion** — issuance of wearables/names and treasury decisions sit with the DAO, adding governance and dilution uncertainty.
