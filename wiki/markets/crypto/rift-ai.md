## Overview

Trade stocks, crypto, commodities, and forex 24/7, all from a single app. Powered by AI trade signals, real-time news, and mobile one-tap execution. Most trading apps close when Wall Street closes. Rift doesn't. Trade spot equities, commodities, crypto, and forex every minute of every day - with the same tools, the same analytics, and the same execution speed whether it's Tuesday at 10 AM or Sunday at 3 AM.

Rift is backed by a16z, Sequoia Capital, and Lightspeed Venture Partners.
