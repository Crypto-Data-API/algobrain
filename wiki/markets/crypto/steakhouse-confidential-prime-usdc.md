## Overview

Steakhouse Confidential Prime USDC (steakcUSDC) is the share token of a Morpho Vault v2 on Ethereum curated by Steakhouse Financial. The vault generates yield by lending USDC against blue-chip collateral such as cbBTC, WBTC and wstETH through Morpho's lending infrastructure. Depositors deposit Confidential USDC (cUSDC, an ERC-7984 token whose balances are encrypted using fully homomorphic encryption) via app.zama.org, and ownership is represented through confidential vault shares.
