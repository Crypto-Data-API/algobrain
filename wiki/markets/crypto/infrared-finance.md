# Infrared Finance

**Infrared Finance** (IR) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#2009** by market capitalization. Infrared is focused on building infrastructure around the Proof of Liquidity (PoL) mechanism pioneered by Berachain. The protocol aims to maximize value capture by providing easy-to-use liquid solutions for BGT and BERA, node infrastructure, and vaults.

## Overview

Infrared is focused on building infrastructure around the Proof of Liquidity (PoL) mechanism pioneered by Berachain. The protocol aims to maximize value capture by providing easy-to-use liquid solutions for BGT and BERA, node infrastructure, and vaults. Through building solutions around Proof of Liquidity (PoL), Infrared is dedicated to enhancing the user experience and driving the growth of the Berachain ecosystem.
