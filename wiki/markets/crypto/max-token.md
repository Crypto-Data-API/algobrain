MAX Token is the utility token issued by MaiCoin Asset Exchange (MAX). MAX Exchange is focused on supporting its trading community and providing the most secure trading platform. MAX Tokens will be rewarded through Transaction Mining and can also be used for Staking on the platform to receive rewards.
