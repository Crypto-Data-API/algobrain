## Overview

VIN is an open-source decentralized DeFi project that launches a brand-new DeFi model—linked with the YFSX token, interconnected across multiple pools, with every transaction receiving dividends and being burned. It is governed by the community, where holders are shareholders and LPs are ecosystem owners, building a robust and sustainable value ecosystem based on long-term consensus.VIN Token is a versatile BEP-20 standard token built on BNB Chain, leveraging the network’s low gas fees, lightning-fast transactions, and robust EVM compatibility to deliver utility-driven value. Designed for seamless integration with BNB Chain’s thriving ecosystem, it aligns with the network’s ethos of accessibility and innovation, supporting connections with leading DEXs like PancakeSwap, DeFi platforms, and digital wallets.
