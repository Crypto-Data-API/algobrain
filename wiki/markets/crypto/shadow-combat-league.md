## Overview

Shadow Combat League (SCL) is a next-generation combat sports league where humanoid robots compete in real-world arenas, powered by AI, robotics, and blockchain technology.

The project combines the spectacle of elite sports organizations like UFC and Formula 1 with cutting-edge robotics to create a new form of global entertainment.

At its core, SCL is building a tokenized ecosystem that connects:

Live robotic fight events

Fans and spectators

Teams and robot operators

Investors and partners

The SCL token powers participation across the ecosystem, including:

Event access and fan engagement

Team and fighter backing

Governance

Betting and prediction markets

Through a structured league system (Dominion, Forge, Foundation, and Shadow divisions), SCL enables both elite competition and global participation.
