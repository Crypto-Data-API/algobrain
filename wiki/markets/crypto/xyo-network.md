# XYO Network

**XYO Network** (XYO) is one of the original and largest [[depin|DePIN]] (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network) projects, built on [[ethereum|Ethereum]] as an ERC-20 token. XYO specializes in collecting and validating real-world data — particularly **location data** — and bridging it to Web3 and Web2 consumers across AI, geolocation, and IoT. Its core technologies are **Proof of Location** and **Proof of Origin**, cryptographic methods for attesting where and how data was produced.

The network reports a very large device footprint (the team has cited 10M+ nodes), driven largely by its consumer **COIN app**, which lets users earn rewards for geomining and validating location. XYO has since expanded with the **XYO Layer One (XL1)** blockchain, a purpose-built chain for DePIN/RWA/AI data workloads, alongside a dual-token model (XYO as the externally traded ecosystem token; XL1 as the internal gas/utility token).

## Use Case, Narrative & Category

XYO sits in **DePIN** with a location-data / IoT / oracle flavor, and is categorized under Internet of Things (IoT), Oracle, NFT, Metaverse, and Ethereum Ecosystem. Use cases include Real-World Asset (RWA) tracking, location verification, real-world gaming/treasure hunts, and supplying validated geospatial data to AI and enterprise consumers.

The hardware-network thesis: instead of building proprietary location infrastructure, XYO crowdsources device-level location attestation (phones via COIN, sentinels, bridges) and pays contributors in token, then monetizes validated data to Web2/Web3 buyers. Differentiators are its large existing node base and long operating history. A unique angle is the corporate structure: the for-profit **XY Labs Inc.** was the first U.S. crypto company qualified by the SEC for a Regulation A offering, and its tokenized shares ($XYLB) trade on the tZERO ATS — tying XYO to the [[real-world-assets|RWA]]-tokenization narrative.

## Valuation Framing

XYO is a **cycle-old DePIN survivor priced on accumulated history rather than pending unlocks**. The standout structural feature is that supply is effectively fully circulating (MC ≈ FDV ≈ $48.8M), so — unlike newer DePIN launches such as [[deep|DeepBook]] or [[keeta|Keeta]] — there is no emission/unlock overhang dragging price; what you see is what the market is. The qualitative bull case is that the large existing node base (the team cites 10M+ devices via COIN) plus the new XL1 chain converts crowdsourced location/RWA data into recurring revenue from paying Web2/Web3 buyers. The bear case is twofold: (1) node count is a vanity metric — value requires actually *selling* validated data — and (2) the standing "Alleged SEC Securities" tag is a live regulatory overhang that has affected exchange availability for similarly-flagged tokens. At ~$48.8M cap the market prices XYO as a small-cap DePIN option with both a real installed base and an unresolved regulatory question.

## Overview

XYO is the original and one of the largest Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks (DePIN), boasting over 10 million nodes and consistently leading DePIN in both scale and revenue generation. Launching its Mainnet ahead of any other DePIN, XYO specializes in collecting and validating real-world data, bridging it seamlessly to Web3 and Web2 companies across AI, geolocation, and other critical data sectors.
At the core of XYO are Proof of Location and Proof of Origin technologies, enabling accurate validation of real-world data for innovative use cases such as Real-World Asset (RWA) tracking, DePIN applications, real-world gaming, and treasure hunts. Recently, XYO expanded its ecosystem by launching the XYO Layer One blockchain, purpose-built to support the intensive data demands of DePIN, RWA, AI, and similar data-driven industries.
XYO employs a unique dual-token model:
XYO Token: An externally traded token supporting the DePIN ecosystem, rewards, staking, and governance, enhancing overall ecosystem value and scarcity.

XL1 Token: Internal to the XYO Layer One blockchain, facilitating transactions, gas fees, and other blockchain-specific use cases.
The dual-token economics are strategically designed to increase scarcity and promote sustainable growth. The XYO token is available on major exchanges, including Coinbase. The XL1 token will launch publicly later in 2025.
To accelerate network adoption and growth, XYO created the popular COIN app. Beyond the XYO Foundation, the team founded the U.S.-based, for-profit entity XY Labs Inc., notable as the first U.S. crypto company qualified by the SEC for a Regulation A offering, permitting share sales to both accredited and non-accredited investors. XY Labs further stands out as one of the pioneering U.S. companies to tokenize and publicly list its shares. These tokenized shares trade as $XYLB on the tZERO ATS, positioning XY Labs prominently in the growing wave of Real-World Asset (RWA) tokenization.
