# ECOMI

**ECOMI** (ticker **OMI**) is the issuer of the OMI token, the utility currency of the **VeVe** digital-collectibles ecosystem — a mobile-first marketplace for licensed-IP NFTs (Marvel, DC, and other brand collaborations). OMI is an ERC-20-style token deployed across [[ethereum|Ethereum]], Base, and Energi, and it functions as the in-app currency and loyalty mechanism for the VeVe app. As a digital-collectibles/NFT-app token, OMI is highly cyclical and closely tied to the boom-bust dynamics of the NFT collectibles market.

## Notable History

- **2021 mania peak:** OMI hit its $0.01343 ATH on 2021-03-19 during the height of the NFT collectibles boom, as VeVe drops (notably Marvel) drew large crowds.
- **Post-mania decline:** OMI fell ~98.5% from ATH as NFT collectibles speculation deflated; VeVe has continued operating and expanding licensing/IP deals but the token has not recovered.
- **Multi-chain deployment:** OMI is now bridged across [[ethereum|Ethereum]], Base, and Energi.

## Risks

- **NFT-marketplace cyclicality:** OMI is high-beta to the boom-bust digital-collectibles market; the -98.5% ATH drawdown and ~-14.8% weekly drop illustrate the downside.
- **IP/licensing dependence:** VeVe's value proposition relies on licensed-IP partnerships; loss or non-renewal of key brand deals would hurt demand.
- **Platform/counterparty risk:** OMI value depends on VeVe's continued operation; it is an app-utility token without a claim on assets.
- **Large supply / dilution:** A 750B max supply caps unit price upside and leaves uncapped issuance headroom above the current ~311B total.
- **Liquidity / venue concentration:** Thin volume and limited CEX support raise execution and manipulation risk.
- **Regime risk:** Speculative NFT/collectibles tokens are especially fragile in an [[crypto-fear-and-greed-index|extreme-fear]] bear market.
