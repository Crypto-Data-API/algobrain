## Overview

Tung Tung Tung Sahur ($TripleT) is a community-driven memecoin on Solana, launched fairly via pump.fun with no pre-mints. It draws from a viral TikTok "brainrot" meme featuring an AI-generated wooden creature that mimics "tung tung tung" drum sounds to wake people for Sahur during Ramadan in Indonesia and Malaysia. Blending cultural tradition with absurd horror-comedy, it became a sensation with tens of millions of views, UGC, and crossovers into NBA, Roblox, Fortnite, and potential films. As the longest-lasting brainrot character, $TripleT celebrates meme culture's virality, fostering a loyal holder base for fun, hype, and potential growth in the AI-meme space.
