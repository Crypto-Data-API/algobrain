# Cortex (SN18)

**Cortex** (SN18) is a Bittensor subnet providing decentralized LLM inference, historically associated with Corcel's API product. Miners serve text completions; validators score quality, latency, and consistency. The subnet competes directly with SN1 [[apex-5|Apex]], SN4 [[targon]], SN19 [[nineteen-ai|Nineteen]], and SN64 [[chutes]] for inference demand.

## How It Works

Miners host LLMs and respond to validator queries. Validators benchmark responses against reference model outputs and score accuracy, latency, and uptime. Corcel's consumer-facing product (Corcel Chat) routes some of its inference traffic through SN18, giving the subnet visible off-chain demand.

## Trading Relevance

Inference-category exposure. Alpha-18's fundamental-value case rests on whether SN18 captures inference revenue through Corcel's products or direct API customers. The inference category is crowded -- SN64 Chutes has pulled ahead in emission share by combining inference with serverless compute positioning.
