## Overview

DIVO is a SocialFi platform that empowers fashion and advertising professionals to monetize their content, protect intellectual property, and build audience relationships using advanced AI tools. At its core, DIVO is a multi-functional platform designed to serve as a professional-grade social media network for the global fashion industry. It provides creators, agencies, and brands with the tools to publish content, protect ownership, transact securely, and collaborate across borders—without intermediaries.
