## Overview

Brickken enables businesses to create digital tokens backed by tokenized Real World Assets (RWA). Through the Token Suite, Clients can raise private or public capital by creating their own custom white-label token store &amp; token offering. 
Capturing the whole token lifecycle, token issuers can also manage both their assets &amp; investors under a seamless dashboard enabling cap table management, dividend distribution, and full investor - compliance.
