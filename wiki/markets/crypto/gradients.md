# Gradients

**Gradients** (SN56) is a Artificial Intelligence (AI), Bittensor Ecosystem, Bittensor Subnets project. It ranks **#850** by market capitalization.

## Overview

- **What it does:** no-code AutoML fine-tuning. Users submit a dataset and evaluation criteria; the subnet runs a tournament among miners to produce the best fine-tuned model. Described as "Kaggle for fine-tuning on Bittensor."
- **Team:** Rayon Labs (also builds SN19 [[nineteen-ai|Nineteen]] and Rayon Finance).
- **Trading relevance:** training-infrastructure play with a productized end-user workflow. Alpha-56 has tracked Rayon Labs' product launch cadence closely.
- **Alpha token:** alpha-56.
