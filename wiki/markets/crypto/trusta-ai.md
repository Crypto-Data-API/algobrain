## Overview

Trusta.AI is devoted to leveraging revolutionary AI innovation breakthroughs to bring fundamental changes to the entire AI + Crypto ecosystem. We believe that by bringing "more trust, less friction" in transactions, we can create a smoother and more secure digital experience for human users and AI agents.

Trusta.AI's vision is to build a Trusted Identity Network for Crypto-Intelligence (Crypto+AI). In this ecosystem that encompasses both Human Intelligence and Artificial Intelligence, using Trusta Identity as the foundation, all data, reputation, and credit will be accumulated. Ultimately, we will establish a universal credit infrastructure for all intelligences (human intelligence + artificial intelligence). But now, We believe it is time to decentralize Trusta.AI for adapting to the coming of AI era.
