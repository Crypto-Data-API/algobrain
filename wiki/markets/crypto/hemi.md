**Hemi** (HEMI) is a cryptocurrency. It ranks **#1664** by market capitalization. The Hemi Network represents a different way of thinking about Layer-2 scaling, by approaching Bitcoin and Ethereum as components of a supernetwork.

This modular protocol aims to scale these networks and maximize their utility, creating a more connected and efficient blockchain ecosystem.

## Trading Profile

### Venues & liquidity

HEMI trades across a deep, liquid two-venue market. It is available on **Binance** (HEMI/USDT spot plus a USD-margined perpetual) and on **Hyperliquid** (HEMI-PERP, with leverage up to roughly 40-50x). The Binance spot and perp listing anchors centralized order-flow and price discovery, while the on-chain Hyperliquid perp adds a transparent, funding-driven venue. Having both a CEX and a DEX perp means traders can source liquidity on either side, cross-check funding and mark prices, and construct spot-vs-perp or CEX-vs-DEX structures. Because HEMI is a low-market-cap, low-price name, on-venue depth is thinner than for large caps: size positions with the order book in mind, expect wider slippage on large clips, and lean on the two-venue split to distribute execution rather than sweeping a single book.

### Applicable strategies

- [[funding-rate-arbitrage]] — capture funding on HEMI-PERP against a spot or offsetting perp leg while netting out directional exposure.
- [[hl-vs-cex-funding-divergence]] — arbitrage funding gaps between the Hyperliquid HEMI-PERP and the Binance USD-margined perp on the same underlying.
- [[cash-and-carry]] — hold Binance/on-chain HEMI spot versus a short perp to harvest positive basis on this two-venue name.
- [[liquidation-cascade-fade]] — a thin, low-cap perp market is prone to sharp liquidation flushes; fade the over-extension once the cascade exhausts.
- [[breakout-and-retest]] — trade confirmed breakouts of range boundaries with a retest entry, suited to HEMI's low-float, headline-sensitive moves.
- [[oi-confirmed-trend]] — use Hyperliquid open-interest alongside price to separate real, OI-backed trends from thin liquidity noise.

### Volatility & regime character

HEMI is a high-beta, low-market-cap infrastructure/BTCfi altcoin (a modular Bitcoin-Ethereum Layer-2 token). It behaves with pronounced reflexivity: small notional flows move price sharply, and drawdowns from its all-time high have been severe. Directionally it tends to track broad crypto risk appetite, carrying strong positive beta to BTC and ETH, with amplified upside and downside versus those majors during trend and risk-off regimes.

### Risk flags

- **Liquidity / venue concentration** — very low market cap and 24h volume mean thin books; large orders move price and depend heavily on the two supporting venues.
- **Token unlocks / emissions** — circulating supply is a small fraction of the 10B max supply (low Market Cap / FDV ratio), so future unlocks and emissions are a persistent supply-overhang risk.
- **Narrative dependence** — as a BTCfi / modular-L2 story token, price is sensitive to sector narrative rotation and headline flow.
- **Perp funding dislocations** — in a thin two-venue perp market, funding can spike or diverge sharply between Hyperliquid and Binance, punishing crowded positioning.
