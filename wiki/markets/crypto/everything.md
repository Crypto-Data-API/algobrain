## Overview

Everything unifies multiple decentralized finance protocols into one cohesive, interoperable ecosystem. At its core, the $EV token functions as the primary mechanism for governance, utility, and value accrual, aligning incentives across all products while enabling users to participate in decision-making, access platform features, and benefit from the ecosystem’s overall growth and long-term sustainability.
