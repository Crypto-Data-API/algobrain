## Overview

Startale USD (USDSC) is Startale’s native stablecoin, fully integrated into the Startale App, Soneium and more. It enables instant onramps, seamless swaps, cross-chain transfers, and frictionless transactions across dapps and DeFi protocols. Designed for simplicity and stability, USDSC offers a dollar-denominated experience for holding, earning stable yields and transacting onchain with deep integration into Startale App for effortless access and usability.
