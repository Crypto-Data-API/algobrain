## Overview

mRe7ETH is an onchain investment product structured to reflect market-neutral DeFi ETH yield strategies deployed across blockchain ecosystems. The strategy is monitored by Re7 Labs, acting as the appointed Strategy Manager. Yield is auto-compounded into the token price, updated once per week. mRe7ETH is fully transferable, protected by Fordefi custody, and accessible to investors in eligible jurisdictions. Available on Optimism.
