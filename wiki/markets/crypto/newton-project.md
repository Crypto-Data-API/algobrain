# AB (Newton Project)

**AB** (ticker **AB**) is the rebranded continuation of the **Newton Project**, a blockchain ecosystem dating to 2018 (genesis 2018-12-18). It positions itself as a high-performance, interoperable Layer-1/sidechain ecosystem focused on **real-world asset (RWA)** integration, with the AB token currently issued as a BEP-20 on the [[bnb|BNB Chain]]. Note the snapshot symbol is **AB**, not the legacy "NEW" Newton ticker — the project migrated identity.

## Architecture / how it works

AB (formerly Newton) describes itself as an advanced blockchain ecosystem built for **decentralization, interoperability, and RWA integration**. Its stated architecture comprises:

- **High-performance mainnet** — the base settlement layer of the ecosystem.
- **Industry-specific sidechains** — purpose-built chains so different real-world-asset verticals (commerce, supply chain, etc.) can run with tailored parameters while settling to the mainnet.
- **Cross-chain compatibility** — interoperability bridges to major networks such as [[ethereum|Ethereum]] and [[solana|Solana]], so assets and the AB token can move across ecosystems.
- **Token issuance on BNB Chain** — the tradeable AB asset is a BEP-20 living in the [[bnb|BNB Chain]] ecosystem, separate from the project's own mainnet branding.

Historically, the **Newton Project (2018)** framed itself around a **community-economy / commerce** model — a "human-centered" economic infrastructure with NewPay, NewMall and similar consumer-facing products and a NewForce contributor model. The **AB rebrand** repositions the same lineage toward the more current RWA + interoperability narrative. As with most rebrands, the wiki treats forward-looking architecture claims as **self-reported and unverified**; the durable facts are the 2018 genesis, the large fixed supply, and the BNB-Chain trading footprint.

With circulating ≈ total ≈ max, the token is **effectively fully diluted** — there is no large hidden unlock schedule, which removes a major dilution risk that affects most tokens in this cohort. The large fixed supply (100B cap) keeps the per-token price in the sub-cent range, which raises the percentage move required to re-rate the market cap and creates psychological "cheap coin" optics that can cut both ways.

## Value accrual / governance

AB is the **native gas, utility, and governance** asset of the ecosystem: it pays fees across the mainnet/sidechains, is used within the project's RWA/commerce products, and underpins community governance. Because it is functionally fully circulating, value accrual depends on **demand** — real adoption of the RWA/commerce stack and Binance-ecosystem rotation — rather than on emission-scarcity dynamics. There is no large emissions buyer-of-last-resort: with no unlock cliff to absorb, the price is a relatively clean read on supply/demand for the float.

AB trades as a BEP-20 token on [[bnb|BNB Chain]] (contract `0x95034f653d5d161890836ad2b6b8cc49d14e029a`), with reported CEX pairs on **Kraken (AB/USD)** and **Bitget (AB/USDT)**. 24h volume of only ~$783K against a ~$102M market cap signals **thin liquidity** — turnover is well under 1% of market cap per day, so larger orders will likely face slippage and the order book is shallow relative to the headline cap. **No perpetual-futures (perp) listing is confirmed** in the snapshot, so there is no clean way to short or hedge AB on a derivatives venue. Because the token is ~99% circulating, there is **no unlock overhang** to fear — the trading risk is liquidity, not dilution.

## Risks

- **Thin liquidity:** ~$783K/day volume on a $102M cap makes exit liquidity poor and price prone to slippage; no perp to hedge.
- **Legacy / rebrand risk:** Newton → AB pivots can signal a search for product-market fit; long-term holders are deeply underwater (~-94% from the $0.0173 ATH).
- **Large supply:** 100B max supply keeps the unit price sub-penny and amplifies psychological resistance to upside.
- **RWA execution:** real-world asset integration is regulatory- and partnership-heavy; delivery is unproven from snapshot data alone and competitors (Chainlink, Polymesh, Centrifuge) are well ahead.
- **Macro / regime risk:** Extreme Fear ([[fear-and-greed-index|Fear & Greed]] = 21, 2026-06-23) pressures low-liquidity mid-caps disproportionately.

## Trading playbook (bear / Extreme-Fear, bottoming regime)

- **Liquidity-first sizing.** Sub-$800K daily volume means this is a small-position-only name; size as illiquid venture risk.
- **No hedge.** No perp — you cannot short or hedge AB directly; manage risk with position size and hard stops.
- **Narrative-gated entries.** In a bottoming regime, RWA/Binance-Alpha rotations can lift AB sharply, but moves fade fast without follow-through volume. Require a concrete catalyst plus rising turnover before adding.
- **Respect the "cheap coin" trap.** A sub-penny price is not inherently cheap given a 100B supply — judge by market cap and demand, not unit price.
- **Bear-market default:** watch-list / starter-size only until verifiable RWA traction or sustained volume appears.
