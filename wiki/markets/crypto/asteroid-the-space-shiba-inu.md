## Overview

Asteroid is the Space Shiba Inu designed by Liv Perrotto. In April 2026, Elon Musk honored Liv's wish for Asteroid to become the official mascot of SpaceX. Asteroid therefore represents a densely meaningful narrative encompassing a young girl's passion for space discovery and SpaceX's extraordinary vision and mission. Trading fees from this coin are directed to Liv's mother, Rebecca Perrotto, via Bags.fm, for ever.
