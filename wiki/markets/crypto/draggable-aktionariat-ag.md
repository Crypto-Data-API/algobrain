## Overview

DAKS represents a legal security token in Switzerland of the company Aktionariat AG.

Founded in 2021, [Aktionariat AG](www.aktionariat.com) ("Aktionariat") describes itself as a platform for security tokens which provides tools for corporations to sell their own shares regulated to its domiciled country and non-custodial wallet provider for investors on [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/ch/app/aktionariat/id1518326813?l=en) and [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aktionariat.app).

With headquarters in Zürich, Switzerland, Aktionariat is foremost working with swiss corperates but is planning to expand to other countries soon. As of Februar 2023, 60+ swiss corporation (29 already live) working with Aktionariat and over 8000 people use the mobile wallet to invest in this corporations.
