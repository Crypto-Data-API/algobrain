## Overview

Capybobo is building a next-generation Web3-native Art Toy IP, merging gaming, collectibles, and real-world design. Their ecosystem features a GameFi foundation, rarity-driven airdrops, NFT-linked blind boxes, and PBT (Physical-Backed Token) authentication.
Products: Skill-based mobile/web games, NFT-linked in-game skins, physical art toys/outfits, cross-redeem mechanics, and a native $CAPY token.
Customers: Web3 gamers, art toy collectors, streetwear fans, and global pop culture enthusiasts.
