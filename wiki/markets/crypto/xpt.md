## Overview

XPT – Proof of Execution Token

XPT is not a meme coin. It’s infrastructure.

Built inside the OBI real estate ecosystem, XPT is a Proof of Execution Token — meaning participation requires verifiable action within the app. It rewards real work, not speculation.

Designed to: Enable direct real estate services. Reduce unnecessary commissions; Prevent AI-driven spam, fake listings, and bot activity; Protect real users and real transactions. 

As platforms like Zillow face increasing listing manipulation, XPT creates accountability through verified execution.

This is long-term utility. Execution over hype.
