## Overview

Luxxcoin is a Solana-based “MemeFi” project that blends meme culture with game-style, on-chain participation. The protocol runs public 60-day seasons scored by a transparent formula that emphasizes time-weighted holdings, holding streaks, efficient entries, and low churn. Each season ends with a snapshot at 12:00 UTC and distribution at 18:00 UTC on the same day. The LUX token (total supply 100B) underpins the ecosystem, with 45B LUX allocated across 45 seasons (~7.5 years). There is no presale or transfer tax. Governance and treasury operations are managed by multi-sig, and official links are published through one verified handle and the project website to reduce impersonation risk.
