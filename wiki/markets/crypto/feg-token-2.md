## Overview

FEED EVERY GORILLA (FEG) is a next-gen DeFi project built exclusively on the BNB Chain, delivering sustainability, security, and continuous rewards for its community.

Our SmartDeFi™ Token Launchpad, available only on BNB Chain, is a FREE, twice-audited platform for creating secure, asset-backed crypto projects. FEG Token stakers earn rewards from transaction volume generated by FEG Token itself and all SmartDeFi projects launched on the platform.

Join us as we reshape DeFi — powered by FEG and SmartDeFi on BNB Chain!
