# Crypto Markets

**Crypto markets** encompass the global, 24/7 ecosystem for trading [[blockchain]]-based digital assets including [[bitcoin|BTC]], [[ethereum|ETH]], altcoins, stablecoins, and tokenized assets. Unlike traditional financial markets with defined trading hours, crypto trades continuously across hundreds of centralized and [[decentralized-exchanges|decentralized]] venues worldwide, making it one of the most accessible yet volatile asset classes.

---

## Market Structure

| Characteristic | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trading Hours** | 24/7/365 -- no closing bell, no weekends off |
| **Market Cap** | ~$2-3 trillion (varies widely with cycles) |
| **BTC Dominance** | 50-65% of total market cap |
| **Major Venues** | [[binance]], [[coinbase]], [[hyperliquid]], [[uniswap]], [[dydx]] |
| **Instruments** | Spot, [[perpetual-futures]], options, futures, tokenized assets |
| **Regulation** | Fragmented globally; evolving rapidly (MiCA in EU, SEC/CFTC in US) |

---

## Market Segments

- **Blue chips**: [[bitcoin|BTC]], [[ethereum|ETH]] -- highest liquidity, institutional adoption
- **Major altcoins**: [[solana|SOL]], [[bnb|BNB]], [[xrp|XRP]] -- high liquidity, established ecosystems
- **DeFi tokens**: [[uniswap|UNI]], [[aave|AAVE]], [[dydx|DYDX]] -- protocol governance and revenue sharing
- **Meme coins**: [[pepe|PEPE]], [[fartcoin|FARTCOIN]], DOGE -- speculation-driven, high volatility
- **AI/Narrative tokens**: [[tao|TAO]], RNDR -- sector rotation plays
- **Stablecoins**: USDT, USDC -- the settlement layer and "dry powder" indicator

---

## Key Differences from Traditional Markets

- **Volatility** -- 50-100% annualized vol is normal; 10-20% daily moves during events
- **Leverage** -- up to 50x on [[perpetual-futures]] (vs 2-4x typical equity margin)
- **Settlement** -- on-chain settlement is final and transparent; no T+1 delay
- **Custody options** -- [[self-custody]] eliminates counterparty risk (but adds personal responsibility)
- **Data transparency** -- [[on-chain-analysis]] provides real-time flow data unavailable in TradFi

---

## Trading Relevance

- 24/7 trading means overnight and weekend risk is always present -- position sizing must account for this
- Crypto markets are highly reflexive: narratives, social media, and funding rates create amplified feedback loops
- Cross-venue arbitrage between centralized exchanges and [[decentralized-exchanges|DEXs]] is a major strategy
- [[bitcoin-etfs|ETF flows]] increasingly link crypto to traditional market hours and macro sentiment
- [[crypto-winter|Crypto winters]] and [[halving]] cycles create multi-year structural trading opportunities
