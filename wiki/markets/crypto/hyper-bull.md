## Overview

HyperBull is a non-custodial Solana token built as an open beta to amplify $ANSEM holders. It turns real trading activity into recurring yield: fees generated by the HBULL/SOL market are split, with 20% going to liquidity providers and 80% flowing to a treasury that buys back $ANSEM and redistributes it as yield through ANSEM/HBULL pools. The result is a self-reinforcing loop that rewards people for holding and providing liquidity to both tokens rather than a one-time emission.
