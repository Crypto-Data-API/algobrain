# Coin98

**Coin98** (C98) is a **multi-chain DeFi wallet and "super-app"** — not a layer-1 blockchain itself — that bundles a non-custodial wallet, a liquidity-aggregating exchange, and a cross-chain bridge into one interface. Its goal is to be a gateway connecting mainstream users to [[defi]] across many networks. The **C98 token** is the platform's native utility and governance token. Coin98 was an early **Binance Launchpad** project (2021) and is built by the Vietnam-based team **Coin98 Labs**. It ranks **#984** by market capitalization.

## Overview

Coin98 is an all-in-one DeFi Platform that aims to fulfill untapped demand in the industry and become a Gateway bridging TradFi users to any DeFi services on multiple blockchains. It accomplishes this mission through a full suite of products, including Coin98 Wallet, Coin98 Exchange, and Space Gate (cross-chain bridge).

Coin98 Wallet: Allows users to store, send, receive, manage crypto assets and connect to numerous dApps on multiple blockchains. It supports over 20 blockchains such as Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Solana, Polygon, Avalanche, Terra, etc on both mobile (iOS &amp; android) and Chrome extension versions.

Coin98 Exchange: A multichain liquidity aggregator that allows users to swap, stake, lend, borrow, earn crypto with the best rates and low slippage.

Space Gate: A cross-chain bridge that enables swapping and transferring values across multiple networks. It supports token swaps between ERC20 tokens, BEP20 tokens, SPL tokens, and more.

The C98 token is the native utility token of the Coin98 platform and will be used to pay services fees, as staking incentives, for governance, and unique membership rights.

### What distinguishes Coin98

Coin98 is best understood as **application/middleware infrastructure rather than its own L1**: it sits on top of [[ethereum]], [[bnb]] Chain, [[solana]], Polygon, and ~20 other networks, abstracting away the complexity of moving between them. Its value proposition is **convenience and aggregation** — a single non-custodial app for swapping, staking, lending, borrowing, and bridging — which competes with other multi-chain wallets (e.g., [[metamask|MetaMask]], Trust Wallet) on UX and chain coverage rather than on novel base-layer technology. The team has also expanded toward broader Web3 super-app ambitions over time.

## Architecture: How the Coin98 Stack Works (deep dive)

Coin98 is a **product suite**, not a chain. Its components:

- **Coin98 Wallet (Super App)** — a non-custodial mobile (iOS/Android) and browser-extension wallet supporting 20+ chains. Keys are held on-device; the app manages multiple account derivations across EVM, Solana (SPL), and other ecosystems from one interface. Its differentiator versus [[metamask|MetaMask]] is broad **non-EVM coverage** (Solana, Cosmos-style chains, etc.) in a single app.
- **Coin98 Exchange (DEX aggregator)** — routes swaps across underlying AMMs/DEXs to find best execution and low slippage, plus staking/lending/borrowing entry points. It is an aggregation layer on top of existing liquidity, not its own AMM.
- **Space Gate (cross-chain bridge)** — moves value between networks (ERC-20 ↔ BEP-20 ↔ SPL, etc.). Bridges are the highest-risk component of any multichain wallet and a frequent industry hack target.
- **Saros / ecosystem expansion** — the team has spun out and incubated DeFi products (e.g., a Solana-native DEX/AMM, launchpad activity) under broader Web3 ambitions. Treat specific product status as time-sensitive and verify against current sources.

Because every component is **middleware on top of other chains' security**, Coin98 inherits the risks of those chains and of its own bridge contracts — it does not add a new base layer of security.

## Token Utility & Value Accrual

C98 is an **ERC-20-style token issued across multiple chains** (with canonical deployments on BNB Chain, Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, and others). It is used for governance, staking incentives, fee/discount mechanics within the Coin98 product suite, and access to certain features. Because C98 is a product token rather than a chain's gas token, its demand is tied to **usage of the Coin98 apps**, not to securing a blockchain.

**Value accrual critique:** wallet/aggregator tokens are notoriously weak at capturing value — users can use the wallet without ever needing the token, and swap routing earns thin fees in a competitive aggregation market. C98's demand therefore leans on governance participation and discretionary fee-discount/incentive design rather than a hard, unavoidable sink. With a **fixed 1B max supply** (fully circulating, MC/FDV ≈ 1.0), there is at least no future-dilution overhang — a positive versus inflationary peers.

## Risks

- **Product token, not infrastructure:** C98 does not secure a blockchain; its value depends on Coin98 app adoption, which faces intense competition from other multi-chain wallets.
- **Small-cap / liquidity risk:** Market cap is ~$14M (rank ~#984); thin liquidity means high volatility and slippage.
- **Counterparty/association history:** Past association with **Alameda Research** (now defunct) is a reminder that early backers do not guarantee longevity; assess current treasury, team, and runway independently.
- **Multi-chain attack surface:** Wallet and bridge products carry security risk (smart-contract bugs, bridge exploits); cross-chain bridges in particular have been frequent hack targets industry-wide.
- **Cycle risk:** Down ~99.8% from ATH in an Extreme Fear market.
